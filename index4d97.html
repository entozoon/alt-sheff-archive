Defend Tel Rumeida vigil

Sat 10 Dec 2016, 12-1pm, outside Sheffield Town Hall

Year after year, they and their fellow Palestinians in Tel Rumeida have endured physical and verbal assaults, vandalism of their property, arbitrary detention and restricted movement. They live under constant threat of harm and even death from fanatical settlers.
