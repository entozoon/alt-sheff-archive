Boycott Sodastream

Sat 28 Sep 2013, 12-1pm John Lewis, Barkers Pool

Saturday 28th September is a national day of protest against Sodastream. John Lewis sells these machines. Join the gathering at 12 noon at the Barkers Pool entrance to John Lewis. An event run by Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity Campaign.

Repair Cafe at Heeley City Farm

Sat 28 Sep 2013 10am-4pm, Heeley City Farm, Richards Road, S2 3DT

The repair cafe is a place for people who can mend stuff to bring their tools and get together with people with broken stuff, and share skills and get stuff mended over a cuppa. Bring small electrical appliances, toys, shoes, clothes, pots, kitchen equipment, computers, bikes, audio equipment, furniture, and they`ll see if they can help you mend it. And if you know anyone who is handy with repairing skills, see if they`d like to help out for an hour or two.

Contact details and direction at www.repairsheffield.org
