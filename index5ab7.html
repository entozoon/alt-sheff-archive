Sheffield gets a TV channel!

Tue 23 Sep 2014

Sheffield Live! Television is due to begin broadcasting today, via Freeview channel 8, Virgin Cable and the internet. The channel will be open, diverse and inclusive, locally owned and run for community benefit. It will broadcast news about Sheffield alongside programmes covering Sheffield music, sport, culture and much more, "Made in Sheffield, Made by you". It will collaborate with existing community radio station, Sheffield Live! which has been broadcasting via FM for nearly seven years. The license for the channel was secured after Ofcom (the media regulator) advertised licenses in Sheffield and 20 locations.

www.sheffieldlive.org

OPERA

Thu 18-Sun 28 Sep 2014, Castle House, Exchange St./Waingate,

OPERA (Open Public Experimental Residential Activity) is a Studio Polpo led initiative exploring the intersections of art, performance and living. During the Festival of the Mind Bazaar OPERA will inhabit part of Castle House in Sheffield city centre (the former Sheffield Co-operative department store). They will install temporary eating, living, sleeping and washing facilities and open this experimental residential space to invited guests and members of the public.

Each evening residents of the 'Castle House' will share a meal, discuss city centre collective living and stay the night.

For more information and to sign up visit www.experimentalresidential.wordpress.com or contact Studio Polpo: office@studiopolpo.com

Castle House tours and exhibition

Until 28 Sep 2014, Castle House, Angel St. S3 8LS (near old market)

Learn about the rich history of the iconic Sheffield co-operative department store, now sadly closed. In 1964 Castle House was officially opened by Brightside and Carbrook Co-operative, supported by 106,000 cooperative members. With a food hall, clothing, furniture, flooring, offices and a restaurant, this for many working class people was the ultimate shopping experience, and member-owned rather than capitalist.

The building is open daily 10.30am-5pm. Tours daily at 2pm can be booked at the Castle House information desk (open 10.30-5pm).

Part of Festival of the Mind, organised by the University and the city of Sheffield.
