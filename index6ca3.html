Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change

Tue 21 Jan 2014, 7pm Quaker Meeting House, St James St, S1 2EW

You are warmly invited to the next meeting of SCACC, hosting the wider Sheffield Climate Alliance, and also some people will be meeting at the Blue Moon cafe to eat beforehand from 6pm - do join them there for an informal get-together if you like.

This month they will mainly focus on two current campaigns:
- Anti-fracking locally, as part of keeping fossil fuels in the ground - what is your council doing, and what else should be done as part of the campaign?
- Climate Jobs as the alternative way forward - how can we see a major programme locally (such as set out in the mini-Stern report) investing in making homes warmer and businesses more energy efficient?

There will also be progress to report on:
- Joining the Stop Climate Chaos Coalition
- Application for funding to develop campaigning work
- Joint campaigning with churches, universities and pension funds on fossil fuel divestment

More details: SCACC website

South Yorkshire People's Assembly - 1st Annual General Meeting

Tue 21 Jan 2014, 6.30-8.30pm Sheffield Hallam University, Howard St. S1 1WB

South Yorkshire People's Assembly started last summer, as a popular opposition to the cuts and cruel policies of the current elitist government. It has a bank account and a steering group has spent time organising local meetings and activities. It is now time to consider the way forward, especially as A full National Meeting will take place in March in London, of the National People's Assembly against Austerity.

The main AGM agenda items will be to agree on a new constitution and to elect an Organising Committee.

A draft constitution has been prepared by a working group and will be presented to the meeting
for approval/amendment.

The financial accounts of SYPA will be available at the meeting and subject to approval.

They look forward to seeing you at the meeting!

To find out more about your local group and the national movement, please visit www.sypeoplesassembly.org
