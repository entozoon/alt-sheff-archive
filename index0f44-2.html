Film: "Families Like Ours"

Sat 8 Nov 2014, 7pm Royal Standard pub, St. Marys Rd. S2 4AX

A screening of Families Like Ours, a new film to raise awareness of homophobia and transphobia, made in Sheffield by members of the LGBT community. Families Like Ours is a film by the members of E.D.E.N Incubator, funded by the Big Lottery Fund Awards for All programme and engaged members of the LGBT community. The project has included workshops training the members of the LGBT community in film skills, through academic and practical sessions. They are planning further screenings through the year; if you are interested in arranging a screening please make contact.

Free entry. There will be some acoustic acts to play afterwards and a chance to meet some of the filmmakers behind the film.
