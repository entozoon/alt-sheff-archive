Performance: Courage of Conscience: Uncovering World War 1 Hidden Stories

Thu 30 Jun 2016, 11-1pm, Chesterfield Library

Stories, poetry and drama about Derbyshire conscientious objectors, and the communities who resisted and questioned war, a century ago.

The final performance, following a series of workshops since October last year, with young writers (16-30 yrs old). The workshops were led by former Derbyshire Poet Laureate River Wolton, assisted by young writers Katherine Robinson and Matthew Knighton. For more details please contact Ali Betteridge, Literature Development Officer, Derbyshire County Council alison.betteridge@derbyshire.gov.uk or tel. 01773 831359

Co-operatives Fortnight

Co-operatives Fortnight, launched in 2010, is the time when co-operatives up and down the country remind people what's great about choosing co-operation (instead of competition which capitalist businesses claim solves problems).

Among the activities will be "Big Co-op Cleans", making a difference and showing that we achieve more together than we can alone. Anyone can help to organise a Big Co-op Clean, or encourage people to choose co-operatives in other ways, with free resources from Co-operatives UK.
