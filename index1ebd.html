BIG BOULDER MUSIC & ARTS FESTIVAL

Sun 7 Sep 2014, 12-6pm in Heeley Park.

Community-owned Heeley Park, run by Heeley Development Trust, is the site of this annual free event. It includes live music, food stalls and a bar, and free or low cost arts and crafts for local children and families.

They invite artists, crafters, creatives and makers, new and small businesses, freelancers and community projects to be involved. Activities can range from stalls and exhibitions to running workshops.

Report from Heeley Online
