SHARROW FESTIVAL

Sat 13 Jun 2015, till 8pm, Mount Pleasant Park, Sharrow Lane, nr London Rd. S11 8AE

Sharrow's free multicultural festival is back earlier this year, with a special covered Community Stage, and a hip-hop jam in the all-weather pitch with Chillfoo Front (sounds) and Marco (paint jam). Loads more to see and do, including a climbing wall, drumming and street dance workshops, five-a-side football with Football Unites Racism Divides, global foods to try, a huge variety of stalls and kids' activities. HANNAH FESTIVAL

8 to 14 Jun 2015 launch week

Named after socialist and feminist activist Hannah Mitchell, Hannah Directory is a yearly print publication, launch week and website for the north of England. It celebrates great activities going on here, and helps to make more of it happen. Look out for the annual directories in leaflet racks around Sheffield and the north.

Hannah Festival

BROOMHILL FESTIVAL

5 - 21 Jun 2015, Broomhill, Sheffield 10

A great annual local community celebration, held every June since 1975, raising money for local charities. This year there are dozens of events taking place around Broomhill including performances, exhibitions, talks, literature, heritage and family events.

www.broomhillfestival.org.uk

The Great Yorkshire Vegan Festival

Sat 13 Jun 2015 IN LEEDS

Shame this is not in Sheffield, a vegan festival with 150 fantastic stalls in Leeds Town Hall, offering a wide variety of vegan products including cakes, chocolate, hot takeaway food, make up, clothing, pet treats and much more. Free samples and special offers, outdoor barbecue, cookery demonstrations, live music, history of veganism exhibit and activities for kids.

Run entirely by volunteers. Admission ¬£2 (under 16 free) on the door, all proceeds to animal welfare charities.
