Karl Marx Reading Group

Sun 8 Feb 2015, 4pm Red Deer pub (upstairs) 18 Pitt St. S1 4DD

The reading group which began with Volume 1 of "Capital" by Karl Marx, now reading "Capitalist Realism" by Mark Fisher, starting with chapters 1-5 of this small, recent book. Suggestions for further reading are being considered, such as Chavs: The Demonization of the Working Class by Owen Jones.

Newcomers are welcome, to confirm details email: reading.group.sheffield@gmail.com The Red Deer is on Pitt Street, a small road off Mappin Street, just off West Street.

One Billion Rising - preparation dance workshop: Sheffield 50+

Sun 8 Feb 2015, 11am City-Limits Dance Studios, 607-613 Penistone Rd S6 2GA

One Billion Rising is holding a flashdance as part of the global movement to end violence against women (see Saturday 14th Feb below). These events are Dance Workshops: Learn the "Break the Chain" dance with the help of experienced dance teachers, (and the practice video) in preparation for the flashdances in Sheffield and Rotherham city centres.
