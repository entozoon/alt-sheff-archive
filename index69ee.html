Sat 27 Apr-Sun 9 Jun 2019

HSBC Boycott Campaign for May Day

Sat 4 May 2019, 12.00 noon, HSBC Bank, Fargate

The 2019 May Day Boycott Action will again be outside HSBC on Fargate, with Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign (SPSC). They are now focusing particularly on Caterpillar whose equipment has a long history in the oppression of people in Palestine. Rachel Corrie, a member of International Solidarity Movement, was crushed by a Caterpillar D9 while trying to prevent the demolition of a Palestinian home.

SPSC are calling on HSBC to divest from Caterpillar. Join them outside HSBC on Fargate to tell the bank and the people of Sheffield that while the bank continues to invest in Caterpillar they are supporting Israeli war crimes.
