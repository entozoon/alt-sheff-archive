SHEFFIELD DOC/FEST

Jun 12-16, 2013

A celebration of the art and business of documentary making. In its 20th year Sheffield Doc/Fest is now world famous. As well as presenting a year-round programme of workshops, screenings, labs and mentoring opportunities, this is the week for pitching opportunities, controversial discussion panels and film-making masterclasses. There is a huge range of inspirational documentary films from across the globe and 80 other events, parties and networking opportunities. With over 2,600 film, TV and digital delegates from 60 countries, Doc/Fest 2013 is the place to be, for anyone interested in digital and factual film.

Register to attend on the website; various pricing options.
