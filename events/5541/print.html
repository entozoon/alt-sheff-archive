Festival of Debate Open Mic Night

Date, Time & Location Fri 19 Apr 2019, 7.30pm, DINA, 32 Cambridge St. S1 4HP
Details Wordlife presents an evening of social change using spoken word, featuring Jackie Hagan and Amy King on the microphone, along with people who apply (to sign up to the open mic email joe@weareopus.org.) This event is part of the "Living Together" strand of the Festival of Debate 2019, kindly sponsored by Learn Sheffield, Create Sheffield and Cohesion Sheffield. Tickets £6/£4 concessions + £1 fee (includes charity donation). Booking at Tickets For Good Festival of Debate
