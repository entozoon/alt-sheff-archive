Jazz in the Field

Date, Time & Location Sat 12 Jun 2010, 7pm-10pm Edale playing field, Edale, Hope Valley, S33 7ZA
Details A concert in aid of the Medical Foundation (caring for victims of torture) featuring the amazing Baghdaddies and local support musicians. Will be inside a marquee if the weather's bad. Licensed bar selling real locally brewed ale, barbecue and beautiful scenery. Tickets on the gate: £10 for adults/£6 concessions/free to asylum seekers and under-12's. The playing field is two minutes' walk from Edale train station. For more information www.jazzinthefield.org
