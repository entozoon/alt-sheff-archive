World Naked Bike Ride public meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 24 Mar 2010, 7.30pm at The Fat Cat pub, Alma Street S3 8SA
Details The World Naked Bike Ride is becoming a global movement, to draw attention to oil dependency and the negative social and environmental impacts of a car dominated culture. Sheffield's event will be in June, this is a discussion meeting with DVD film show, all welcome, with clothes. World Naked Bike Ride website Sheffield Naked Bike Ride website
