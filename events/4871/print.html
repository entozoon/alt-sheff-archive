"Get Yourself Together"

Date, Time & Location Thu 24 Nov 2016, 7.30pm, Theatre Delicatessen, 17 The Moor (old Woolworths shop) S1 3LR
Details A show about being ill and being fit for work. A show about the DWP and being from Bolton. A show that explores the thin line between mental health as a clinical and a political issue. Get Yourself Together is a new show from Royal Exchange Manchester support artist Josh Coates. Part of Festival of Debate 2016, hosted by not-for-profit social enterprise Opus Independents (which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine).
