Permaculture Design Course

Date, Time & Location Sat & Sun 8 & 9 Jan 2011, continuing till June
Details Permaculture is an approach to land use design based on the relationships found in natural ecologies. It combines social change with sustainability to maximise effect and minimise work. This non-residential training will cover the full design course syllabus, with a focus on urban resilience. Working in partnership with Hazelhurst CSA (Community Supported Agriculture) it will provide opportunities for practical design and implementation. Teachers: Suzi High and Joe Atkinson plus apprentices. They can help with accommodation if you are travelling from out of town. See full details and reserve your place: www.permaculture.org.uk
