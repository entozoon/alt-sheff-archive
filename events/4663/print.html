ECCLESFIELD GALA

Date, Time & Location Sun 4 Sep 2016, 12-5pm Ecclesfield Park, S35 9XL, opposite Morrisons Supermarket
Details This gala was started by Macmillan Nurses over 30 years ago and is still run by a small group of volunteers in aid of Macmillan support for people suffering from cancer, and to bring the community together. Dog Show, falconry, music and dance, wrestling, climbing wall, classic motorbikes, amusement rides, stalls and games for all the family. Ecclesfield Gala on Facebook
