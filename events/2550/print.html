Newfield Green health event

Date, Time & Location Thu 14 Feb 2013, 10am-1pm, Newfield Green Library, Heeley
Details Hazelhurst Community Supported Agriculture will be one of the stalls looking for ways to make links with other groups and engage new people at a Health Event at Newfield Green Library. People can also come along to find out about health services in the area, such as the Smoking Cessation Service, Activity Sheffield and the Wildlife Trust. If you have any ideas get in touch with naomi@hazelhurst.coop www.hazelhurst.coop
