The Big Library Debate: What Future for Sheffield Libraries?

Date, Time & Location Wed 29 May 2013, 6pm-7.30pm, Library Theatre, Tudor Square
Details We know that tens of billions worth of tax are avoided by companies such as Starbucks each year; yet Starbucks keep multiple outlets open in Sheffield whilst non-profit making libraries face closures. What has gone wrong here? People must share their voice and make their feelings known. One way to do this is to join the Big Library Debate, featuring award-winning children's author and library campaigner Alan Gibbons, and the people of Sheffield.
