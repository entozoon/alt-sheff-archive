"Making Immigration Work"

Date, Time & Location Mon 30 March 2015, 5:45-7pm Town Hall (Mandela Room)
Details Professor Randall Hansen from the University of Toronto talks on the theme of ‘Making immigration work: How Britain and Europe can overcome their immigration crises’. Free entry, but please register your attendance. Organised in association with the Political Studies Association Annual Conference and hosted by the Crick Centre. Part of the Festival of Debate taking place in Sheffield in the run-up to the General election, during March and April. It provides opportunities to get together and discuss a range of issues which affect everyone. Informed decision making and strategic planning are essential for democracy, so come and get involved. They also welcome volunteers, from attending events and taking part in discussions to volunteering time to help run an event, promote the festival and much more. Unless otherwise stated events are free to attend, but if possible please register via the appropriate Eventbrite link on the Festival of Debate website below to give organsisers an idea of numbers.
