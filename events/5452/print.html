Public meeting: Casualisation in Education

Date, Time & Location Tue 18 Dec 2018, 7-8.30pm, Sheffield University Arts Tower, Western Bank S10 2TN
Details A meeting about Casualisation in Education, with special guest Louise Haigh MP. Higher education casual contracts and zero hours contracts have become the norm in UK Universities. This meeting is to publicise what is happening, and campaigning work to fight against this. Organised by UCU and Save Staff Pensions. Facebook event page
