SHEFFIELD PRIDE

Date, Time & Location Sat 2 Jun 2012, till 7pm, Endcliffe Park
Details A great annual event celebrating the lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgendered (LGBT) community, open to all. Organised by a committee of elected volunteers, the event helps to increase the visibility of an often marginalised community, highlighting homophobia but most importantly celebrating the differences as well as the similarities between individuals of the LGBT community. And inviting everyone to a big party! Parade from 11am along Ecclesall Road, the festival runs till 7pm.
