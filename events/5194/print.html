Sheffield Green Party Annual General Meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 8 Jan 2018, 7pm, Central United Reformed Church, 60 Norfolk Row S1 2JB
Details Members and supporters of Sheffield Green Party are invited to this monthly meeting which includes the Annual General Meeting. The agenda includes: Westways Primary School dispute and strike and the current state of education. Toby Mallinson (National Education Union) Peter Garbutt (Sheffield Green Party) Plus contributions from those involved in education Q&A (Questions and answers discussion) Followed by a short formal AGM and pub visit. Refreshments served. Arrive at 7.00pm for 7.15pm start; ends 9.00pm. Central United Reformed Church is on the corner of Chapel Walk and Norfolk Street. Sheffield Green Party
