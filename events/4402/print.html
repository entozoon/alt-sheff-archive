Owen Jones - The Politics of Hope

Date, Time & Location Mon 29 Feb 2016, 8pm, The Octagon, University of Sheffield
Details As part of Off the Shelf Festival, a talk by Sheffield-born Owen Jones, columnist for The Guardian and New Statesman. His first book, the international bestseller "Chavs", was long-listed for The Guardian First Book Award. His second book, "The Establishment - And How They Get Away With It", has won yet more critical acclaim. Owen will talk about his writing and work, his passionate views on political and economic injustice and his belief in solidarity and vision for the future - the politics of hope.
