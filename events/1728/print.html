&#39;Creative Philosophy&#39; class for young people between 14-17 years

Date, Time & Location Mon 7 Nov 2011, 6pm, 7 Garden St. Sheffield S1 4BJ
Details A free 'Creative Philosophy' class as part of the Pandemic situationist arts events. All those between 14 and 17 years old are welcome, so if you know anyone who might be interested, please pass this on. This is a class devised by Mike Drabble, who has taught it in various schools and colleges around Sheffield and Wakefield. He is fully CRB checked. This course has in the past helped students in improved critical thinking, planning and prioritising, confidence and public speaking. Anyone interested please contact pandemic.sheffield@gmail.com
