Global Justice Sheffield campaigning meeting

Date, Time & Location Thu 3 Sep 2015, 7-9pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Global Justice aims to change the actions, policies and institutions of governments, corporations and wealthy minorities, which are preventing an end to poverty and injustice. Part of a global movement, it works alongside people protesting in the developing world. The organising meetings are to discuss forthcoming events and campaigns. Global Justice Sheffield
