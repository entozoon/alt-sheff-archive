Friends of the Earth meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 15 Mar 2010, 8pm at the Red Deer pub, 18 Pitt Street, Sheffield, off West Street (upstairs meeting room)
Details Friends of the Earth Sheffield Group are people from all walks of life who share a common concern for the state of the planet and a desire to do something about it. Membership of Sheffield Friends of the Earth is free if you subscribe to the electronic newsletter. Sheffield FOE website
