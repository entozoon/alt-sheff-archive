South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG) meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 26 Jun 2012, 6.30pm Scotia Works, Leadmill Road S1 4SE
Details SYMAAG unites refugee, asylum and migrant organisations with campaign groups and supporting individuals. It campaigns both for the rights of those seeking asylum and of those coming to work in this country. If you want to come to any SYMAAG meeting but find it hard to afford transport do not be put off! Please bring your ticket or a receipt and SYMAAG can pay you back at the meeting, or check in advance. www.symaag.org.uk Twitter: @SYMAAG Tel: Sheff 2412780
