Candlelit vigil for refugee children

Date, Time & Location Fri 2 Dec 2016, 5pm-6pm, meet at Sheffield train station
Details Sheffield Stand Up To Racism is holding this candlelit vigil for refugee children. Meet at Sheffield train station for a walk to the Town Hall where speeches will be held. Please bring candles and children's shoes to symbolise the horrendous journey of many refugee children. Facebook event page
