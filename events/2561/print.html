"Is Leninism dead?"

Date, Time & Location Mon 18 Feb 2013, 7.30pm, Bath Hotel, Victoria St. S3 7QL
Details Is Leninism dead? - A Workers Liberty Public meeting. With the crisis in the Socialist Workers' Party bringing into question, for some, the concepts of Leninism and democratic centalism - should the left still use the ideas of Lenin to organise? Why do organisations such as Workers' Liberty think we have a lot to learn from Lenin? Is this the death of Leninism?
