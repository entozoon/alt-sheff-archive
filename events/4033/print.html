Peace and Justice Public Meeting: Central Constituency

Date, Time & Location Wed 22 Apr 2015, 7pm Friends Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Four organisations have joined together to put the issues of peace and justice into the election. The issues are the wars abroad, middle East, Palestine, Trident, and Islamophobia. Come along with your questions for Central Ward election candidates. Attending: Paul Blomfield (Labour) Joe Otten (Liberal Democrats) Peter Garbutt (Green) Stephie Roe (Conservative) (invited, not confirmed) The organising groups are Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign, Sheffield CND, Sheffield Stop the War Coalition, and Sheffield Yemeni Community Association
