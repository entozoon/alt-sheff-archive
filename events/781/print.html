Film: 'City of Life and Death'

Date, Time & Location 23-29 Jul 2010 at the Showroom Cinema
Details Director Chuan Lu's third feature film takes place in 1937 during the infamously brutal Nanking Massacre. The film tells the story of several figures both historical and fictional, including a Chinese soldier, a school teacher and a Nazi businessman. www.showroomworkstation.org.uk/cityoflifeanddeath
