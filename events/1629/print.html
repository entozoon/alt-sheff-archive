Course: Hazel Hurdle Making

Date, Time & Location Sat 5 Nov 2011, Sheffield
Details Hurdles are traditional fence panels that have been used for a variety of different functions, from animal enclosures to walls of houses, for thousands of years. This course will give you a chance to learn with Alistair Hayhurst - a master of this craft. Low-Impact Living Initiative (LILI) is a non-profit organisation helping people reduce their impact on the environment, improve their quality of life, gain new skills, live in a healthier and more satisfying way, have fun and save money.
