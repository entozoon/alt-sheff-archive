Budget Day PROTEST

Date, Time & Location Wed 3 Dec 2014, 5.30-6.30pm Sheffield Town Hall
Details As people struggle to make ends meet, millionaire Gideon Osborne will yet again tell parliament on Wednesday that austerity is here to stay. In fact he will go even further than previously and announce that cuts be enshrined into law for the next few years. We all know what this means: * More food banks * Tougher benefit sanctions * Scapegoating of migrants * Misery for thousands Show your anger at the government - bring pots and pans/anything that makes lots of noise! Local activists have been invited to speak. Sheffield People's Assembly Facebook event page
