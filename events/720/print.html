Free School Weekend meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 22 Jun 2010, 7pm Rutland Arms (in the beer garden) 86 Brown Street, Sheffield S1 2BS
Details Sheffield Social Centre Free School Weekend will be held 9-11 July 2010. It'll be a learning and skill-sharing event with evening music and words events. Sheffield Social Centre is a collective for libertarian DIY space in the city, with core principles of non-hierarchy, anti-capitalism and equality/non-discrimination. Interested? If you think you would like to run a workshop come along to this organising meeting or get in touch to apply through the website: http://sheffieldsocialcentre.org.uk
