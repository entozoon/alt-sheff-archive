Introduction to Wood Carving for Adults

Date, Time & Location Sat 22 Feb 2014, 9.30-4pm, J G Graves Woodland Discovery Centre, Ecclesall Woods, Abbey Lane S7 2QZ
Details A one-day, hands-on course course to introduce students to the skills and techniques of woodcarving, to boost their confidence and enthusiasm to take on new projects. Gain a good understanding of the fundamentals of woodcarving; you can expect to complete a piece of work in the time available. It includes health and safety, basic design work, how to sharpen and use a range of tools, ways of holding and securing work, roughing-out and initial wood removal, developing and detailing the work with various tools and surface treatment and finishing.
