Pre-election Hustings: What About Women?

Date, Time & Location Tues 23 Mar 2010, 7.15pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW (near Anglican cathedral)
Details All welcome to hear parliamentary candidates for Sheffield seats from the Conservative, Green, Labour (TBC) and Liberal Democrat parties each explain what their party plans to do for women both nationally and internationally, followed by questions and answers from the floor.
