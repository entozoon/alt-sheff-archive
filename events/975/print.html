Talk: Mapping world religions and religiosity

Date, Time & Location Wed 6 Oct 2010, 8pm at University Arms, 197 Brook Hill, Sheffield, S3 7HG
Details A talk by Danny Dorling, Sheffield University. The 2nd edition of the Real World Atlas (of which Danny Dorling is one of three authors) is being published in October. It contains a new set of maps on the distribution of people around the world by religion. Danny will show these maps and examine the issues of how people people come to believe what they believe, on both religion and human nature, what it is possible for people to achieve and when we might collectively fail. A talk open to the public, organised by the Sheffield Humanist Society, all welcome. University Arms is near Sheffield University tram stop. By bus: 51 & 52 best; 94 & 95 close by. www.sheffieldhumanists.org.uk
