Peace in the Park meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 27 Apr 2011, 6pm at Highfield Library, London Rd. S2 4NF
Details The Peace in the Park group are planning for this summer's festival. All meetings are open to anybody, so don't be shy if you want to get involved or have any ideas. All 2011 meetings are at Highfield Library from 6pm and until 8pm (often followed by pub), dates follow the pattern of first Tuesday and last Wednesday of each month.
