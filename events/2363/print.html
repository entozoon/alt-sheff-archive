LaDIYfest Sheffield opening party

Date, Time & Location Fri 2 Nov 2012, 7pm-11pm, Harland Cafe, 72 John St. (off Bramall Lane/London Rd.) S2 4QU
Details Sheffield’s anti-capitalist, feminist festival LaDIYfest returns for its second year, taking over venues across the city for a weekend of practical activities, discussion workshops, spoken word and live music from 2nd – 4th November with all funds raised going to local women’s charities. Music and drinks to ease you into the weekend, open to all. Donations for charities on the door instead of tickets. https://www.facebook.com/events/163730467098116 www.ladiyfestsheffield.wordpress.com
