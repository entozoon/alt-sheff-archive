South Yorkshire Miners’ Strike Anniversary: Public Meeting

Date, Time & Location Fri 7 Mar 2014, 7pm, Sheffield Library Theatre, Surrey St. S1 1XZ ‎
Details 30 years after the Great Miners’ Strike to mark the anniversary, a public rally organised by South Yorks NUJ (National Union of Journalists). Speakers: Ken Capstick, former NUM chairman Flis Callow, Women Against Pit Closures Caroline Poland, Women Against Pit Closures Barbara Jackson, Orgreave Truth and Justice Campaign Arthur Critchlow, striking miner arrested at Orgreave Mike Simons, journalist plus film clips from forthcoming film The Enemy Within, and a photo exhibition.
