South Yorkshire Refugee Law and Justice is a small charity working in partnership with others in the city. It helps asylum seekers to make fresh claims for protection when they cannot find lawyers and have nowhere else to turn. This fundraiser is a major event for SYRLJ - any money raised will make a huge difference to their ability to give practical support to asylum seekers in the most need.
