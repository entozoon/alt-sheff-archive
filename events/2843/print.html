Steel City Punx Picnic 2013

Date, Time & Location Sat 13 Jul 2013, from 11am, Penelopes, 53-55 Arundel Gate S1 2DN
Details All day punk festival, with vegan BBQ fundraiser for Sheffield Hunt Sabs, stalls and loads of bands: Oi Polloi - Edinburgh Oi tinged Anarcho-Punk legends Bus Station Loonies - Anarcho Cabaret punks Happy Spastics - Anarchist punks from Edinburgh. Captain Hotknives - Comedy stoner bloke from Bratfud Slug - Anarcho punk from London Noise Complaint - Thrash Punk from London 4 Past Midnight - Punk rock from Glasgow Hated Til Proven - Thrashy political skacore from Warrington Total Bloody Chaos - loud no frills punks from Blackburn Septic Psychos - Chesterfield punk rocks The Lagan - London based Celtic punk Brassick - Raw female fronted punks from Worcester Dry Heaves - Sheffield old school Hardcore The Tinnitus Kids - New noisy two piece from Edinburgh All ages welcome, under 16s accompanied by adult. (Venue note: Penelopes door is on the side of the Odeon Cinema building). Doors 12 noon till 12 midnight. £12 advance, £14 on the door. Tickets and full details at Sheffield Punk Gigs
