Interfaith Walk

Date, Time & Location Sat 21 Nov 2015, 1.15pm Mother of God Roman Catholic Church, Abbeydale Rd S7
Details An interfaith walk, everybody welcome, no need to book. Also visits Medina Masjid Mosque, Wolseley Road, and Airey Fairey, London Road. Finishes approx 4.00 pm, Cemetery Road Baptist Church. Total distance about 2 miles. There will be a short talk at each place about their beliefs, buildings and what goes on there. Plus opportunity to talk with people from all faiths or none, as you walk. Refreshments can be purchased at Airey Fairey. Sheffield City of Sanctuary
