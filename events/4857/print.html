Principle 5 co-op resource base AGM

Date, Time & Location Tue 22 Nov 2016, 3pm Union Street co-op, 18-20 Union St. S1 2JP
Details The annual general meeting of "Principle 5" co-operative resources base, which aims to provide education about the nature and benefits of co-operation, for the development of co-operatives. They are building a library of books and other materials, and hold training sessions. ("Principle 5" of the International Statement of Co-operative Identity is about education.) All welcome. Arrive 2.30 for 3.00pm start. Union Street co-working space co-op also sells food/refreshments. The meeting will be on the ground floor. Principle 5
