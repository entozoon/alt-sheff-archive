Remploy workers campaign

Date, Time & Location Sat 24 Sep 2011, 10.30-2.00 outside the Town Hall
Details Remploy workers are campaigning with the GMB union to save their jobs against threatened closure of factories. Sheffield Anti Cuts Alliance (SACA) will join them 12.00-2.00, and continue leafleting for TUC-backed demonstration at the Tory Party Conference October 2nd in Manchester. All support welcome. Sheffield Anti Cuts Alliance is a grass roots working class anti cuts organisation bringing together trade unionists, campaigners, students, the unemployed, pensioners and disabled people against the cuts. http://sheffieldanticuts.wordpress.com
