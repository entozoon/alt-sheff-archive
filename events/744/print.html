Stannington Carnival

Date, Time & Location Sat 10 Jul 2010 Stannington playing fields and in Lomas Hall
Details This is one of the most popular carnivals in Sheffield in terms of numbers of people attending, with stalls, bouncy castles, entertainment, competitions, displays and stuff. Local groups' opportunity to raise funds and promote themselves, profits to local organisations. Not sure of times, doesn't seem to have a website. Contact for details: pm.wray@tiscali.co.uk
