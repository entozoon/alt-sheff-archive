Film and discussion evening: GM food

Date, Time & Location Thu 11 Nov 2010, 7.30pm, The Harlequin, Nursery Street S3 8GG
Details Genetically modified food (GM): What is it? what are the issues? In the 1990s growing GM commercially food was not allowed. Now there is big business pressure and government looks set to allow this as soon as 2013. Come and find out more at an evening of films and discussion about genetic engineering and genetically modified organisms (GMOs). Free entry, open to all. 'Say No to GMO' website
