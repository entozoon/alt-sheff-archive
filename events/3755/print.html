Sheffield Friday Night Ride: Yoga

Date, Time & Location Fri 7 Nov 2014, meet Public Entrance to Ponds Forge 6.30pm
Details Sheffield Friday Night Ride (FNR) offers a fun monthly evening bike ride for everyone. Rides are prepared in advance on themes. This time: Yoga. As so many Yoga centres are popping up in Sheffield, a ride that starts in the city centre at a generic gym, Ponds Forge. Then riding to places where different forms of yoga takes place: Ashtanga, Buddhist philosopy/8 limbs of yoga and finishing at the Sheffield Yoga Centre for a cup of tea and a 30 minute taught Iyengar lesson specifically designed for cyclists.
