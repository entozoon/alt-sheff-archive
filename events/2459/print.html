Solstice Moment of Peace - 11:11am

Date, Time & Location Can be done anywhere or join the gahering inThe Winter Garden - gather at 11:00 for Moment of Peace at 11:11
Details The 2012 winter solstice has become a focal point for ideas about planetary change. A unique opportunity exists to harness this attention into a global event that celebrates the essential unity of humanity and the power of positive intention. Millions of people around the world are choosing to pause at the same time, on the same day, with the same intention of envisioning a world that benefits everything and everyone! Whatever your background, wherever you are, join in the global moment of prayer, meditation and positive intention at this time. If you can make it join the gathering in the Winter Gardens in Sheffield centre www.peace2012.net and www.unify.org
