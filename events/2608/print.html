One Step meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 3 Apr 2013, 8pm, Walkley/Upperthorpe
Details One Step is a small group that meets once a month to find practical ways to take one step a day, a week or a month to leave the world a better place. This month they are looking to help each other to get started (or better) with growing food. Please email contact@one-step.org.uk for the location, or see www.one-step.org.uk for more information about the group.
