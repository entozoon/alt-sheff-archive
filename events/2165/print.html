Family Bike Ride to Blackburn Meadows

Date, Time & Location Sun 15 Jul 2012, 10am from Effingham Street, Sheffield 4
Details Family friendly bike ride of 12 miles, at an easy pace, children welcome, no special bike or equipment needed. It will mostly be along the Five Weirs Walk. Sheffield District Cyclists Touring Club (CTC) is celebrating the club centenary this year. It covers South Yorkshire and parts of North Derbyshire. www.sheffieldctc.org
