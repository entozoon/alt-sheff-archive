The World Development Movement: Examining Fairtrade

Date, Time & Location Thu 1 Sep 2011, 7-9pm, United Reform Church 60 Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details The Sheffield branch of the World Development Movement (WDM) hosts a discussion on Fairtrade, how we campaign on this issue, the pros and cons of fairtrade and a national consultation by the Fairtrade Foundation. With Alison Trezise from SHIFT (Sheffield Initiative on Fairtrade). WDM Sheffield website
