What way out of the economic and ecological crises?

Date, Time & Location Sat 24 Apr 2010, 10am-12.30, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James Street, Sheffield S1 2EW
Details A meeting to discuss and debate the origins of the global economic and ecological crises, and progressive policies to address them. The meeting will be of particular interest to labour movement, community and social movements activists. Organised by the Sheffield Workers Educational Association (WEA) Speakers: Dr Jamie Gough and Dr John Smith, both Faculty of Social Science, Sheffield University, and Professor Ian Rotherham, Faculty of Development and Society, Sheffield Hallam University. Open to all. Free. Refreshments provided.
