Hazelhurst Community Supported Agriculture - AGM

Date, Time & Location Mon 29 Sep 2014, Sharrow Old Junior School, South View Rd S7 1DB
Details Annual general meeting of the Hazelhurst Community Supported Agriculture co-operative. Please arrive at 6.30pm for a 7pm start; end 9pm. There will be light refreshments available. Members could bring some food to share. There will be places on the committee available in 2014/15, including that of Secretary. Members please do consider whether you would like to stand for election, and take forward exciting plans in the coming year. If you are thinking of standing, you can let the Management Committee know in advance, by email. Similarly, if you want to bring a resolution to the AGM for consideration by the membership, please email resolution in advance no later than 15th September 2014. Hazelhurst CSA co-operative
