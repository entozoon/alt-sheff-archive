"Stop G4S" talk

Date, Time & Location Wed 12 Jun 2013, 7.30pm - 9.00pm St Matthews Carver Street S1 4FE
Details Palestine solidarity members have long had resaons to oppose security company G4S for its complicity in running Israeli security in the West Bank and Israel. Now they are joined by campaigners for asylum seekers, and against privatisation. G4S has been involved in brutal deportations and prisons. This meeting includes speakers from these various strands. The doors open for coffee at 7pm Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity Campaign
