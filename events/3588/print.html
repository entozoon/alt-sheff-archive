WALKLEY FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sat 21 Jun-6 Jul 2014, Walkley Sheffield 6
Details The 13th Annual Walkley Festival is held over two weeks, at various locations, showing Walkley talent and diversity. Includes Ruskin Park Fun Day with 5 hours of live music, Whacky Bike Race and Horse Race, Walkley’s Wonderful Art Show, Fairs at the Buddhist Centre, St Bart’s Church and tea parties, Flower Festival and readings by Walkley Feral Writers, and far more.
