"Battle of the Empty Stomachs"

Date, Time & Location Thu 27 Feb 2014, 6pm-7pm, Sheffield University Arts Tower, Lecture Theatre 3
Details As part of Israeli Apartheid Week 2014, The Palestinian Society of Sheffield University presents a video-link to Samer Al-Issawi, refused permission by the Israeli government to travel to the UK. Hear what the man who went on a hunger strike for 277 days has to say, and get to ask him questions in person. Please try and be there on time, to minimise disruption during the call.
