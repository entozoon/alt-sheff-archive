Climate questions hustings

Date, Time & Location Thurs 15 Apr 2010, 7pm (NOT 8pm earlier stated) Central United Reformed Church
Details Another chance to quiz the candidates, at a hustings organised by Friends of the Earth with World Development Movement (WDM) and Sheffield Campaign Against Climate Change (SCACC) The speakers will be:
  • Nicola Bates, Conservative candidate Sheffield Hallam
  • Paul Blomfield, Labour candidate Sheffield Central
  • Jillian Creasy, Green candidate Sheffield Central
  • Paul Scriven, Lib Dem candidate Sheffield Central
Venue: Central United Reformed Church, 60 Norfolk St. Sheffield S1 2JB (Norfolk Room, on the ground floor). Start 7pm (refreshments available) for 7.30pm - 9pm, not 8pm start as earlier stated.
