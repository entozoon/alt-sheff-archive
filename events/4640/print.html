Convoy to Calais

Date, Time & Location Sat 18 Jun 2016
Details The Convoy to Calais is a huge national demonstration of solidarity with refugees, supported by unions, pressure groups and social movements. They need people to come on the convoy from Sheffield and to donate money to buy goods required by the refugees in the Calais camp. This is practical aid and a huge moving protest at the way governments across the continent are failing refugees.
