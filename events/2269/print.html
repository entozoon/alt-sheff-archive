SCACC AGM and Public Meeting - A Frack-Free Future: Opposing UK&#39;s second "Dash for Gas"

Date, Time & Location Tues 16 Oct 2012, 6.45/7.30pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Sheffield Campaign Against Climate Change holds its AGM at 6.45pm followed by a public meeting from 7.30 with speakers from Frack Off and Friends of the Earth. The subject will be the latest crazy idea to extract gas by pumping chemicals underground, puncturing and poisoning the water table and even causing earth tremors.
