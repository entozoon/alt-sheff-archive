Abolish Exams! A Socialist Vision of Education

Date, Time & Location Mon 3 Jun 2019, 6pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St. James St. S1 2EW
Details Testing in schools has nothing to do with learning. The market-and-targets-driven discipline is causing a mental health crisis in children, and stress for teachers, but there are alternative socialist visions for education.
