Repair Cafe

Sat 26 Jul 2014 10am-4pm, Reception Classroom by the pond, Heeley City Farm, Richards Rd. S2 3DT

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Fix and reuse, don't throw it away! Repair Cafe is a regular workshop event hosted by Repair Sheffield. Broken small household items? (toasters, shoes, clocks, weighing scales, etc.) Bring them along and the volunteer fixers will have a go at mending them. This isn't just a fixing service though, they'll show you how to mend it for yourself and pass on valuable knowledge on how to do things for yourself - including getting repairs done with confidence of what's wrong. There is no charge but if an item is successfully fixed a small donation towards costs would be welcome. All the fixers are volunteers and the project is unfunded. Contact for more information at info@repairsheffield.orgHeeley City Farm
