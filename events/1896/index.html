Sheffield Humanist Society talk: Uganda Humanist Schools Trust

Wed 7 Mar 2012, 7.30 for 8pm, University Arms, 197 Brook Hill, S3 7HG

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Steve Hurd presents a talk organised by the Sheffield Humanist Society. Uganda Humanist Schools Trust: Opportunities and Challenges. The Trust helps a number of schools in Uganda who are working to offer liberal, humanist education to needy children. Founded by Ugandan humanists and run as charities, the schools are inclusive and do not discriminate on grounds of religion, social or ethnic background. Steve Hurd founded the UHST after working in Uganda as a teacher and director of development projects.Talk open to the public, all welcome. Sheffield University tram stop, or bus: 51 & 52 best; 94 & 95 close by.Uganda Humanist Schools Trust www.sheffieldhumanists.org.uk
