SYMAAG women's meeting

Date, Time & Location Thurs 10 Dec 2009, 11am at St.Matthews Church rooms, Carver Street, Sheffield S1 4FT
Details Meeting to plan future campaigns for the rights of women seeking asylum. All women welcome. South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG) is a campaigning group which aims to improve the treatment of people who come to South Yorkshire, whether because of threats to their personal safety in their home country or for economic reasons.
