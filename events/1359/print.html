Yes to Fairer Votes in Broomhill

Date, Time & Location Sat 12 Mar 2011, 12 noon, Meet outside Boots
Details The Sheffield Yes to Fairer Votes Campaign will be leafletting the Broomhill shopping centre, to inform people about the referendum on the Alternative Vote to be held on 5th May, and to let people know why we are campaigning for a Yes vote.
