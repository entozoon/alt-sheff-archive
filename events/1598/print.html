Green Party Conference comes to Sheffield

Date, Time & Location Fri 9-Tue 13 Sep 2011, Sheffield Hallam city campus, Howard St. S1 1WB
Details The UK Green Party is holding its annual conference in Sheffield this year, with attendance of around 500 expected. Non-members interested in attending have three options: Book as an 'observer' at the same rates as members allowing you to attend all sessions but without voting rights. Or attend panel discussions open to the public without charge. Or cut price one day attendance offer. Fee refundable if you join the party at the venue during the conference!
