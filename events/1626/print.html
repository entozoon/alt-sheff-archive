Course: Green Wood Stool Making

Date, Time & Location Sat 1 & Sat 8 Oct 2011, Sheffield
Details Two-day course on the ancient craft of making stools, from selecting the wood to construction, including making the legs on a pole lathe. Low-Impact Living Initiative (LILI) is a non-profit organisation whose mission is to help people reduce their impact on the environment, improve their quality of life, gain new skills, live in a healthier and more satisfying way, have fun and save money.
