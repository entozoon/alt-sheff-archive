"Does Your Face Fit?"

Date, Time & Location Sat 30 Nov 2013, 11am-3pm, Sharrow Old Junior School, South View Rd. S7 1DB
Details An event which showcases and celebrates the work that young volunteers have done this year around cultural diversity in Sheffield. Celebrate multicultural Sheffield through an afternoon of inter-cultural food, music, dancing, theatre and fashion. A halal lunch will be provided. Does Your Face Fit Team: 07804 871110 Facebook page
