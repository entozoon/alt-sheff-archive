Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 18 Sep 2012, 7pm-9pm at Quaker Meeting House, 10 St. James St. S1 2EW
Details SCACC organises days of action and demonstrations, petitions to Sheffield Council, supports related national and local campaigns and runs film shows and talks for local groups. The regular meeting is open to newcomers, all welcome, generally on the 3rd Tuesday of the month. Anyone interested in climate campaigning and linking their organisation to Sheffield Climate Alliance is welcome to attend. From 6.00 pm the same evening there is also an informal pre-meet in the Blue Moon Cafe. France has already declared itself a FRACK-FREE zone but what about the Sheffield City Region?
