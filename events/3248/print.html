South Yorkshire Unite Community Branch AGM

Date, Time & Location Wed 12 Feb 2014, 7pm, Central United Reform Church, Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details S.Y. Community branch is now 1 year old, with over 200 members, and proud to have defended some of the most vulnerable people in our community, providing support and victories for benefit claimants both in and outside court. Actions have included direct action against workfare providers, ATOS assessments, and zero-hour contracts, stood up to the dangers of racism and fascism, and supported workers in struggle. It works closely with the South Yorkshire Peoples' Assembly and Sheffield Anti Cuts Alliance. This Annual General Meeting includes: National Unite speaker. Report from NEYH community co-ordinater Report from Branch secretary And election of officers. Free buffet and drinks (open to all). Only branch members can vote so if you are, please bring your union card. Facebook event page www.facebook.com/UniteCommunitySheffield
