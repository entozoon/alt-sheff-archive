Peace in the Park meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 26 Nov 2013, 6pm-8pm, Regather 57-59 Club Garden Rd. S11 8BU
Details The volunteers are again organising Sheffield’s annual free Peace in the Park festival for June 2014. Profits go to good causes and it’s a fantastic event. Don’t be shy if you want to get involved or have any ideas, come along to one of the open meetings, these are open to everybody, every two weeks, alternating between Tuesday and Wednesday.
