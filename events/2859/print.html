Cycle Sheffield meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 5 Aug 2013, 7.30pm, Broomhall Centre, Broomspring Lane, S10 2FD
Details The monthly meeting of Cycle Sheffield, followed by pub social. This activist group is for all kinds of cyclists; it organises events, information and rides, and campaigns for cycling to be taken seriously as a means of transport. www.cyclesheffield.org.uk
