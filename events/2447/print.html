Sheffield Anticuts Alliance meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 11 Dec 2012, 7-9 pm, Central United Reformed Church, 60 Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details The December meeting of Sheffield Anticuts Alliance, a grass roots working class anti-cuts organisation bringing together trade unionists, campaigners, students, the unemployed, pensioners and disabled people against the cuts. All welcome. Please submit items for the agenda by Monday 10th December. Note the venue is on the corner of Chapel Walk and Norfolk Street, use main entrance and follow corridor round to back of venue. www.sheffieldanticuts.wordpress.com
