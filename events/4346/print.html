Regather Cycling Film Night - ShAff Takeover

Date, Time & Location Thu 14 Jan 2016, 7pm Regather Works, 57-59 Club Garden Rd. S11 8BU
Details From his humble beginnings in his hometown of Sheffield, England, Steve Peat has established himself as the one of the most iconic cyclists in the world. Through 20 years of archival footage, photos, intimate interviews with Steve, this is a captivating tour of the history of the sport of downhill mountain bike racing and offers a never-before-seen view into the life and legacy of this enduring icon. Entry £7, £5 Unwaged/Concessions. Full details at www.regather.net
