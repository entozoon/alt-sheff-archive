Sharrow Festival and Fringe

Date, Time & Location 26 Jun - 11 July 2010
Details Sharrow's big annual celebration, with music from blues to bhangra, food, circus and drumming workshops, and loads for kids to do including graffiti walls. Information stalls on political, health, charity and community issues. And it's all free. The Fringe Festival runs for the two weeks around the actual Sharrow Festival, dates above, and the main event is 3 July 12-8pm Mount Pleasant Park, S11 8AE. Website and more details
