Beer and Music Fest

Date, Time & Location 8-10th of Sep 2017, Gardeners Rest, Gardeners Rest, 105 Neepsend Lane S3 8AT
Details To celebrate the Gardeners Rest becoming a community owned pub, a beer and music festival to show what they are all about, good music, real ale and good times. There is a top line up of music over the weekend: Headlining Friday: 2 Birds One Stone Captives on the Carousel Then Saturday a whole day of music with: Martin Christie Ichabod Morigans Fire Before Breakfast Banjo Jen and 20ft Squid Blues Band On the Sunday: 12 dead in Everett Vivacity Choir and Jenny Hankin The Gardeners Rest
