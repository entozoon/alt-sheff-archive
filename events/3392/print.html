Learn to Make Spoons and Spatulas course

Date, Time & Location Sun 18 May 2014 9.30am-4pm, Woodland Discovery Centre, Ecclesall Woods, Abbey Lane S7 2QZ
Details A one day course with Dave Jackson, at the J G Graves Woodland Discovery Centre, in beautiful Ecclesall Woods. Learn the basics of splitting and shaping greenwood using a pole lathe and make rolling pins, spoons or spatulas. Suitable for complete novices and those with experience. www.ecclesallwoodscraftcourses.co.uk
