SHEFFIELD ZINE FEST

Date, Time & Location Sat 30 Apr 2016, 11am-4pm HUBS Building, Paternoster Row S1 2QQ
Details The 4th annual Sheffield Zine Fest, a lively event celebrating and selling DIY magazines, comics and other literature. Part of the Year of Making. This year it is supported by SHU Students Union LGBT+ Liberation Group and fundraising for SAYiT (Sheffield LGBT youth group). Girl Gang Sheffield provide evening entertainments. Sheffield Zine Fest
