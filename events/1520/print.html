Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change (SCACC) meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 14 Jun 2011, 8pm-9.30pm at Highfield Library, London Rd. S2 4NF
Details SCACC organises days of action and demonstrations, petitions to Sheffield Council, supports related national and local campaigns and runs film shows and talks for local groups. The regular meeting is open to newcomers, all welcome. It is usually on the 2nd Tuesday of each month. SCACC website
