Exhibition: A Nonviolent response to Terrorism?

Date, Time & Location Sat 6 Mar to Sat 20 Mar 2010, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James Street, S1 2EW (near Anglican cathedral)
Details An exhibition giving examples of terrorism and peaceful action to tackle it. Non-violence requires creative thinking and activity - at street level, between communities, within countries and on a global scale. The need is to link together aspects of peace, security, development and the environment. Opening times: Monday–Thursday: 9am–5pm and 7pm–9pm, Fridays: 9am–4.30pm Saturday: 9am–2.30pm For more details contact Frances Morgan tel Sheff 2560187 www.sheffieldquakers.org.uk
