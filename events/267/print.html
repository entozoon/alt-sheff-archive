Language Exchange event

Date, Time & Location Tues 15 Dec, 6 - 8pm at The HUBs, Hallam Union, 6 Paternoster Row, Sheffield (opposite the Showroom Cinema)
Details Free, fun event bringing people together to meet, share languages, celebrate cultures and give people chance to speak their native language or learn a new one. Run by Languages Sheffield/Hallam Volunteering. All welcome (not just students).
