Green Drinks Social Evening

Date, Time & Location Thu 16 Apr 2015, 7pm, Upstairs; Nourish Cafe (opposite Town Hall) 18 Pinstone St. S1 2HN 17
Details You are warmly invited to join Sheffield and Rotherham Green Party people for a pizza party at the Sheffield Campaign HQ while watching the Leaders' Debates. Come along and enjoy pizza, good company, and the excitement of the campaign. The debate starts at 8pm, but come from 7pm to get a good seat and enjoy the build up. Pizzas will be paid for by Sheffield and Rotherham Green Party, but any donations towards cost will be most appreciated. Feel free to bring beverages and your own glass/corkscrew, etc. Sheffield Green Party
