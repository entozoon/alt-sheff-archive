Northern Feminist Picnic

Date, Time & Location Sat 31 Aug 2013, from 2pm, Weston Park
Details Feminist and queer LaDIYFest picnic and hangout to share schemes and ideas. Bring veggie/vegan food to share or just bring a packed lunch. Please help spread the word and invite as many people as possible from as far away as possible! LaDIYFest Sheffield is a DIY, anti-capitalist, community based, feminist group run by volunteers, led primarily by women but all are welcome to get involved. It aims to highlight female and LGBTQ art, projects and activism in the community, promoting social change in a non-discriminatory environment. Weston Park is a 5 min walk from Sheffield University tram stop. Coming by train, just hop on the blue route supertram from the train station. At Weston Park, meet by the bandstand. http://ladiyfestsheffield.wordpress.com
