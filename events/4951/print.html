Buried Sunshine

Date, Time & Location Fri 5 May 2017, 7.30-11pm, Broomhall Centre, Broomspring Lane S10 2FB
Details An evening of theatre and music in support of Sheffield Climate Alliance´s campaign worker role, and Sheffield Anarchist Book Fair (which is on 20th May this year). "Buried Sunshine" is a one-woman play for Global Divestment Day, a dramatic tale of coal, courage and criminals, based on testimonies of people in Colombia. Along with entertainment and inspiration from Sheffield Socialist Choir and supporting musicians. All welcome. Bar, cakes and goodies. Tickets £5/£3+fee from Tickets For Good or more on the door.
