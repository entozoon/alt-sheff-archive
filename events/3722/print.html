Rotherham Green Party meeting

Date, Time & Location Thu 20 Nov 2014, 7.30pm upstairs at The Bridge Inn, 1 Greasbrough Rd S60 1RB
Details Rotherham Green Party members meeting which will continue with discussion informally in the pub afterwards. David Burley from Frack Free South Yorkshire will have a short slot in the meeting. Not a public meeting as such but anyone who is interested is very welcome to come, The Green party has trebled in size in recent months. The Bridge Inn is about 2 minutes walk from the Interchange. Just cross over the bridge in the direction of the train station and the Inn is directly on your right. Sheffield & Rotherham Green Party (SRGP) tel.
