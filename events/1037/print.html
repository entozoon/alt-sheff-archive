Talk: Fatima Bhutto - Songs of Blood and Sword

Date, Time & Location Mon 18 Oct 2010, 7.30pm (doors 7pm) The Auditorium, University of Sheffield Students’ Union, Western Bank, S10
Details Fatima Bhutto comes from one of the world's best known political dynasties - four members of her family were assassinated including her aunt Benazir Bhutto in 2007. Her story is the epic tale of an extraordinary family which mirrors the tumultuous events of Pakistan itself. Her compelling book Songs of Blood and Sword is both political and personal providing a timely insight into the world of Pakistani politics told by a direct witness.
