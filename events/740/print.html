'Asylum Monologues'

Date, Time & Location Tue 13 Jul 2010, 7pm Library Theatre, Central Library, Surrey Street, Sheffield S1 1XZ
Details Ice and Fire Theatre presents first-hand accounts of the UK asylum system in the words of people who have experienced it, including those of a 12-year old girl who was detained twice in immigration detention. This is the flagship play of Ice and Fire's outreach project Actors for Human Rights and has been seen by over 15,000 people across the UK. It is followed by a panel discussion on refugee and asylum issues with invited speakers. Entry is free, with optional donations in aid of ASSIST Sheffield. Not suitable for people under 15. Arrive early to secure a seat! For more information go to www.iceandfire.co.uk
