Asylum Law and Justice meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 12 May 2010 6pm at the Arts Tower, University of Sheffield
Details Meeting of a group setting up a free legal support service to people seeking asylum in South Yorkshire with support from practising and retired solicitors, law students and others. Whether you have any legal training or not, come along and help do something practical to give people seeking asylum support. Contact Gina for more information email: ginaclayton @ blueyonder.co.uk
