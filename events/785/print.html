'A Little Patch of Ground'

Date, Time & Location Thu 15 - Sun 18 Jul 2010 at The Point, 16 South Parade, Doncaster, DN1 2DR
Details Multi-media performance about a unique vegetable garden created by participants aged 12-60. The group met every week to share time, food, reflections, ideas, fears and hopes. The performance reflects their personal stories and relationships with the wider natural world through a cross-fertilisation of soil, images, edible produce, objects, words and songs. Thursday 15th July, 7.00pm Saturday 17th July, 1.30pm (part of 'Seeing With New Eyes' see below) Sunday 18th July, 1.00pm (part of Doncaster Cultural Festival) Free and suitable for all ages.
