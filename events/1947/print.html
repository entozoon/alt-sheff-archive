Festival of Inter-Activity and Art

Date, Time & Location Thu 29-Sat 31 Mar 2012, Burngreave Chapel, Melrose Rd. (off Barnsley Rd.)
Details A three-day festival of interactive art, lantern-making, delicious hot food and chai, films, laughter workshops and many surprises. With the help of Khachapuri Cafe, Cupola Contemporary Art, KIAC and many more.
  • Thursday 29th:
  • 3-7pm Lantern-making
  • 7-11pm Chapel illuminations independant films and projections
  • Friday 30th:
  • 6-11pm Interactive art and activities, lantern-making and walk-about surprises
  • Saturday 31st:
  • 11am-9pm Laughter workshop, lantern-making, art and activities
