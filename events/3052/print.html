Peace in the Park special open meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 11 Dec 2013 6pm-8pm, Regather 57-59 Club Garden Rd. S11 8BU
Details Every year Peace in the Park welcomes new people in organising the festival. You are invited to a special open meeting where you can find out how Sheffield's most popular free festival is organised and how you could get involved for June 2014. They look forward to welcoming you. By the People, For the People! Profits go to good causes and it's a fantastic event. Don't be shy if you have any ideas, come along to one of these open meetings.
