South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG) meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 2 Mar 2010, 7pm at Scotia Works, Leadmill Road, Sheffield S1 4SE
Details South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group is a campaigning group which aims to improve the treatment of people who come to South Yorkshire, whether because of threats to their personal safety in their home country or for economic reasons. A partnership between various groups. The meetings are open to all who share SYMAAG's aims. Contact email dignitynotdetention@yahoo.co.uk
