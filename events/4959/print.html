Radical Readers book group

Date, Time & Location Wed 12 Apr 2017, 6.30 pm Sheffield Central Library
Details This month Sheffield Radical Readers will be discussing George Monbiot’s Feral, a radical rethinking of how the environmental movement needs to move from trying preserve what we have to carving out new spaces for nature free from human intervention. If you want a free library loan copy of the book, there is a stack of them at Jepps Books which you can find in Theatre Delicatessen, 17 The Moor, Sheffield, S1 4PF. Just pop in, say you are interested and swear a blood oath to return the book, otherwise they have to pay a fine! The group meets on the second Wednesday of every month) in Sheffield Central Library (while it’s still there). There will be biscuits.
