Access Space on tour

Date, Time & Location Tue 4-Sat 8 Feb 2014, 35 Chapel Walk, S1 2PD
Details Sheffield's cool tech-and-arts project Access Space is having a "pop-up" week in residence at Chapel Walk in the city centre. Events will include workshops in computer building with free software, 3D printing and crowdsource funding and lots of other stuff, as well as an exhibition of peoples' art. Venue details: www.35chapelwalk.com Access Space
