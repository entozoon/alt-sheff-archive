Heeley to Hazelhurst CSA walk

Date, Time & Location Sat 23 Jan 2011, 10.20 for 10.30 set off
Details Enjoy a gentle New Year's walk from Heeley to the proposed Hazelhurst Community Supported Agriculture (CSA) Co-op site in the Moss Valley. Bus back to Heeley from the nearby village of Ford. About 4 miles, 7 km. An opportunity to catch up with latest developments about the project, and meet up with Moss Valley Wild Life Group. Refreshments available at the Bridge Inn at Ford, including tea and coffee, and children welcome. Full details at http://www.transitionsheffield.org.uk/node/83
