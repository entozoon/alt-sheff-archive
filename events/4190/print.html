AMPHIFEST

Date, Time & Location Sat 5 Sep 2015, 12-5pm, South Street Amphitheatre, above Sheffield Station S2 5AS
Details A great festival in the amphitheatre in Sheaf Valley Park, with music, dance, food and stalls including a bike workshop. Free kids zone with donkey rides and much more.
