Nottingham Radical Bookfair

Date, Time & Location Sat 17 Nov 2018, 10.30am-5pm, Nottingham Mechanics Institute
Details Sheffield has no radical bookfair, but in Nottingham, Five Leaves Bookshop runs one. It includes stalls by national and local publishers, second-hand booksellers and a full supporting programme throughout the day (details on the website). Subjects include (alphabetically) Architecture, Biography, Black British, Book Festival, Bread and Roses, Cultural Studies, Labour History, LGBT, Music, Poetry, Politics, Race, Trade Unions, Travel Writing. Free, with free events. No need to book. Cafe and bar on site. Full wheelchair access to rooms and facilities. Leaflets/papers on a free table of flyers and giveaways for campaigns and other groups to use. Nottingham Radical Bookfair
