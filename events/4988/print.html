Wordlife Open Mic

Date, Time & Location Tue 16 May 2017, 7pm, DINA, 32 Cambridge St. S1 4HP
Details Wordlife is one of the leading literature organisations in the North. Join them for a politics-themed literature event alongside featured performers. Want to share some of your poems or prose on political issues? Email joe@wordlife.co.uk. A Festival of Debate event, hosted by not-for-profit social enterprise Opus Independents (which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine). Pay what you decide on the door.
