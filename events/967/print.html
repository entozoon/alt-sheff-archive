Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change meeting (SCACC)

Date, Time & Location Tues 14 Sep 2010, 7.30-9.30pm at Highfield Library, London Rd. S2 4NF
Details SCACC organises days of action and demonstrations, petitions to Sheffield Council, supports related national and local campaigns and runs film shows and talks for local groups. The regular meeting is open to newcomers, all welcome. The regular meeting will be preceeded by a meeting at 7pm to organise details of the forthcoming AGM (on 28th this month). SCACC website
