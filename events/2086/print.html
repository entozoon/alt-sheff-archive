SHEFFIELD GREEN FAIR

Date, Time & Location Sat 30 Jun 2012, St Mary’s Community Centre, Bramall Lane.
Details The annual celebration of everyone and everything that is green and environmental in Sheffield and Yorkshire, bigger and better than ever. There will be entertainment, live music, DJs, eco-fashion show, bar and wholefood cafe, over 70 stalls presenting the green alternatives, and fun for children all day long. 6 exciting zones featuring green technology, sustainable living, health and healing, campaigns and community action, fashion, arts and crafts, fair-trade and organic goods. Even a circus workshop. Admission £2 (£1 concessions), 16 and under free. Sheffield Green Fair website
