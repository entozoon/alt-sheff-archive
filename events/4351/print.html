Water Voles: Britain&#39;s fastest disappearing mammal

Date, Time & Location Thu 18 Feb 2016, 2pm-3.30pm, Central Library, Surrey St. S1 2LG
Details An illustrated talk by Christine Gregory, on "Water Voles: Britain's fastest disappearing mammal and their fortunes in Derbyshire". She explores what water voles are and how they live. She considers alien species, climate change and damaged ecosystems as well as the recovery of our rivers and the positive success stories of water vole re-introductions, habitat restoration and conservation. Free to attend, but places must be booked. Telephone Sheff 2734712 or email information.library@sheffield.gov.uk
