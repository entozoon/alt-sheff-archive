Sheffield Pride Week: Climax Pre-Pride BBQ & Garden Party

Date, Time & Location Fri 5 Jul 2013, 5pm-2am, University of Sheffield Student Union, in Bar One and Interval
Details Part of Sheffield Pride Week, leading up to the main event on Saturday 6th of July. Climax offers you a barbeque, summer cocktails, live bands, rainbow giveaways, all the best tunes and a ton of other stuff going on. Back on its home turf, Climax Pre-Pride will be running from 5pm all the way through till 2am - so come along, and come as you are! www.sheffieldpride.org.uk
