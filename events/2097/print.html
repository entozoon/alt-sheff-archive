Sheffield Anti-Cuts Alliance AGM

Date, Time & Location Wed 18 Jul 2012, 7pm-9pm, United Reformed Church (opposite the Crucible, central Sheffield)
Details Sheffield Anti Cuts Alliance is a grass roots working class anti cuts organisation bringing together trade unionists, campaigners, students, the unemployed, pensioners and disabled people against the cuts. This is the annual general meeting, the opportunity to elect officers, make amendments to the constitution, and discuss the future strategy, direction and organisation of SACA. According to the current constitution (see website) nominations and voting is limited to delegates of affiliated organisations, though the meeting is open to all supporters to attend and contribute to the discussion. www.sheffieldanticuts.wordpress.com
