How do we argue for a better society?

Date, Time & Location Wed 5 Nov 2014, 7pm-9pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Sheffield Equality Group hosts a discussion on how we can argue for more economic equality. Is it facts, facts, facts - or stories - or questions? Is it saying the rich are evil, or that it benefits all of us? Come along to this informal workshop. Free, all welcome. Sheffield Equality Group
