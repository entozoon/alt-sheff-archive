3D printing demonstration

Date, Time & Location Thu 6 Feb 2014, 1pm-3pm, 35 Chapel Walk, S1 2PD
Details A demonstration of 3D printing, the new technology that may be the future of DIY local-scale manufacture. Includes a presentation about Refab Space, the DIY FabLab and hack space with laser cutting etc. By Access Space resident technology guru and hardware hacker, John Moseley. Free entry, booking not necessary. Part of the week of external "pop-up" activities by Access Space, the local walk-in centre for support with open source computing. The Chapel Walk space will be open 10.30am-5.30pm with more than ninety artworks on display, and designs and commercialised products made by participants in Sheffield. Venue details: www.35chapelwalk.com Access Space
