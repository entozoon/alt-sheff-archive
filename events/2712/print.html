"Free Radicals" fundraiser for ASSIST

Date, Time & Location Sat 11 May 2013, St. Andrew’s Hall, 19 St Andrew’s Road, Nether Edge S11 9A
Details The fabulous Free Radicals play for ASSIST – soul, funk and raising money for asylum seekers – what better Saturday night out could you ask for? Also featuring local poet River Wolton and the Broccoli Hitch Indie band. With many thanks to the Derwent Woodcraft Folk for their support. Venue details are at http://standrewshall.wordpress.com. Tickets £7/£4 concessions. If you would like to book your tickets in advance, please contact by email efa@assistsheffield.org.uk
