Protest law talk

Date, Time & Location Wed 27 Sep 2017, 6pm, Irwin Mitchell Solicitors, Riverside East, 2 Millsands S3 8DT
Details Helen White of Howells Solicitors will talk about her work as a criminal lawyer representing anti-fracking activists and people protesting the removal of trees in Sheffield. Helen is an experienced Chartered Legal Executive Advocate (CILEx) and has a passion for human rights law and criminal defence. Learn about your rights and how these cases work, so you can be prepared if you face the law or someone close to you does. This is a free event open to all.
