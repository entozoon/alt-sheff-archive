Trick or Treatment? Alternative Medicine on Trial

Date, Time & Location Mon 8 Feb 2010, 6:30pm at Lescar Hotel, 303 Sharrow Vale Road, Sheffield, S11 8ZF
Details A talk by Simon Singh for 'Skeptics in the Pub', which is about getting people together to have a relaxed and enjoyable evening while listening to talks on a wide range of topics, usually by a notable scientist or skeptic. It doesn't matter what your prior beliefs are, or even if you're unsure what they are yourself. Everyone and everyone's viewpoint welcome.
