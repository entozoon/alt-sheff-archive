Can Corbyn cut inequality? Politics, policy and mass movements

Date, Time & Location Wednesday 7th October, 7pm-8:30pm at Sheffield Quaker Meeting House
Details Informal discussion about the likely next leader of Labour and what it means for income inequality. Maximum wages, fairer taxes, and People's Quantitative Easing have the potential to make a huge difference - but will any of it happen, and how could we help? All welcome. Free, donations for tea and biscuits.
