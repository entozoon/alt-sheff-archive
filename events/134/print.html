"Talking About Africa" Workers Educational Association course

Date, Time & Location Starts Wed 30 Sept 2009, 6.30-9.30pm at The Circle, Voluntary Action Sheffield, Rockingham Lane (off Division Street), Sheffield S1 4FW
Details Discussions about Africa now, with contributions from local Africans, experts and informed media watchers. Using newspapers, outside speakers and films, we’ll be working together to further our understanding of this huge continent, says tutor Graham Birkin. The course costs just £3. For more information contact Graham at graham@birkin194.fsnet.co.uk tel. Sheff 2423609.
