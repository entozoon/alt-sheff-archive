Conference: Just Economics

Date, Time & Location Sat 16 Nov 2013, 10am-4pm, St. Mark’s Church, 4 St. Mark’s Cres. Broomhill S10 2SG
Details St. Mark’s Centre for Radical Christianity invites you to “Just” Economics. This is a conference with Ann Pettifor, an internationally respected economist and an excellent speaker. She argues that in relation to the current financial crisis, austerity measures are not needed, will not work and are undoubtedly unjust. The economy must serve human need rather than human needs being sacrificed to a one-eyed and amoral view of economics.
Non-members £14, members £12, students £5. Booking: Sheff 2663613, via website below, or email info@stmarkscrc.co.uk (early booking recommended). You can also book a packed lunch (sandwich, cake, fruit and soft drink) for £4.50 or bring your own. Coffee and tea included in main booking fee. Registration is from 9.15am.
