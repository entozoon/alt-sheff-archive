Sheffield Social Centre collective meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 11 Jul 2011, 7pm The Rutland Arms, 86 Brown St. S1 2BS
Details Sheffield Social Centre is a collective for libertarian DIY space in the city, open to all who share its core principles of non-hierarchy, anti-capitalism and equality/non-discrimination. Meeting to discuss a Free School later in the year. Website: http://sheffieldsocialcentre.org.uk
