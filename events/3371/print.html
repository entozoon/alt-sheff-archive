Consultation about the future of community radio

Date, Time & Location Tue 8 Apr 2014, 11am-3pm, The Creative Lounge in The Workstation
Details The Community Media Association is holding this short consultation event on the government DCMS Consultation about the future of community radio. This is of immense importance to all involved in community broadcasting and the wider third sector - not just community radio. A light lunch will be provided and this will be a good opportunity to network with other community media people and help influence Government legislation of the sector.
