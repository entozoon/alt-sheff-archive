SHARROW FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sat 16 Jul 2016, Mount Pleasant Park, Sharrow Lane, nr London Rd. S11 8AE
Details Join in as the people of Sharrow come together for this great annual multicultural community festival, with a focus on creativity and participation. Community Stage featuring Black Thorn, Early Cartographers and Chanel Yates. Drumming and street dance workshops, sports, global foods to try, a huge variety of stalls and kids' activities. www.sharrowfestival.btck.co.uk
