Course: Living willow structures

Date, Time & Location Sat 4 Dec Ecclesall Woods Sawmill, Sheffield
Details This one-day course introduces willow as a beautiful material that is easy and very satisfying to work with. Learn how living willow can be used to create exciting living structures such as arbors, domes, fences, magical creatures and furniture. Work together to make a simple structure of your own design. Low-Impact Living Initiative (LILI) is a non-profit organisation whose mission is to help people reduce their impact on the environment, improve their quality of life, gain new skills, live in a healthier and more satisfying way, have fun and save money.
