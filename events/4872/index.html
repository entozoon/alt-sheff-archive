Wordlife 10th Birthday Party Ft. Hollie McNish

Fri 25 Nov 2016, 7.30pm Weston Park Museum, Western Bank, S10 2TP

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Poetry and spoken word have always had radical beginnings. Wordlife celebrates its past ten years with featured readings from some of the best-known spoken word artists in the country and the premiere of four new poetry films projected onto Weston Park Museum itself. Hollie McNish's politically-driven poetry has gone viral twice. Her work attacks big issues like immigration and feminism head on. Also featuring Scott Tyrrell and Kayo Chingonyi. Part of Festival of Debate 2016, supported by Year of Making and Arts Council England, hosted by not-for-profit social enterprise Opus Independents (which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine).
