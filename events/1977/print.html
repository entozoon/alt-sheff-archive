AGM of South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG)

Date, Time & Location Tue 1 May 2012 7pm-9pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St Sheffield S1 2EW
Details The Annual General Meeting of SYMAAG, which unites refugee, asylum and migrant organisations with campaign groups and supporting individuals, campaigning both for the rights of those seeking asylum and of those coming to work in this country. Guest speaker Catherine Ramos from Justice First, who will talk about their report on the treatment of deported Congolese asylum seekers. If you want to come to any SYMAAG meeting but find it hard to afford transport do not be put off! Please bring your ticket or a receipt and SYMAAG can pay you back at the meeting.
