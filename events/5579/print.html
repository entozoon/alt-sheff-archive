"Over The Margins"

Date, Time & Location Wed 17 Apr 2019, 7.45pm, Theatre Deli, 202 Eyre St. S1 4QZ
Details A play that explores the theme of barriers, borders, obstacles, and margins; how people overcome or cross them, whether physical, geographical or personal. The themes are very current and relevant to our times, touching on hunger, famine, bravery, travel/migration/moving to ´greener pastures´ and based on direct experiences the group members have had.
