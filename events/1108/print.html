Human Rights Day Vigil

Date, Time & Location Fri 10 Dec 2010, 4.30-6pm in front of Sheffield Town Hall
Details Sheffield Amnesty International group is organising this vigil for Human Rights Day, and would love you to come and support. According to the UN Office of the High Commissioner for Human Rights, the theme of Human Rights day this year is "Speak Up...Stop Discrimination." www.sheffield.amnesty.org.uk
