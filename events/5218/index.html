Sheffield Climate Alliance planning workshop

Tue 16 Jan 2018, 7pm, Quaker Meeting House, St James Street, S1 2EW

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

The next meeting for Sheffield Climate Alliance will be dedicated to a Planning Workshop lead by Aaron Thierry. It will give everyone present a chance to suggest the main goals for the next 12 months and where to prioritise the focus. Everyone welcome. Lauren will also be at the Blue Moon Cafe in the hour beforehand if anyone would like to join her for food and chat.Sheffield Climate Alliance
