St Marys Vintage and Craft Market

The 6th great vintage and craft market, with talented local artists and loads of 20s, 30s 40s, 50s and 60s designs, and even a retro soundtrack playing! Also featuring the Bygone Bikes Classic Cycle Show. An array of pre- and post-war lightweight machines, components, memorabilia. Home-made food available.
