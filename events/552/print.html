FREE film 'The Yes Men save the world'

Date, Time & Location Thurs 22 Apr 2010, 7.30pm The Auditorium, University of Sheffield Union of Students, Western Bank, S10 2TG
Details An inspirational and funny campaigning film. The Yes Men pull off all kinds of amazing actions, pretending to be representatives of top companies, telling the truth about their exploitative practices and getting away with it. It sold out at the Showroom cinema when it was shown there earlier this year. All welcome, a WDM film show, in collaboration with Amnesty, Cinema Syndicate and Speak.
