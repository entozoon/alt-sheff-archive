Sheffield Friday Night Ride - Snooker!

Date, Time & Location Fri 4 May 2012, start 6.30pm Tudor Square, outside the Crucible Theatre S1 1DA
Details Sheffield Friday Night Ride (FNR) offers a fun monthly evening bike ride based on a theme, this time it is snooker. Come and ride to celebrate the snooker World Championships at the Crucible, following the colours of the snooker balls in order, making sure to pot a red between each one. White Lion, Red Lion, Big Yellow Self Storage, Red Lion on Charles St, Brown Bear, etc. Get the idea? Finishing back at the Crucible. The ride is all on roads or paths, about 14 miles in all.
