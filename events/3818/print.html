One Billion Rising - preparation dance workshop

Date, Time & Location Fri 6 Feb 2015, 1.30-2.30pm Together Women Project, 106 Arundel Lane S1 4RF (behind the Hubs)
Details One Billion Rising is holding a flashdance as part of the global movement to end violence against women (see Saturday 14th Feb below). You can organise your own event. Dancing can happen anywhere, at any time, with anyone and everyone. No corporation can own or control it. This event is a Dance Workshop: Learn the "Break the Chain" dance with the help of experienced dance teachers, (and the practice video) in preparation for the Flash Dance in Sheffield and Rotherham city centres. Contact Beckie on RebeccaB@togetherwomen.org or call Sheff 2758282 or just turn up. All women welcome at this dance workshop. (The main event welcomes women and men).
