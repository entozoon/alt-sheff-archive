Can There be Peace in Israel/Palestine Without Return of the Refugees?

Thu 14 Feb 2019, 6pm, University of Sheffield Arts Tower, Western Bank S10 2TN

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

A Panel discussion organised jointly by South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group, Sheffield Amnesty International, Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign, and the Palestine Society of Sheffield University.Speakers:

  • Salma Karmi-Ayyoub, Criminal Barrister, and Co-Chair of Lawyers for Palestinian Human Rights
  • Prof Ralph Negrine, Emeritus Professor of Political Communication, whose research includes TV coverage of the 2009 UK government expenses scandal, and post-war Jewish immigration to Israel from Arab lands.
