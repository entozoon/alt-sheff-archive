Public meeting: Palestine Solidarity

Date, Time & Location Wed 18 July 2012, 7.30pm St Matthews Rooms, Carver St. S1 4FE
Details A public meeting by Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign, presenting Hilary Smith, who will be talking about her recent visit to Palestine. It will include a view of the work of Jordan Valley Solidarity and Resistance against the Israeli Apartheid state by Palestinian citizens. Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign
