Sheffield Social Centre Collective meeting

Date, Time & Location Thu 5 Apr 2012, 7.30pm, 268 Verdon Street, Burngreave S3 9QS
Details General meeting of Sheffield Social Centre, a collective for libertarian DIY space in the city. If you're interested in the social centre project, or intrigued by it at the very least, you're very welcome.
