Opus presents Gadabout #7: Chris T-T

Date, Time & Location Sat 7 May 2016, 7.30pm Eten Cafe, 2-4 York St. S1 2ER
Details Emerging from roots in the late 90s lo-fi and psych folk, Chris T-T first gained acclaim for sarcastic, city-obsessed urban alt-folk music before developing into an influential figure of UK songwriting, crossing barriers between punk, psych and English folk. Chris will discuss his 2005 album of protest songs, 9 Red Songs, and its forthcoming companion piece, 9 Green Songs, before performing a full set. Part of Festival of Debate 2016, hosted by Opus Independents (which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine).
