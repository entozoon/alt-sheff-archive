Rail Fare Rises Day

Date, Time & Location Tue 2 Jan 2018, 7am-11am, Sheffield Station
Details Natalie Bennett and other Green Party activists will be at Sheffield Station from 7am to 11am on the annual “Rail Fare Rises” day as commuters return to work facing an average 3.4% increase in the cost of already eye-wateringly expensive travel. All welcome to come down and say hello, give out leaflets and sign the petition (also online at action.greenparty.org.uk/railways
