Start up

Date, Time & Location Mon 11 Jan 2016, 12.30-2pm Beauchief Baptist Church, Hutcliffe Wood Rd S8 0EY
Details • Are you aged 50 or over? • Would you like to see new activities happening in your area? • Do you want to develop an idea and start something yourself? Ignite Imaginations can support your ideas and help start up groups and events for people aged 50 and over in your local community. Come and meet to find out how to get involved. Start up is a programme run by Ignite Imaginations supporting Older People to start new activities and projects from gardening to disco dancing, art classes to reading clubs. 3 course meal £3.50 on arrival, booking necessary: Sheff 3273255. If you can’t come, contact directly on Sheff 2686813 or email admin@igniteimaginations.org.uk
