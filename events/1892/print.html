Palestine fundraising dinner and quiz night

Date, Time & Location Sat 10 Mar 2012, 7pm-10pm , Fairplay, Alexandra Rd West, CHESTERFIELD S40 1NP
Details Chesterfield Palestine Support Group & Stop the War Coalition raised £1600 last year for children’s projects in Gaza, which was devastated after the Israeli bombardment in 2009, and is still under siege by Israel. This event includes a delicious three course dinner with an Italian flavour, choice of wine, real ale, bottled beer and soft drinks all included in the price. The evening also features a talk from a recent visitor to Palestine about the current situation there. There is no visitor parking at the Fairplay centre but free parking is available at Mecca Bingo, you can walk through to the venue from there. Tickets £15/£10 (unwaged). To reserve your table phone 01246 203552, 07919 486101, 01246 271650, or 07768 927669.
