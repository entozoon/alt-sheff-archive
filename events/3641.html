Workshop: BDS (boycott, divestment and sanctions against Israel)

Sat 13 Sep 2014, 12.30-4.30pm at the Yemini Community Association (Hadfield Institute) 43 Attercliffe Common S9 2AE

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Following the huge increase in support for the Palestinian cause, this is a half day workshop on the campaign for boycott, divestment and sanctions (BDS) against Israel. The workshop will include an opportunity to learn more about specific campaigns and how to get involved in existing BDS campaigns as well as starting new areas of work. All welcome.Full details are on website: Sheffield Palestine Solidarity CampaignFacebook page
