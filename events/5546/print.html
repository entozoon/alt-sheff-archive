"Here's Looking at UKIP"

Date, Time & Location Wed 27 & Thu 28 Mar 2019, 7.45pm, Theatre Deli, 202 Eyre St. S1 4QZ
Details Here’s Looking at UKIP is a show about an identity crisis, what it means to be British, a patriot, a small nation with a brutal past and an uncertain future. Is Great Britain really all that great? Is it OK to be proud of the British Empire? Can you love a country that does not love you back? An unflinching, funny, furious look at Britain’s problems with “foreigners”, historical amnesia and delusions of grandeur, to try and figure out how we got here - and where we’re going next. Theatre Deli
