Festival of Debate: "Regather Roast"

Date, Time & Location Sun 8 Mar 2015, 1-5pm Regather Works 57-59 Club Garden Road S11 8BU
Details In association with Regather, serving up food for body and mind, the Regather Roast will offer a traditional three course Sunday roast of fresh, local produce - high welfare lamb, stuffed mushrooms and delicious organic vegetables - accompanied by a discussion led by Leon Ballin (Soil Association), who will explore the challenging question, ‘Eating seasonally is good, isn’t it?’ Tickets £20. Bring your own drinks. Pre-booking is essential via website www.regather.net. The Festival of Debate is taking place in Sheffield in the run-up to the General election, during March and April. It provides opportunities to get together and discuss a range of issues which affect everyone. Informed decision making and strategic planning are essential for democracy, so come and get involved.
