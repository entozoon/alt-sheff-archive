ROTHERHAM SHOW

Date, Time & Location Sat 12 - Sun 13 Sep 2015, 10am-5.30pm, Clifton Park, Rotherham
Details This is the largest free show in the North of England, celebrating the diversity of the borough with an action-packed weekend of entertainment for the whole family. Music arena, dog show, produce and craft show, vintage vehicles, fairground, food, hundreds of stalls and far more. On Saturday the Main Arena entertainment will include Reel Stunts in Action, K9 Freestyle Dog Dancing Display and a Yorkshire Strong Man Car Pull and Tug of War, and Rother FM Music Festival at the Bandstand, with some brilliant music including tribute bands Queen II and Take That 2 on Saturday and the amazing Jive Aces on Sunday.
