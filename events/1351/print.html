International Women's Day activities

Date, Time & Location Tue 8 Mar 2011, 12 noon to 2 pm outside the Town Hall
Details Sheffield WILPF (Women's International League for Peace & Freedom) is running a stall on this 100th anniversary of International Women's Day, focusing on the cuts and how they affect women in particular. There will be a letter for people to sign, protesting to the Prime Minister and suggesting an alternative budget strategy, also some statistical information from the UNITE trade union and a flyer on WILPF's IWD statement. A women-only action. Please circulate this as widely as you can.
