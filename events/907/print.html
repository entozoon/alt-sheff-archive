EDL in Bradford

Date, Time & Location Sat 28 Aug 2010, Bradford (West Yorkshire)
Details Various groups are calling counter demonstrations against the far right EDL (English Defence League) in Bradford city centre on the 28th. Leeds Anti-Fascist Network Assemble at 11am - for details see http://srfnetwork.org and Northern Indymedia website. We Are Bradford assembles at 12.00 for a peaceful multicultural celebration of Bradford www.wearebradford.org.uk The matter is also discussed on Sheffield Indymedia website.
