Sheffield Friends of the Earth meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 16 Aug 2010, 8pm at the Red Deer pub, 18 Pitt Street S1 4DD Sheffield just off West Street (upstairs meeting room)
Details Friends of the Earth Sheffield Group. People from all walks of life who share a common concern for the state of the planet and a desire to do something about it. Membership of Sheffield FOE is free if you subscribe to the electronic newsletter.
