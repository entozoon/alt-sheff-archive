Benefit dance night for community rebuilding in Palestine

Date, Time & Location Thu 19 Mar 2015, 7.30pm Crookes Social Club, Mulehouse Rd. S10 1TD
Details In April, Steve Kinneavy is joining a team of volunteers from Amos Trust travelling to Palestine to live and work alongside local people to help rebuild their community. Steve will help a family return to their home and community by helping to rebuild their house. He is taking part in this project to highlight the injustice of Palestinian homes being demolished by the Israeli authorities, and as an act of support and solidarity for Palestinians working non-violently for a just peace. Come and start the weekend early and support a fantastic cause! Vintage dance music from the excellent Redback Special guests Sheffield Socialist Choir Bar and raffle Tickets £5 in advance (£3 concessions) £6 on the door (£4 concessions).
