Conference: Fairness for Women

Date, Time & Location Sat 8 Nov 2014, Wortley Hall, Wortley, near Sheffield S35 7DB
Details Equality - what equality? Fairness for women? A one-day conference sponsored by Unite The Union. Topics include pensions, children and families, health and employment. Speakers include Siobhan Endean, Equalities Officer for Unite; Linsay Hopkinson, health worker; Councillor Jackie Drayton, Sheffield City Council; and Eleanor Lewington, NAW pensions spokesperson. Cost (minimum) £5 individuals and £10 organisations, including refreshments and buffet lunch. Transport help is possible. Places limited, please book via Pat Peters/Ann Munsey, NAW, 57 Pearson Place, Sheffield S8 9DE (sorry no known online details or contact number).
