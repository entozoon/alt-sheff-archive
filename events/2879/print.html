"Pinknic!"

Date, Time & Location Sun 15 Sep 2013, 12pm-7pm, Weston Park S10 2TP
Details A first for Sheffield, the first pink picnic - "Pinknic!" This FREE, family-friendly day-long event is aimed at all LGBT communities within Sheffield, including their friends, family and supporters. Entertainment will be provided, so just bring your picnic food, chairs or blankets and come and join in for a chilled out Sunday afternoon in the park. See you there! Facebook event page
