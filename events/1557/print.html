Transition Summer Party 2011

Date, Time & Location Wed 20 Jul 2011, from 7pm Café Euro, 72 John St. (off Bramall Lane/London Rd.) S2 4QU
Details Transition Sheffield is part of the growing Transition Town movement, tackling climate change and peak oil with practical hands-on solutions that work. Welcome to the community propelled, sustainably grown, and energetically inspiring summer social! A gathering to connect, feast, share, and celebrate transition culture in Sheffield. Come and bring savoury food to share. The evening may include a quiz, slideshow, film-short, and live music (voices/instruments welcome) www.transitionsheffield.org.uk
