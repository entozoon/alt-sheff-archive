DIVERSITY FEST

Date, Time & Location Sat 17 Sep 2016, 12-8pm Hagglers Corner, 586 Queens Rd. S2 4DU
Details A free celebration for people of every gender, sexuality, ethnicity, age and ability. A statement of unity and mutual respect between the many communities in Sheffield. The programme includes events in the Hagglers Corner cafe, upstairs and outside. Talks, spoken word, music, dance, DJs, films, kids activities, collaborative art work and more. Entry free.
