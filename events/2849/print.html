"Who Were the Rochdale Pioneers? A Short History of the Co-Operative Movement"

Date, Time & Location Sun 7 Jul 2013, 3pm, Regather Co-operative, 57-59 Club Garden Rd. S11 8BU
Details A workshop with Co-Op Film Archive film footage and discussion around the history and development of the co-operative values and principles that were introduced by the Rochdale Pioneers. It will be addressing the advantages and disadvantages of co-operative working and how the values and principles have developed and made the difference between success and failure of the co-operative movement. Discussion to be led by co-operator and Sheffielder Steve Thompson - elected to the Co-operative Group South Yorkshire & Chesterfield Area Committee and the North Regional Board. Cream tea with Regather-made scones and jam. To book, please email gareth.roberts@regather.net so they can have an idea of numbers (and scones!) Regather Sheffield Co-operatives
