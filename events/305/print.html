FridayNightRide on the theme of Hospitals and Health

Date, Time & Location 22 Jan 2010, meet 6.30pm at West End Pub, 412 Glossop Road
Details Sheffield FridayNightRide is a bit of cycling fun on a monthly Friday near a full moon. An evening bike ride for everyone, planned by theme. This one will visit the sites of hospitals and former hospitals in Sheffield. NOTE THE STARTING POINT IS NOT AS PREVIOUSLY MENTIONED. If it is very likely to be wet then the ride will be cancelled for that night, and notice will be sent out on email, the web and Twitter by the morning of the ride. www.sheffieldfridaynightride.org.uk
