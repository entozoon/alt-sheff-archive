World Development Movement (WDM) meeting: lobbying on food speculation

Date, Time & Location Thu 1 Dec 2011, 7-9pm, Central United Reformed Church, 60 Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details Meeting of anti-poverty campaigning organisation The World Development Movement; the European parliament will soon be debating changes in banking regulation, including rules on financial speculation on food. This is a chance to understand the issues, what the European commission has proposed and what needs to be changed, and how to get this message across to the MEPs. WDM Sheffield website
