Climate Change Training Day

Date, Time & Location Sat 31 Jan 2015, 10am-4pm, St Mark’s Church, Broomfield Rd, Broomhill S10 2SE
Details The Hope for the Future campaign is running this training day for anyone interested in learning how to help their church and local community engage politically with the issues surrounding climate change. The day will include:
  • A talk from the Bishop of Sheffield, Steven Croft, about how faith aids us in tackling climate change.
  • Paul Blomfield MP talking about the most effective way of lobbying MPs.
  • Climate change from the perspective of the overseas church.
  • Examining the barriers to effective action on climate change and how people can overcome them.
  • Practical action we can take.
The day is free but a donation of £10 per person requested to help cover the running costs. Further information on the Hope for the Future website. Places are limited in order to provide personalised training and quality time with the speakers - please register your interest by emailing hopeftf@gmail.com. Hope for the Future
