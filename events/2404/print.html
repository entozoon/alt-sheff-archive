South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG) meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 13 Nov 2012, 6.30pm Exchange Brewery 2 Bridge St. S3 8NS
Details SYMAAG unites refugee, asylum and migrant organisations with campaign groups and supporting individuals. It campaigns both for the rights of those seeking asylum and of those coming to work in this country. From November SYMAAG is holding meetings in the B1 Training Room at Northern Refugee Centre’s new office at The Exchange Brewery, 2 Bridge Street, Sheffield S3 8NS. The building is on the left with big green doors just before you cross Lady’s Bridge toward The Wicker. Entrance is through the small green door (with the SYMAAG sign on). If you want to come to any SYMAAG meeting but find it hard to afford transport do not be put off! Please bring your ticket or a receipt and SYMAAG can pay you back at the meeting. Or check in advance. www.symaag.org.uk Twitter: @SYMAAG Tel: Sheff 2412780
