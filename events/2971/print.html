Chile: Arpilleras, Photographic and Political Caricatures

Date, Time & Location From 7pm on Mon 4 to Fri 8 Nov 2013, Exhibition Space, New Jessop Building, University of Sheffield
Details 40 years ago a military coup plunged Chile into a brutal right-wing dictatorship, the first country in the world to experience the shock of "neoliberalism". Many fled the country, some arriving here to make Sheffield their new home. Now, in commemoration of the anniversary, there is a series of events including this exhibition "Arpilleras, Photographic and Political Caricatures". Launched Monday 4th at 7pm, the exhibition will be on display until 8th November. Chile 40 Years On website South Yorkshire Chilean Solidarity website
