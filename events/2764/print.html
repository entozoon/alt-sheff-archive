SHEFFIELD PRIDE

Date, Time & Location Sat 6 Jul 2013
Details Sheffield Pride is the city's annual community festival and parade celebrating lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgender life, and welcoming friends, family and everyone to join in the fun. 2013 is set to be the biggest and best yet, with a brand new city centre location and a lineup stretching all through the day and night, something for everyone to enjoy. There are also events in the week leading up to Saturday, at various venues. www.sheffieldpride.org.uk
