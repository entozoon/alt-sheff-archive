Current Affairs course

Date, Time & Location Starts Thu 14 Apr 2016, 10.30-12.30, Victoria Hall Methodist Church, Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details An 11 week discussion course looking at social, environmental, political and ethical issues, UK and world-wide. Topics to be agreed with the class. Last meeting June 30th. (No meeting June 2nd, for half-term.) Enrol for the term. Fees for 11 weeks £71.50, or FREE if on means-tested state benefits, or Working Tax Credit with income under £15,276. A Workers Educational Association (WEA) course. Enrolment online www.wea.org.uk/yh or phone WEA Regional Office 0113 2453304, or on the day - please contact tutor first to say you are coming. Limited places available on the day. Courses need a minimum number of 12 participants in order to run, with an upper limit of 16. If you cannot get to the first week, please let the tutor know beforehand that you intend to come later. The best way to guarantee a place is to enrol in advance online or by phone.
