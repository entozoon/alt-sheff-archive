Parade and Rally for Palestine

Date, Time & Location Sat 30 Nov 2019, meet 11am, Heeley Retail Park, Chesterfield Rd. S8 0RG
Details To mark the UN International Day of Solidarity with Palestinian People there will be a parade and rally from Heeley Retail Park (driving) to the Town Hall via the University. To join in with your car arrive 11am prompt at Heeley Retail Park to drive to the Town Hall. Alternatively come to the Town Hall for mid-day where there will be a short rally. Please email enquiries@sheffieldpsc.org.uk if you are able to bring your car or if you want to be a passenger. Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign
