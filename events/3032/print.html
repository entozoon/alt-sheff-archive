Workshop: "New Songs for Street Singing"

Date, Time & Location Sat 2 Nov 2013, 10am-4pm St. Augustine’s Church Hall, Brocco Bank S11 8RQ
Details So many critical issues are sending people out to demand a better life for all. South Yorkshire singer-songwriter Ray Hearne leads a workshop to kindle creativity and produce some punchy pieces with lyrics suitable for the streets. Join in if you are an activist to feed in ideas for songs for your campaign, and/or if you are a singer looking for new repertoire. Please bring food to share for lunch. Tickets: £18 / £15 (low waged) / £12 (concessions) Tel: 07455 128622. A presentation by Sheffield Socialist Choir, which recently joined other choirs to sing at the 50,000+ strong union rally outside the Tory Party conference in Manchester.
