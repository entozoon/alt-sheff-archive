REARRANGED: Space for Cycling Campaign intro evening

Date, Time & Location Thu 5 Feb 2015, 6.15pm, Exchange Place Studios, Exchange St. S2 5TR
Details The Space for Cycling campaign was one of the highlights of last year in Sheffield. The 2015 campaign will be bigger and better, aiming to change Sheffield for the better, for a people-friendly city where riding a bike is a safe, enjoyable and convenient transport choice for everyone, regardless of age or ability. Come along and find out about the campaign and how you can help. All welcome. (This meeting was originally lined up for Jan 29th but cancelled due to snow).
