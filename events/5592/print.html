Free Radicals gig

Date, Time & Location Sat 4 May 2019, doors open 7.30pm, DINA, Cambridge Street
Details Celebrate May Day with Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign. A Free Radicals gig: classic funk and soul music to dance to, alongside Sheffield LGBT+ choir Out Aloud. Scarborough performer Joe Solo will also be singing. Part of the May Day celebrations organised by Sheffield Trades Council. Tickets (£5 + £1.10 booking fee) via Party for the People (Sheffield ticketing agency that gives some of its fees to charity). Party for the People
