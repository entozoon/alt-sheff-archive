Setting up a Repair Café: a transition`workshop

Date, Time & Location Thu 8 Nov 2012, 7pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Fancy learning something new this autumn? Come to a transition`workshop! Repair Café are free meeting places and they are all about repairing things (together). What do you do with a chair when the leg has come loose? With a toaster that no longer works? Or a woollen jumper with moth holes? Throw it away? No way! You can repair it at a Repair Café. You can find out more about them here: www.repaircafe.org Join Gordon to learn more and talk about setting one up in Sheffield. More details from gordon.ferguson@phonecoop.coop. Workshop cost £3/£2. Please book with the person running the workshop. Do YOU have a skill to share? Transition Sheffield would love to hear from you! www.transitionsheffield.org.uk Twitter @TransitionSheff
