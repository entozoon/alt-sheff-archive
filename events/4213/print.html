Performance: Courage of Conscience: Uncovering World War 1 Hidden Stories

Date, Time & Location Thu 30 Jun 2016, 11-1pm, Chesterfield Library
Details Stories, poetry and drama about Derbyshire conscientious objectors, and the communities who resisted and questioned war, a century ago. The final performance, following a series of workshops since October last year, with young writers (16-30 yrs old). The workshops were led by former Derbyshire Poet Laureate River Wolton, assisted by young writers Katherine Robinson and Matthew Knighton. For more details please contact Ali Betteridge, Literature Development Officer, Derbyshire County Council alison.betteridge@derbyshire.gov.uk or tel. 01773 831359
