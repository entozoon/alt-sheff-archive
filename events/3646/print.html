Fairness on the 83 exhibition.

Date, Time & Location Thu 18-Sun 28 Sep 2014, 10.30-5pm, Castle House, Angel St (former Co-op store)
Details An exhibition on social inequalities in Sheffield, shown through the 83 bus route linking the north and south of our divided city. It includes filmed interviews with people who catch the 83. Part of the the excellent Festival of the Mind, "Fairness on the 83" is an initiative of Opus Independents, who also produce local magazine Now Then, available online (which includes the monthly Alt-Sheff column "LocalCheck".)
