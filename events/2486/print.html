Sheffield Vegan Approach

Date, Time & Location Sat 23 Mar - Sat 20 Apr 2013
Details The Sheffield Vegan Approach offers you the opportunity to try being vegan for a month from 23rd March - 20th April 2013. You will attend a FREE two part "Introduction to Veganism" course, including nutrition talks, cookery demos, films and a vegan buffet lunch. You will also be introduced to a local experienced vegan who will be available throughout your vegan month to answer any questions and give tips and advice. The sessions will take place at the start and end of your vegan month on Saturday 23rd March and Saturday 20th April 2013 in a central Sheffield venue. To take part, please register online at www.theveganapproach.com
