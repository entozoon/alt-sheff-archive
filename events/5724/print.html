March for Democracy: Stop Johnson, stop No-Deal Brexit

Date, Time & Location Sat 7 Sep 2019, 11am-12.30pm, Barkers Pool, outside City Hall, S1
Details Last week, thousands crowded into Barkers Pool against Boris Johnson and his Brexit Parliamentary prorogation. It was apparently the biggest demonstration in Sheffield in a decade, and part of the UK-wide pressure to defend democracy, in the streets. This week, after rallying at Barkers Pool there will be a march, with promise of a better sound system this time. Organised by Sheffield Left Against Brexit, Another Europe Is Possible, and other green/left wing labour-movement-based campaigners. Facebook event page
