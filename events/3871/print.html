South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG) Executive Committee meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 24 Feb 2015, 6.30pm Exchange Brewery, 2 Bridge St. S3 8NS
Details SYMAAG unites refugee, asylum and migrant organisations with campaign groups and supporting individuals. It campaigns both for the rights of those seeking asylum and of those coming to work in this country. SYMAAG meets at Northern Refugee Centre’s office at The Exchange Brewery, 2 Bridge Street, Sheffield S3 8NS. The building is on the left with big green doors just before you cross Lady’s Bridge toward The Wicker. Entrance is through the small green door (with the SYMAAG sign on). www.symaag.org.uk Twitter: @SYMAAG Tel: Sheff 2729332 extension 332
