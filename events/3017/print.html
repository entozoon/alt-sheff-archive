CycleSheffield meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 4 Nov 2013, 7.30-9pm, Heeley Development Trust, Ash Tree Yard, 62-68 Thirlwell Rd, S8 9TF
Details CycleSheffield meeting, with tea, coffee and biscuits supplied. All welcome! The agenda is helpfully online, click on this link. It includes Green Routes consultation, feedback from correspondence between SCC and CycleSheffield, and council plans in general for cycling over the next 6 months. Route to Heeley Development Trust: Follow the Sheaf Valley Cycle Route to Saxon Rd/Chesterfield Rd, Turn left then turn right into Thirlwell Rd by the Red Lion. This group is for all kinds of cyclists; it organises events, information and rides, and campaigns for cycling to be taken seriously as a means of transport, with the facilities it needs. www.cyclesheffield.org.uk
