World Development Movement (WDM) pub meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 13 Aug 2012, 8-10pm, Red Deer pub, 18 Pitt St. S1 4DD
Details The World Development Movement (WDM) is a UK-based anti-poverty campaigning organisation. Their welcoming pub meetings are held on the 2nd Monday of every month to discuss ideas, action and projects. A chance to debate and understand issues and generate creative ideas about how to change things. WDM Sheffield website
