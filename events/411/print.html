Cuba's response to the disaster in Haiti

Date, Time & Location Mon 1 Mar 7.30pm at St Matthew's Rooms, Carver St, Sheffield S1 4FE
Details This is an eye-witness report from a recent visit to Cuba's medical brigade in Haiti, by Phil Lenton, coordinator for trade union sponsored NGO 'Salud International'. Cuban doctors in Haiti swung into action after the earthquake, reopening 3 hospitals in Port-au-Prince and attending to people without the ridiculous use of US-style military control. All donations received at the meeting will help sustain the Cuban medical brigade in Haiti. Meeting costs will be met by Sheffield Cuba Solidarity Campaign
