Falafel and film evening

Date, Time & Location Fri 13 May 2011, from 7pm St Matthews Rooms, Carver Street (off Division St.)
Details Lovely Palestinian food and the showing of a film about 1948. One of three important events over the coming days, to commemorate the 63rd anniversary of the Palestinian Catastrophe, the Nakba. Games and presents for the children. All proceeds to children's projects in Gaza. On the door: £8/£6 Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity Campaign and Sheffield Students Palestine society
