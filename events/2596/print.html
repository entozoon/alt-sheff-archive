Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 19 Mar 2013, 7pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St, S1 2EW
Details The regular meeting of Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change (SCACC) is held on the 3rd Tuesday each month. SCACC organises days of action, demonstrations, film shows and talks for local groups, on the vital matter of climate change. Anyone interested in climate campaigning or linking their organisation to Sheffield Climate Alliance is welcome to attend.
