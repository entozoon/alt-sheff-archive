Heeley Festival

Date, Time & Location Sat 19 June 2010
Details Expect a summer fayre extravaganza at Heeley City Farm, with live music, stalls and activities plus the Farm Kitchen and Energy Centre. Includes a 'Family Fuel Buster Fun Day', free activity sessions for families in the Energy Centre (at Heeley City Farm) where staff will advise on ways to reduce and manage your fuel bills and increase the energy efficiency of your home. Plus Heeley Wheelie Festival 12-4pm at nearby Heeley Millennium Park.
