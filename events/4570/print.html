Migration Matters Festival

Date, Time & Location Mon 20 Jun 2016, Theatre Delicatessen, 17 The Moor (old Woolworths shop) S1 3LR
Details Monday events continuing the Migration Matters Festival. 11am & 1pm, Everyday First Aid, by The British Red Cross. Make first aid an everyday skill! 1pm-8pm, World Refugee Day: Local to Global, by City of Sanctuary, SYMAAG & SAGE. Exhibition and stalls from charities and organizations responding to the current refugee crisis. 6pm-6.40pm, Hamsa: The Documentary (+ Q&A), by The International Refugee Assistance Project. One woman’s story inspires a global movement. 8pm, Sanctum, by Quite Right Theatre. One man show about a Kosovan refugee. Part of Refugee Week, celebrating sanctuary in Sheffield, which was the UK’s first City of Sanctuary. A partnership between Theatre Delicatessen and University of Sheffield supported by Arts Council England. All events are "Pay What You Decide". Migration Matters Festival continues through to 25th June 2016. Migration Matters Festival
