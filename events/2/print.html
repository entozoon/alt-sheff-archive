Lobbying Ed Miliband on climate justice

Date, Time & Location Sat 11 July 2009, 11.15am - 2.15pm, in Doncaster
Details In advance of the Copenhagen summit, perhaps the most critical gathering in human history, churches, schools and campaigners are lobbying Ed Miliband on 'Climate Justice - Creation Care : March, Celebrate and Have Your Say with Ed Miliband MP. Demand that developed countries cut domestic carbon emissions by at least 80% by 2050 and assist poorer countries to develop sustainably and adapt to climate change.'
