Public Lecture: The Rise of Innovation Districts

Date, Time & Location Thu 15 Jan 2015, 7.30pm-8.30pm (doors open 7pm) Sheffield Cathedral, Church St. S1 1HA
Details Bruce Katz, internationally renowned policy adviser on economic and urban development, comes to Sheffield for a public lecture on the innovation district model. Bruce regularly advises public, private and civic leaders on sustainable policy reform; he co-led the housing and urban transition team for the Obama Administration and was Chief of Staff to the Secretary of Housing and Urban Development under President Clinton. Bruce will be joined by Julie Wagner, Senior Fellow at the Metropolitan Policy Program, and Kelly Kline, Economic Development Director of the City of Fremont, California for a panel discussion and Questions & Answers. Organised by the University of Sheffield, Sheffield City Council and Creative Sheffield. This is a free, ticketed event. Booking here: http://goo.gl/BlNxKU
