Shale gas extraction in South Yorks: public event

Date, Time & Location Wed 16 Jul 2014, 3pm-8pm, Mayflower Bar & Eatery, High Street, Austerfield, Doncaster DN10 6QU.
Details IGas Energy Plc, onshore oil and gas exploration company, are looking for areas for fracking (now banned in France and Germany). This event is designed to calm public fears about the risks. If you can get to Austerfield, south of Doncaster, why not drop in and let them know what you think about unconventional oil and gas extraction? www.igas-engage.co.uk Facebook event page www.frackfreesouthyorkshire.co.uk
