ASSIST carol service

Sat 10 Dec 2011, 3pm, Sacred Heart church, 479 Langsett Rd, Hillsborough S6 2LN

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

You are warmly invited to the ASSIST carol service, with music from round the world as well as the traditional Christmas story. ASSIST helps destitute asylum seekers left without support or accommodation if their claim is refused in the UK courts. They would love to have lots of people to join from all over the city so please spread the word.The church is on the tram route and easily accessible by bus and car.
