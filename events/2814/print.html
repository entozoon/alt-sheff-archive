ROTHERHAM SLAMFEST

Date, Time & Location 6-7 Jul 2013, Rotherham
Details Rotherham Art Events free community-run music festival, “not for profit” but to get musicians and artists involved in performance and promotion in Rotherham. Various gigs over two days. Saturday: 12-late in All Saints Square (town centre-main stage), 2 stages in Clifton Park, High House pub, The Trades Club (near rail and bus stations), and Imperial Buildings. Sunday: "Party in the Park" 12-8pm in Clifton Park. Full line-up: www.slamfest.net
