"Making Space"

Date, Time & Location 22 Jan-Sat 9 Feb 2019, 10am-6pm weekdays, 11am-4pm Sats, New Roots, 347 Glossop Rd. S10 2HP
Details An exhibition about squatting, trespass and housing direct action, by Sam Burgum, researcher at the University of Sheffield. Alternative radical histories of property ownership and protest from the Magna Carta to current day activist campaigns, well worth a visit. (New Roots is a volunteer-run, non-for-profit community shop. It also features ethical local and Fairtrade products, organic fruit and veg, vegan and gluten-free refreshments, and activity space).
