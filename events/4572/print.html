Migration Matters Festival

Date, Time & Location Wed 22 Jun 2016, Theatre Delicatessen, 17 The Moor (old Woolworths shop) S1 3LR
Details Events for Wednesday, continuing the Migration Matters Festival. 6pm-7.30pm, The Invisible Guest, by Howl Yuan. “He changed his English name many times.” 8pm, Rather, by Eclipse Theatre & Amaal Sharif. "Do the right, or ignore all that’s happening in the world?" Part of Refugee Week, celebrating sanctuary in Sheffield, which was the UK’s first City of Sanctuary. A partnership between Theatre Delicatessen and University of Sheffield supported by Arts Council England. All events are "Pay What You Decide". Migration Matters Festival continues through to 25th June 2016. Migration Matters Festival
