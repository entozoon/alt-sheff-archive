DEMONSTRATION: Rally For Our Future

Date, Time & Location Sat 14 Jul 2012, from 11.30am, city centre
Details Rally to defend education, public services, pensions, pay and jobs. Called by the NUT and NASUWT unions, supported by various unions, trades councils and Sheffield Anti-Cuts Alliance. This is one of two national protests targetting the seats of Cameron and Clegg, with national speakers. Assembly 11.30am at Devonshire Green to march to Barkers Pool for the rally in front of City Hall.
