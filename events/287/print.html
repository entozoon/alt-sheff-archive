Transition Ceilidh

Date, Time & Location Sat 16 Jan 2010, 8pm-10pm at The Grapevine Centre, Northcote Road, Heeley, S2 3AT
Details Fun and dancing organised by the Transition Sheffield group. Everyone is welcome, including children. Food will be available and it will be a non-alcoholic event. For those who feel like continuing to socialise then The Sheaf pub is not far away. www.transitionsheffield.org.uk
