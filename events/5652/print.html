Indian Feast and Dance Extravaganza

Date, Time & Location Thu 27 Jun 2019, 6.30-9.30pm, Hallam View Restaurant, 6th Floor Owen Building, Howard St. S11WB
Details This event is in aid of WEST - Women in Engineering Science and Technology charity. Each year they give out Awards to young women that are training in non-traditional trades, eg carpentry, bricklaying, motor vehicles. The Indian Feast and Dance is a great food experience, at Sheffield Hallam University, with fantastic dance demonstrations, by local artists, Son de America, Steel City Rappers, Lizzie Dripping and Charu. Tickets from We Got Tickets website or directly, call 07941 141126 or email rozglencoe@talktalk.net Women in Engineering Science and Technology (WEST)
