Start up

Date, Time & Location Thu 10 Dec 2015, 10-1pm Woodhouse Methodist Church Hall, Chapel St. S13 7JL
Details Start up • Are you aged 50 or over? • Would you like to see new activities happening in your area? • Do you want to develop an idea and start something yourself? Ignite Imaginations can support your ideas and help start up groups and events for people aged 50 and over in your local community. Come and meet to find out how to get involved. Start up is a programme run by Ignite Imaginations supporting Older People to start new activities and projects from gardening to disco dancing, art classes to reading clubs. If you can’t come contact directly on Sheff 2686813 or email admin@igniteimaginations.org.uk
