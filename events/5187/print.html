Christmas Vegan Festival

Date, Time & Location Sat 16 Dec 2017, 12-7pm, Students Union Foundry & Auditorium, Western Bank S10 2TG
Details Sheffield University Students Union presents its second vegan festival, with a Christmas theme this time. There will be hot food, specialist ingredients, gifts, talks, documentaries and cooking demonstrations. Open to the public, and all ages.
