Mental Self-Help Celebration

Date, Time & Location Thu 25 Apr 2013, 10am-1pm, The Circle, 33 Rockingham Lane S1 4FW
Details A celebration with info and presentations on the work of mental health self-help groups and other user-led projects in Sheffield. It will highlight things like the right to control, peer support, and art and self-help. Organised by Sheffield SHIP and Sheffield City Council. Free entry, but pre-booking is essential, email Patrick Wood p.wood@vas.org.uk or tel. Sheff 2536626.
