Dronfield Music Festival

Date, Time & Location Sun 7 Aug 2011, 12 noon-11pm at Dronfield S18 8XF
Details Dronfield's answer to Glastonbury; all proceeds go to Dronfield and Sheffield based charities from this musical festival which celebrates over a decade of action. Acoustic stage, main stage, and loads of stalls from food to henna tattoos. There's even a rumour that Sheffield Live! will be there. A fantastic family day out, in nearby north-east Derbyshire. Tickets in advance £10/£4 kids/senior citizens, under 8s free, family tickets £23. Bus service 43 from Sheffield. Full details on website, see the 'Coming Along' page. www.dronfieldmusicfestival.co.uk
