Greenpeace meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 9 Oct 2012, 8pm Red Lion, Charles St (Hubs end) Sheffield S1 2ND
Details Monthly meeting of Greenpeace's active South Yorkshire group, held on the 2nd Tuesday of each month. Current campaigning includes anti-Shell protests to stop drilling for oil in the Arctic. A cross-party committee of Members of Parliament has concluded that any oil spill there would be devastating to the fragile wildlife. Newcomers always welcome. Greenpeace South Yorkshire website
