Allowed Out Workshop

Date, Time & Location Sat 30 Jun 2012, 10.30 am–1pm. Quaker Meeting House, St James St. S1 2EW
Details Songs and stories of LGBT activism. All members of the LGBT community are warmly invited to take part in free, friendly writing workshops, facilitated by local writer River Wolton. Some of the writing will be used (with author's permission) in a multi-media performance by Out Aloud LGBT choir at University of Sheffield’s 'Festival of the Mind' on 26 September, full details on website. www.storiesofactivism.group.shef.ac.uk/?page_id=125
