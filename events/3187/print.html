Repair Cafe

Date, Time & Location Sat 29 Mar 2014, 10 - 4pm Heeley City Farm, Richards Rd. S2 3DT
Details The next cafe is fast approaching! A place for people who can mend stuff to bring their tools and people with broken stuff to get together and share skills and get stuff mended over a cuppa. Bring: Small Electrical appliances, toys, shoes, clothes, pots, kitchen equipment, computers, bikes, audio equipment, furniture ... They will see if they can help you mend it If you would like to volunteer as a repairer, please email info@repairsheffield.org to be registered. Ring Sheff 2580482 option 6 for more details Let's get fixing, Sheffield!! Heeley City Farm is 20 mins walk from Sheffield, Bus 33 or 47.
