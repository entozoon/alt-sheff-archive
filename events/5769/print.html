Fairtrade Conference (Nottingham)

Date, Time & Location Sat 26 Oct 2019, 10.30am-3.30pm, Albert Hall Conference Centre, North Circus St. Nottingham NG1 5AA
Details Fairtrade (East Midlands) holds its conference this year in Nottingham. Chance to chat with local campaigners, the Fairtrade Foundation team and other experts. Talks addressing the big issues for Fairtrade in 2019 like the Climate Crisis (the biggest threat to many Fairtrade farmers across the world) Attending is completely free, including a free lunch on the day, if you sign up in advance (link below).
