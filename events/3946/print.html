Hidden Perspectives LGBT Photo Exhibition

Date, Time & Location 23-27 Feb 2015, 10am-6pm, Jessop West Foyer, University of Sheffield
Details Celebrating LGBT History Month, an exhibition of local amateur and professional photographers showcasing images submitted by volunteer contributors to celebrate LGBT lives in Sheffield, and diversity at the University. The exhibition seeks to give a platform to LGBT people to express themselves through an artistic process and will also include work by University of Sheffield students on the pioneering interdisciplinary LGBT Studies module. It continues the legacy of previous LGBT projects at the University of Sheffield, celebrating everyday life, milestones in equality and to highlight challenges that the LGBT community face. Free admission, open to the public. Hidden Perspectives
