Celebrate and defend multicultural Tower Hamlets

Date, Time & Location Sat 3 Sep 2011, London: coach from Sheffield
Details Across Britain communities continue to face the racism and violence of the far right English Defence League (EDL). On 3 September the EDL is mobilising nationally in an attempt to bring its anti-Muslim hatred and violence to Tower Hamlets. In response, the people of East London have called on people to join them in celebration and defence of their multicultural community. Sheffield Unite Against Fascism is running a coach from Sheffield. Tickets £17, £8 low waged/unwaged. Please book now - email info@sheffielduaf.org.uk or ring 07545 197939
