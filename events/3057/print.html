Sharrow Festival planning meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 4 Nov 2013, 8pm Old Sharrow Junior School, South View Rd S7 1DB
Details Next planning meeting for Sharrow Festival Committee organising the free annual Sharrow Community Festival in the Sharrow ward of Sheffield. The Sharrow Festival has been organised by local volunteers since 1996. They receive no statutory funding and always welcome support and donations. Sharrow Festival website
