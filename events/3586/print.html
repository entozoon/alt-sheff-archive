World Development Movement (WDM) meeting

Date, Time & Location Thu 4 Sep 2014, 7pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details WDM aims to change the actions, policies and institutions of governments, corporations and wealthy minorities, which are preventing an end to poverty and injustice. Part of a global movement, it works alongside people protesting in the developing world. This month: planning a lobby of the British American Business roadshow when it comes to Sheffield on Wed. 1st October, and another action for the noTTIP campaign. The meeting of WDM is held on the first Thursday of each month, to discuss coming events and campaigns. It is usually followed by informal gathering in the nearby Church House pub, approx. 9.15-10.15pm. If you plan to come only to the pub then please contact (phone 07800 666553 or leave a message at the QMH), as occasionally the group does not go to the pub. WDM Sheffield website
