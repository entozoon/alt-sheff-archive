Films: "How to Fight and Win"

Date, Time & Location Wed 6 Nov 2013, 7pm, Shalesmoor
Details Workers' Liberty runs regular film showings, food, and cheap drinks are available to buy, and they always have a good discussion. In this film showing a series of short clips around the theme of 'How to fight to win'. This will involve clips from: The 3 Cosas Campaign (www.facebook.com/3coca) for rights of workers at the University of London. The occupation of Vestas wind turbine factory against closure in 2009. The awe inspiring campaign teachers in Chicago waged against a federal government intent on destroying the school system, fighting their own union bureacracy on the way to make sure they got the strike to go ahead. Entry £4 / £3 unwaged including food. Cheap drinks available. Venue is secret! Meet at Acorn Street carpark, Shalesmoor. Workers' Liberty on Facebook
