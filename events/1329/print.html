Performance: Asylum Monologues

Date, Time & Location Sat 2 Apr 2011, 5pm Stephenson Music Hall, Oakholme Road S10 3DF.
Details The highly acclaimed Asylum Monologues comes to Sheffield as part of Platform Festival at the University of Sheffield. A first-hand account of the UK's asylum system in the words of people who have experienced it, facts, political statements and public opinion. A free event, but arrive early to secure a seat. iceandfire
