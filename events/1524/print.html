Stannington Carnival

Date, Time & Location Sat 9 Jul 2011 from 12 noon, Stannington Park and Lomas Hall
Details Annual carnival, run by Stannington Carnival Organisation and Festival Friends (SCOFF!) Stalls, parade, displays, and a chance for local organisations to raise funds and promote themselves. The usual fun stuff for a day out, all in a good cause. Volunteers welcome as well, no doubt. www.actionforstannington.org.uk
