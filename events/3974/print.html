ASSIST stall at Netheredge Market

Date, Time & Location Sun 22 Mar 2015, 11am Everard’s Yard, Glen Rd. Netheredge
Details Stock up on your ASSIST greetings cards and grab a cup of tea or coffee from Everard’s marvellous cafe! ASSIST supports destitute asylum seekers in Sheffield by providing accommodation, food and support to those in most need or distress. If you want to find out more about ASSIST there will be plenty of volunteers to chat to. ASSIST Sheffield
