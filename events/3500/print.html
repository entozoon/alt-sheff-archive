A Gig for Estelí

Date, Time & Location Fri 11 Jul 2014, 7.30pm The Riverside, 1 Mowbray St. Neepsend S3 8EN
Details The launch of Early Cartographers new EP, On Swings and Roundabouts, is a fundraiser for the Sheffield Estelí Society. There is also support from experimental acoustic solo artist Jim Ghedi, and Solana, a latin-infused, celtic influenced, multicultural acoustic band. More about the event, and tickets on the Eventbrite page below Sheffield Estelí Society Early Cartographers Facebook event page onswingsandroundabouts.eventbrite.co.uk
