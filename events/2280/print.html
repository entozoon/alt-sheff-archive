Organic Gardening course

Date, Time & Location Wed 17 Oct 2012, 2-4pm Hollinsend Methodist Church, corner of Hollinsend Rd & Mansfield Rd, S12 2EF
Details Second session of 10 on growing your own fruit and vegetables, for beginners or experienced growers. A free ten week, classroom based course looking at the theory of organic gardening, soil management, ecology, sustainability and growing food naturally. Also with some practical sessions in Hollinsend Community Food Garden. Wednesdays 2-4pm from October 10th till December 19th. Buses 120 and 44 go to Hollinsend Methodist Church, Free to all.
