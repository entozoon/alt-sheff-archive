Campaign for Nuclear Disarmament (CND) meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 21 Oct 2009 at 7.15pm at The Quaker Meeting House, St James Street, Sheffield S1 2EW (near the Anglican cathedral)
Details All welcome to CND's monthly meeting to discuss the latest developments in the world of nuclear weapons including Obama's decision to halt plans to site missile defence sites in the Czech Republic and Poland, report back from the national demonstration on Oct 3rd at Menwith Hill USA Missile Defence Base near Harrogate and from national conference on Oct 10th & 11th. Come and share ideas for future events. Contact: email sheffieldcnd@hotmail.com or See website
