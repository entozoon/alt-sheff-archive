SHEFFIELD PEACE FAIR 2011

Date, Time & Location Sat 12 Nov 2011, 10.30-4pm, Sheffield Town Hall
Details A day of campaigning and fundraising for many of the lively peace organisations and other progressive activist groups in our city. Festive gifts for all the family. Plenty of stalls with information and items for sale, fun, entertainment and food available too. Voluntary donation on the door. Children free entry.
