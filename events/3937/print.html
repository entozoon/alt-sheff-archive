Sheffield’s Manifesto for a Feminist Government

Date, Time & Location Sat 25 April 2015, 1-3pm Theatre Delicatessen, 17 The Moor, S1 4PF
Details With inequality, sexism and violence a part of so many women’s lives, Sheffield’s Feminist Manifesto is a chance to create a list of demands for a government to inspire, liberate and find solutions to these problems. The meeting will conclude with the writing of a manifesto drawn together from your contributions and presented to all those wanting your vote on 7th May. Part of the Festival of Debate taking place in Sheffield in the run-up to the general election, during March and April. It provides opportunities to get together and discuss a range of issues which affect everyone. Informed decision making and strategic planning are essential for democracy, so come and get involved. They also welcome volunteers, from attending events and taking part in discussions to volunteering time to help run an event, promote the festival and much more. Unless otherwise stated events are free to attend, but if possible please register via the appropriate Eventbrite link on the Festival of Debate website below to give organisers an idea of numbers.
