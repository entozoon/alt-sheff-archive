Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity Campaign AGM

Date, Time & Location Sat 2 Feb 2013, 1.30pm-5pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Annual General Meeting of the Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity Campaign, with guest speaker Abdel Takriti (University of Sheffield) on the Impact of the Palestinian revolution on the Middle East. Discussion will include activities over the last period, assessment of the current situation and plans for the coming year. It is important that supporters are fully involved with the discussions, so please come if you are able. (Note this meeting was postponed from October due to a clash with a TUC national demonstration.) Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity Campaign
