Current Affairs course

Date, Time & Location Thu 8 Jan 2015, 10.30-12.30 Victoria Hall Methodist Church, Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details Current Affairs, a WEA (Workers Educational Association) course begins today, as a discussion course looking at social, environmental, political and ethical issues, UK and world-wide. Topics to be agreed with the class. Enrol for term. Fees for 11 weeks £71.50, or FREE if on means-tested state benefits, or Working Tax Credit with income under £15,276. Enrol online www.wea.org.uk/yh, or phone WEA Regional Office 0113 2453304, or on the day - but please contact tutor first (01904 621510 or email info@paulboizot.co.uk) to say you are coming, and to check if course is full.
