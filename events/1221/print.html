World Development Movement (WDM) Campaign meeting

Date, Time & Location Thu 6 Jan 2011, 7-9pm Sheffield United Reform Church, 60 Norfolk St.
Details Film : Life and Debt, which looks at the devastating impact of globalisation and World Bank policies on Jamaican industry and agriculture, showing the ways in which the World Bank is implicated in keeping countries poor. Afterwards there will be some time to discuss the film and how this relates to WDM's "No new debt" campaign. Contact WDMSheffield@googlemail.com for more information. WDM Sheffield website
