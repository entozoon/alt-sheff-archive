Beyond Words: recording session

Date, Time & Location Fri 27 Apr 2012, 10am-6pm, Yellow Arch Studios, 30-36 Burton Rd. S3 8BX
Details A call for singers and musicians who are refugees, asylum-seekers or exiles. The Sanctuary Worldbeat Sessions are bringing people together to write, rehearse and record a new song for special release as a vinyl single and music video in Refugee Week 2012. It's free. Spend all day mixing it all up in a top recording studio with musicians from Rafiki Jazz star world music band and poet Noah Okecho. Call to register and for more information: Arts on the Run: Refugee and Arts Network Yorks and Humberside, tel. Sheff. 3463047, or email office@konimusic.co.uk
