Low Pay and Payday Loans event

Date, Time & Location Fri 4 Apr 2014, 12-1pm, The Circle, 33 Rockingham Lane S1 4FW
Details The TUC (Trades Union Congress) is organising a series of events around the country to highlight low pay and the cost of living crisis. The event for Sheffield is on 4th April. Speakers include Paul Blomfield MP, Joanne Thomas, Usdaw and Neil Foster, TUC, The Circle, 33 Rockingham Lane, is between West Street and Division Street. Fair Pay Fortnight
