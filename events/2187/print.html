Friends of the Earth meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 20 Aug 2012, 8pm Red Deer pub, 18 Pitt St. S1 4DD
Details Friends of the Earth Sheffield are people from all walks of life who share a common concern for the state of the planet and a desire to do something about it. Membership is free if you subscribe to the electronic newsletter. The Red Deer is just off West Street (meeting in the upstairs room) Sheffield FOE website
