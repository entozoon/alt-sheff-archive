Free vegan food fayre

Date, Time & Location Sat 5 Dec 2009, between 11am and 3pm at The Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James Street, Sheffield S1 2EW (near the Anglican cathedral)
Details Sheffield Animal Friends free event. Sample some delicious cruelty-free food and find out about the benefits of a vegan lifestyle for your health, the animals and the planet, why not come along? There will also be lots of info on how to get active in campaigning, as well as stalls from animal charities and campaign organisations where you can find out how to support their work. Email for any more details sheffieldanimalfriends@gmail.com
