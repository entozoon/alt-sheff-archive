BookSwap !

Date, Time & Location Sat 13 Oct 2012, 11am-3pm, Winter Garden, Surrey St. S1 2LH
Details Bring any books (in good condition) to swap with a varied selection of books for adults and children, fiction and non-fiction. Books are swapped one-for-one. Recycle, replenish and replace reading material! Admission free. No need to book. Supported by Oxfam, this is part of Off the Shelf, Sheffield's 21-year old annual festival of words, with a great programme of over 200 events this year. Between 13th October and 3rd November 2012, there is everything from community events to readings to workshops, at various venues. Advance booking for events is recommended, see the website (below) for details. www.offtheshelf.org.uk
