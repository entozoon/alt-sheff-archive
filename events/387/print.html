Pre-election public meeting: Asylum Rights and Wrongs

Date, Time & Location Fri 19 Mar 2010, 6-8pm at Victoria Hall Methodist Church, Norfolk St. Sheffield S1 2JB
Details Major public meeting to question General Election candidates on their support (or otherwise) for asylum rights.
  • Sam (seeking asylum from Zimbabwe)
  • Nick Clegg (Sheffield Hallam MP and Liberal Democrat leader)
  • Paul Blomfield (Labour candidate for Sheffield Central)
  • Jillian Creasy (Green candidate for Sheffield Central)
  • Nicola Bates (Conservative parliamentary candidate Sheffield Hallam)
Organised by SYMAAG , ASSIST, City of Sanctuary and Student Action for Refugees
