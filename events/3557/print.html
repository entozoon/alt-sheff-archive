ABBEYFIELD PARK MULTICULTURAL FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sat 5-Sun 6 Jul 2014, Abbeyfield Park, Abbeyfield Rd S4 7AT
Details This is a massive annual multicultural community festival, organised by the local residents with a wide variety of musical acts, food and craft stalls from across the community and across the world. And for 2014 it will be a two-day event, allowing people to see the Tour de France ride by! Free entry. Abbeyfield Park website
