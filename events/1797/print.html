Sheffield CND&#39;s AGM

Date, Time & Location Wed 18 Jan 2012, 7pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Sheffield CND (Campaign for Nuclear Disarmament) holds it's annual general meeting with speaker Janet Fenton of Scottish CND. She will talk about implications for the Trident missile programme of Scottish independence. CND Vice-Chair Jeremy Corbyn MP recently discovered that the Lib-Dem-led review of alternatives to Trident will never be published. It seems that the government is afraid to discuss this matter, and is trying to suppress the debate. There is a new invigorated campaign on this issue being launched by national CND. Come and join in, newcomers are welcome. Sheffield CND website
