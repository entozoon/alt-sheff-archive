Hiroshima Day Commemoration

Date, Time & Location Mon 6 Aug 2012, 11am-2pm, outside Sheffield Town Hall
Details All are welcome to join Sheffield Campaign For Nuclear Disarmament in marking the 67th anniversary of the day the atomic bomb was dropped on the Japanese city of Hiroshima, causing so many deaths and so much suffering and devastation. Come and make a paper crane, a symbol of peace and reconciliation. At 11am the Lord Mayor will sign the Mayors For Peace declaration.
