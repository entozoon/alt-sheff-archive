Public meeting - Justice for Palestine: what can we do?

Date, Time & Location Tue 12 Aug 2014, 7.30pm, The Workstation, Paternoster row (opposite the Hubs) S1 2BX
Details Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign invites you to come and talk with people about how you can get involved in campaigning for Palestinian justice, including supporting the movement for boycott, divestment and sanctions (BDS) against Israel. Everyone welcome. Please circulate to all who want to get involved in effective campaigning for Palestine. Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign
