Talk: A million trees and counting ...

Date, Time & Location Wed 25 Apr 2012, 7pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details The founder and executive director of Trees for Life will be giving a talk in Sheffield, entitled 'A million trees and counting - the journey to restore the Caledonian Forest', as part of a tour to celebrate the planting of the millionth tree.
