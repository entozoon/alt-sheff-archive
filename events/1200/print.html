Transition Ceilidh

Date, Time & Location Sat 16 Jan 2011, 8pm - 10pm, The Grapevine Centre, Northcote Road, Heeley, S2 3AT
Details Fun and dance with Transition Sheffield, which is part of the growing Transition movement, tackling climate change and peak oil with practical hands-on solutions that work. Everyone welcome including children. Food will be available and it will be a non-alcoholic event. For those who feel like continuing to socialise then The Sheaf pub is not far away. www.transitionsheffield.org.uk
