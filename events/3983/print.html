"Feminism against Imperialism: Building solidarity and connecting struggles"

Date, Time & Location Tue 3 Mar 2015, 6.15pm, Sheffield Uni Nelson Mandela Auditorium
Details As part of Israeli Apartheid Week, Sheffield Student Union Palestine Society are organising this talk by Dr. Rania Al Masri, professor, writer, political activist and environmental scientist at the American University of Beirut. The talk will shed light on the important role of women in the struggle against different forms of imperialism and the importance of connecting different struggles for the aim of building solidarity. SHU Palestine Society
