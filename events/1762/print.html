Art and the Labour Movement

Date, Time & Location Fri 11 Nov 2011, 7pm, Wortley Hall, Sheffield S35 7DB
Details An open table discussion with Darrall Cozens, all welcome. An event running alongside Ashes and Diamonds exhibition, a tribute to the mining industry and those who fought so long and hard to defend it, raising awareness and debate on relevant issues for today. Organised by the National Justice for Mineworkers Campaign and supported by Derbyshire NUM Venue details: www.wortleyhall.org.uk
