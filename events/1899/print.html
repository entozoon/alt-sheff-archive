Sheffield Amnesty International meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 14 Mar 2012, 7.30pm at Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Amnesty International is a world wide organisation of nearly 3 million people all working to protect Human Rights. The Sheffield branch meets monthly on the 2nd Wed of every month, except August. Actions on human rights abuses, contacts, successes and updates from various campaigns. Newcomers welcome.
