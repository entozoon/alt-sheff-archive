Grand Concert: XVth International Brigade Memorial Trust

Wed 13 Jul 2011, * * * SORRY COMPLETELY SOLD OUT * * *

Share on

PLEASE NOTE THIS EVENT IS COMPLETELY SOLD OUT: SORRYRoy Bailey, Ray Hearne, Dolores Long, Jim McDonald, Mike Wild, Dave Burland, Fay Hield, Keith Kendrick, Sylvia Needham, Tony Trippett, Los Sinsontes, Paul Trippett, Paul Jordan, George Pearson, Robin Garside, Simon Heywood, Shonaleigh, The Black Hares, Rob Hindle and Geoff Lawes in a concert compered by Pete Smith to commemorate the many people from the UK and elsewhere, who died fighting Franco's fascist forces in the 1930s. An anti-fascist exhibition is also running at the Jessop West Building of Sheffield University, and a plaque has been installed in the Peace Gardens. £6/£5 concessions or £8 on door. Buses 120, 123, 44, short walk from Sheffield station or Sheffield College tram stop.Details: tel Sheff 2016350 www.international-brigades.org.uk
