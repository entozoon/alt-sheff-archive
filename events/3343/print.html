2014 State of Sheffield report workshop

Date, Time & Location Fri 11 Apr 2014, 10.30am-1pm, Reception Rooms, Town Hall S1 1WB
Details Sheffield First (the Local Strategic Partnership) workshop to present the 2014 State of Sheffield report and consider the research implications arising from it. The session includes contributions from Julie Dore, Leader of the Council, Sharon Squires, Director of Sheffield First, and the co-authors of the report Professor Gordon Dabinett (TRP and RESS) and Andi Walshaw (Sheffield City Council). The session is open to anyone interested in contributing to the future research agenda for the city, but places are limited. Booking via Sheffield First by the 4th April. Sheffield First
