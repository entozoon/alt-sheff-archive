Cinema Palestino 2010

Date, Time & Location Fri 26 - Sun 28 Nov 2010, Showroom Cinema, Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign and Yorkshire Palestine Cultural Exchange once again are collaborating with Showroom cinema to enable wider appreciation of Palestinian cinema. This is a special mix of poignant and uplifting documentary as well as a humorous take on the experience of the Palestinian diaspora. The Showroom are doing a £20 offer to see all Cinema Palestino films over the weekend, the Showroom restaurant is putting on Palestinian food all the weekend with a £25 deal for a meal for two including a bottle of wine and two tickets to a film. 50% of the net profits from the season will be donated back to children's projects sponsored by Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign See Showroom website for full details
