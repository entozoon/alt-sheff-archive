Public meeting - Natalie Bennett "Why Sheffield Needs Green Solutions"

Date, Time & Location Thu 24 Nov 2016, 7pm, Banner Cross Methodist Church, 12 Ecclesall Rd South S11 9PE
Details Public meeting and question and answer session with Natalie Bennett, former Green Party leader and now Sheffield Central constituency MP candidate. Plus an opportunity to find out more about Green Party activity in and around south west Sheffield with Cllr Magid Magid (Broomhill & Sharrow Vale ward) in attendance, and Ecclesall ward activists. 6.45pm for 7pm start. Banner Cross Methodist Church is located in the south-west of Sheffield at the junction of Ecclesall Road South and Psalter Lane. Refreshments will be served.
