Public Sector Pensions Dispute Day - N30

Wed 30 Nov 2011, national strike. Meet 11.30 Howard St. or 12.00 at Barkers Pool S1 2HB

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

A joint union action, with up to 3 million public sector workers striking against attacks on their pensions, among the vicious cuts planned by the coalition government. In Sheffield, workers from across South Yorkshire meet at Barkers Pool to march through the city centre, followed by a rally. A Sheffield Hallam University feeder march will leave from Howard Street S1 1WB at 11.30. Staff and students will be marching to join the TUC rally at Barkers Pool for 12 noon. Other strikers, students and supporters are welcome to join this. Sheffield Anti Cuts Alliance (SACA) website below has an Activists Resource Page full of helpful links and downloads for the campaign, and regularly updated information on what is happening. www.sheffieldanticuts.wordpress.com
