To Walk In Your Shoes

Date, Time & Location Sat 18 Jun 2016, Theatre Delicatessen, 17 The Moor (old Woolworths shop) S1 3LR
Details It’s been almost a decade since the press complaints commission issued guidance to address what it agreed was widespread inaccurate reporting on the issue of asylum, yet the media still fuels negativity and a one sided view. To Walk In Your Shoes is a documentary exploration that represents the side without a voice. 2pm and 4pm To Walk in Your Shoes (excerpts), by Red Bobble Theatre: What if you had an hour to flee your country? Exhibition by Rachael Munro-Fawcett, continues daily 10.30am– 6pm, until Friday. Part of Refugee Week, celebrating sanctuary in Sheffield, which was the UK’s first City of Sanctuary. A partnership between Theatre Delicatessen and University of Sheffield supported by Arts Council England. All events are "Pay What You Decide". Migration Matters Festival continues through to 25th June 2016. Migration Matters Festival
