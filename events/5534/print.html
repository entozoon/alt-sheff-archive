SCHOOL CLIMATE STRIKE

Date, Time & Location Fri 15 Mar 2019, 11am-2pm, outside Sheffield Town Hall S1 2HH
Details Strike from school to demand action on the climate catastrophe. Students, pupils and young people, globally are waking up to a reality where we can no longer choose to ignore this issue. This is a Global Emergency. 100,000 species go extinct annually due to pollution. Resources are running out, and we only have 10 years left to save the planet. We must stand up and fight for our future or face an uninhabitable planet. All welcome. “The continuation of civilisation is in your hands” David Attenborough tells world leaders. Greta Thunberg, vegan, of the Fridays for Future climate movement in Sweden at 15, began standing outside her parliament protesting instead of going to school every Friday (see video).
