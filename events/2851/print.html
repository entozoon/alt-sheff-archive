Sheffield Unite Community Branch meeting - open to all

Date, Time & Location Tue 9 Jul 2013, 5pm, Central United Reformed Church, Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details Sheffield Unite Community Branch is a union open to everyone in the community. The agenda for this meeting will include continuing opposition to workfare, community education in Sheffield, the Bedroom Tax/benefit cuts, Unite September social, and more. Come along and have your say about what you think they should be doing over the next month. All welcome. Unite Community Facebook page
