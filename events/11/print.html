Public meeting: 'We Want Our Buses Back'

Date, Time & Location Thu 30 Jul 2009, 7pm to 9pm, at The Quaker Meeting House, St James Street, Sheffield S1 2EW (near the cathedral)
Details First bus drivers are striking because the company is treating them as it has treated passengers - with contempt. Good public transport is key to a clean environment but the current privatized, deregulated system is reducing trust in bus services, not increasing it. This meeting is open to all, and a number of speakers are being invited. Refreshments provided. Website: wwobb.org
