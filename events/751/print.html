Introduction to Permaculture Courses

Date, Time & Location Starts Sat 17-Sun 18 Jul 2010
Details Hazelhurst Community Supported Agriculture project is running this course for people intending to get involved in the project at Hazelhurst. It will be very high quality teaching by Joe Atkinson Learning Coordinator of the UK Permaculture Association. Trainees will learn Permaculture principles and focus on the design of the Community Supported Agriculture land. It includes camping on the 9 acre field. 9.30am–5.30 pm with fabulous social event Saturday night. Venue: Hazelhurst CSA Coop Ltd, Big Pond Field, Hazelhurst Lane S8 8BG Another run of the course is Sat 7/Sun 8 August for those who might be on holiday in July. Cost £30-£120 based on income. £10 deposit to confirm booking. Send cheques (payable to Leeds Permaculture Network) Suzi High, 15 Heath Grove, Leeds, LS28 8HP. Tel. 0113 2307461
