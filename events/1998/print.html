Huddersfield Luddites 200 Festival

Date, Time & Location Fri 27-Sun 29 Apr 2012, in Huddersfield, West Yorkshire
Details A group of technology politics activists, environmentalists, trade unionists, artists and historians that have come together to celebrate the 200th anniversary of the Luddite uprisings. They are planning a festival in nearby Huddersfield, which was the main centre of Luddite activity in West Yorkshire. The festival will include theatre, music, art, storytelling, re-enactments, film, talks, workshops, demonstration of old/alternative technology, and children's activities. www.luddites200.org.uk Facebook event
