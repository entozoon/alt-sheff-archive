Road Rage! Benefit gig

Mon 22 Apr 2013, doors at 7pm, The Shakespeare, 146-8 Gibraltar St. S3 8UB

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Join Road Rage! for an evening of rebellious rabble-rousing and find out how people are resisting the Tories latest plans to tarmac the countryside. Benefit gig in aid of direct action against new roads in the UK, with a host of treats to tempt you along ...A chunky slice of Sheffield music to while away the night to, including:SIGRID & THE FAMILY BANDGrowling, sparkling, tumbling blues songs from this particularly fine set of musical siblings.ROWAN RHEINGANSFlowing fiddle sounds and perhaps the odd banjo twang too from one of this town's most especially wonderful singers. AL BAKERSongs and ideas carefully-melded and freshly-delivered to give your mind a healthy shake up. OWLY ALPenetrating, mellifluous, passionate sounds and stories from a welcome visitor to Sheffield.Plus several more besides. Also a dinner's-worth of beautiful, animal-friendly (or vegan, if you like) FOOD will be laid on, so no need to eat before you come! And most excitingly, an extra-special ROAD RAGE! RAFFLE, your chance to give a little bit of financial support to the protection of the earth AND win a snazzy prize at the same time. £5/£3 (low/unwaged).Facebook event
