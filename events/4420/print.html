Talk: Access to Justice

Date, Time & Location Mon 14 Mar 2016, 9.30am-3.15pm, Moot Court, Collegiate Campus, off Ecclesall Road
Details An event looking at reduction in legal aid, leading to reduction in access to justice. The event will feature case studies, student presentations and a panel discussion. Part of Social Justice Week, a joint initiative between the Sheffield Hallam University's Helena Kennedy Centre for International Justice and the Department of Humanities, offering a range of events. A diverse range of agencies, individuals and organisations linked by commitment to the principles of social justice come together annually to disseminate what they have achieved and to re-affirm and build on these shared values and public commitments. The week includes a range of presentations, discussions, events and exhibitions that will engage our communities in understanding what they do around social justice, aiming to inspire participation and engagement in future events.
