BIG BOULDER MUSIC & ARTS FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sun 4 Sep 2016, 12-6pm in Heeley People’s Park, Sheffield S8 9TF
Details Heeley People’s Park, Sheffield’s first and largest, community owned and managed public green space is the site of this annual free event, now in its fifth year. It includes live music, food stalls and a bar, and free or low cost arts and crafts for local children and families. They invite artists, crafters, creatives and makers, new and small businesses, freelancers and community projects to be involved. Activities can range from stalls and exhibitions to running workshops. www.HeeleyPark.org
