'DiverCity'

Date, Time & Location Sun 16 May 2010, 2-5pm at PACA, 127 Page Hall Road, Sheffield, S4 8GU
Details Pakistan Advice and Community Association is proud to present DiverCity - an event celebrating difference and building unity between communities. Includes music, world dance, entertainment, and information about different cultures. Refreshments will be available. The aim of the event is to foster greater understanding between communities, helping to build community cohesion. It is supported and funded by Sheffield City Council's New Arrivals Service.
