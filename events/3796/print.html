Sheffield Friday Night Ride: "The Typographic Tour"

Date, Time & Location Fri 16 Jan 2015
Details Sheffield Friday Night Ride (FNR) offers a fun monthly evening bike ride for everyone. Rides are prepared in advance on themes, and this one is The Typographic Tour. Prepare to learn and have fun as you cycle around some of Sheffield's more interesting examples of fonts. Loads of further details on the website. Sheffield Friday Night Ride
