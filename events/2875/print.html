Skeptics in the Pub: "Implicit Bias and Scepticism"

Date, Time & Location Mon 26 Aug 2013, 7.30pm Farm Road Sports & Social Club, Farm Rd. S2 2TP
Details Feminist philosopher Jennifer Saul throws into doubt much of what we think we know in a way that very much demands action. Unconscious and automatic biases against members of groups that are stigmatised in our societies can be seen as giving rise to a new sort of scepticism. She has just completed a book entitled Lying, Misleading and What is Said: An Exploration in Philosophy of Language and in Ethics. The website (below) gives more details. Skeptics in the Pub is about getting people together to have a relaxed and enjoyable evening while listening to talks on a wide range of topics, usually by a notable scientist or skeptic.
