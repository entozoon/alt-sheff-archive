Jamal Juma - Stop the Wall Campaign

Date, Time & Location Tue 8 Oct 2013, 7:30pm, St Matthews Carver St. S1 4FE (to be confirmed)
Details The Israeli apartheid wall snakes through Palestinian lands making Palestinian communities little more than a series of ghettos.
