Lecture: Energy and the Politics of the Future…

Date, Time & Location Tues 6 Oct 2009, 7pm at Sheffield Cathedral
Details One of a series of lectures hosted by Sheffield Cathedral and Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change The Numbers: Dr Alison Cooke, Fellow I Mech E, Chair of the Institute of Mechanical Engineers responds to the Future Climate Project. This will include an assessment of the contribution David MacKay’s ground breaking book "Sustainable Energy – without the hot air" can make to developing realistic policies. All lectures held in Sheffield Cathedral at 7pm. Tickets £2 (concessions £1) for single lecture, all three lectures £5 (concessions £2.50) available in advance by post from Julia Alder at Church House, 95-99 Effingham Street, Rotherham S65 1BL or from Blue Moon café (next to the cathedral) or on the door.
