Dandelion Days - Weekend of herbs, health & nature

Date, Time & Location Fri 8-Sun 10 Aug 2014, Unstone Grange (near Sheffield)
Details Dandelion Days is a weekend of music and entertainment alongside lots of workshops, talks and practical sessions on herbal medicine, foraging, growing your own food, Yoga, Qigong, and many more. Both camping and in-house accommodation are available and all meals are included in the ticket price. Check out the website for more info and to buy tickets. Limited day tickets available for Saturday 9th August. The event is being organised by a not-for-profit group of professional and lay herbalists in and around Sheffield. www.dandeliondays.co.uk
