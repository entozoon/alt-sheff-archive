EcoHomes open week

Date, Time & Location Sun 3 Mar 2013, in Chesterfield
Details As part of Nottinghamshire and Derbyshire EcoHomes open week, two houses in Chesterfield with substantial environmental improvements will be open for viewing. They are close to each other so both can included in one trip. For more details about these and other events during the week, see: www.everybodys-talking.org/ecohomes/events
