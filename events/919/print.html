World Development Movement (WDM) strategy meeting

Date, Time & Location Thu 2 Sep 2010, 7-9pm at Central United Reform Church, 60 Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details The World Development Movement (WDM) is a UK-based anti-poverty campaigning organisation with a worldwide reputation for tackling hard-hitting, controversial issues. Strategy meetings are held every 3 months, looking at longer-term plans of Sheffield WDM group. WDM Sheffield website
