Cuban exhibition celebrates revolution

Date, Time & Location Exhibition thru Oct 2009
Details Sheffield poet Carolyn Waudby exhibits poems about Cuban life since the revolution, in Dreams and Defiance at Sheffield Hallam University's Atrium and Mezzanine Gallery, Furnival Building, Arundel Street throughout October. The exhibition marks the 50th anniversary of the Cuban revolution, with images by Sheffield photographer Paul Schatzberger. Carolyn's blog
