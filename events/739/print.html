'7 Years with Hard Labour'

Date, Time & Location Tue 6 Jul 2010, 7pm Library Theatre, Central Library, Surrey Street, Sheffield S1 1XZ
Details Ice and Fire Theatre presents accounts from former Burmese political prisoners now living in the UK. Through their engaging and often humorous stories, a damning picture of a ruthless military regime is painted. Followed by a discussion with Index on Censorship and former Burmese political prisoners living in the UK. Entry is free, with optional donations in aid of Burmese political prisoners and the Karen Community Association in Sheffield. Not suitable for people under 15. Arrive early to secure a seat! For more information go to www.iceandfire.co.uk
