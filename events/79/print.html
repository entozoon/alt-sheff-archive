Rotherham Show

Date, Time & Location Sat 12 and Sun 13 Sep 2009, 11-6pm at Rosehill Park, Rawmarsh, Rotherham S62 7HJ
Details This year for one year only Rotherham Show is held in a different park while the usual venue is being improved. OK, it's Rotherham, not Sheffield, but most people who go find that it's well worth a visit. This is Rotherham's best, biggest, brightest annual festival. Loads of music by Rotherham musicians in the 'One Town, One Community' Festival from around the world; stalls, diversity festival, dance workshops, and loads more.... Line-up includes: Son Para Todos Peace Artistes Kasai Masai Bharat Indian Group Paradise Steel Band Flossie Malavalle Muldoon's Picnic Diversity D The Glavins Tony Drabbble Vuka Arts Satnum Singh Toein' in the Dark To find out more, or ask about running a stall, tel. 01709 720744 or 01709 746946 or Email for details
