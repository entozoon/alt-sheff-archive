Cuba Solidarity Campaign: Talk by daughter of Che Guevara

Date, Time & Location Thu 9 Nov 2017, 6.30pm, Quaker Meeting House, St. James St. S1
Details Aleida Guevara, Cuban paedatrician, daughter of Che, will be talking about recent events in Cuba. It is 50 years in October since Che was murdered in Bolivia. Aleida will also talk about his legacy.
