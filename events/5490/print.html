DIVERSITY FEST 2019

Date, Time & Location Sun 29 Sep 2019, 1-9pm, Hagglers Corner, 586 Queens Rd. S2 4DU
Details Returning for another year, the celebration for people of every gender, sexuality, ethnicity, age and ability. A statement of unity and mutual respect between the many communities of Sheffield. The festival features three stages (2 indoors) and dozens of performances - music, poetry, stand-up, drumming, dance etc. All run by volunteers on a tiny budget. Bar, and food served till about 4pm. Free to attend, all welcome. Diversity Fest
