Free Radicals Benefit Gig for Chilypep

Date, Time & Location Sat 11 Oct 2014, 7.30pm Crookes Social Club, Mulehouse Rd. Crookes S10 1TD
Details The Free Radicals are a fantastic soul and funk band, performing to raise vital funds for Chilypep and their Young Women Against Violence Empowerment Project. Please come along to the gig and enjoy a great evening and help raise funds for Chilypep, a local Sheffield charity and its new ‘Y-Wave’ group, supporting young women to raise awareness about domestic and sexual abuse, and campaign for better services and support for young people affected by it. Tickets £8 and £4 concessions, available from www.chilypep.org.uk/blog/entry/free-radicals-benefit-gig-for-chilypep The venue is the former Crookes Working Men’s Club: Crookes Social Club see also Facebook
