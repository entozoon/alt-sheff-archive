Sheffield Anarchist Feminist Network - Poster and Sticker Session

Date, Time & Location Wed 15 Sep, 7-9pm, Rutland Arms, 86 Brown Street, S1 2BS
Details Sticker and poster session to make anarchist-feminist related material to distribute in sheffield and to add to projects like the feminist poster project: http://feministposterproject.wordpress.com Anyone is welcome to come, create, share ideas or just watch. Some other members of the group will be writing things for the zine, so come and join with that if you like.
