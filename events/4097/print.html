SHEFFIELD PRIDE

Date, Time & Location Sat 4 Jul 2015, midday till late, Sheffield Students Union, University of Sheffield
Details Uplifting live music, DJs, beer tent, and loads of stalls and entertainment at the friendly free festival for everyone in the Sheffield lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgender communities, along with friends and families and supporters. This year there will be various sections to the day:
  • Midday-5pm Daytime event including free family area with live performances throughout the afternoon from local bands and artists alongside activities and loads to do for young and old alike. Sponsored by the University of Sheffield
  • 5pm-8pm Screenings of LGBT-themed movies in the Students' Union Auditorium
  • 8pm-11pm Evening event including 8.30pm Candlelit Vigil in conjunction with the Centre for HIV featuring some soul-searching performances and talks, an opportunity to spend a few moments thinking of the victims of HIV, Homophobia and Transphobia here and around the world, today and in the past
  • 9pm The performance areas kick off again; cabaret, drag, vocalists, dancing and DJs through till 1am
  • 11pm-3.30am After-party with dancers, flames, neon, glitter and a headline performance from Neon Jungle
Entry for the day and evening events is £3 and if you come to both, you will not need to pay twice. The after party is £6. Sheffield Pride
