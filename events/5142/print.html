Space to Think - Economic fallout of Brexit

Date, Time & Location Wed 6 Sep 2017, 9.30-11am, Voluntary Action Sheffield, The Circle, 33 Rockingham Lane S1 4FW
Details This Voluntary Action Sheffield "Space to Think" event is an opportunity to look at the economic impact of Brexit on the voluntary sector in Sheffield, and broader issues facing the city including devolution, social care, housing and local government funding. Come to discuss what the impact might be, and how we might collaborate to address it. Guest Speaker: Laurie Brennan, Policy and Improvement Manager, Sheffield City Council.
