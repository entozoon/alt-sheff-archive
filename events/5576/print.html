Music for the Homeless 2019

Date, Time & Location Sat 8 Jun 2019, Don Valley Bowl, 12.30pm - 11pm
Details The Cathedral Archer Project in association with the organisers of Mosfest bring you a unique day of music, fun and friendship all in the aid of charity with an option to sleep out under the stars. Featuring a diverse range of music: ska, indie, rock, hip-hop, opera, rock-n-roll, Brit pop. Acts include; Tom Hingleys - Kar-Pets The Neville Staple Band Bang Bang Romeo Missing Andy The Smiths Ltd Josephine & the Artizans Velcro Teddy Bears 48K´s Ryan Jarvis Tickets £20 Adults, £10 juniors and under 8s free. Sleep out pitches available here: www.archerproject.org.uk - click on the Events page. Pitches are £25 for a 5 berth tent (2 adults and maximum of 3 children per tent, or 2 adults per tent) A free sleeping bag option is also available. Music for the Homeless
