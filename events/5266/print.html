Universal Basic Income: Public Meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 6 Jun 2018, 6.30-8.30pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details A free workshop with Dr Mark Bryan from the University of Sheffield, in association with UBI Lab Sheffield. Exploring proposals for a Sheffield pilot of a basic income scheme. Outlining the evidence and proposal to date, the workshop will explore a series of questions that will inform the work to bring a basic income pilot to Sheffield. This free event is part of Festival of Debate 2018, coordinated by not-for-profit company Opus Independents. Tickets (free) via Tickets For Good
