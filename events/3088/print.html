Transition Chesterfield Potato Day

Date, Time & Location Sat 25 Jan 2014, central Chesterfield
Details The Potato Day run annually by Transition Chesterfield is so popular that they now take pre-orders. As some of the popular varieties of potatoes, onions, shallots and garlic can run out quite early on the day, you can order in advance using their order form. Prices are pretty competitive, especially as you can just buy a few tubers to try out. Transition Chesterfield
