Peace and Craft Fair

Date, Time & Location Sat 4 Nov 2017, 11am-4.30pm, Sheffield Town Hall, Pinstone St. S1 2HH
Details The annual celebration of community and creativity brings together campaigning groups and local crafters selling artisan items which are not normally available on the high street. A family friendly environment with ethical and affordable festive gifts, a wide range of quality crafts direct from the makers, and campaigning and charity group stalls. Throughout the day there will be singing accompanied by live music, an accordionist, and a pop-up café. Entry £1, children free. Peace and Craft Fair
