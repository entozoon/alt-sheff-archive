Fruit n Juice: Queer Apocalypse for Trans Day of Visibility

Date, Time & Location Sat 30 Mar 2019, 11pm-4am, Theatre Deli, 202 Eyre St. S1 4QZ
Details It‘s 2019 and it may seem like the world is ending, but queerness is forever, and it‘s International Transgender Day of Visibility so let‘s celebrate. Party like it‘s the end of the world at this apocalypse-themed takeover of Theatre Deli. Featuring DJs, drag, burlesque, and ‘The Bunker‘ chill-out space to shelter in, with visual art from the local trans, non-binary and genderfluid community. Theatre Deli
