Dabke Dance Group performances

Date, Time & Location Fri 19 Jul 2019, 7pm, Sheffield College, Hillsborough Campus, Livesey St. S6 2ET
Details The Dabke Dance group are young people from the Dar al Fonon Centre in Nablus in Palestine. Tickets are free but a donation to the travel costs of the young people would be welcome. Hillsborough Campus is opposite the dog track on Livesey Street. Easily accessible by tram, take a Malin Bridge tram, get off at Hillsborough, the venue is about 5 minutes away. There is plenty of on-street parking available. They also perform the following day at Devonshire Green. Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign
