CycleSheffield social

Date, Time & Location Mon 21 Jul 2014, 9pm, Red Deer pub, 18 Pitt St. S1 4DD
Details Thanks to years of promoting cycling, by attending fairs, fetes and other events, and following the Tour de France, CycleSheffield now has over 500 members.
