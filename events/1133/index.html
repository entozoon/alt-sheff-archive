PAYP: Free environmental activities for 8-13 year olds

Tues 21 Dec 2010, 1.30-3.30pm Abbeyfield Park S4 7AT

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

This Session: Christmas crafts, holly and willow wreaths, table decorations, pot pourri ...Positive Activities for Young People. Each session is a different activity ranging from den building and bug hunting to arts and crafts projects and sports activities. The sessions are open access and the young people are free to come and go as they please. All activities are outdoors so please wear suitable clothing and sensible shoes. Each session lasts between 2 and 2.5 hours. Refreshments provided: juice and fruit in the sunshine and hot choc in the cold winter months. Sheffield Wildlife Trust is not a child care provider and under 8's are welcome to attend but must be accompanied and supervised by a parent or guardian. Under 8's activity box with treasure trails and bug hunting activities. You don't need to book but if you are bringing a group please let them know so they can bring sufficient resources.
