Off The Shelf Festival of Words

Date, Time & Location Sat 11 Oct - Sat 1 Nov 2014, various venues
Details Off The Shelf Festival of Words takes place each October in Sheffield and is one of the largest and most well respected literary festivals in the UK. It covers a wide range of events, including poetry, author visits, walks, exhibitions, workshops, and storytelling. Some events are free, and they are in various venues. The festival organisation is not-for-profit. Off The Shelf Festival.
