Dr. Bike

Date, Time & Location Wed 1 Feb 2017, 10.30am-5.30pm, Union St. co-working space, 18-22 Union St, S1 2JP
Details As part of the drive to make Sheffield a cycling city, you can bring your bike to a fantastic Dr. Bike session for a FREE check-up. Their qualified mechanics will fix minor problems, and give advice on anythings that need workshop attention. Sessions are every Wednesday at Union St. co-working space, and other venues at various times over the summer. Funded by CycleBoost and Inmotion. Email for details: fix@cycleboost.org or call Sheff 3991100
