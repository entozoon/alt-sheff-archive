Make your Mark youth consultation

Date, Time & Location Mon 8 May 2017, 9am-12.00, Town Hall Reception Room
Details Make your Mark is the largest national youth consultation of its kind. Young people across the country vote on one of ten issues presented by Youth Parliament.Young people in Sheffield are invited to have a say and debate and discuss these issues. In association with Sheffield Futures. Free to attend. A Festival of Debate event, hosted by not-for-profit social enterprise Opus Independents (which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine).
