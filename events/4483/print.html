Amnesty International meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 13 Apr 2016, 7.15pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Amnesty International is a worldwide organisation of nearly 3 million people all working to protect Human Rights. The Sheffield branch meets monthly on the 2nd Wed of every month (except August). Actions on human rights abuses, contacts, successes and updates from various campaigns. Newcomers welcome. This meeting will be the reading of a play by local award wining author Frances Gray. All the details can be found on Facebook Sheffield Amnesty International Facebook
