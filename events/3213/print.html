New fairtrade shop opens

Date, Time & Location Mon 24 Feb 2014, 95 Whitham Road, Broomhill S10 2SN.
Details A new fairtrade shop, " Fair Grounds", opens today at Broomhill, during Fair Trade Fortnight. They are looking for people who are passionate about fairtrade to help get it ready and to help with the running. The aim is to provide the opportunity to explore and purchase a wide range of high quality fairtrade products from around the world in an informative and transparent environment. Also sale or return to voluntary groups who wish to make use of fairtrade products. This is a new venture for Fair Grounds, details of activities up to now, and further info about the shop will be available on the website soon at: www.fair-grounds.org.uk For details, including leaflet, email: nina@fair-grounds.org.uk or 07815 999946.
