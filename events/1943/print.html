Occupy Sheffield general assembly meeting

Date, Time & Location Sat 24 Mar 2012, 3pm, Bank Street Arts, 32-40 Bank St. S1 2DS
Details After Sheffield’s historic 99-day Occupy camp, the movement is looking to the next steps, and General Assemblies are occurring at various locations. These are open public gatherings, available to all, for everyone to talk, debate, and educate each other. The theme for this meeting is ideas for this summer’s Peace in the Park event. https://occupywiki.org.uk/wiki/Sheffield
