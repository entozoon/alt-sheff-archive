Green Homes Sheffield open days

Date, Time & Location Sat 3 and Sun 4 Oct 2015
Details Green Homes Sheffield, individual people, friends and families are doing open days in their homes, over two weekends this month to share by example, experiments to face up to the impacts of the climate/pollution problems affecting housing here in Sheffield. Come and look, then try out some of the carbon-free future solutions; insulation, renewable energy and co-operation. Details of all available at the website below.
