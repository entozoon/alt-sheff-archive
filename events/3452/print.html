Poetry and Music Night for Sheffield Anarchist Bookfair

Date, Time & Location Wed 25 Jun 2014, 7.30pm Red Deer pub, 18 Pitt St. S1 4DD
Details Sheffield Anarchist Bookfair is coming up (Saturday 12th July 10am-6pm at Showroom Workstation). To get into the mood, come and hear some activist poetry, including South Yorkshire people's poet Gav Roberts, Chrystine Moon, Andy Owen Cook and others. The Red Deer is on Pitt Street, a small road off Mappin Street, just off West Street, map at www.red-deer-sheffield.co.uk/location.htm). The event will be upstairs, entry donation requested (fundraising to pay for the bookfair). Sheffield Anarchist Bookfair
