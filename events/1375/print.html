Vegetarian job vacancy

Date, Time & Location Deadline 4 April 2011
Details Heeley City Farm are particularly looking for people who have experience or an interest in vegetarian food and cooking for the following vacancy.

Heeley City Farm wish to appoint a Farm Kitchen Assistant (Maternity Cover) to work 30 hours per week (including weekend working).

Salary: £6.06 per hour plus optional 4% pension.

Purpose of job: To work with other staff and volunteers in the Heeley City Farm café.

About the Café: The Farm Kitchen is the onsite café at Heeley City Farm. It is a totally vegetarian café which also caters for vegan diets. Much of the food and cakes are made from scratch and we try to use organic produce from the farm's gardens and growing sites. Working in the café also involves working alongside adults with learning difficulties who volunteer in the café.

The position would be a great opportunity for someone who wishes to learn more about vegetarian catering.
