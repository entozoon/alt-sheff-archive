Campaigning and Influencing Course

Date, Time & Location Wed 2 Nov 2011, 2-4pm, Springwell Gardens Community Centre, Rotherham
Details Rotherham Federation of Tenants and Residents (RotherFed) is running a 5 week campaigning and influencing course to help people run successful campaigns that can make a difference in their community. The course is friendly and informal and open to all, facilitated by Steve Ruffle. All the sessions are held on Wednesday afternoons. People can attend all sessions or just the ones they are interested in. The sessions are free of charge and are open to all but places need to be booked. This final session Public Speaking will examine some of the skills needed to prepare and deliver speeches in public and help those who wish to present resolutions to our delegates meeting, plan and practice their speeches. To book your place on any of the sessions email info@rotherfed.org or phone 01709 368515.
