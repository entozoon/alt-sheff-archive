Sheffield Amnesty International Hosts: Refugee Crisis, Europe & Sheffield

Date, Time & Location Wed 8 Jun 2016, 6pm, Norfolk Lecture Theatre, Sheffield Hallam University, Howard St. S1 1WB
Details Sheffield Amnesty International local group, in partnership with Journey to Justice, are hosting a panel discussion to explore the unprecedented "worst refugee crisis since World War 2" and its impact on the United Kingdom and to Sheffield in particular. Should we, and can we help? Will the situation worsen? What will leaving the EU mean? This is your chance to discuss the crisis and its impact on our city and how best we can address the situation. All welcome to join in with the discussion. For further details please contact Sheffield Amnesty at amnestysheffield@gmail.com. The event is free but all donations are welcome.
