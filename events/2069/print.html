Sheffield Humanist Society meeting: "The Case for Drug Legalisation"

Date, Time & Location Wed 6 Jun 2012, 8pm University Arms, 197 Brook Hill S3 7HG
Details The next meeting of Sheffield Humanist Society will be asking whether the current international and domestic policy on drugs has failed. Is it unethical? Should drugs be legalised? Alex Martin, University of Sheffield Atheist Society will explore the history of drug prohibition and explain why he thinks Humanists should support the legalisation of drugs. The University Arms (map) is close to the tram, get off at the Sheffield University stop, and several buses pass nearby including the 51 & 52, stopping almost next to the pub, with the 95 & 123 a short walk away. Parking is available in the streets behind the pub. www.sheffieldhumanists.org.uk
