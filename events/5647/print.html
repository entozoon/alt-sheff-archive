Miners’ Strike 35th Anniversary Annual Orgreave Rally

Date, Time & Location Sat 15 Jun 2019, 1pm, Orgreave Lane, Sheffield S13 9NE
Details Come along and support the Annual Orgreave Rally organised by the Orgreave Truth and Justice Campaign, commemorating the 35th anniversary of the Miners’ Strike and the events at Orgreave. Please bring banners, placards, drums, whistles, family and friends. Let’s show the Government we will not be silenced; we won’t go away. Speakers: Chris Kitchen, General Secretary, National Union of Mineworkers (NUM) Rose Hunter, North Staffs Miners Wives Action Group (WAPC) John Dunn, Former Miner and Orgreave Truth and Justice Campaign Matt Wrack, General Secretary, Fire Brigades Union (FBU) Steve Hedley, Senior AGS, National Union of Rail, Maritime and Transport Workers (RMT) Sarah Champion, Member of Parliament for Rotherham Joe Rollin, Orgreave Truth and Justice Campaign & Unite the Union Chris Peace, Orgreave Truth and Justice Campaign & Labour Party Prospective Candidate, North East Derbyshire Music by Sheffield Socialist Choir and Unite Brass Band
