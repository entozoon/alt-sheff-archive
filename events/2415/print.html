Community Cohesion Conference

Date, Time & Location Thu 22 Nov 2012 - 9.30am-12.30pm, Sorby House, 42 Spital Hill, S4 7LG
Details The Cohesion Advisory Group community conference is held annually to maintain connections with the community by reporting back, seeking advice and talking together about sustaining community cohesion in the city. It provides a forum to promote dialogue with partners from all sectors and to learn from each other.
