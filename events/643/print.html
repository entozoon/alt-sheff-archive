Greenpeace meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 8 Jun 2010, 8pm NOTE: at the Sheffield Showroom, NOT Red Lion
Details Different venue (not the usual Red Lion) - Sheffield Showroom, Paternoster Row, Sheffield S1 2BX - for the monthly meeting of Greenpeace South Yorkshire group. This is a special meeting for new and existing activists to get together and find out more about Greenpeace and current campaigns. There will be DVDs and background information about the Tar Sands campaign and lots more. Please bring a friend. More details of meetings and current activities in the Active Supporters newsletter, see Greenpeace South Yorkshire website
