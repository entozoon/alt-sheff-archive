The Big Human Rights Debate

Date, Time & Location Sat 3 Oct 2015, 2pm Sharrow Community Forum, South View Rd
Details Sheffield Central MP Paul Blomfield, Young Legal Aid Lawyers, and Sheffield for Democracy bring the British Institute of Human Rights (BIHR) to town: 15 Days of Action for 15th Anniversary of the Human Rights Act, part of MP Paul Blomfield's Big Conversation. The aim is to raise awareness about human rights in Sheffield, discussing and debating how human rights are relevant in our lives, and what the future holds for the universal protections we all share.
