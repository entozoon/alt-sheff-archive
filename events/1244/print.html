Sheffield Seed Swap

Date, Time & Location Sun 20 Feb 2011, 1pm-4pm, Old Sharrow School, South View Rd
Details A free event. Seeds, information, plants, cups of tea, and cake! Meet local growers to exchange seeds, get seeds if you have non to exchange, pick up useful tips of experienced growers, be part of a local seed network. No seeds or experience required, just come along ! You are invited to share your seeds that you have saved yourself, and to take some seeds that others have saved. If you do not have any seeds to bring your self, this is fine, you can still take seeds home with you. This event is free but donations welcome. http://maxsalad.co.uk/?page_id=102
