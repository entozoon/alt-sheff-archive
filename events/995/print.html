South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG) meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 12 Oct 2010, 7pm Scotia Works, Leadmill Road S1 4SE
Details Amongst other items, this meeting will discuss what issues to raise when SYMAAG and other asylum-rights groups meet Deputy Prime Minister and Sheffield MP Nick Clegg in late October. What would you like to say to Nick Clegg about this Government's treatment of people seeking asylum and other migrants? If you want to come to any SYMAAG meeting but find it hard to afford transport don't be put off! Please bring your ticket or a receipt and SYMAAG can pay you back at the meeting. Or check in advance.
