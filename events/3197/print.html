Public meeting: Sheffield Voices for the Five Campaign

Date, Time & Location Tue 25 Feb 2014, 7.30pm, United Reform Church 60 Norfolk St. S1 2 JB
Details Sheffield Voices for the Five Campaign is the local voice of the international Voices for the Five Campaign to secure freedom for the Cuban anti-terrorism agents who remain unjustly imprisoned after 15 years in US jails. This public meeting is part of the lead up to the international Commission reviewing the case. Speakers include Geoff Bottoms and Steve Cottingham. Note the venue is opposite the stage doors of the Crucible theatre. Sheffield Cuba Solidarity Campaign on Facebook
