Transition Chesterfield fruit tree pruning workshops

Date, Time & Location Sun 17 Feb 2013, 10.30am-4pm, Hunloke Community Gardens, Church St South, Chesterfield
Details Two fruit tree pruning workshops: in the morning Andy Holdaway will talk about and demonstrate both pruning new trees to establish a good shape, and maintenance pruning of more mature ones. The workshop starts at 10.30am and should finish at about 1. Starting at 1.30pm till 4-ish, Martin Baxter will demonstrate top grafting, a method of changing the variety of fruit a tree produces, or even having several different varieties on the same tree. For more information and to book a place, email workshop@transitionchesterfield.org.uk
