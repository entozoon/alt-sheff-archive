Volunteering with ASSIST - Information session

Date, Time & Location Mon 7 Jan 2019, 1-2pm, Room 43, Victoria Hall, Chapel Walk entrance S1 2JB
Details A short presentation about the good work of ASSIST, which helps refused asylum seekers who are abandoned by the UK government without a home or any financial support. Learn about the situation, volunteer roles currently available, and how to apply for them. All welcome. Victoria Hall Chapel Walk entrance is opposite Superdrug. ASSIST Sheffield
