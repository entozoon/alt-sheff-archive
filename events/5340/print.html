What is a Green Space Really Worth?

Date, Time & Location Wed 17 Oct 2018, 5.45pm, Waterstones Bookshop cafe, Orchard Square S1 2FB
Details A talk by Dr Julian Dobson of the University of Sheffield Department of Landscape. She asks if we love our parks and green spaces, how can we give them the investment they need?
