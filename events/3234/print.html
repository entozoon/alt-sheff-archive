"From Darwin until now: A short history of a big idea"

Date, Time & Location Tue 4 Feb 2014, 8pm, The University Arms, 197 Brook Hill, S3 7HG
Details The evolution of evolution, its proponents, its detractors, its changes and its victories. Josh Maher, PhD Candidate in Genetics at Cambridge University will go through the history of Darwinian thought from the first idea to the full theory that is today. A talk open to the public, organised by the Sheffield Humanist Society, all welcome. www.sheffieldhumanists.org.uk
