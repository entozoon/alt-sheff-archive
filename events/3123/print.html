Date, Time & Location Tue 10 Dec 2013, 6.30pm University of Sheffield Arts Tower
Details Students For Estelí and Sheffield-Estelí Society invite you to a public meeting with Carlos Fonseca Terán in the University of Sheffield Arts Tower, room to be confirmed. He works in the International Relations Department of the FSLN and is the son of Carlos Fonseca Amador, a founder member of the FSLN who was killed during the insurrection. Sheffield-Estelí Society
