IDAHO - International Day Against Homophobia and Transphobia

Date, Time & Location Thu 17 May 2012, 5.15-6pm, Peace Gardens, Sheffield
Details Join the Centre for HIV and Sexual Health, and Sheffield City Council, assembling in the Peace Gardens to make some noise against homophobia and transphobia.
