South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG) meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 3 Aug 2010, 6.30pm at Scotia Works, Leadmill Road, Sheffield S1 4SE
Details The main item at the meeting will be a discussion of the effects of budget cuts on migrants and those who work with them, starting with a presentation by Jim Steinke of Northern Refugee Centre. See BBC link for Jims interview with BBC Radio Sheffield on this subject. If you are not already a member, you can join at the meeting. People seeking asylum, refugees and migrants are especially encouraged to join and lead the work of SYMAAG. Contact dignitynotdetention@yahoo.co.uk
