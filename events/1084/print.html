Sheffield Friday Night Ride: Chip Shops Ride I

Date, Time & Location Fri 19 Nov 2010, Start 6.30 p.m. at The Lescar, Sharrow Vale Rd, S10
Details Sheffield Friday Night Ride (FNR) offers a fun monthly evening bike ride for everyone. Rides are prepared in advance on themes. This month a brilliant combination: a celebration of cycling and chips! No need to get your tea before you start - FNR have arranged chips to be ready at each chippy!
