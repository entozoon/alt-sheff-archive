FURD Fun Fest

Date, Time & Location Sat 1 Aug 2015, 12-4pm U-MIX Centre, 17 Asline Rd. S2 4UJ
Details Football Unites Racism Divides (FURD) annual festival is back again this year at the U-Mix Centre (Lowfield Park). FURD in partnership with Sheffield Futures CYT have a host of activities for a fun day out including main stage entertainment, various stalls as well as a football tournament for Under 13s and Under 16s, barbeque, food and drink stalls, Streetkick, kids activities including Bouncy Castle, Bungee Run and more. Entry FREE. For more information about the festival please call Sheff 2589601. Football Unites Racism Divides (FURD)
