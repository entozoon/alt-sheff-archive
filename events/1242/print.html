Pictures of Palestine

Date, Time & Location Sat 5 Feb 2011, 2-4.30pm, St Matthews community Rooms, Carver Street, city centre (off Divison St)
Details Come and hear about the experiences of four Sheffielders who have recently returned from visiting Palestine, and find out more about the situation there and experiences of practical solidarity in Palestine. Dick Pitt (back from a fact-finding tour on the Jewish National fund, Catherine Gaze (solidarity worker in the West Bank), and Leni Solinger and Hilary Rosser (visited West Bank and Israel). Tea/coffee available from 1.30 p.m. Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign are hosting this meeting, all welcome. www.sheffieldpsc.org.uk
