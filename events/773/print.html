RASC (Residents Against Station Closure) AGM

Date, Time & Location Mon 19 Jul 2010, 7pm at Victoria Centre, Stafford Road.
Details RASC continues the battle against the plan to close the bridge over Sheffield station to pedestrians without a train ticket. EMT, the owners, recently and provocatively began random stops again, so the meeting agenda will include political and legal challenges to EMT as well as organising future protests.
