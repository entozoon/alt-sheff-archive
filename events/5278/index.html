Wheelies For Water

Fri 22 Jun 2018, meet 6pm outside Sheffield Town Hall

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Two water treatment plants in Sheffield are now licensed to receive toxic water from Fracking (unconventional oil and gas extraction processes). Join this bike ride around the city centre and then out to Blackburn Meadows Water Treatment Works by 8pm (near Meadowhall) to spread the word to Sheffield residents - "No, not here and not anywhere!" 8pm onwards: Speakers from local Frack Free groups, Music and entertainment at the site. 9/10pm group ride back to city centre and to the pub.Non-cyclists also welcome, meet at Blackburn Meadows at 8pm for the gathering and entertainment.
