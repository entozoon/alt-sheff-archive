Sheffield Equality Group meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 5 Feb 2014, 7pm Sheffield Quaker Meeting House, St James St. S1 2EW
Details Sheffield Equality Group is an autonomous group affiliated to the national Equality Trust. Meetings (on the first Wednesday every month) aim to be an open space where people can discuss and plan actions around income equality. All welcome. Discussion about income inequality and plans for the new year, including the Basic Income Survey, the commons, the welfare state and more. Free, donations for tea and biscuits welcome. www.sheffieldequality.wordpress.com
