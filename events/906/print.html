Skeptics in the Pub

Date, Time & Location Mon 13 Sep 2010, 7.30pm Showroom Cafe Bar, 15 Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details Skeptics in the Pub is about getting people together to have a relaxed and enjoyable evening while listening to talks on a wide range of topics, usually by a notable scientist or skeptic. Talks are typically about a common belief that can be demonstrated to be false or cannot be justified by the available evidence. This month's presentation is about the "Angels of Mons" and other wartime legends and apparently supernatural forces intervening in wars. Speaker David Clarke (PhD in English Cultural Tradition and Folklore), is the author of a book on the subject, and a senior lecturer in journalism at Sheffield Hallam University.
