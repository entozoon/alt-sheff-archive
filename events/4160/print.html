"Man of Iron" (Polish film)

Date, Time & Location Mon 14 Sep 2015, 5.30pm Showroom Cinema, Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details Filmed in Poland during the Solidarity protests in 1980, this is a story of government-backed espionage and world-changing history. Even activist and future President Lech Wałęsa has a role. Showing as part of a season of films, "Masterpieces of Polish Cinema" running now until 28th September. Showroom Workstation
