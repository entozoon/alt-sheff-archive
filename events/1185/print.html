Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change (SCACC) meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 14 Dec 2010, 7.30-9.30pm at Highfield Library, London Rd. S2 4NF
Details SCACC organises days of action and demonstrations, petitions to Sheffield Council, supports related national and local campaigns and runs film shows and talks for local groups. The regular meeting is open to newcomers, all welcome. The main subject for discussion this month will be how to develop a more effective Climate Alliance in Sheffield - how can different organizations campaign together, and what could this look like in the next few months?
