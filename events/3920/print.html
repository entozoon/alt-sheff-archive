Streetfilms: Inspiration for a more liveable Sheffield

Date, Time & Location Thu 9 Apr 2015, 6-8pm 18-22 Union Street S1 2JP
Details Streetfilms produces short films showing how smart transportation design and policy can result in better places to live, work and play. What lessons can Sheffield learn from other world cities which are embracing public space, living streets and alternatives to the car? An evening of Streetfilms followed by a panel session and discussion hosted by Cycle Sheffield. The Festival of Debate is taking place in Sheffield in the run-up to the General election, during March and April. It provides opportunities to get together and discuss a range of issues which affect everyone. Informed decision making and strategic planning are essential for democracy, so come and get involved. They also welcome volunteers, from attending events and taking part in discussions to volunteering time to help run an event, promote the festival and much more. Unless otherwise stated events are free to attend, but if possible please register via the appropriate Eventbrite link on the Festival of Debate website below to give organsisers an idea of numbers.
