Sheffield Social Centre collective meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 8 Aug 2011, 7pm The Rutland Arms, 86 Brown Street, Sheffield S1 2BS
Details Sheffield Social Centre is a collective for libertarian DIY space in the city. If you're interested in the social centre project, or intrigued by it at the very least, you're very welcome - the project is open to all who share its core principles of non-hierarchy, anti-capitalism and equality/non-discrimination. This is a planning meeting for the Free School event to be held in October, ideas and input welcome. Website: http://sheffieldsocialcentre.org.uk
