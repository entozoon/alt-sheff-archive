LOWEDGES FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sun 12 Aug 2012, 12 to 5pm, Greenhill Park, Gresley Road S8 7HN
Details In 2011 over 10,000 people came to Lowedges Festival. This year there will be 4 arenas with live bands, falcons, dogs, ducks, medieval re-enactment, classic cars and bikes, Sheffield Wednesday FC, farmers market, car boot, craft market, steam train, mini fun fair, tractors, bicycle training, climbing wall, pony rides, entertainment for children, even a full ice rink for all ages free of charge, plus much more...
