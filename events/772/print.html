Plans on show for proposed Sainsburys at Wadsley Bridge

Date, Time & Location Niagara Sports and Social Club, Niagara Road, Wadsley Bridge S6 1LU
Details General public viewing: Friday 16 July from 12pm until 7pm Saturday 17 July from 10am till 2pm. Too bad if you weren't invited to the invitation-only preview evening on Thursday to question representatives from Sainsburys and Fletchers. The public gets 11 hours to see the preliminary plans by Sainsburys and Fletchers to submit a planning application for the development of land at Fletchers bakery site off Claywheels Lane. Wadsley bridge can look forward to a new 50,000 sq ft Sainsburys megastore and restaurant (and recycling area aww, bless 'em). 400 full and part-time jobs, 425 car parking spaces and so much congestion for this busy area that a new road junction is needed to Claywheels Lane from Penistone Road.
