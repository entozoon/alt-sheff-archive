Unite Against Fascism (UAF) election campaign

Date, Time & Location Now till election day
Details UAF, an organisation which exists to combat fascist parties like the British National Party (BNP) has organised a number of leafleting sessions in Sheffield. These will help counter the lies of the BNP, especially in the week of the election. Details are on the UAF local website at www.sheffielduaf.org.uk/activities
