Ketermâya Refugee Camp

Date, Time & Location Thu 7 Apr 2016, 7pm Regather Works, 57-59 Club Garden Rd. S11 8BU
Details Filmmaker Lucas Jedrzejak (Bison Grass Films) in association with Regather Cooperative previews some of his unseen footage from a refugee camp in Ketermâya, Lebanon, plus some shorts from Wellredfilms. After a Q & A with Lucas about his experiences while filming in the camp, local speakers will lead a discussion on the refugee situation in Sheffield and the UK. Part of Festival of Debate 2016, hosted by Opus Independents (which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine). ‘Pay what you feel’ on the night. Ticket details here.
