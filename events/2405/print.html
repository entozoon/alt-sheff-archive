SYMAAG Open Meeting: “Asylum and Fortress Europe”

Date, Time & Location Tue 27 Nov 2012, 6.30pm, Exchange Brewery 2 Bridge St. S3 8NS
Details Meeting about asylum implications of ”fortress Europe” produced by EU policies. SYMAAG (South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group) unites refugee, asylum and migrant organisations with campaign groups and supporting individuals. It campaigns both for the rights of those seeking asylum and of those coming to work in this country. Note that from November SYMAAG is holding meetings in the B1 Training Room at Northern Refugee Centre’s new office; The Exchange Brewery, 2 Bridge Street, Sheffield S3 8NS. The building is on the left with big green doors just before you cross Lady’s Bridge toward The Wicker. Enter through the small green door (with SYMAAG sign on). www.symaag.org.uk Twitter: @SYMAAG Tel: Sheff 2412780
