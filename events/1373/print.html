Cinema in the Park

Date, Time & Location Sat 2 Apr 2011, 8-10pm, Endcliffe Park, Rustlings Road
Details On yer Bike! No Limits presents a special open-air cinema event, powered entirely by bike power. Showcasing award winning student films from the UK and around the world. Check out one of the most unusual cinema experiences in Sheffield this year. Bring your bike, a blanket, family and friends...nice eats available by Treatz n Beatz. In association with Art in the Park, Magnificent Revolutions and EWB. Part of www.nolimitsfilmfestival.com (See above) Probably the UK's biggest international student film festival. Tickets on sale @ Sheffield Union Box office, Hallam Hubs, Bungalows & Bears, The Harley and Rocky Horrors
