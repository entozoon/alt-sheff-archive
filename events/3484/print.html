Farm Fest! @Heeley City Farm

Date, Time & Location Sat 28 Jun 2014, 12noon-8pm, Heeley City Farm, Richards Rd. S2 3DT
Details Heeley City Farm has its very own Summer Festival! A music festival - concentrating on the music this year but there will still be loads of other things to do too. Jess from Common Thread will be face painting and silly hat making. PLUS in the Energy Centre - Human Fruit Machine, Madame Zucchini, Childrens' Crafts - Recycled crafts with lots of glitter and glue. Stalls and more to be confirmed. The Farm Stage is back again, with all the usual amazing bands, booze (Provided by the ever luscious Blue Bee Brewery), entertainment and sunshine (start dancing)! (Please note change of date to avoid clashing with Sharrow. Don't get confused, the farm stage is going ahead on the 28th! See you there) Lineup: DJs, Chuffin' Al and Pete Dangerous Hot Diamond Aces Ubuntu Commando Mango Rescue Team Wreck the Place Firegarden Absconditus Connor Nutt FREE admission! Catch the No 47 bus to Herdings from town - it drops you right outside the Farm Heeley City Farm
