Course: Rustic Chair Making

Date, Time & Location Sat 19 - Sun 20 Nov 2011, Sheffield
Details Two-day course on making a basic rustic chair, working with green wood, freshly felled. This is soft and easy to work with hand tools, and because of this, green woodwork creates little waste. It uses small trees and logs, so it is green in all senses of the word. Anyone can do this course, with little or no experience. Low-Impact Living Initiative (LILI) is a non-profit organisation whose mission is to help people reduce their impact on the environment, improve their quality of life, gain new skills, live in a healthier and more satisfying way, have fun and save money.
