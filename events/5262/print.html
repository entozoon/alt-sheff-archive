Talking gigs: Maya Youssef

Date, Time & Location Sun 3 Jun 2018, 7.30-10.30pm, The HUBS 6 Paternoster Row S1 2QQ
Details A unique opportunity to hear Maya Youssef – internationally-renowned virtuoso player of the Syrian qanun (78-stringed plucked zither) – talk about her music, her story and Syria, as well as playing a full set of music with her trio. Her recent highly-acclaimed debut album is her “personal journey through the six years of war in Syria”. Tickets £15/£7 (Under 23s). This event is part of Festival of Debate 2018, coordinated by not-for-profit company Opus Independents. Tickets via Talking Gigs
