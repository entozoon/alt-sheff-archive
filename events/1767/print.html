Jamming Against the Cuts

Date, Time & Location Sat 5 Nov 2011, 1pm meet Sheffield City Hall steps
Details Calling all musicians, poets, dancers and artists. As part of Pandemic, reclaiming space, reappropriating images, UKuncut Sheffield will turn a bank into a live music venue. Bring your rhymes. Bring your rhythms. Bring your instruments, your sense of humour and, most importantly, your sense of injustice to highlight bankers' bonuses and corporate greed in what is sure to be UKUncut's most exciting event this year.
