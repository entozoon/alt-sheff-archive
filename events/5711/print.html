Extinction Rebellion flag and banner making workshop

Date, Time & Location Fri 6 Sep, 6pm-8.30pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 James St. S1 2EW
Details The XR Sheffield Arts Group is hosting several Flag and Banner workshops in preparation for upcoming actions and events starting from September. Whether you are creative, hands on or just eager to help, please come down and lend a hand. There will be plenty for everyone and anyone to get involved. (NB This event was moved: originally planned for Sat 7th at Nether Edge) Please email xrsheffieldart@gmail.com if you need more details, or if you can offer any of the following: bamboo sticks, sewing machine, large sheets of second hand fabric, stencils / block prints, printing inks / paints.
