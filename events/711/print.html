Feeding Us Fat: Who is to Blame?

Date, Time & Location Thur 3 Jun 2010, 3-5pm
Details Who is to blame for the obesity epidemic in the UK? The food industry, parents, schools, the advertising industry? You are invited to join leading academics from the Centre for Food Innovation at Sheffield Hallam University to debate the possible causes of the current obesity epidemic including the roles of the NHS, food industry, parents, schools, the Government and the wider community. This event is open to all; members of the public, students, public health professionals, academics and any other interested parties are welcome. Fruit platters and hot and cold refreshments will be served. BOOKING ESSENTIAL before midday on Wednesday 2nd June.
