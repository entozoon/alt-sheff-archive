Basketmaking course

Date, Time & Location Sat 15 May 2010, Ecclesall Woods Sawmill, Sheffield
Details Basketmaking is one of the oldest of all crafts. It pre-dates pottery and cloth weaving, and led to the invention of both. Spend a day with Ray Lister, of Creative Country Crafts, who has been making and teaching about English baskets for over 20 years, using colourful, locally-grown organic willow.
