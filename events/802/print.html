Ranger Event: Young People's Bushcraft Skills

Date, Time & Location Wed 28 Jul 2010, 10am-3pm at Roe Woods, Norwood Road entrance
Details Dangerous fun for young people aged 10-14 years, who must be accompanied by an adult. Making shelters, campfire cooking, fire and tool safety, wilderness fun. Lunch cooked on the fire! Suitable clothing and stout footwear needed, refreshments available, no dogs allowed. Booking is essential and places are limited. Enquiries: Sheff 2403578 www.sheffield.gov.uk/rangers
