Sheffield Together Against Trump

Date, Time & Location Fri 13 Jul 2018, 5pm Barkers Pool S1 2JA
Details Sheffield joins the country-wide protest against the visit of the warmongering and misogynistic Donald Trump and his "Make America Hate Again" government. Protest sponsored by Sheffield Trades Union Council, and supported by a whole list of organisations including unions, Labour and Green party, Stand Up to Racism, Disabled People Against Cuts, and Sheffield People's Assembly.
