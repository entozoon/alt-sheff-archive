Fundraiser evening: Palestine Women’s Scholarship Fund

Date, Time & Location Sat 2 Nov 2013, 7.30-10.30pm, St. Andrew’s Church Hall, Hannover Street, Sheffield S3 7RQ
Details Eritrean meal and entertainment including a singalong with Sheffield Socialist Choir (also see item above). The Palestine Women’s Scholarship Fund is managed in Sheffield by a small coalition of women working in partnership with the Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign, and in Gaza by a panel drawn from the Board of Directors of the Red Crescent Society and the Union of Health Work Committees. Tickets: £12 waged / £8 unwaged Tel: 07946 557740. Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign
