Flyering against vivisection at Sheffield University

Date, Time & Location Thur 15 Apr 2010, 12-2pm
Details The University of Sheffield is responsible for the deaths of 30,000 animals ever year, used for animal experiments and then disposed of. Sheffield Animal Friends will be giving out information about animal experiments at the uni, meeting at midday at the university tram stop. For more information on the campaign and big demo in June, check out the website Stop Animal Experiments in Sheffield Sheffield Animal Friends website
