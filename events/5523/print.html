Sheffield Trades Council AGM

Date, Time & Location Tue 26 Feb 2019, 7pm, Trades and Labour Club, Duke St. S2 5QQ
Details The Annual General Meeting of Sheffield Trades Council, the progressive voice of Sheffield’s trade union movement. You don’t have to be a delegate to attend – all trade unionists are welcome as visitors. Guest speaker: Roger McKenzie, UNISON Assistant General Secretary. Followed by elections of all officers and executive committee (please ensure your union branch is affiliated for 2019 if you wish to vote). Sheffield Trades Council
