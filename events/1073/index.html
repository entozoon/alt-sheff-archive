Celebrating Community Involvement

Tue 23 Nov 2010, 12 noon-3.30pm at Springwell Gardens Community Centre, Rotherham

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Rotherham Federation of Tenants and Residents (RotherFed) open afternoon celebrating the best in community involvement with speeches, stalls, workshops, food and much more. The afternoon kicks off with free pie and peas at 12.15pm, keynote speech at 1pm by Phil Morgan, Chair of the International Union of Tenants Congress and former director of Tenant Services at the Tenants Services Authority. Phil will also be leading an optional workshop during the afternoon on the latest developments in housing regulation and funding. Other workshops include circus skills, card making, crafts and armchair aerobics, and best practice in resident involvement and practical DIY skills. There will be a BSL interpreter present.This celebratory event will be held at Springwell Gardens Community Centre and Eastwood View Church, open to all but places are limited, so please book by contacting Roth 368515, text 07969 426707 or email info@rotherfed.org. More details at www.rotherfed.org.uk
