Women's CycleSheffield Bike Ride

Date, Time & Location Sun 7 Mar 2010, 9.30-2pm
Details Postponed from the snowy 21st, this women's bike ride is now on the day before International Women's Day. Starting at Barkers Pool in the city centre at 9.30 (or Millhouses Park around 10.15). Circular route through various quiet roads and paths, parks and woodlands. Route is less than 12 miles. If people want to join, or leave, at other points along the way that's fine, text for details 07813 615309 More details at www.cyclesheffield.org.uk
