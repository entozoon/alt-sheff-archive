XR 12 Days of Crisis

Date, Time & Location Sat 7 Dec 2019, beginning of march 8.30am on Devonshire Green, Division St. S1 4GX
Details Pre-election, Extinction Rebellion Sheffield will stage a day of flooding-themed events at a secret location in Sheffield city centre as part of the national ‘12 Days of Crisis’ campaign to address the ecological crisis upon us, the mass species extinctions and runaway climate change. Societal collapse and mass deaths cannot be ignored by criminal government inactivity. Extinction Rebellion believes it is a citizen’s duty to rebel, using peaceful civil disobedience. Activities include: Democratic people’s assembly "Discobedience" Rebels dressed for severe weather and flooding Crisis carol singing Flooding-themed visuals Closing ceremony around 4pm For a basic introduction, see the Climate Factsheet for Rebels. XR are now holding Collaborative Leadership Calls to help coach people to improve their leadership skills and capacities. And while your time and energy are the most important things, if you are financially able to donate money, see their "Fundrazr" crowdfunder. Facebook event page Extinction Rebellion Sheffield
