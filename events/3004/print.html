NUT Yorkshire and Humberside Strike Action

Date, Time & Location Tue 1 Oct 2013, 10am-12 noon, details below
Details National Union of Teachers are staging a regional strike over pay, pension, and working conditions, in view of the continuing demoralising onslaught of the government on the profession which is educating the next generation. The main rally will be in Sheffield. Please support them at school picket lines and come along to the march and rally in Sheffield if you are able: Assemble at Barkers Pool, City Hall, at 10am leaving 10.30am to march to Ponds Forge International Sports Centre. Rally 11am to 12 at Ponds Forge International Sports Centre, Ponds Forge, Sheaf Street.
