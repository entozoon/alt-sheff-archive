Rotherham Open Arts Festival

Date, Time & Location 1-30 Sep 2010 The Old Market Building, Westgate, Rotherham Town Centre. S60 1AN
Details International Brigade Memorial Trust's new exhibition, Antifascistas is part of Rotherham Open Arts Festival, along with live music, theatre and dance performance, local artists' work, including Phlegm, Sheffield graffiti artist, Tony South, Mark Kemp and Zahir Rafiq, and free arts workshops for all ages.
