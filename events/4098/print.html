LOWEDGES FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sun 9 Aug 2015, 12-5.30pm Greenhill Park, Gresley Road, Lowedges S8 7HN
Details The popular Lowedges Festival returns to Greenhill Park, and it is big. Music, dance, falconry display, dog show and agility activities, history area, animals, classic vehicles, funfair area, food from around the world, football and bike skills areas, face painting and quiet area in the bowling area with cream teas. Free entry. An annual community festival organised jointly by Greenhill and Bradway Tenants and Residents Association and the Friends of Greenhill Park. Details on the Sheffield Help Yourself website
