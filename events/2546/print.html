One Step: Economics and Banking

Date, Time & Location Wed 6 Feb 2013, 8pm-9:30pm, Walkley / Upperthorpe
Details One Step is a small group meeting monthly in the Walkley / Upperthorpe area to look at practical steps to take each day, week or month to leave the world a better place. In February the guest speaker from the Positive Money campaign explains what is wrong with the modern banking system and how it could be changed for the benefit of all. Please email contact@one-step.org.uk for location details, or visit www.one-step.org.uk for more information about past/future meetings.
