SHARROW FESTIVAL - resheduled!

Sat 22 Aug 2015, till 8pm, Mount Pleasant Park, Sharrow Lane, nr London Rd. S11 8AE

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Sharrow's free multicultural festival was postponed due to rain earlier this year, so it's back on again in August.Assuming the previous plans hold, expect a special covered Community Stage, and a hip-hop jam in the all-weather pitch with Chillfoo Front (sounds) and Marco (paint jam). Loads more to see and do, including a climbing wall, drumming and street dance workshops, five-a-side football with Football Unites Racism Divides, global foods to try, a huge variety of stalls and kids' activities. This year, Peace In The Park bring their own event, 'Little Peaces'. Music line-up (full details on the website below) includes:Bell Hagg Orkestar Black ThornBongo Bert & SofiaBrave New Storm Clara PandyEarly Cartographers Hot Diamond Aces Isembard's WheelKath Auto Malarkey AffairMulembas D'Africa DancersNycha DanceRomano JiloSon De America Steel City Rhythm The Mango Rescue TeamThe Soul Organisation The UnsceneTruly Apparent 5-minute performances from some talented local people.Follow the festival action in a live broadcast by Sheffield Live! radio, 93.2 FM, and join the after-party at the Cremorne pub (see below).www.sharrowfestival.btck.co.uk
