AGM of Sheffield Campaign Against Climate Change

Date, Time & Location Tue 15 Oct 2019, 6.30pm, Quaker Meeting House, St James St. S1 2EW
Details Annual General Meeting of Sheffield Campaign Against Climate Change (SCACC, the governing body for Sheffield Climate Alliance). The talk will include a focus on further volunteer recruitment and funding this year. Ci Davis from Extinction Rebellion will be giving a full insight into the recent actions of XR in London, and Rick Watson will be proposing a new idea that propels action locally even further. The papers for the AGM are at the link below. Everyone is welcome to attend, and refreshments will be available to purchase. Doors will be open from 6.15pm, with refreshments available to purchase. The actual AGM will start at 6.30pm in the Main Hall upstairs. Sheffield Climate Alliance
