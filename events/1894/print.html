Consumer boycott campaign monthly action: The Co-op Campaign

Date, Time & Location Sat 17 Mar 2012, 11am-12:15pm, outside the Co-op in Pinstone Street, opposite The Peace Gardens
Details In 2005, Palestinian civil society issued a call for a campaign of boycotts, divestment and sanctions (BDS) against Israel until it complies with international law and Palestinian rights. Here in Sheffield campaigners are currently concentrating on the consumer boycott of Israeli goods and the cultural boycott calling on artists not to perform in Israel, and protesting against the presence in this country of Israeli of artists and institutions acting as cultural ambassadors of the Israeli state. Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign
