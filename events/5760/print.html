"System Change Not Climate Change"

Date, Time & Location Thu 17 Oct 2019, 7pm, Central United Reform Church, 60 Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details A launch event of a new book edited by Martin Empson, on the crisis in biodiversity and climate change. He brings together leading socialist and environmental activists, to argue that the capitalist system is the cause of environmental destruction and there needs to be a revolutionary transformation of society. A public meeting organised by the Socialist Workers Party, all welcome. Socialist Workers Party
