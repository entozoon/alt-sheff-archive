"The Stories of Activism in Sheffield"

Date, Time & Location Sat 18 May 2013, 9.45-3pm, The Circle, 33 Rockingham Lane S1 4FW
Details A project run by the WEA (Workers' Educational Association), "The Stories of Activism in Sheffield" offers a 2-day course in learning to collect and record our history. Includes oral history interview training, support when interviewing activists, and the opportunity to reflect on experiences. Day 1 will be exploring Sheffield's radical 20th century history, with contributions from Stephen Ludlum (University of Sheffield Politics Dept) and various activists. Day 2 will be at a time and venue to be agreed by the group. Applying for a place involves sending a brief outline (under 100 words) about why you would like to be involved and what you could contribute (deadline 2nd May). Contact Gary Rivett, Department of History, University of Sheffield, Jessop West, 1 Upper Hanover Street, S3 7RA. Tel Sheff 2222574, email g.rivett@sheffield.ac.uk
