Sharrow Festival

Date, Time & Location Sat 2 Jul 2011, 12-8pm, Mount Pleasant Park, Sharrow Lane, nr London Rd. S11 8AE
Details If you've never been to Sharrow Festival, go. It's a great annual multicultural community festival bringing people together with a focus on creativity and participation. There's also the Sharrow Fringe Festival on the days surrounding the main festival event. Performances this year include Verlan Musique, Capoeira display and Pallav Roy. Local groups and businesses, great music, a worldwide variety of food, the local Sure Start team, Football Unites Racism Divides streetkick activities, Hip Hop zone, live grafitti art....the list goes on. Follow the festival on Sheffield Live 93.2 FM. www.sharrowfestival.btck.co.uk
