Performance: Asylum Monologues

Date, Time & Location Sat 2 Apr 2011, 3.45pm-5.45pm, Stephenson Music Hall, Oakholme Road S10 3DF
Details Ice and Fire perform first hand accounts from asylum seekers along with the people that support them and the officials they come across on their journeys. Moving accounts based on testimony, public opinion and political statements. www.iceandfire.co.uk
