Little Acorns – workshop for children

Date, Time & Location Sun 23 Sep 2018, 11.30am, The Spiegeltent, Barkers Pool S1 2JA
Details A drop-in session with Jo Peel and Christopher Jarratt. Children can help re-wild the urban environment with colourful found objects. Each child will plant their own bit of nature into objects usually discarded or thrown away, helping to create an unusual urban garden. Free to attend, part of the 2018 Festival of the Mind.
