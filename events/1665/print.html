Peace in the Park 2012 planning meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 28 Sep 2011, 6pm-8pm, Highfield Library, London Road, Sheffield S2 4NF
Details The group meeting to plan for Sheffield´s best free festival. All meetings are open to anybody, so don´t be shy if you want to get involved and/or have any ideas. Current co-ordinators to discuss any ideas with the group, new attendees bring along enthusiasm and ideas, roles left within the group that need filling! Good causes: the group will shortly be taking nominations from charities so please come along and tell about the cause you support. This meeting is a good chance for people interested in becoming a co-ordinator / directly involved at the festival to say hello and explain what they would like to do so please bring along fresh ideas for next year´s festival ... cake also welcome!
