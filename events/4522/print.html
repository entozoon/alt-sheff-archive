HEELEY FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sat 18 Jun 2016, 12-5pm, various venues
Details The biggest community event in Heeley. Last year the festival had over 5000 visitors. It takes place in and around Heeley People’s Park (formerly called Millennium Park) and various other venues (Heeley City Farm, Christ Church and Anns Grove School). Organised by Heeley Entertainment and Activities Team (HEAT) in conjunction with Heeley Development Trust, Ann’s Grove School, Heeley City Farm, Heeley Church and local residents. Entry free. Buses 18 or 56 from city centre. Opportunities for volunteering, performing or coming up with new ideas. Stall prices start at just £15. Car boot on the Anns Grove School site at £10 per vehicle/pitch. Heeley People’s Park Heeley Development Trust Heeley City Farm
