Sheffield Amnesty International meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 14 Jan 2015, 7.15pm at Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Amnesty International is a worldwide organisation of nearly 3 million people all working to protect human rights, helping to protect prisoners of conscience. The Sheffield branch meets monthly on the 2nd Wed of every month (except August). Actions on human rights abuses, contacts, successes and updates from various campaigns. Even writing a letter can change a life. Newcomers are welcome. Sheffield Amnesty International Facebook
