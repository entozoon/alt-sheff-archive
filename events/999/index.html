Transport: The Future For Sheffield - Workshop on Travel Policy & Carbon Emissions: Effect on our Health & Well-being

Tue 26 Oct 2010, 12.15pm for 1.50pm start, finish 5.30pm

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

An event run by Action For Involvement, for people interested in the subject of transport. Don't let the car users dominate it! OPEN TO EVERYONE - No previous knowledge or experience is required or assumed. Facilitators: Perry Walker, Head of Democracy and Participation, New Economics Foundation (NEF) and Wendy Stern, Founder and Chairman, Action for Involvement. Speakers:

  • John Bann, Head of Transport & Highways, Sheffield City Council
  • Professor Lenny Koh, Associate Dean, Management School, The University of Sheffield
  • Dr Jeremy Wight, Director of Public Health, Sheffield NHS & Sheffield City Council
Sheffield Travel Marketplace exhibitors:
  • Chesterfield Biogas City Car Club
  • East End Quality of Life Initiative
  • Get Walking, Keep Walking
  • Sheffield Campaign Against Climate Change
  • Walkit!
Action for Involvement is a not-for-profit, web-based think tank specialising in low cost workshops for people in our communities to meet key decision-makers and discuss topical issues with them. A grassroots organisation, focussed on value for money, governance and sustainability in the Public or Private Sector local and national service delivery. Hosted by DLA Piper LLP, 1 St Paul's Place, ((above Nero Café by the Peace Gardens), S1 8JX. Register to attend at event registration £5 plus an eventbrite handling charge. A £4 discount code is available for Concessions/Members/Friends obtained via contact form. £1.50 Suggested donation for event pack and refreshments. Annual Membership: £8 via Paypal in advance. £15 Standard Charge applies to anyone booking but not turning up, turning up without registering, or after registration closing date. For specific queries call 07946 453258.
