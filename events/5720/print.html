Demonstration against Boris Johnson's coup

Date, Time & Location Sat 31 Aug 2019, 11am, City Hall steps, Barkers Pool S1 2JA
Details Labour members Olivia Blake and Louise Haigh MP have called this demonstration against the parliamentary shut-down proposed by Boris Johnson. Join trade unions, school climate strikers, Green and other pro-democracy, anti-Brexit forces.
