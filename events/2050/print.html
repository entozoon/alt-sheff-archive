Rotherham MAY DAY MARCH

Date, Time & Location Sat 5 May 2012, 10.30am gather outside Rotherham Town Hall
Details The only May Day march in South Yorkshire. Gathering at 10.30am outside Rotherham Town Hall. The march will start at 11.00am and will head to a rally in All Saints' Square for 11.30am, through till 1.30pm May Day is OUR day. Calling all workers, unemployed, women, youth, old, disabled, trade unionists, friends, groups and organisations against the extremes of the Con-Dem government to demonstrate. May Day belongs to the workers and the people! Please bring your own banners, chants and drums! Called by Rotherham Against the Cuts, and the united people of Rotherham, supported by Rotherham Trades Council. Facebook event
