Protest: "Love Your Taxes!"

Date, Time & Location Sat 14 Feb 2015, 2pm-3pm, Fargate (the bottom end, near Boots) city centre
Details On Valentines Day a series of protests in Sheffield will start with a bang on Fargate. You are invited to make a noise about tax, lobbying parties to introduce a tax dodging bill. Bring pots, pans, instrument of any kind to make some noise about tax dodging. Or bring along a sign that says what you think about tax avoidance and creating a fairer society. Or say why you totally love companies who pay their taxes! In the run-up to the election, protests will be held at various tax dodgers across the city. They'd love to see you there :) Facebook event page taxdodgingbill.org.uk
