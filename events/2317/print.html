Sheffield Save Our NHS meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 8 Oct 2012, 7pm, Victoria Hall Methodist Church, 60 Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details Sheffield Save Our NHS is fighting the appalling government plans for cuts, competition and privatization for the benefit of private companies. Get involved, show your support for the NHS.
