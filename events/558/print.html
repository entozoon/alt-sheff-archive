'Burger off!' event for May Day

Date, Time & Location Sat 1 May 2010, from 12pm Sheffield centre
Details An event for May Day for all people who are interested in workers rights, environmental protection and animal rights to join in together in protest against the disgusting fast food multi-nationals who are among the worst offenders in all cases. The "Burger off!" event will include a Free vegan burger giveaway stall (on Fargate from 12-2pm) with demos at McDonalds, 20/22 High Street and KFC near Castle Market at the same time and until 3/4pm (to be confirmed).
