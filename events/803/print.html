Ranger Event: Meet the Team

Date, Time & Location Sat 31 Jul 2010, 10am-1pm Concord Park
Details As part of "World Ranger Day" why not drop in and meet the North Ranger Team, find out what the Rangers do, what services they can offer you, how to become a volunteer and also learn a little about the history of Concord Park and the Cruck Barn. Just call in for a cuppa and a chat. FREE Meet at The Cruck Barn off Oaks Lane, Concord Park, Shiregreen. Enquiries: Sheff 2403578 www.sheffield.gov.uk/rangers
