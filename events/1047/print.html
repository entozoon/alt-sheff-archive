Play: Sex & Docks & Rock'n'roll

Date, Time & Location Thu 16 Dec, The Civic Theatre, Barnsley
Details Red Ladder Theatre Co. presents a play by Boff Whalley, of Chumbawamba, set in 1960s Liverpool. The story of one family, against a background of strikes, picketing and jailings. 'History with choruses you can sing along to' in the great tradition of radical, political and entertaining theatre. For venue and full details tel. 0845 1800363 www.barnsleycivic.co.uk
