Picnic in the Park - Rotherham

Date, Time & Location Sun 18 Jul 2010, 12.30-3pm, Clifton Park
Details Rotherham Ethnic Minority Alliance (REMA) invites people from all communities to come along to a picnic in the park, take a bit of time off, and maybe make some new friends. Part of the Big Lunch day, to encourage community spirit. REMA website
