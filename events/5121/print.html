Film: "The Doom Doc"

Date, Time & Location Sun 9 Jul 2017, 8pm, Showroom Cinema, Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details A Sheffield-made documentary exploring heavy underground music and DIY culture in the UK. It explores issues such as drug use, mental health and gentrification. This premiere screening will be followed by a Q&A (questions and answers) with Connor Matheson (director/producer), Rob Graham (Drenge) and Joe Allen (Kurokuma) Showroom Cinema
