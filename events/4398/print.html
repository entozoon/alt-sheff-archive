Green Party launch of local manifesto for a better Sheffield

Date, Time & Location Thu 10 Mar 2016, 7pm, Channing Hall, 45 Surrey St. S1 2LG
Details Natalie Bennett, UK Green Party leader, is coming to Sheffield to mark the launch of their local manifesto for a better Sheffield. There will be a speech from Natalie and from green councillor lead speaker Rob Murphy. Afterwards will be discussion of how to get involved in the Council election campaign. Sheffield Green Party
