Debate: Is Fairtrade Enough?

Date, Time & Location Thu 3 Mar 2016, 6-8pm HUBS Building, Paternoster Row S1 2QQ
Details No one would argue that Fairtrade is a bad thing, but does it go far enough? In recent years several studies have cast doubt on the impact Fairtrade accreditation has on the developing world. An informal discussion with speakers about the impact of Fairtrade and what consumers can do to ensure that their choices have a positive impact. Organised by Sheffield Hallam Students Union and Sheffield Festival of Debate, hosted by Opus Independents which also publishes the "Localcheck" Alt-Sheff column in "Now Then" magazine.
