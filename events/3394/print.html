Chesterfield May Day 2014

Date, Time & Location Mon 5 May 2014, Chesterfield (near Sheffield)
Details The 37th annual Chesterfield May Day is held on the first Monday after the beginning of May, one of the biggest celebrations of working class solidarity in the country. 10.30am Assembly outside Chesterfield Town Hall on Rose Hill. 11.00am The march moves off through the town arriving at New Square for the Rally. The afternoon is full of activities and entertainment celebrating international workers day. Music, dancing and stalls of community and campaigning organisations. Speakers: Toby Perkins, MP Matt Wrack, FBU union Clara Osagiede, RMT union Angela Sabino, Democratic Women's Movement of Portugal Music: Bleeding Hearts Ducie Maia Chesterfield May Day
