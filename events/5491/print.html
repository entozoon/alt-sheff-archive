PEACE IN THE PARK

Date, Time & Location Sat 8 Jun 2019, Ponderosa Park, Crookes Valley Rd. S10 1BA
Details Peace in the Park is an excellent Sheffield festival, with multiple music stages, food, dozens of stalls, entertainment and fun for all ages. All the local activist and community groups are there and it is a great day out. Run entirely by volunteers, and funded by gigs all year round, the proceeds are given to different charities every time. Tell your friends and come along, see you in the park! Peace in the Park
