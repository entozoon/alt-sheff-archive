Public Meeting: The Crisis in Yemen

Date, Time & Location Fri 5 Feb 2010, 6.30pm at Burngreave Vestry Hall, Burngreave Road
Details Yemen is labelled as the new haven of terrorism in the Middle East. This meeting provides an opportunity to hear the reality behind the rhetoric and discuss the present situation in Yemen. Organised by the long-established Sheffield Yemeni Community, supported by Sheffield Stop the War Coalition.
