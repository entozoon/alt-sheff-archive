SYMAAG meeting (South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group)

Date, Time & Location Tue 12 Mar 2013, 6.30pm Exchange Brewery, 2 Bridge St. S3 8NS
Details SYMAAG unites refugee, asylum and migrant organisations with campaign groups and supporting individuals. It campaigns both for the rights of those seeking asylum and of those coming to work in this country. If you want to come to any SYMAAG meeting but find it hard to afford transport do not be put off, just bring your ticket or a receipt and SYMAAG can pay you back at the meeting.
