Doc/Fest

Date, Time & Location 9-14 June 2017
Details The annual festival of documentary film, based around the Showroom Cinema. Inspirational documentary films from across the globe, discussion panels and filmmaker masterclasses, chance to pitch new documentary projects, and lots of parties. Sheffield Doc/Fest is open to all to submit films, session ideas and to apply to the Meetmarket. The youth jury and Doc/Fest also regularly recruit volunteers and staff, both temporary and permanent.
