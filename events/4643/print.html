WOODSEATS FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location 8 - 10 Jul 2016
Details The annual festival held in various venues around Woodseats, all run by enthusiastic volunteers from the local community. Arts and crafts, music, performance, sport and leisure activities. Stalls free for local community groups and charities, £30 for private (non hot food) stalls, and local businesses get priority for our limited hot food stalls at £40.
