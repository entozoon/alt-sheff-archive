BROOMHILL FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location 10-18 Jun 2017, Broomhill, Sheffield 10
Details Annual local community celebration, held every June, raising money for local charities. This year there are dozens of events taking place around Broomhill including a children’s football tournament, open gardens, performances, exhibitions, talks, literature, heritage and family events. All this year’s events are listed on the website, or find a paper copy of the programme available from various outlets. www.broomhillfestival.org.uk
