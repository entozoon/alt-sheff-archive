Family Fuel Buster Fun Days

Date, Time & Location Wed 4 & Thu 5 Aug 2010, 11am-3pm
Details One of a series of free activity sessions for families, in the Energy House at Heeley City Farm. A selection of activities on recycling and energy-saving, with staff to advise on ways to reduce and manage fuel bills and increase home energy efficiency. Become a "Fuel Buster" by making your energy saving promise, completing the Energy House trail and designing your very own "Fuel Buster" badge. FREE.
