"It Started with One Well"

Date, Time & Location Thu 24 Mar 2016, 7pm Regather Works, 57-59 Club Garden Rd. S11 8BU
Details Sheffield Against Fracking and Regather Cooperative present a feature film screening and debate around the controversial topic of fracking. After cuts to green energy subsidies and the recent granting of licences to drill fracking test sites across the UK, many of them in the Peak District, it is more important than ever that this issue is brought to the fore. Part of Festival of Debate 2016, hosted by Opus Independents which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine. ‘Pay what you feel’ on the night. Details here.
