"Unexplained"

Date, Time & Location Wed 24 Oct 2018, 7.30pm, Firth Hall, Firth Court, University of Sheffield, Western Bank S10 2TN
Details Researcher and writer Richard MacLean Smith talks about his book "Unexplained". Based on his podcast series Unexplained, it covers strange unsolved mysteries from around the world, analysed with open minded scepticism. Subjects range from political conspiracy theories to historical cases, and whether things traditionally thought to be supernatural may in fact lead to new scientific discoveries.
