Details A Course for the general public, introducing Organics, Permaculture and Biodynamics, in 10 two-hour sessions. Food production for the home and allotment kitchen garden. Ideas and concepts which will explain the fundamental principles of organic food growing. These topics are relevant and useful whether you are a beginner or more experienced. Experienced grower and tutor Richard Clare believes that ornamental and amenity gardening are very different topics. We need a less militaristic and mechanistic approach to our food supply.
