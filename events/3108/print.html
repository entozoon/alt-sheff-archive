Cycle Forum

Date, Time & Location Tue 19 Nov 2013
Details The Cycle Forum is where Sheffield City Council meets with people who have an interest in cycling, every couple of months. It is formal meeting, with an agenda and minutes, chaired by a Councillor. Organisations are represented, like CycleSheffield, CTC, the Passenger Transport authorities, cycle shops, universities, health organisations, large employers, etc. All meetings are open to the public for observation, although questions from the public are only accepted with 24 hours notice. Details: on the Council website And good up-to-date discussion of issues on CycleSheffield website Items on the agenda this time include: Cycle lanes through Frecheville to Birley Moor The Sheaf Valley Cycle Route; Campaign against the withdrawal of bus lanes on Ecclesall Road Bikes on light-rail Green Routes Cycle Audits and cycle facilities at the bottom of the Moor Ending of the city-centre Cycle Paramedic service
