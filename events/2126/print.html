Open Mic Night

Date, Time & Location Tue 19 Jun 2012, 6.30pm, Burton Street Foundation, 57 Burton St. Hillsborough S6 2HH
Details Burton Street Open Mic Night returns, after a 6 month break. Sing, play, perform, dance, join in, listen, or simply sit back and enjoy the atmosphere, with the House Band hosting the event. They look forward to seeing old and new faces! Burton Street Foundation is a not-for-profit community organisation.
