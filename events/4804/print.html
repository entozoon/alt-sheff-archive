Global Justice meeting

Date, Time & Location Thu 6 Oct 2016, 7-9pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Global Justice aims to change the actions, policies and institutions of governments, corporations and wealthy minorities, which are preventing an end to poverty and injustice. Part of a global movement, it works alongside people protesting in the developing world. The organising meeting is held on the first Thursday of each month, to discuss coming events and campaigns.
