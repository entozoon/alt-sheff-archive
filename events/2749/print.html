NOSH street food market event

Date, Time & Location Thu 30 May 2013, 5pm-10pm, The Old Junior School, South View Road, Sharrow S7 1DB
Details An eclectic mix of street food, craft beer, art and craft stalls, live music, a charity art auction, and even Zuma classes. All profits towards the Sharrow Lantern Carnival and Sharrow Festival. Organised by Art in the Park, Creative Action Network and somewhereto Facebook event
