Taking Back Our NHS conference

Date, Time & Location Sat 12 Mar 2016 10am-2pm, The Roco, 338 Glossop Rd. S10 2HW
Details Sheffield Save Our NHS (SSONHS) are holding a half day Saturday conference to mark the Parliamentary Second Reading of the NHS Bill (on Friday March 11th). The keynote speaker is Dr David Wrigley, Carnforth GP and member of BMA Council and GP Committee. Also Regional and local speakers on the NHS Bill, local issues and the junior doctors´ dispute and workshops on Primary Care, Working in the NHS, and Mental Health. Get updated on the crisis facing the NHS under current Government policies and contribute to plans to help keep the NHS as a viable publicly funded and publicly provided service. The conference is free but donations will be requested to cover expenses. Optional Sandwich Lunch £3.50. More details on website. To register go to Eventbrite page or email SSONHS, indicating if you would like to take the lunch. Registration is from 9.45am and the event will start at 10.00am. Sheffield Save Our NHS
