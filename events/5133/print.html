The Great UpCycle Fete

Date, Time & Location Sat 29 Jul 2017, 11am-4pm, St. Mary’s, Bramall Lane S2 4QZ
Details UpCycling is the creative transformation of waste into something useful or beautiful. This is a free-entry fundraiser to celebrate and support St. Mary’s and the excellent TimeBuilders skill-sharing project. The event includes upcyled arts and crafts, FoodCycle international buffet, traditional tea tent, climbing and caving, birds of prey display, games and family fun. ​They welcome enquiries if you want to sell upcycled crafts, display upcycled sculptures, or your organisation’s work fits within the upcycle theme. Architecture students who fancy designing and building a temporary upcycle pavilion are particularly welcome (click here). Timebuilders
