Festival of Debate: Northern Arts Festival evening

Date, Time & Location Sat 7 Mar 2015, 6-11pm Theatre Delicatessen, 17 The Moor, S1 4PF
Details As part of the Festival of Debate, in association with Charlotte and Tim, an evening of live entertainment from northern artists. With the general election drawing near, what better time to ask our artists to respond to the current political landscape? A real variety of acts and drinks/refreshments available at the bar.
