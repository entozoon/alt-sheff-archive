Car-free Division Street trial

Date, Time & Location Sat 19 & Sun 20 Oct 2019, 10am to 4pm on each day
Details Sheffield City Council have agreed to a trial road closure of Division Street on Saturday 19th and Sunday 20th October. As part of the agreement to close the road Cycle Sheffield will do some of the work required for the organisation of the day, and marshalling the closure points. (If you can help please contact them.) More info about the event on their website, along with nice artist impressions of what Division Street (or any street) could become like. Cycle Sheffield
