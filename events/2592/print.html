SHEFFIELD GREEN FAIR

Date, Time & Location Sat 13 Jul 2013, St Marys Community Centre, Bramall Lane.
Details The annual celebration of everyone and everything that is green and environmental in Sheffield and Yorkshire, as always a great day out. Entertainment, live music, DJs, eco-fashion show, bar and wholefood cafe, loads of stalls presenting the green alternatives, and fun for children all day long. Zones featuring green technology, sustainable living, health and healing, campaigns and community action, fashion, arts and crafts, fair-trade and organic goods. Admission £2 (£1 concessions), 16 and under free. Sheffield Green Party
