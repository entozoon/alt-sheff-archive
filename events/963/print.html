Local Health Workshop - North East Sheffield

Date, Time & Location Wed 15 Sep 2010, 9am-1pm, Ellesmere Suite, Sorby House, Burngreave
Details A workshop for people who have an interest in improving the health and wellbeing of communities in the North East Community Assembly area, including Elected Members, Public Health Officers, Community Reps, Council Officers. It will build on the previous good work in the area and focus discussion on the following priority areas: Overall health of communities Barriers to accessing services Appropriate use of services The actions identified at the event will be presented to the Community Assembly as recommendations to influence the Assembly Plan. Includes lunch. To book a place contact the Health Improvement Team on sfhw@sheffield.gov.uk. Tel Sheff 2734152
