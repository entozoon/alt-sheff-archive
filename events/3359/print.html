Clothes Swap

Date, Time & Location Sat 5 Apr 2014, 12-5pm, The Shakespeare, 146-148 Gibraltar St. S3 8UB
Details Come and swap clothes, clear out your wardrobe and have a change on the cheap, without giving all your money to the high street capitalist retailers. Runs on the first Saturday of each month. £4 entry fee, proceeds go to St. Wilfrid’s Centre, which gives welfare and educational opportunities to help homeless and vulnerable people can find understanding, a sense of belonging and the practical support to regain their self esteem.
