Sheffield Environment Weeks

Date, Time & Location Sat 27 Apr-Sun 9 Jun 2019
Details We can be proud of the hundreds of activities that make up Sheffield Environment Weeks, which has run for many years. They range from talks on a range of subjects, to projects like urban agriculture and wildlife conservation. To get involved see the timetable on the website. Officially it runs 27th April to 9th June but some events are on before this. Sheffield Environment Weeks
