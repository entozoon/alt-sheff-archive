"Written in the Stars: How to Live Happily in a Deterministic Universe"

Date, Time & Location Tue 3 Feb 2015, 7.30PM St. George's Lecture Theatre, 17 Mappin St. S1 4DT
Details The Sheffield Humanist Society presents Professor Jim Al-Khalili OBE, theoretical physicist, author, broadcaster, and President of the British Humanist Association looking at some of life's big questions. Do we have free will? Is our future predetermined? Jim will discuss what modern physics can contribute to questions of this nature, and will also offer his personal view on the values of Humanism.
