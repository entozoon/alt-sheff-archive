Make Do & Mend

Date, Time & Location Sat 20 Jun 2015, 12-3pm, Winter Gardens, 90 Surrey St. S1 2LH
Details In the spirit of the 1940s, Ignite Imaginations are making do and mending! Join in to create your own recycled masterpieces. They will help you turn buttons, fabrics and everyday objects into bunting, badges and keepsakes. Crafts suitable for age 7+, just drop by and join in!
