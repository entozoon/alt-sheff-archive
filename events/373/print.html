Are We Protected? Migrants Issue Around Criminal Justice

Date, Time & Location Tue 16 Feb 2010, 9.45am-2.30pm, run by South Yorkshire Local Criminal Justice Board (LCJB)
Details Migrants, community leaders and people working within South Yorkshire’s migrant communities are invited to this discussion day which aims to improve migrant communities’ confidence in the criminal justice system, by listening to individuals’ experiences as victims and offenders, then working together to identify problems and create solutions.
