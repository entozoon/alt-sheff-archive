Sponsored sleepout

Date, Time & Location Sat 6 Feb 2010 from 7pm on the University of Sheffield concourse in front of the Students Union
Details Student Action for Refugees (STAR) sponsored sleepout to raise awareness of the plight of destitute Asylum Seekers, and raise money for ASSIST, the charity in Sheffield that supports them.
