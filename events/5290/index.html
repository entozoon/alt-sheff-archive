UK Society for Co-operative Studies 51 st Annual Research Conferenc

Fri 31 Aug-Sun 2 Sep 2018, Sheffield Business School, Sheffield Hallam University S1 1WB

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

The UK Society for Co-operative Studies holds its Annual General meeting in Sheffield this year, hosted by FairShares Institute for Cooperative Social Entrepreneurship. The event title is "Diversity in Co-operation: People, place, and organisations", under the following themes:Co-operative HistoryThe Politics of Co-operationCo-operation in Public ServiceCo-operative EducationCo-operative Retailing, Marketing and Sustainable DevelopmentNew Approaches to Co-operationLeadership and Management in a Co-operative ContextCo-operative Governance and Accounting There is a call-out for offers of papers, posters, panels and practical activities, click here.UK Society for Co-operative Studies
