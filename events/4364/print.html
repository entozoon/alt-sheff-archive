Sheffield Vegan Approach 2016

Date, Time & Location Starts Sat 4 Jun 2016, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Want to save animals? Improve your health? Help the planet? Why not try going vegan for a month? This FREE introduction to veganism event includes nutritional information, cookery demos, films, expert speakers, vegan food and stalls and an opportunity to meet like-minded people. You will also receive: Free vegan food samples Free recipe booklets Free online resources Free access to online coaching Anyone taking part in the vegan month needs to arrive for the launch event on Saturday 4th June, from 10am-4pm. An Introduction to Veganism workshop will be followed by a Free Vegan Food Fair at the same venue, with your chance to sample an amazing selection of sweet and savoury vegan dishes.The food fair will be open to the public from 12 noon. All welcome! If you would like to donate to help covering funds for this event, click here or contact the group to donate by cash/cheque. Already vegan? They need lots of promotion of the event, volunteers on the day to help make it successful, and experienced vegans who would like to act as "buddies" for the month, supporting and advising Vegan Approach participants. Please get in touch if you would like to help. Sheffield Vegan Approach
