Film: '24 City'

Date, Time & Location 12-15 Jul 2010 at the Showroom Cinema
Details An inspiring exploration of the changing face of an industrial Chinese city. As a factory faces closure to make way for luxury flats, the workers tell their stories about the factory which manufactured military aircraft as well as tales about their lives. Three generations reveal their experiences, capturing a recent history of China. http://www.showroomworkstation.org.uk/24city
