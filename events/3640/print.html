No Workfare, No Abuse of Workers

Date, Time & Location Thu 11 Sep 2014, from noon, Savers Health & Beauty, 8-10 Haymarket S1 2AX
Details As part of the Week of Action against Benefit Sanctions, UNITE Community Sheffield are holding protesting against Savers stores and its use of workfare - forced labour - and against their abusive treatment of workers, paid and unpaid. Come and see the protest outside the Savers shop in Sheffield Haymarket. Facebook event page
