Boycott, boycott, boycott!

Date, Time & Location Mon 23 May 2011, 7.30pm St. Matthews rooms, Carver St. S1 4FE
Details An evening workshop and discussions to review Israeli goods boycott work over the last year, and to plan the way forward for 2011/2012. Please come along to contribute to planning and hear more about the rapidly growing international movement. All welcome who want to get involved in any way, from letter writing to direct action.
