'Don’t go to Israel!' cultural boycott

Date, Time & Location Tue 11 May 2010 at Buxton (transport see below)
Details The cultural boycott is growing rapidly and recently Gil Scott Heron agreed to cancel his concert in Israel. There are currently new campaigns to call on Joan Armatrading and Elvis Costello not to perform in Israel this June. Joan is currently touring and protestors will be leafleting outside all her concerts. She is performing in Buxton on Tuesday 11th May and Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign plans to be there. Email boycottisraeligoods@live.co.uk if you can come and they will sort out transport. Join the facebook campaign for Joan at www.facebook.com/group.php?gid=116625421691276 and write to her via her website at www.joanarmatrading.com
