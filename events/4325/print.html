Charity Roller Derby: Death Leopards vs Steel Panthers

Date, Time & Location Sat 16 Jan 2016, Doors open 2pm, Concord Sports Centre, Shiregreen Lane S5 6AE
Details Sheffield Steel Rollergirls intra-league teams the Death Leopards and Steel Panthers will battle it out in a charity bout in support of the Refugee Support Network. Roller derby is a fast-paced, full contact team sport played on roller skates. Sheffield Steel Rollergirls new skater training programme is open to all self-identifying women over the age of 18. Watch a video explaining more about the exciting sport of roller derby here. The event will include stalls by local businesses, a cake sale and much more. Come join support local roller derby and raise money for charity! Tickets £6 in advance/£8 on the day for adults, £3/£4 for children aged 5-12 and £15/£20 for a family ticket.
