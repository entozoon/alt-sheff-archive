Crowd funding: raising money for your project

Date, Time & Location Fri 7 Feb 2014, 1pm-2pm, 35 Chapel Walk, S1 2PD
Details Crowd funding is a new way to attract funding, but what works? What does not? And why? James Wallbank leads Access Space, a fertile co-working environment for community projects and enterprise startups. He will talk from experience and research, about crowd funding and the possibilities of successfully raising money for your project or enterprise through Kickstarter. Free entry, booking not necessary. Part of the week of external "pop-up" activities by Access Space. The Chapel Walk space will be open 10.30am-5.30pm with more than ninety artworks on display, and designs and commercialised products made by participants in Sheffield. Venue details: www.35chapelwalk.com Access Space
