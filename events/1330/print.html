Greenpeace meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 8 Mar 2011, 8pm Red Lion, Charles St (Hub end) S1 2ND
Details Monthly meeting of the very active Greenpeace South Yorkshire group, held on the 2nd Tuesday of each month. Newcomers always welcome. Current issues include this next weekend's Lib Dem Conference in Sheffield, tuna fishing, Go Beyond Oil lobbying, pressure on MPs to press for sustainable energy as part of the Energy Bill, and Rainbow Warrior 3. More details of meetings and current activities in the Active Supporters newsletter, see Greenpeace South Yorkshire website
