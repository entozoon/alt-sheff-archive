Occupy Sheffield Peoples Forum/Street Theatre "A Bedroom Farce "

Details 10.30 Welcome. Coffee & biscuits. 11.00 "The Bedroom Farce" 11.30 Sheffield People's Forum (including feedback from the Occupy Sheffield People's Forum on Debt in March and feedback from the Sheffield People's Assembly on May 22nd at Sheffield Hallam) 12.30 Picnic lunch to bring and share.
