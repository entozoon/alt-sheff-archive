Abbeyfield Park Multicultural Festival

Date, Time & Location Sun 10 Jul 2011, 1-6pm, Abbeyfield Park, Abbeyfield Rd S4 7AT
Details The hottest event north of the city: a massive annual multicultural community festival. Organised by local residents with a wide variety of musical acts, food and craft stalls from across the community and across the world. Free entry. abbeyfieldparkmulticulturalfestival.wordpress.com
