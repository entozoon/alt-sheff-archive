Heeley City Farm Fundraising Jumble Sale

Date, Time & Location Sat 7 Mar 2015, 12-2pm Anns Road Community Centre, Anns Road S2 3DJ
Details Have a rummage for a bargain and help to raise some money for Heeley City Farm to feed the animals and continue with projects in the community. Women's, men's and kids' clothes, bric-a-brac, books, toys and household items. Held in Ann's Road Community Centre across the road as this has more space for rummaging! 50p admission (kids free). Ring Sheff 2590482 option 5 for more information or email s.hardy@heeleyfarm.org.uk. Any donations of good quality items are also most appreciated and they need volunteers to help too.
