Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign: Open forum

Date, Time & Location Wed 28 Mar 2012, 7pm, Showroom Cinema, Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details An opportunity for new and existing supporters to find out more about specific areas of the Palestine Solidarity Campaign. These sessions are held every four months. Draft Agenda 7pm Opportunity to buy coffee from bar next door. 7.15 – 7.45 Introduction to the work and options on offer 7.45 – 8.30 Small group discussion/workshops: History of Palestine; BDS (Boycott, Divestment and Sanctions Campaign); Women and Palestine, inc Women’s Scholarship Fund; What we raise funds for (eg. Children’s Projects) 8.30 Feedback and Panel session for questions re current situation 9.00 Close; opportunity for informal discussion until 10 pm. (coffee/drinks from the bar) SPSC would really prefer people to pre-register, to know numbers and preferences but you can still turn up without registering. To register email, stating what you are interested in, to openforum@sheffieldpsc.org.uk. Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign
