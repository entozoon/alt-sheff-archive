Talk: A New Feminist Perspective on Pornography

Date, Time & Location Mon 15 Oct 2012, 7.15pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Dr Julia Long talks about her new book "Anti-Porn: The Resurgence of Anti-Pornography Feminism" in response to the mainstreaming of pornographic culture. Tickets £4/£3 concessions.
