Feminists Over Fifty Social Group

Date, Time & Location Wed 11 Oct 2017, 10.30am, Showroom Cinema cafe, Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details Feminists Over Fifty is a feminist group in Sheffield for women. Many may have been part of the feminist movements of the 1960s, 70s and 80s, or have been drawn to feminist ideas and literature. The group is a friendly feminist social and discussion group. They meet on the second Wednesday of every month. Please just turn up or contact by email: feministsoverfifty@gmail.com or ring Sheff 2306600. Feminists Over Fifty on Meetup
