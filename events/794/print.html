Family Fuel Buster Fun Days

Date, Time & Location Wed 28 & Thu 29 Jul 2010, 11am-3pm
Details One of a series of free activity sessions for families, taking place in the Energy House at Heeley City Farm. Staff will be on hand to advise on ways to reduce and manage fuel bills and increase the home energy efficiency. A selection of environmental activities on recycling and energy-saving. Become a "Fuel Buster" by making your energy saving promise, completing the Energy House trail and designing your very own "Fuel Buster" badge. FREE.
