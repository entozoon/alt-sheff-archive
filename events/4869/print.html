Our Much-Valued NHS is Under Pressure, But Why?

Date, Time & Location Tue 22 Nov 2016, 7pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Is the pressure on the NHS due to increasing demand and expectations? Or reductions in funding, staff and other resources for health and social care? Or inherent inefficiency? Or lack of effective prevention? Or the wear and tear of constant reorganisation and increasing privatisation? Are the new NHS plans a sign of progress, or simply cuts in disguise? Join an expert panel to debate how we can secure the NHS for future generations. Part of Festival of Debate 2016, hosted by not-for-profit social enterprise Opus Independents (which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine).
