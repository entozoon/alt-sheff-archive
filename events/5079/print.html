Amnesty International meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 14 Jun 2017, 7.15pm at Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Amnesty International is a worldwide organisation of nearly 3 million people all working to protect Human Rights. The Sheffield branch meets monthly on the 2nd Wed of every month (except August). Actions on human rights abuses, contacts, successes and updates from various campaigns. Newcomers welcome. If you've never been to an event and would like to find out more, just send an email or Facebook message. Amnesty Sheffield on Facebook
