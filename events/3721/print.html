Craft Fair at Burton Street Foundation

Date, Time & Location Sun 16 Nov 2014, 11-4pm, 57 Burton St S6 2HH
Details Real live reindeer will be flying in, not to mention Afghan knitting, a Lush workshop, live music performances and plenty more. All this at the Craft Fair at Burton Street Foundation, the brilliant Hillsborough-based organisation which supports adults and children with learning and physical disabilities, and has facilities for all the community. www.burtonstreet.org.uk
