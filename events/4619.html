Sheffield Space for Cycling: the Big Ride

Sat 11 Jun 2016, 10.30am from Devonshire Green S3 7S

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

A safe and fun mass participation ride around Sheffield. The route will include a short circular route of approximately 6km beginning in Devonshire Green and incorporating some of Sheffield's best known routes including Ecclesall Road, finishing back in the city centre.The ride will be suitable for everyone including families with children and regular commuters. They want to show Sheffield that cycling is a fun and normal part of everyday life, so please feel free to dress up, wear Space for Cycling red, bring placards and bring friends and family along.Bikeweek.org.uk
