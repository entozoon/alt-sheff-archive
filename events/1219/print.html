  • Guest speaker: Prof Lenny Koh from University of Sheffield looking at international examples of cities that have successfully reduced car use
  • Q & A with panel including Richard Hebditch from the Campaign for Better Transport
  • Workshops with suggested themes such as What can be done about public transport? Community Transport: plugging the gaps in mainstream transport? Walking back to happiness - why not walk it? How can cycling become "mainstream"? Smarter car use: reducing single occupancy private car journeys one by one? Reducing car use: is it really necessary?
