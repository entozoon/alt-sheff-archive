Climate Change Election Discussion and Debate

Date, Time & Location Tue 28 Apr 2015, 7pm, St Marks Church, Broomfield Rd, Broomhill S10 2SE
Details An opportunity to hear from and question Sheffield prospective parliamentary candidates about their position on climate justice issues. With:
  • Howard Denby, UKIP Sheffield Heeley candidate
  • Jillian Creasy, Green Parry Sheffield Central candidate
  • Oliver Coppard, Labour Sheffield Hallam candidate
The event will be in a discussion, seminar style and will provide plenty of opportunity for asking questions of the candidates. Hosted by Hope for the Future and Christian Aid.
