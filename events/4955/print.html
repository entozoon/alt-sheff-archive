Sheffield Green Party monthly meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 3 Apr 2017, 7pm, Central United Reformed Church, 60 Norfolk Row S1 2JB
Details This is the key meeting for members to meet others and to discuss policies or actions. Open to supporters as well, non members are welcome to attend. Usually on the first Monday of the month, please arrive 6.45pm for 7pm start. This month, people who attended the Global Greens conference will report back on how the conference works, post-Brexit views and policy, interactions with Greens from around the world and key motions and outcomes.
