International Day of the Disappeared

Date, Time & Location Tue 30 Aug 2011, 4.30-5.30pm, outside the Town Hall
Details All around the world, families are waiting to find out what happened to loved ones who have been taken away from them by agents of the state or by people acting with its support or acquiescence. 30th August is International Day of the Disappeared, and Sheffield Amnesty International are commemorating this event by having a vigil, raising awareness of the disappeared by featuring cases from around the world. Please come along and support this event. Candlewire blog discussing Amnesty International, human rights issues and events focussed on the city of Sheffield
