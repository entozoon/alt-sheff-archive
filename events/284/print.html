Sheffield Renewables Christmas Party

Date, Time & Location Wed 16 Dec 2009, 7pm-9pm, Rutland Arms, 86 Brown St. Sheffield S1 2BS
Details Sheffield Renewables is a not-for-profit social enterprise developing renewable energy projects. Join them at The Rutland Arms for a friendly drink to celebrate progress made in 2009. Feel free to call in whenever you like from 7pm to 9pm. It should be a fun way to meet the Sheffield Renewables volunteers and find out more about the work. Sheffield Renewables website
