Event: Air Pollution in Sheffield

Date, Time & Location Thu 10 Mar 2011, 5.30pm, The Circle, 33 Rockingham Lane S1 4FW
Details A free public information event about air pollution, all welcome. The Environmental Law Foundation is a charity founded in 1992 with access to environmental justice at the heart of its cause. One of the projects it runs is the Sustainable Communities Project which aims to educate and empower communities about the planning process, particularly on issues which affect their environment.
  • Maxwell Ayamba, Sheffield Black & Ethnic Minority Environmental Network
  • David Purchon, Chartered Environmental Health Practitioner
  • Naeem Siraj, Barrister, Godloves Solicitors, Leeds
  • Dr Ogo Osammor, Air Quality Officer, Sheffield City Council
The event is free and open to all, with question and answer sessions after each presentation. Run by the Sustainable Communities Project funded by The Department of Communities & Local Government through the Empowerment Fund. Environmental Law Foundation website Tel. 020 7404 1031
