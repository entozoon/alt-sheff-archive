Sleaford Mods: Invisible Britain

Date, Time & Location Sun 24 Apr 2016, 6pm Showroom Cinema, Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details The film "Invisible Britain" follows Sleaford Mods on a tour of the UK in the run-up to the 2015 General Election. They visited the neglected, broken down and boarded-up parts of the country that many would prefer to ignore. Part of Festival of Debate 2016, hosted by Opus Independents which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine.
