Hack Circus Christmas party

Date, Time & Location Sun 14 Dec 2014, 6pm-midnight, Showroom Cafe, 15 Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details Hack Circus a magazine designed in Sheffield, about the entertaining and engaging side of inventive thought and subversive technology. There is also a fortnightly podcast with various subjects and guests, and theme tune by Joseph Thorpe from Sheffield. The quarterly Hack Circus events are launches of the magazine issues, also available on the website and YouTube. This is the issue 5 launch. Expect unique entertainment, inspiring artworks and excellent company. www.hackcircus.com
