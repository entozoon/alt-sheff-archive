Occupy Sheffield general assembly

Date, Time & Location Mon 16 Apr 2012, 6.30pm 60 Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details Occupy Sheffield, like occupations all over the world, is a people's protest against the current unsustainable, undemocratic and unjust system. They work towards alternatives; structural change towards authentic global equality where the resources of the world go towards caring for people and the planet, not the military, corporate profits or the rich. This is what democracy looks like. They are currently holding general assemblies where everyone is welcome. Central United Reform Church is on the corner of Norfolk Street and Chapel Walk. www.occupysheffield.org.uk
