Open Rights Group: www.blocked.org.uk

Date, Time & Location Wed 10 Sep 2014, 6.30pm Access Space, AVEC Building, 3-7 Sidney St. S1 4RG
Details This is the group for anyone interested in digital rights: copyright, freedom of expression, censorship, parody, privacy, open data, free software. This month a short talk about the impact of internet filtering in the UK and a demonstration of www.blocked.org.uk, launched in July as part of the Censorship Monitoring Project. Followed by discussions and a workshop session. If you know of any Sheffield organisations whose websites are being blocked by the filters then please invite them along to get help to get their sites unblocked. Some examples of ones already found are www.GaySheffield.co.uk, an advice, support and health service by Sheffield Teaching Hospitals (blocked by filters on the Three network) and www.sheffieldfeministnetwork.wordpress.com, a feminist activism and friendship website blocked by strict filters on TalkTalk. You do not have to be a member to come to the meet-ups. Open Rights Group Sheffield
