Sheffield Campaign Against Climate Change (SCACC) meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 19 Jul 2011, 7.30-9.30pm Highfield Library, London Rd. S2 4NF
Details SCACC organises days of action and demonstrations, petitions to Sheffield Council, supports related national and local campaigns and runs film shows and talks for local groups. The regular meeting is open to newcomers, all welcome. Sheffield Climate Alliance business will take up the first 30 minutes of this month's meeting. SCACC website
