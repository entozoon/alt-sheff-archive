Power Trip: Fracking in the UK [film, free screening]

Date, Time & Location Tue 15 May 2018, 7pm, Regather Works, 57-59 Club Garden Rd. S11 8BU
Details "Power Trip: Fracking in the UK" (63mins) takes you onto the front lines of UK resistance in the battle to stop the controversial energy extraction process known as Fracking. It shows what happens beyond the few seconds glimpsed on the mainstream TV news. Follow grandmothers who set up a campaign group (Lancashire Nanas) and teamed up with younger activists (Reclaim the Power) to shut down the Fracking sites of Cuadrilla. Blackpool and Balcombe, West Sussex, became the frontline of Fracking resistance as trucks were occupied and gates blocked. The film also explores how social media is being used by both Fracking companies and activists.
