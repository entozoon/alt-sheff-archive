Talk: How Quackery Corrupts Real Science

Date, Time & Location Mon 21 Mar 2011, 7.30pm Showroom Cafe Bar, 15 Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details Skeptics in the Pub is about getting people together to have a relaxed and enjoyable evening while listening to talks on a wide range of topics, usually by a notable scientist or skeptic. This month David Colquhoun, PhD, FRS, a noted British pharmacologist who also runs DC’s Improbable Science blog dedicated to critical assessment of 'Alternative Medicine' and other forms of pseudoscience and science fraud. The events are free though they ask for a £2 donation, and the meetings are open to all whatever your beliefs and views so please come along. http://sheffield.skepticsinthepub.org
