Talk: Frances O’Grady, TUC General Secretary

Date, Time & Location Thu 19 Feb 2015, 6pm Octagon Centre, University of Sheffield, Western Bank S10 2TJ
Details Frances O’Grady is the first woman to hold the post of General Secretary of the Trades Union Congress (TUC). Fair pay, social justice, and protecting the public service ethos are central to her work. She opposes privatisation and leads the TUC campaign to save the National Health Service. This is the third ‘In Conversation’ evening by Sheffield Political Economy Research Institute (SPERI). Free tickets but you need to book online Sheffield Political Economy Research Institute
