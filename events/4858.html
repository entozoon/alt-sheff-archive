A Day for L.I.F.E. (Sheffield Tent City)

Sun 4 Dec 2016, 11am-3pm, Unit 8 Farfield Industrial Estate, Hillfoot Rd S3 8AA

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Tent Cities are being set up in cities around the UK as heartless Government austerities policies force thousands into homelessness. In Sheffield, "L.I.F.E. (a new beginning)" is running the Tent City in the concrete jungle of Park Hill Flats, South Street, with the help of many individuals offering food, tents and practical support. This family fun day is being organised by RCM Events to bring people together to raise awareness and supplies for those in Sheffield who need it most.FoodMusicFace paintingBouncy castleGamesRaffleAnd more to be announcedFree entry for all.L.I.F.E.
