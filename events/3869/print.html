South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG) meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 9 Feb 2015, 6.30pm Exchange Brewery, 2 Bridge St. S3 8NS
Details SYMAAG meeting Monday (note change of day) with discussion on migration across the Mediterranean Sea, Fortress Europe and opposition to the UK Government’s “Let Them Drown” policy. SYMAAG unites refugee, asylum and migrant organisations with campaign groups and supporting individuals. It campaigns both for the rights of those seeking asylum and of those coming to work in this country. www.symaag.org.uk Twitter: @SYMAAG Tel: Sheff 2729332 extension 332
