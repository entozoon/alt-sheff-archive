Friday Night Ride: "Sheffield Theatres"

Date, Time & Location Fri 5 Apr 2019, start 6.30pm
Details Sheffield Friday Night Ride (FNR) offers a fun monthly evening bike ride for everyone. This ride is to locations based on the theme of Sheffield Theatres. Prompt 6.30pm start from Devonshire Green to reach Merlin Theatre by 7pm. By the end of the evening the ride may even include performance. Friday Night Ride
