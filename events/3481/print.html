Sheffield Anti Cuts Alliance AGM

Date, Time & Location Tue 10 Jun 2014, 7pm Central United Reformed Church, 60 Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details A big decision at the AGM for Sheffield Anti Cuts Alliance: it has been agreed that a national network of non-party, anti-cuts groups is needed, and that the People's Assembly now fulfils that role. Several of those active in SACA have been working for some time with the local South Yorkshire People's Assembly, and some have become elected officers within it. There there is no point in running 2 parallel umbrella anti-cuts organisations, so the proposal is that SACA be dissolved, in accordance with the constitution, and remaining funds and activities be transferred to the South Yorkshire/Sheffield People's Assembly. This requires a vote at the AGM to dissolve SACA as a distinct organisation. sheffieldanticuts.wordpress.com sypeoplesassembly.org
