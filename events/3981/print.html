TTIP campaigning workshop

Date, Time & Location Wed 11 Mar 2015, 7-9pm,
Details Workshop lead by Guy Taylor, TTIP campaigner for Global Justice Now, on the secret plans for a USA and EU trade deal which could spell disaster in many different ways. The deal is known as the Transatlantic Trade and Investment Partnership, or TTIP. Get up to speed with learning how to lobby MPs/MEPs on TTIP. The workshop will cover the key TTIP issues, then show how to argue points, i.e. ask difficult questions and how to respond. Full details, including venue to be announced. If you would like to attend this workshop, please email globaljusticesheffield@gmail.com. Global Justice Sheffield
