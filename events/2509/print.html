Benefit film and dinner: "And Still They Dance"

Date, Time & Location Sat 23 Feb 2013, 5pm Yemeni Community Centre, 43 Attercliffe Common, S9 2AE
Details Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity Campaign presents a new film, the culmination of 7 years work. In 2005 a group of Palestinian children and young dancers visited Sheffield from Al Aseria Children's Centre, Jabalia refugee camp, Gaza. This is a film of their visit, their current dreams, and the impact on their lives of the Israeli attack in Operation Cast Lead in 2008. It is a moving and powerful film. The food will be fantastic as usual. Tickets £10 waged/£7 concessions. Money raised will go to the children's centres in Gaza. Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity Campaign
