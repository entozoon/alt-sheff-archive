World Development Movement (WDM) campaigning meeting

Date, Time & Location Thu 3 Nov 2011, 7-9pm, Central United Reformed Church, 60 Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details The World Development Movement (WDM) is a UK-based anti-poverty campaigning organisation. This is a preparation meeting to come up with ideas for engaging the public on stalls, focusing on plans for the Peace Fair and the No New Debt campaign. Also preparing notes for stall volunteers and thinking about actions during World Bank Out of Climate Finance Week. WDM Sheffield website
