"Before Now" - Childrens&#39; workshops

Date, Time & Location Wed 18 Feb 2015, 2pm-5pm Greenhill Library, Hemper Lane S8 7FE
Details FREE Art workshops aimed at 8-12 year olds, working with a professional artist. Explore what Sheffield was like before now, and create your own artworks based on our local history. This is an open access session on a first come first serve basis. Maximum of 15 places, so if you would like to book on to secure your place email admin@igniteimaginations.org.uk or call Sheff 2686813. Note if you do not arrive within 15 mins of the start of the workshop, your place may be given away. Children under 8 years must be accompanied by an adult.
