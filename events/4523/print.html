Sheffield Vegan Meet Up

Date, Time & Location Sat 23 Apr 2016, Regather, 57-59 Club Garden Rd. S11 8BU
Details Sheffield Vegan Meet Up is an evening event giving a chance to find out more about a lifestyle completely free of any animal exploitation. Come and make friends, drink and eat vegan food. A different venue is chosen every month, this time it is at the excellent Regather co-operative on Club Garden Road. Full details on the Facebook page Sheffield Vegan Meet Up on Facebook
