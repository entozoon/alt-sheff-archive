CND meeting (Campaign for Nuclear Disarmament)

Date, Time & Location Wed 21 Apr 2010, 7.15pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW (near Anglican cathedral)
Details All welcome to CND's monthly meeting where the main topics for discussion and campaigning on at present are the forthcoming Nuclear Non-proliferation Treaty Meeting in May and the debate 'Keeping The Lights on in Sheffield-is our future nuclear free or nuclear?' at Sheffield Hallam University on Wednesday 28th April at 7pm.
