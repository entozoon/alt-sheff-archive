Book launch: 'Women's Voices'

Date, Time & Location Mon 11 Oct 2010, 1-2.30pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James Street, Sheffield, S1 2EW
Details This new publication has been created by a diverse group of women from different countries and cultures, now living in South Yorkshire. The poems, stories and other pieces, such as recipes for a better world, celebrate the experiences and achievements of the women to produce their writing. Free admission. To book a place email pdaniel@northern.ac.uk
