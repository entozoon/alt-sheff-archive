"This Evil Thing"

Date, Time & Location Fri 1 Dec 2017, 7.30pm, Christ Church (CofE Church) Pitsmoor Rd. S3 9AQ
Details ‘This Evil Thing’ is the engaging and inspiring story of the World War One Conscientious Objector, Conisbrough Methodist minister Bert Brocklesby. Actor and playwright Michael Mears brings his one-man performance to Pitsmoor’s Christ Church. Its writing marked 100 years since conscription was bought in, in 1916. Tickets £6: to book a ticket and pay on arrival, email ingridhhanson@gmail.com michaelmears.org - This Evil Thing
