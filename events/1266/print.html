'We Love Heeley City Farm' Childrens Activity Day

Date, Time & Location Sun 13 Feb 2011, 11am-3pm Heeley City Farm, Richards Road, S2 3DT
Details Come and tell us how much you love Heeley City Farm!! Take part in childrens craft activities in the reception classroom and then treat yourself to some homemade heart shaped cakes and cookies in The Farm Kitchen. Design your own eco bag! Also Family Fuel Buster activities in the Energy House Free admission. Small charge for some of the activities. Heeley City Farm is 20 mins walk from Sheffield, Bus 33 or 47 Heeley City Farm website
