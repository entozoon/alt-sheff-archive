CND meeting (Campaign for Nuclear Disarmament)

Date, Time & Location Wed 17 Mar 2010 at 7.15pm at The Quaker Meeting House, St James Street, Sheffield S1 2EW (near the Anglican cathedral)
Details All welcome to CND's monthly meeting to discuss the latest developments in the struggle against nuclear weapons. Sheffield CND meets on the third Wednesday of each month, except August. On the agenda this month: feedback on the views of candidates for the forthcoming parliamentary elections from the main political parties in Sheffield on the issue of nuclear weapons and planning our campaign against Trident replacement prior to the forthcoming Nuclear Non-Proliferation Treaty conference in May. www.sheffieldcnd.org.uk
