Film screening: Reciprocal Relations

Fri 10 Jun 2016, 6pm, Bloc Projects, 71 Eyre Lane, S1 4RB

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Films on topics ranging from automated language tuition, the coercion of memory and the politics of accelerated living - a neoliberal mode and its associated pressures of performance, speed and wellbeing.Followed at 8pm by Leafcutter JohnMusic and light performance by Leafcutter John, of Polar Bear, performing material from his recent album Resurrection.Part of Sheffield Fringe 2016: Object Documentary. The biennial Sheffield Fringe 2016 is held at Bloc Projects, in Sheffield's Cultural Industries Quarter, coinciding with Sheffield International Documentary Film Festival (Doc/Fest). A nine-day programme of film screenings, talks and performances. Many of the short documentaries and artists' films have very interesting politically and socially engaged content. All events are free and booking is not required. Bloc Projects
