Sheffield Amnesty International meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 12 Jun 2013, 7.30pm at Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Amnesty International is a world wide organisation of nearly 3 million people all working to protect Human Rights. Here in Sheffield meetings are held on the 2nd Wed of every month (except August) to discuss updates from various campaigns. Newcomers welcome. Sheffield Amnesty International website
