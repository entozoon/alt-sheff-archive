Peace in the Park meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 18 Mar 2014, 6pm, Regather Works 57-59 Club Garden Road S11 8BU
Details Organising Sheffield’s annual free Peace in the Park festival for June 2014. Profits go to good causes and it’s a fantastic event. Don’t be shy if you want to get involved or have any ideas, come along to one of the open meetings, these are open to everybody, every two weeks, alternating between Tuesday and Wednesday. The venue is near London Road (see map to the location) Peace in the Park website
