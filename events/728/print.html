Women's international cooking event: 'Come dine with us!'

Date, Time & Location Sat 3 Jul 2010 Highfield Trinity Church Hall, London Road S2 4US
Details Women will cook their own dishes from their different countries and learn, share and enjoy each other's cultures and cuisines. They're also producing a recipe book so that anyone can learn to cook dishes from all the countries represented. Any women from any community are welcome to attend the event!
