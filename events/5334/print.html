Book launch: Biography of Mohammed Taj

Date, Time & Location Fri 5 Oct 2018, 5.30pm, 7 Union St. S1 2JP
Details Launch of a book about Mohammed Taj, the first Asian President of the TUC, a Bradford bus driver and committed anti-racism campaigner. This event includes a new and second-hand book sale. Part of "Vision Sheffield", a 2-week festival of political change, of alternatives, of celebrations of socialist achievements and demands for the many not the few. Organised by The Peoples Assembly and Sheffield Trades Union Council. Further details from sheffieldpeoplesassembly@gmail.com or 07941 332993.
