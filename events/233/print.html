Protest march: Abbeydale Grange School Needs Your Help!

Date, Time & Location Sat 21 Nov 2009, meet 10.30am at Mount Pleasant Park, Sharrow, set off 11am into town for a protest rally at Town Hall
Details Abbeydale Grange School faces closure. The Liberal Democrat Cabinet makes the decision on 9th December. We must Act and Act Now! Join the protest march into town for a protest rally outside the Town Hall with a range of guest speakers. Please bring as many friends and supporters of the school as you can.
