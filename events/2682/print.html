CHESTERFIELD COMMUNITY ARTS FESTIVAL 2013

Date, Time & Location Thu 2 to Mon 6 May 2013
Details A family festival of music, dance, arts, laughter and fun, as Chesterfield presents its first ever Community Arts Festival. Around ninety artists and arts groups have responded to a colourful callout which was circulated across the borough and their contribution is shaping a Festival programme that aims to fill the town with fun throughout the May Bank Holiday Weekend. To find out what is on over the weekend, click on the dates across the top of this web page. www.chesterfieldfestival.co.uk
