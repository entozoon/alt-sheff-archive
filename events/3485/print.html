SHARROW FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sat 21 Jun 2014, Mount Pleasant Park, Sharrow Lane, nr London Rd. S11 8AE
Details Sharrow Festival is a great annual multicultural community festival bringing people together with a focus on creativity and participation. Local groups and businesses, great music, a worldwide variety of food.
