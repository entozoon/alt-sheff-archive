Same Gender Tango: taster session

Date, Time & Location Sun 24 Jun 2012, 1pm-4pm, Affinity Bar, Arundel Gate (next to T J Hughes)
Details For those who are irresistibly attracted to tango, but not to the gender stereotypes that are imposed in some tango salons ... For ladies that lead, fellas that follow and all other dancing combinations you can imagine .... Teacher Lora Hudson brings to Sheffield Argentinian tango freed from gender role restrictions. Come and join in for this taster session. If there is enough interest by the participants in the taster session, Lora is willing to set up a class in Sheffield, so if you would like to attend a regular class but cannot make it to the taster session, please get in touch.
