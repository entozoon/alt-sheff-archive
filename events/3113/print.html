Peace in the Park pub quiz fundraiser

Date, Time & Location Mon 25 Nov 2013, 9.30pm Nottingham House pub, 164 Whitham Rd. Broomhill S10 2SR
Details Each month there is a Peace in the Park pub quiz in a different pub across Sheffield, raising money for next year's festival. This month it will be happening at the Nottingham House. If you live in the area, why not go along and give some spare cash to the festival? Thanks! Peace in the Park
