Composting Bonanza Day @ Heeley City Farm

Date, Time & Location Sat 11 May 10am-4pm, Heeley City Farm, Richards Road, S2 3DT
Details Big compost day - join Matt and Tony the composting gurus for advice on home composting. Learn how to recycle your food waste and save it from landfill and make your own top quality compost. They will have special offers on compost and manure. 45l bags for £3 or 4 for £10, well rotted manure £2.50 for 60l bag. Composting bins and water butts also available to buy. All the money generated from sales is used to fund the farm's many projects. Phone Sheff 2580482 for more details. Heeley City Farm is 20 mins walk from Sheffield, Bus 33 or 47.
