Tool Bank (Green City Action)

Sat 1 Mar 2014, 10am-12pm, Abbeyfield House, Abbeyfield Park, Abbeyfield Rd. S4 7AT

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Affordable Tools for your Tasks! Green City Action's Tool Bank has gardening, painting and decorating, plastering, general woodworking plus shelf and wall fixing tools.Residents in S3, S4 & S5 are asked to help developing the Tool Bank by saying what you do and don't want from the service. Please spare 10 minutes to fill in an anonymous questionnaire before 10th March, and to say thanks you can enter a free prize draw with chance to win a £30 gift voucher for a shop of your choice, 1 week's free tool hire (worth upto £30), a solar powered security light (worth £25-£30), a £5 Asda gift voucher or Sproutapouch window microsalad growing kit. The questionnaire is online at http://tiny.cc/osfs3w or you can call in to the Tool Bank whilst it is open.Part week tool hire costs £1-£15 depending on tool type. You have to pay a deposit of £5-£20. The deposit is returned in full when the tool is returned on time and in good condition.More information: www.GreenCityAction.org.uk Facebook: www.facebook.com/GreenCityActionCall Sheff 2440353 or email: Sarah@GreenCityAction.org.uk
