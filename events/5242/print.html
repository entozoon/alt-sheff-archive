The Mayoral Election: Devolution or Government Dictat?

Date, Time & Location Tue 24 Apr 2018, 7pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James' St. S1
Details In May, Sheffield City Region will elect a mayor, and Sheffield for Democracy is hosting a hustings event to introduce the candidates and their views to voters. Come and find out why they are standing and their plans for the city region. Hear their takes on the recent differences between the four local authorities - Sheffield, Rotherham, Barnsley and Doncaster - and how they can be resolved.
