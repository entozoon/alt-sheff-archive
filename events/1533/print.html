South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG) meeting.

Date, Time & Location Tue 12 Jul 2011, 6.30pm, Scotia Works, Leadmill Road, Sheffield, S1 4SE
Details SYMAAG meeting featuring "Arab Spring/European Winter", a discussion of how the fight against dictatorships in the Arab world relates to the barring of asylum seekers from these countries by the states of "Fortress Europe". There will be a speaker from the Yemeni community. Also under discussion is the significance of the ratification of the Convention for Decent Work for Domestic Workers at the International Labour Conference (despite the refusal of the UK Government to support it) Contact SYMAAG at dignitynotdetention@yahoo.co.uk or via Sheff 2412780
