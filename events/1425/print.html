Sheffield Equality Group meeting

Date, Time & Location Thu 14 Apr 2011, 7pm at Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Sheffield Equality Group meetings aim to be an open space where people can discuss and plan actions around income equality. This month, a discussion on UK Uncut actions in Sheffield, future events, and what is 'a fair start' in life. Philosopher John Rawls asks us to imagine a world where wealth could be shared out according to a principle of justice. What should be the difference between those who have the most, and the least?
