Sheffield Social Centre Collective meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 3 Oct 2011, 7pm The Rutland Arms, 86 Brown St. S1 2BS
Details Sheffield Social Centre is a collective for libertarian DIY space in the city, a project open to all who share its core principles of non-hierarchy, anti-capitalism and equality/non-discrimination.
