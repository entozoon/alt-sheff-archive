Women, Work and Trade Unions.

Date, Time & Location Mon 16 May 2011, 7.15pm at Sheffield Quaker Meeting House, St James Street S1 2EW
Details Sheffield Feminist Network Women's Group meeting. All women are welcome to this talk by Josie Pasek, Unison shop steward and trade unionist, talking about the disproportionate effect of cuts on women's lives and on women's involvement in Trade Unions and in local politics.
