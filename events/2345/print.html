Anti-EDL demonstration, Rotherham

Date, Time & Location Sat 13 Oct 2012, All Saints Square, central Rotherham
Details Unite Against Fascism are calling a peaceful multicultural rally of unity in opposition to the racist supporters of the English Defence League due to arrive in Rotherham. All welcome, to defend multicultural Rotherham. Bus from Sheffield in solidarity with the people of Rotherham leaves at 11am, tickets £6 waged/£3 unwaged, tel 07545 197939 to book your seat. Leafleting of Rotherham town centre begins at a meeting outside the market at 11am. Email info@sheffielduaf.org.uk if you want leaflets to distribute or collect them at the public meeting.
