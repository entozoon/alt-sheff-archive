Does Solidarity With Refugees Matter?

Date, Time & Location Wed 21 Jun 2017, 6-8pm, Roco Creative Co-op, 338-346 Glossop Rd. S10 2HW
Details What does solidarity mean in the context of the refugee crisis? Does it prompt action or is it a token word that disguises ever more closed borders? This free event, organised by White Rose Research network, opens discussions on the motivations, meanings and consequences of solidarity with refugees. Part of Festival of Debate, hosted by not-for-profit social enterprise Opus Independents (which also publishes a monthly Alt-Sheff column in Now Then magazine). Festival of Debate
