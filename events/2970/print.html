Chile 40 Years On: Una Autora (II)

Date, Time & Location Tue 22 Oct 2013, 7pm, Portobello Building, University of Sheffield.
Details 40 years ago a military coup plunged Chile into a brutal right-wing dictatorship, the first country in the world to experience the shock of "neoliberalism". Many fled the country, some arriving here to make Sheffield their new home. Now, in commemoration of the anniversary, there is a series of events including this, part of Off the Shelf Festival, an evening with Chilean poetry, short story and novel writer Carmen Rodríguez (from Vancouver, Canadá).
