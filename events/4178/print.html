NETHER EDGE FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sat 5 - Sun 13 Sep 2015
Details 2015: For the first time ever, Nether Edge holds its own festival. Includes cycling, dancing, talks, street food and much more. It all starts at 1.30pm on Saturday 5th, with an up-cycling and repair cafe event at Common Ground, on Machon Bank. Ending with the Farmers' Market on Sunday 13th, which is to donate profits that day to Cavendish Care and Baby Basics. The full programme of events can be downloaded from the website below, or pick up a paper copy at local shops. Organised by a volunteer committee of local people and small businesses, supported by Nether Edge Neighbourhood Group and Nether Edge Farmers' Market. Many events are free, some require pre-booking.
