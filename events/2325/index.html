Our House? Park Hill Flats & the Housing Crisis (walk and debate)

Wed 17 Oct 2012, 6pm Sheaf Valley Park (amphitheatre behind railway station), S1

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

We desperately need more and better housing. Park Hill once showed the way. Discover why that was, in an informal walking tour of the flats followed by a debate on where we go from here. With Satwinder Samra from University of Sheffield and Sauce Architecture and James Heartfield, author of Let's Build.Part of Off the Shelf, Sheffield's 21-year old annual festival of words, with a great programme of over 200 events this year. Between 13th October and 3rd November 2012, there is everything from community events to readings to workshops, at various venues. Admission free. To book visit www.sheffieldsalon.org.uk www.offtheshelf.org.uk
