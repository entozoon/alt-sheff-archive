The Social Progressivity Index

Date, Time & Location Sat 30 Apr 2016, 5-6.30pm Lecture Theatre 3, The Diamond, 32 Leavygreave Rd. S3 7RD
Details A talk by Michael Green, author of "Philanthrocapitalism", CEO of the Social Progress Imperative, and part of the team that has created the Social Progress Index. This is a standard to rank societies based on how they meet the needs of citizens. Event in association with the Alternative Economic Society, part of Festival of Debate 2016, hosted by Opus Independents which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine.
