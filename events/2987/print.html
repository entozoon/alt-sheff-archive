What the Frack is Happening in Donny?

Date, Time & Location Wed 11 Sep 2013, 7.30pm, Doncaster
Details Public meeting about the toxic fracking planned for Doncaster. Come and find out the plans - and how to fight back. Venue: Doncaster Central Development Trust, Church View Centre, Church View, Doncaster DN1 1AF (the old Art College, next to St. Georges, the minster church) Frack Free South Yorkshire has a good website with plenty of information; leaflet for this event at http://www.frackfreesouthyorkshire.co.uk/resources.html, click on "donny_fracking_flyer_double_sided_2.jpg"
