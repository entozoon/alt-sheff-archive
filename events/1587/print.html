Nagasaki Day Peace Picnic

Date, Time & Location Sun 7 Aug 2011, 2pm to 5pm, Japanese Peace Gardens in Meersbrook Park, off A61 Chesterfield Rd. S8 9FL
Details Picnic and entertainment, commemorating the victims of the US nuclear bomb attack on the Japanese city of Nagasaki. The event includes a poetry reading by River Wolton, poet laureate for Derbyshire. The Lord Mayor will also be attending. Sheffield CND website
