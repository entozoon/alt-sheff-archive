Growing Sheffield&#39;s Local Food Economy

Date, Time & Location Thu 13 Sep 2012, 12.30-4.30pm, Sheffield Hallam University, Level 12, Owen Building
Details On the eve of the 3rd Sheffield Food Festival, a free networking event, on how to strengthen and grow the local food economy. Growers, producers, private, public and voluntary sectors are invited to bring views about how to sustain and build on the food economy for the future. This is an opportunity to explore key issues including local food production, local procurement, connecting local interests across the food supply chain, sustainability, health and wellbeing and environmental impacts.
