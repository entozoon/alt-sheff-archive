Reading Group: "Caliban and the Witch"

Date, Time & Location Wed 26 Jul 2017, 7.30pm, The Bath Hotel, 66 Victoria St. S3 7QL
Details Meeting of a left-wing reading group reading "Caliban and the Witch: Women, the Body and Primitive Accumulation" by Silvia Federici. This is an informal discussion over drinks, which will cover chapters 1 and 2. The meeting is in the small side-room to the right of the pub. All welcome including newcomers, no need to book.
