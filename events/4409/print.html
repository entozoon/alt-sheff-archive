CycleSheffield meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 7 Mar 2016, 7pm, Heeley Development Trust, Ash Tree Yard, 62-68 Thirlwell Rd. S8 9TF
Details The monthly meeting of CycleSheffield. This group is for all kinds of cyclists; it organises events, information and rides, and campaigns for cycling to be taken seriously as a means of transport, with the facilities it needs. Tea and coffee etc available. Meetings are usually followed by a social in a nearby pub. www.cyclesheffield.org.uk
