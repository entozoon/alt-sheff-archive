Amnesty International: David Price "Welcome to Sheffield"

Date, Time & Location Wed 9 Jan 2019, 7pm, Central United Reformed Church, 60 Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details Sheffield Amnesty International presents David Price, local historian and author of "Welcome to Sheffield - A Migration History". David will show how Sheffield has become cosmopolitan with more than one fifth of the population being of ethnic minority heritage. He will discuss why so many diverse ethnic groups have come here, how they have been received and what are the implications for the city. Sheffield Amnesty Internationa
