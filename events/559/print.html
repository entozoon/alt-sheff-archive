World Day for Animals in Laboratories

Date, Time & Location Sat 24 Apr 2010 (London - Coach trip from here)
Details Campaigners from across the country, as well as many from overseas, will be gathering in London to mark World Day for Animals in Laboratories. Sheffield Animal Friends are organising a coach, so please get in touch ASAP if you'd like a place. Tickets for the coach cost £15 (£10 if you really can't afford that, although any extra donations to cover the cost of the coach would be much appreciated). Pick up at 8.15am at Meadowhall coach station.
