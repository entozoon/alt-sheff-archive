Cycle display and filmshow

Date, Time & Location Sat 5 Apr 2014
Details 3pm in front of the train station: Danny Butler’s Extreme Mountain Bike Stunt Show 4pm-6pm in the Amphitheatre above Sheffield Rail Station: Cycling filmshow: - Velorama – a new, specially-commissioned film created from stunning BFI archive film about a Century of the Bicycle. - 7 Cogs of Sheffield and North – two new films by Sheffield film-makers about the city’s vibrant mountain-bike community. - From the Ground Up – about bespoke British bike builders BTR fabrications. Part of the celebrations ahead of the Grand Depart of the Tour de France in Yorkshire. Free entry. Full details at the link below Sheffield Adventure Film Festival
