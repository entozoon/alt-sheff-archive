Open Meeting: What do you want from a resource centre / social centre?

Date, Time & Location Wed 27 Jun 2012, 7.30pm, Red Deer pub (upstairs), 18 Pitt St. S1 4DD
Details Call out to all groups and individuals to an open meeting on what is wanted from a Sheffield radical, non-hierarchical resource centre or social centre. Sheffield Social Centre group intends to rent or buy premises and invites other groups looking for space to discuss possibilities, ideas and suggestions on working together for this. www.sheffieldsocialcentre.org.uk
