Meersbrook Tree Day

Date, Time & Location Fri 1 Nov 2013, 10.30-3.30 in Meersbrook Park
Details The council Community Forestry team will be running the fourth Meersbrook Tree Day in Meersbrook Park, supported by Trees for Cities. Fantastic Half Term Event for all the family. Come along and take part in an interesting fun-filled day of activities based around the park's trees Help plant trees and maintain the parks woodland. More details at Heeley Online
