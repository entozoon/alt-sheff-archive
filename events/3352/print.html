Orgreave Truth and Justice demonstration (Wakefield)

Date, Time & Location Fri 28 Mar 2014, (coaches from South Yorks)
Details In the struggle for the truth about what happened at Orgreave during the 1980s miners strike, this is the second demo at the Wakefield office of the Independent Police Complaints Commission (IPCC), Pioneer House, Woolpacks Yard, Wakefield, about the length of time it is taking for the IPCC to decide on an investigation into the criminal activities of the South Yorkshire Police. Free coaches are running from Sheffield Showroom cinema 11am, Barnsley White Bear 11.45am and Rotherham Silverwood Miners Welfare 11.30am and SCALA at 11.45am. Please email orgreavejustice@hotmail.com to reserve your seat stating which coach you would like a seat on. Facebook event page
