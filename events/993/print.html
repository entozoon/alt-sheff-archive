Play: 'On A Clear Day You Can See Dover'

Date, Time & Location Fri 8 Oct 2010, 7.45pm, Crucible Theatre
Details A new play from Ice and Fire Theatre Company. Voices from the front line of Europe's asylum system, set against the background of the growing humanitarian crisis concerning destitute migrants in Calais. 'On a Clear Day You Can See Dover' weaves together factual information and testimonies gathered from interviews with Calais migrants seeking to enter the UK. Tickets £7/£5 with limited number of free tickets for unwaged. For more information contact actorsnorth@iceandfire.co.uk. www.iceandfire.co.uk
