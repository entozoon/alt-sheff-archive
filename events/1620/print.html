'Software Freedom Day' 2011

Date, Time & Location Sat 17 Sep 2011, 11am-4pm, Sheffield Railway Station entrance area
Details Sheffield's Access Space joins the annual worldwide celebration of 'Software Freedom', demonstrating why Linux is far more wonderful than Microsoft and giving away free software CDs. Access Space itself (nearby at the junction of Brown St. and Sidney St.) will also be hosting a celebration with refreshments, offering help in choosing and using free software. Meet free software developers, and learn about the help and support available. www.access-space.org www.softwarefreedomday.org
