Black Rose Reading Group: Revolt and crisis in Greece

Date, Time & Location Tue 26 Jun 2012, 7.30-9.30pm Black Rose Centre 268 Verdon St. Burngreave S3 9QS
Details Discussion group this month covering the collection Revolt and Crisis in Greece (2011) in particular Chapter 14: An Economy that excludes the Many and an "Accidental" Revolt, Yiannis Kaplanis. They may also look at chapters 13 and 15. Everyone welcome, feel free to read more or less, or bring along any good articles / short pieces for future discussions. The reading are available online to download as pdf files at http://libcom.org/files/92827843-Occupied-London-Revolt-and-Crisis-in-Greece%20(1).pdf http://sheffieldsocialcentre.org.uk/black-rose-reading-group-4
