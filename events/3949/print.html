Sheffield Freethought Convention

Date, Time & Location 27 Feb - 1 Mar 2015, Mappin Hall, Mappin Street
Details The University of Sheffield Secular and Atheist Society invites you to the first ever Sheffield Freethought Convention. It will be covering all things related to freethought, humanism and secularism, with an overarching theme of human rights, especially in relation to blasphemy and apostasy laws and freedom of religion.
