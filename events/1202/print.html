Paths to Climate Justice

Date, Time & Location Mon 25 Jan 2011, from 6.30pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Feedback from the Copenhagen climate summit, by Sheffield activists who were there. Positive stories of protest and direct action, developing a deeper understanding of climate justice, earth rights, resilient communities in Sheffield, working in solidarity with people from the global south. 6.30pm: Refreshments photos, videos, and info 7.00pm: Presentations and questions 8.15pm: Way forward - smaller group discussion until 9.15pm
