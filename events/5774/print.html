Sheffield Esteli Ceilidih Event

Date, Time & Location Sun 17 Nov 2019, 7pm, Broomhall Centre, Broomspring Lane S10 2FD
Details Sheffield Estelí Society present the fabulous Home Grown Ceilidh! Live music, fun social dancing, no experience necessary. A fundraiser for the Sheffield Estelí Society, keeping alive our Sheffield link with Nicaragua. Tickets: £7 advance, £8 on the door, £6 concessions. Under 16s free. Tickets from: www.welldressedband.org.uk. Please bring food to share. A bar will be provided Sheffield Estelí Society
