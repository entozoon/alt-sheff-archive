Talk: Crossing borders and health

Date, Time & Location Fri 8 Mar 2013, 5pm-6.30pm, Lecture Theatre 5, University of Sheffield Arts Tower, Western Bank S10 2TN
Details City of Sanctuary Sheffield are teaming up with Sheffield University Crossing Borders group to give this talk on the impact of migration on health, with Professor Laura Tabili. Crossing Borders is an international network of students whose mission is to remove barriers to healthcare for refugees, asylum seekers and undocumented migrants. City of Sanctuary Sheffield
