Sheffield Renewables Intro Night

Date, Time & Location Wed 15 Jul 2009, 8.30pm to 10pm upstairs at Red Deer pub, 18 Pitt Street, Sheffield S1 4DD
Details All are warmly invited to come along to this intro night, a chance to learn about what Sheffield Renewables is up to and to find out about volunteering opportuntities for this exciting, practical project. They could particularly do with recruiting a few people interested in communications, digital media and organising local events. No need to register in advance, just come along. It'll be a relaxed intro - you won't be put under any pressure to commit to things on the night. www.sheffieldrenewables.org.uk
