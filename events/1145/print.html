Theatre: 'Here Be Dragons'

Date, Time & Location Thu 25 - Sat 27 Nov 2010, The Venue, Manchester Rd, Stocksbridge (formerly The Victory Club)
Details The story of Stocksbridge, one of Sheffield's steel industry areas, told from people's own experience. (The Dragon in the title refers mostly to the might and heat of the furnaces, the beast that Men of Steel can tame). The play is all written by local people, and subtitled 'Stocksbridge, the Musical'. It's produced by Steel Valley Beacon Arts, with an impressive cast of 50! Performances Thursday, Friday and Saturday (25th 26th and 27th) at 7.30pm Plus a matinee on Saturday at 2.30pm Tickets £8 and £5 concessions available at the door or from the Venue Sheff 2838692.
