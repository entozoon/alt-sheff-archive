"Does Art Follow the Money? "

Date, Time & Location Sat 22 Nov 2014, 10.45-2.45 Carpenter Room, Sheffield Central Library
Details Sheffield Visual Arts Group is hosting its third annual study day, on the future of the funding of the visual arts in Sheffield. Speakers include Peter Massey of the Arts Council in Yorkshire, MP Paul Blomfield, Kim Streets and John Cowling of Museums Sheffield Book a place by emailing sheffvisarts@gmail.com Admission is free but they are asking for donations to cover costs.
