SHEFFIELD FAYRE

Date, Time & Location Sun 30 - Mon 31 Aug 2015, 10.30-5.30pm, Norfolk Heritage Park, Guildford Ave. S2 2PL
Details Sheffield Fayre is held in Norfolk Heritage Park every August bank holiday Sunday and Monday. Something for everyone: crafts, trade stands, Sheffield Horticulture Show, market stalls and traditional fairground attractions. This year promises the one and only World Famous ‘Ken Fox Wall of Death’. Organised by Sheffield City Council in partnership with The Friends of Norfolk Heritage Park Friends of Norfolk Heritage Park
