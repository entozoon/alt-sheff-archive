"Are We Facing Extinction? What Can We Do About It?"

Date, Time & Location Mon 21 Jan 2019, 7.30pm, Victoria Methodist Church, Stafford Rd. S2 2SE
Details An introductory talk outlining what Extinction Rebellion is and why we need to rebel. The basic premise of this talk is to tell the truth and ask us all to act accordingly and consistently with the information, including our understanding of what actually enables change to happen in the world. Please also invite friends and family not currently involved in extinction rebellion to attend. Facebook event page Extinction Rebellion Sheffield Facebook group
