Gender inequalities and fairness for women: Open meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 12 Jun 2012, 3pm to 5pm, Sorby House, Spital Hill, Burngreave
Details An open meeting to discuss gender inequalities and fairness for women. The results of the meeting will be provided to the Sheffield Fairness Commission and will be considered when they make their recommendations for action in a few month's time.
