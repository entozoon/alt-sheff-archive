Save Sheffield Sure Start Children's Centres

Date, Time & Location Sat 19 Feb 2011, 10.30-11.30am at Highfield Library, London Road
Details Sure Start Centres are under threat of closure and budget cuts. In Sheffield the Council may cut funding to all Sure Starts by 15% when they set the new budget on 4th March. This is a chance to talk with councillors Jillian Creasy and Robert Murphy (Green Party Central Ward) about the proposed cuts. Later (see Wed 23rd) there is a march and picnic, and town centre stalls continue (see Saturdays). Phone: 07415 428696 Email: defendoursurestart@gmail.com Facebook group: Defend Sheffield Surestart Childrens' Centres PLEASE sign the online petition on Sheffield City Council's website (if 5000 people sign the Council will have to have a full debate about cuts to Sure Start on 2nd March (two days before the budget is set). Lots of people have signed paper copies of the petition already but they need a lot more.
