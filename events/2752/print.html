Palestine Solidarity June boycott action

Date, Time & Location Sat 1 Jun 2013, from 12 noon
Details The next Boycott action by Sheffield Palestine Solidarity will be continuing the Sainsburys campaign. Meeting outside the University Sainsburys on Hanover Way at 12 noon and then walking with banner and placards to the Sainsburys at the bottom of London Road. They will be collecting signatures for a petition asking Sainsburys not to deal with any companies supporting Israeli Occupation, which will be handed in at the Sainsburys AGM in London in July. Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign
