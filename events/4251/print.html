March to Stop Tree Felling

Date, Time & Location Sat 14 Nov 2015, 11am Sheffield City Hall
Details Meeting in front of Sheffield City Hall steps, and marching in protest at the continued unpopular, unsustainable and unnecessary tree felling under the Amey contract. Gather at the Town Hall steps to hear speakers before forming a circle of green around the Town Hall, to say ENOUGH IS ENOUGH! Facebook event page
