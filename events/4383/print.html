Show: "Trouser-Wearing Characters"

Date, Time & Location Fri 26 Feb 2016, doors 6.30pm; show 7pm, Carpenter Room, Sheffield City Library, Surrey St. S1 2LG
Details Rose Collis brings her acclaimed one-woman musical cabaret show to Sheffield as part of an Arts Council-funded tour for LGBT History Month 2016. A mixture of music, history and comedy. It combines stories, vignettes and songs about some of her favourite, eclectic LGBT ‘trouser-wearing characters’. Followed by informal Q&A session with Rose Collis and opportunities to buy books by her. Tickets £5 plus booking fee. Ticketsource
