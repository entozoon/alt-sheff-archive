Sheffield Renewables Christmas drink

Date, Time & Location Wed 15 Dec 2010, from 8.30 pm downstairs at the Fat Cat, Alma Street
Details Come along for a drink and a chat with one of Sheffield's most active groups, which is doing something environmentally practical. Plans are in hand for water-powered electricity production at Jordan Dam on the River Don, and at Kelham Island, by this not-for-profit social enterprise co-operative.
