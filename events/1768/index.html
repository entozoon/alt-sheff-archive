Cinema Palestino

25-27 Nov 2011, at Sheffield Showroom Cinema

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Sheffield's 3rd annual Cinema Palestino Film Festival with the very best of films from and about Palestine. Some showings include directors on hand to introduce their films and take any questions that may arise. The Showroom is working with the Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Group on this festival.See all the films in the season for just £25 (£20 concessions). Tickets for individual films can be purchased.www.showroomworkstation.org.uk/cinemapalestinoSheffield Palestine Solidarity Group
