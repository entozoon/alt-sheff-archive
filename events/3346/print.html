Free film: Tax Evasion

Date, Time & Location Fri 28 Mar 2014, 7pm to 10pm, St James Church in Clifton, Rotherham
Details A thought-provoking film on the controversial theme of Tax Evasion. Free viewing for anyone who is interested, and so are the refreshments that will be made available by the church. If you or your friends wish to learn more about what effects tax evasion have, this film is for you.
