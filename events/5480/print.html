Extinction Rebellion - Climate Emergency Truth Protest at BBC

Date, Time & Location Fri 21 Dec 2018, 8am-9.30am, outside BBC Radio Sheffield, 54 Shoreham St. S1 4RS
Details An Extinction Rebellion protest outside BBC Radio Sheffield to demand that our government and media communicate the truth about the climate and wider ecological emergency, to raise the alarm, standing up to hold our media to account. There will be similar protests in London, Manchester and Bristol at the same time. Extinction Rebellion protest, mourning and mass peaceful non-violent direct action continues throughout the country. Radio Sheffield is based in the city centre close to the railway station, on a road very busy during rush hour. Bring drums, pots and pans if you can. Full event details and demands on Extinction Rebellion website Facebook event page
