Peace in the Park: The Festivals Ball

Date, Time & Location Fri 11 Apr 2014, 10pm to 3am, The City Hall Ballroom
Details This year, Sheffield will experience its first ever Festivals Ball, in support of Sharrow Festival and Peace In The Park! The must-go-to event of 2014 with the mighty JUNGLE LION & BISON performing, and loads more acts. Start dusting down your ball gowns and get your tickets early. It's going to be a ball. Tickets will be £7 advance, more on the door, with all profits going to Peace In The Park and Sharrow Festival. 100 Earlybird tickets for a mere £6 at the following friendly venues: Airy Fairy, Golden Harvest, The Cremorne, The Fox & Duck, The West End, The Nottingham House and The Hallamshire House or from Papa Al. www.peaceinthepark.org.uk Sharrow Festival
