Ghana farmers vs. global corporations

Date, Time & Location Wed 18 November, 7pm, Central United Reformed Church, 60 Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details Global Justice Now campaigner Heidi Chow will show two short films and talk about Ghana farmers' campaign to prevent their government from pushing through pro-corporate seed regulations, and show some encouraging examples their of agro-ecological farming practices.
