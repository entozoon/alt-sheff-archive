Education under occupation: schools and universities in Palestine today

Date, Time & Location Wed 6 Apr 2016, 5-7pm Sheffield Hallam University, Room 1028 Owen Building S1 1WB
Details Sheffield Palestine Education Network invites you to a special meeting where Kirsty Wright will speak about the impact of occupation on education. Kirsty has supported humanitarian and recovery programmes with the liberation movements in Eritrea and Ethiopia and with refugees in the Sudan. She has lived and worked in Palestine for 26 years, currently working as a development researcher with a local Palestinian company. She has witnessed the profound social and political changes resulting from the Oslo process and their impacts on all aspects of the daily lives of Palestinians. Kirsty recently completed research on Israeli attacks on the Palestinian education system in general, and on schools and universities in particular, and will report and discuss her research findings.
