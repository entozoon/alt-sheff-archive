Sanctuary Seekers & Health Care Conference (Bradford)

Date, Time & Location Fri 20 Sep 2013, 9:30am-4pm in Bradford
Details Not a Sheffield event, but this is a national conference looking at issues around sanctuary and health at Bradford University, organised by City of Sanctuary and partners, bringing together health commissioners, professionals, service users, 3rd sector workers and others to consider how to address inequalities in this important field. For further information and booking details please contact: events@bradford.ac.uk or tel: 01274 233217.
