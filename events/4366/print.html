Women of Palestine: Living through Trauma, Building Resilience

Date, Time & Location Tue 8 Mar 2016, 7-11pm, St. Andrews Hall, Upper Hanover St. S3 7RQ
Details A special event on International Women’s Day, organised by Sheffield Palestine Women’s Scholarship Fund. Come and explore the realities of daily life for Palestinian women and learn more about their amazing resilience and resistance against the odds. This event is free, please note it is for women only. Full details on the link below. Speakers: Arwa Haikal, from Hebron and Sheffield Kholoud al Ajarma, from Aida Refugee Camp, Bethlehem Kholood Ersheid, from Nazareth Film screening: A new film from The Freedom Theatre of Jenin, who recently toured the UK to great acclaim. Disco: 9pm – A chance to dance and enjoy Claudia’s fabulous disco at the end! Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign
