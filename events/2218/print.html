Sheffield Humanist Society meeting: "A look at current events from a Humanist perspective"

Date, Time & Location Tue 4 Sep 2012, 8pm The Showroom, Paternoster Row, Sheffield, S1 2BX
Details Who knows what this meeting will be covering? Another announcement of the end of the world? More faith and creationist schools? Outrageous happenings in a local church? Bishops in the Lords? The Pope converts to Islam? Gay marriage? Sheffield Humanist Society does not pretend to be able to predict the future or know what the current events will be. So come along to hear about and discuss the news from a Humanist point of view. www.sheffieldhumanists.org.uk
