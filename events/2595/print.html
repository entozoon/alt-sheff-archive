Nicaragua and Sheffield: A Latin American Perspective

Date, Time & Location Tue 12 Mar 2013, 5.30pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St. James St., S1 2EW
Details Students For Estelí, Sheffield’s twin town in Nicaragua, and the Sheffield-Estelí Society are delighted to invite you to this public meeting to hear Guisell Morales-Echaverry, Chargée d’Affaires at the Nicaraguan Embassy, speak about the impact of town twinning and cooperation, and Nicaragua’s current position within the framework of ALBA, an alternative economic model for progressive Latin American nations.
