"The politics of resentment and the inequality spiral"

Date, Time & Location Wed 1 May 2013, 7pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details An informal talk and discussion subtitled "Can we win the argument for a strong welfare system?" It will cover recent research on attitudes to social support and benefits. Also on the agenda, actions arising from the meeting on social support. Hosted by Sheffield Equality Group, an autonomous group affiliated to the national Equality Trust, which meets on the first Wednesday of every month. All welcome Sheffield Equality Group
