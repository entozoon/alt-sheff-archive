Fundraiser: The Songs of Nick Drake

Date, Time & Location Mon 8 Jun 2015, 7.15pm, Hagglers Corner, Queens Road S2 4DU
Details Singer and guitarist Keith James presents his stunningly beautiful act, The Songs of Nick Drake. Since 2001 he has taken this immaculate and intriguingly fragile music to theatres across the UK and toured to over 1000 venues across Ireland, Holland, France, Italy and Spain, including all of Nick’s Schools and Colleges, and even Glastonbury Festival. Tickets £8 plus fee, from www.wegottickets.com Part of Sharrow Festival Fringe of activities and exhibitions in and around Sharrow leading up to the festival on 13th June (details below).
