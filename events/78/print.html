Naming of the Dead ceremony

Date, Time & Location Sat 22 Aug 2009, 11am at Sheffield Town Hall steps
Details The grim milestone of 200 British soldiers killed in Afghanistan has been passed. A naming of the dead ceremony will be held this Saturday where the 200 soldiers' names along with names of 200 of the thousands of Afghanis who have died as a result of the conflict. Please come along, support this event and help highlight the growing human cost of an unwinnable and unjustified war. Spread the word as widely as possible. Sheffield Stop the War Coalition
