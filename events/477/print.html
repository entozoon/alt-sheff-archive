Sheffield NO2ID meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 31 Mar 2010, 7.30pm at The Harlequin Pub, 108 Nursery St, Sheffield S3 8GG
Details Campaigning against the threat to liberty and privacy posed by the rapid growth of the database state, including ID cards, NO2ID is independent of party politics. It publicises the case against state identity management among the general public, the media, and the government. Meetings are public and are open for anyone to attend. If you want to get involved with the campaign or just want to find out more about the issues come along. The meeting room is upstairs. If you can't find it please ask the bar staff. Sheffield NO2ID website: http://no2id-sheffield.net
