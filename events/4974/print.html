International Permaculture Day

Date, Time & Location Sun 7 May 2017, 12-3pm, Burngreave Cemetery
Details The Friends of Burngreave Cemetery invite you to join them for a permaculture get-together, to share lunch, find out what permaculture projects are happening locally and to visit the adventure playground and possibly Fireside Housing Co-op, both next door. If you’re not involved in Permaculture, come and find out about it. If you are, come and tell everyone what you’re up to. Soup, drinks and biscuits provided. Bring extra food to share if you wish. The friends group have a display about the flora and fauna within the cemetery, art installations by artist in residence Vicky, and a library of permaculture books for reference. All are welcome. The entrance to the cemetery is at the junction of Melrose Road and Burngreave Street, S3 9DN. Buses - 1, 20, 75, 76, 88, 256 on Burngreave Road; 3, 83, 83a, on Pitsmoor Road and 97, 98 on Scott Road.
