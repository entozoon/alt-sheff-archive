Talk: Desistance and Recovery

Date, Time & Location Thu 10 Mar 2016, 9am-4pm, Showroom Workstation, Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details The aim of this event is to showcase some of the research being carried out at Sheffield Hallam University in conjunction with Sheffield Addiction Recovery Research Group (SARRG). Part of Social Justice Week, a joint initiative between the Sheffield Hallam University's Helena Kennedy Centre for International Justice and the Department of Humanities, offering a range of events. A diverse range of agencies, individuals and organisations linked by commitment to the principles of social justice come together annually to disseminate what they have achieved and to re-affirm and build on these shared values and public commitments. The week includes a range of presentations, discussions, events and exhibitions that will engage our communities in understanding what they do around social justice, aiming to inspire participation and engagement in future events.
