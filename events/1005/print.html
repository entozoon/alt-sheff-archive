Equality Trust discussion

Date, Time & Location Fri 1 Oct 2010
Details Bill Kerry (co-founder of the Equality Trust) and Terence Birmingham (local groups co-ordinator of the Equality Trust) are coming to Sheffield to discuss how the national Equality Trust can support local groups. Meet in the entrance of the Millennium Gallery off Arundel Gate at 3pm. Please come if you are curious about the work of the national Equality Trust and/or have any views about how they could support local groups. If you can't attend but do want to feed in your ideas, phone Jason on 07956 384142. Sheffield Equality Group website
