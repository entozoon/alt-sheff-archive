"Earth Matters" 5 rhythms benefit for climate change

Date, Time & Location Sat 6 Dec 2014, Sharrow Performing Arts Space
Details Come and dance a day workshop in 5 Rhythms, at the time of year when shops are busily bustling. Instead, take some time to land more deeply in your body and to connect with something greater, dance with Earth, Fire, Water, Air as we are all woven into this web of life together on this amazing complex planet. All profit goes to Sheffield Climate Alliance, to raise money for work with Climate Change. With Sarah Blagg, Dance and Yoga Teacher, Artist.
