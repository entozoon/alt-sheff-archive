Ceilidh dance: Sheffield Climate Alliance fundraiser

Date, Time & Location Sun 17 Nov 2013, 7.30pm Broomhall Centre, Broomspring Lane S10 2FB
Details Home-Grown Ceilidh with The Well-Dressed Band in support of Sheffield Climate Alliance, an alliance of local organisations and individuals who are pressing for effective action to tackle climate change. Come along and meet other like-minded folk. Tickets £5 in advance/£6 on the door. Bring food to share, and your preferred drink (there is no bar).
