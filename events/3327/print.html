The Co-operative Revolution, Meet the Author - political cartoonist Polyp

Date, Time & Location Tue 25 Mar 2014, 7pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details A graphic novel, "The Co-operative Revolution" published in the International Year of the Co-op, illustrates the history and enduring appeal of this robust business model. Come and hear New Internationalist political cartoonist Polyp give a unique glimpse into the creation of the graphic novel, and hear more about its challenging ideas. All welcome
