SYMAAG meeting (South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group)

Date, Time & Location Thu 27 Jan 2011, 6.30pm at Scotia Works, Leadmill Road S1 4SE
Details Please note changed date of this meeting from Tuesday 25th to Thursday 27th because of difficulty in finding a suitable meeting room. After this back to the normal pattern on monthly meetings on Tuesday evenings. Amongst other items, the meeting will discuss migrant domestic workers and the "Home Alone" campaign. Is the detention of asylum-seeking children going to end? News update and discussion. Section 4 Azure Cards: the continuing campaign against them and for the right of people seeking asylum to work. ESOL (English as a Second or Other Language): What will the proposed cuts mean? (see Guardian article for details. As usual, the meeting will hear up-to-date reports on the situations in our members' countries of origin and reports from partner organisations. If you want to come to any SYMAAG meeting or event but find it hard to afford transport don't be put off! Please bring your ticket or a receipt and SYMAAG can pay you back at the meeting.
