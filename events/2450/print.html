Sheffield Friday Night Ride

Date, Time & Location Fri 7 Dec 2012, Start 6.30pm, Charter Square
Details Sheffield Friday Night Ride (FNR) offers a fun monthly evening bike ride for everyone. Rides are prepared in advance on themes. This time the theme is 60s Sheffield, concentrating on buildings and the built environment from about 1955 when the post-war planning really got under way to about 1973.
