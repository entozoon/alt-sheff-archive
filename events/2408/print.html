Sustrans presents Sustained Affairs

Date, Time & Location Fri 16 Nov 2012, 8pm-11.45pm DLS, 7 Smithfield, Shalesmoor S3 7AR
Details Cycle event; ride into the the wonders of the weekend with an evening of sustainable sentiments and good vibes. The line up of fantastically novel and exciting prospects on offer includes: A pedal powered cinema screening short films about the beauty and brilliance of bikes; A range of sustainably-flavoured nibbles and sippables; A winter grotto filled with cosy creature comforts and installations from the Artificial Constructs team. Come highly visible and show your sustainable colours, cycling or not... Cyclists can meet at Sheffield students union at 7.30 to join a ride to the event for 8pm. Facebook event
