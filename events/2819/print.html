Friends of the Earth meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 15 Jul 2013, 8pm Red Deer pub, 18 Pitt St. S1 4DD
Details Friends of the Earth Sheffield Group meet to campaign about the state of the planet with a desire to do something about it. Membership of Sheffield FOE is free if you subscribe to the electronic newsletter. The venue is off West Street (upstairs room). www.sheffieldfoe.co.uk
