Sheffield Green Spaces Forum - AGM & General Meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 18 May 2016, 7pm Room 13:17 Sheffield University Arts Tower, Western Bank S10 2TN
Details SGSF represents all voluntary Friends of parks and other voluntary green space management groups. This General Meeting and AGM includes as guest speaker Ian Rotherham, Managing Green Spaces for Wildlife at University of Sheffield. The venue is on floor 13 of the Arts Tower building. Refreshments provided. Parking available in the evening. Free to attend but donations from Friends of groups etc are most welcome as they have no long term funding as yet.
