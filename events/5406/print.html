Budget Day Rally and Foodbank Collection

Date, Time & Location Mon 29 Oct 2018, 5-6pm, outside the Town Hall, Pinstone St. S1 2HH
Details A protest called by Sheffield People´s Assembly demanding no more cuts, its time to invest properly and publicly in the NHS, council house building, training and jobs, our schools, colleges and universities. Please come along and join in with placards, banners, and food donations. Sheffield People´s Assembly will be making a collection for local food banks. Please bring what you can. And if you would like someone from the People´s Assembly to speak to your organisation do get in touch.
