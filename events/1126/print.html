Film: South of the Border

Date, Time & Location Wed 8 Dec 2010, 7pm-9.30pm Hicks Building, Lecture Theatre 2, University of Sheffield, Hounsfield Rd
Details A chance to see Oliver Stone's recent film focussing on Venezuela, and the progressive movements across Latin America. The film documents the rise of the democratically elected wave of Leftist leaders in South America and their battles aganst the IMF and US sponsored Latin American elites. It features revealing interviews with Presidents Hugo Chavez (Venezuela), Evo Morales (Bolivia), Lula da Silva (Brazil), Cristina Kirchner (Argentina), as well as her husband and ex-President Nestor Kirchner, Fernando Lugo (Paraguay), Rafael Correa (Ecuador), and Raul Castro (Cuba). Stone sheds new light on the social progress taking place in Latin America. Student Friends of Venezuela, in partnership with The Venezuela Solidarity Campaign and Student Broad Left, is proud to be hosting this screening.
