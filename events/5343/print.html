Suffrage Afterlives: What Happened After the Vote Was Won?

Date, Time & Location Tue 9 Oct 2018, 6pm, Town Hall Reception Rooms, Pinstone St. S1 2HH
Details A panel discussion examining how women used the vote and who were the women pioneers in parliamentary politics. These and other questions will be considered by Rachel Reeves MP, Professor Krista Cowman (University of Lincoln), Tara Tobler (And Other Stories publishers) and Professor Matthew Stibbe (Sheffield Hallam University). Chaired by Dr Julie V Gottlieb of University of Sheffield and Curator of the Suffrage 100 strand of the 2018 Off the Shelf festival of words.
