Turn Unstoned: The Harms & Benefits of Recreational Drugs

Date, Time & Location Mon 23 May 2016, 7.30pm, Farm Road Sports & Social Club, Farm Rd. S2 2TP
Details Dr Suzi Gage, epidemiologist at the University of Bristol talks about how the media love to sensationalise the dangers of illicit drug use, whilst downplaying or even ignoring the harms from legal drugs. A trip through the current scientific understanding of the harms, and also potential benefits of recreational drugs, both illegal and legal.
