Postcapitalism Reading Group

Date, Time & Location Tue 1 Nov 2016, 7.30pm, The Bath Hotel, 66 Victoria St. S3 7QL
Details A group which welcomes new members interested in reading political books. The discussion is from a variety of left-wing/anti-capitalist viewpoints. Now starting a new book: "Inventing the Future: Postcapitalism and a World Without Work" by Nick Srnicek and Alex Williams [2015]. This meeting, in the small side room in the Bath Hotel, will be a discussion of Chapter 1.
