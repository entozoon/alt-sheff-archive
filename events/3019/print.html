Film: "Riot from Wrong"

Date, Time & Location Tue 29 Oct 2013, 7pm, Red Deer, 18 Pitt St. S1 4DD
Details Sheffield Anarchist Bookfair Collective presents an award-winning feature length youth-led documentary about the riots of August 2011. Followed by an open plan meeting for the next Sheffield Anarchist Bookfair (on 12th July 2014) "One thing I learned from Riot from Wrong is that 14 young people can get closer to the truth that 90% of working journalists" Dan Hancox, author and journalist. Free entry.
