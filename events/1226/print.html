Skeptics in the Pub: Investigating The Impossible

Date, Time & Location Mon 17 Jan 2011, 7.30pm Showroom Cafe Bar, 15 Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details For over 20 years, psychologist Richard Wiseman has delved deep into the mysterious world of the paranormal. In this talk, Wiseman describes some of his more colourful adventures, presenting a scientific look at a range of seemingly paranormal phenomenon, including fire-walking, ghostly encounters, and ESP. Skeptics in the Pub events are open to all whatever your beliefs and views. Skeptics in the Pub website
