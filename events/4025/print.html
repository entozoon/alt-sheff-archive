HEELEY FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sat 20 Jun 2015, 12-8pm, around Heeley Park
Details The absolutely massive Heeley Festival is back again for another year! It takes place all over Heeley, in sites like Heeley City Farm, View Road and White Horse areas of Heeley Park, Anns Grove School, Heeley Christ Church and The Brothers Arms and Sheaf View pubs. Stalls, food, beer, music and fun! On Heeley City Farm site, till 5pm: Gift, craft and community stalls Woodland Crafts including pole lathe and wooden spoon carving Bouncy Castle and Slide Sheffield Royal Society for the Blind Mobile Bus Bungee Ride Glitter Tattoos Hot Food Stalls including Caribbean Fusion Candy floss, slushie drinks and all the sweet treats kids (and adults!) love at festivals Small Animal House Animal Petting Kid's Crafts Face painting Andy's Birds of Prey Fund-raising Activities for the Farm Snacks and home-made cakes in the Farm Kitchen Summer bargains in the Garden Centre In the Band Field till 8pm: Bell Hagg Orkestar (Joint headliners) Steel City Rhythms (Joint headliners) The Unscene Sheffield Danny Landau Band Bonetti Juxtamahpigeon With DJs Dangerous Pete, Chuffin' Al and Benny Bop Plus Beer Tent with a selection of ciders, beer, lager and spirits. FREE admission but please, please give generously, if you are able, to the Farm fund raising activities happening around the site. They do not receive any funding for the event and every penny donated goes back into projects in the community and animal care bills. They need your support to continue. Stall pitches may still be available if you would like one - money from stall fees also funds activities on the Farm. Contact for Heeley City Farm stall bookings s.hardy@heeleyfarm.org.uk or tel.
