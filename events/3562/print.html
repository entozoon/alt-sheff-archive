ECCLESFIELD GALA

Date, Time & Location Sun 31 Aug 2014, from 12.00 Ecclesfield Park, S35 9XL, opposite Morrisons Supermarket
Details Lots of stalls, games, entertainment, music and dance, dog show, fairground rides and even vintage bikes. This gala was started by Macmillan Nurses over 30 years ago and is still run by a small group of volunteers in aid of Macmillan support for people suffering from cancer, and to bring the community together.
