Play: "Animal Farm"

Date, Time & Location 21-25 Apr 2015, 7.45pm Theatre Delicatessen, 17 The Moor, S1 4PF
Details With many commentators predicting the closest election in years and fringe parties possibly holding the keys to power, revisiting the polemic of Orwell’s masterpiece Animal Farm seems appropriate. Part of the Festival of Debate taking place in Sheffield in the run-up to the general election, during March and April. Tickets on the door £8/£7 concessions/£12 family (2 adults, 2 children). Additional matinee (4pm) on 25 April. In association with Open Door Theatre Company. Festival of Debate
