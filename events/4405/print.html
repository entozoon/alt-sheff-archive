HS2 debate: "Getting the right location for the Sheffield City Region HS2 station: the role of business"

Date, Time & Location Thu 18 Feb 2016, 8am-10.30am, Memorial Hall at Sheffield City Hall
Details A debate on whether the proposed HS2 high speed train line should have a station in Sheffield or at Meadowhall. Organised by Sheffield Chamber of Commerce, The Star, and public and private sector companies which want Sheffield city centre to be chosen. Barnsley MPs and others want a Meadowhall station. A final decision still has to be made. More information and to book a place see link below www.scci.org.uk/events.
