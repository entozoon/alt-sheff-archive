Anniversary gig night: Opus & Now Then 10

Date, Time & Location Fri 30 Nov 2018, 7pm, Abbeydale Picture House, 387 Abbeydale Rd S7 1FS
Details Celebrate a decade in print for Now Then magazine (which incidentally prints an Alt-Sheff column every month). One of the best things about Sheffield, this free magazine is published by Opus distribution, which also helps the local area in many ways, like putting out flyers and running events.
  • Renegade Brass band
  • Anchorsong featuring Tru Thoughts (live string quartet)
  • The Breath (Stuart McCallum; Cinematic Orchestra and Ríoghnach Connolly; Honeyfeet)
