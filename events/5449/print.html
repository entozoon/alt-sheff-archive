Talk: Dangers of radioactivity in fracking

Date, Time & Location Wed 21 Nov 2018, Barlborough Miners Welfare, 1 California Lane S43 4EZ
Details Dr. Ian Fairlie, consultant in environmental radiation, presents a talk on dangers of disturbing underground radioactivity in fracking or exploratory drilling. A shared event with Frack Free Barlborough and Health Professionals of the UK (CHPUK). All members of the public welcome. Frack Free Barlborough
