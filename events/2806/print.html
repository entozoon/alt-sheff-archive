Documentary film "97% Owned"

Date, Time & Location Wed 19 Jun 2013, 7.30pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Where does money come from? Who creates it? Who decides how it gets used? And what does that mean for the millions of ordinary people who suffer when money and finance breaks down? "97% Owned" is a documentary that exposes the privatised, debt-based monetary system that gives banks the power to create money, shape the economy, cause crises and push house prices out of reach. Followed by discussion with supporters of the Positive Money campaign group Positive Money Sheffield
