"Equality in the Pub"

Date, Time & Location Wed 5 Aug 2015, 7pm, Rutland Arms, 86 Brown St. S1 2BS
Details Equality in the Pub is a chance to have a meet, chat about inequality, politics, and more, called by Sheffield Equality Group. All welcome. If warm, it might be in the beer garden. Sheffield Equality Group meetings aim to be an open space where people can discuss and plan actions around income equality. The group is an autonomous group affiliated to the national Equality Trust.
