Transition Hub group planning meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 7 Feb 2012, 5.30pm (for 6) Blue Moon Café, 2 St James Street, S1 2EW
Details The Hub group is a Sheffield-wide Transition group which aims to help in coordinating Transition activities across the city moving towards more sustainable lifestyles. They invite newcomers to get involved in the Hub planning meetings - irrespective of whether you are already involved in a local Transition group. Discussion at the meeting will include Transition Cafe - a quarterly cafe style event which offer an opportunity to meet like-minded people and share progress and ideas. Also Skills Workshops, bank account, insurance, volunteers fundraising and publicity.
