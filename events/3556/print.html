BANNER CROSS FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sat 13 Sep 2014, 11am-4pm, Ecclesall Road South
Details For 2014 the fifth annual Banner Cross Festival and Craft Fair will have around 80 stalls, inside and outside, and entertainment, food and fun for all ages. It is a non-profit making community festival so go and support it, especially if you live near Banner Cross (which is centred around the junction of Ecclesall Road South, Ecclesall Road and Psalter Lane). For details call Sheff 2557198 or email bxng@hotmail.co.uk. Banner Cross Neighbourhood Group
