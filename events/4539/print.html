Workshop: Engaging and Influencing Parliament

Date, Time & Location Wed 11 May 2016, 6-8pm Boardroom, Hallam HUBS building, Paternoster Row S1 2QQ
Details Learn how you can engage and how to develop a campaigning ‘toolkit’, as well as looking at which engagement method to use and when as part of a campaign. Discuss the importance of relationship building with MPs and learn how to access further information about Parliament and its work. Event by Houses of Parliament Outreach Service in association with Festival of Debate 2016, hosted by Opus Independents (which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine). Free to attend, but please register so they can get an idea of numbers. Festival of Debate - Twitter: @FestOfDebate - #FofD - Facebook
