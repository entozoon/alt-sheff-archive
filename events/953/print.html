Hazelhurst Community Supported Agriculture Co-operative volunteer day

Date, Time & Location Sun 19 Sep 2010, Big Pond Field, Hazelhurst Lane, Eckington
Details Volunteer Day with lots of new jobs including digging a ditch. They have a large gazebo for shelter and hot drink refreshments. Please bring food to share, snacks or sandwiches depending what time you are coming. Supervised children are very welcome and they will devise some suitable jobs for them. Dogs by negotiation. Details about how to get to the land and for background information see: Hazelhurst CSA website
