Talk: The situation in Gaza

Date, Time & Location Wed 2 Apr 2014, 7.30pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Eva Bartlett has spent the majority of the last 7 years living in Gaza as an activist and so has more knowledge of Gaza than practically any other "international" living and working there. She was there during cast lead and documents in both writing and photos life, and death, in Gaza. She has (a blog) and also writes for Electronic intifada. Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity Campaign are very pleased that she is able to come and share her experiences in Sheffield. Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity Campaign
