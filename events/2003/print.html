Knife and Folk art fair and exhibition open evening

Date, Time & Location Sat 28 Apr 2012, 3pm-9pm, APG: The Archipelago Works 16-20 Sidney St. S1 4RH
Details Knife and Folk's first event, to raise money and awareness for Amnesty International, as well as celebrate and support local artists, musicians and independent companies. Showcasing Sheffield artists' work and their response to the notions of freedom of speech and human rights. Illustrators, graphic designers, graffiti artists, tattoo artists, 3D crafters, printmakers, animators and more. Live art from the Knife and Folk Collective and friends, interactive artwork and installations, live acoustic music and DJ, food, bar, stalls, and raffle (promising prizes you'd actually want to win!)
