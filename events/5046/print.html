How to Create a Better, Stronger and Fairer Economy

Date, Time & Location Mon 26 Jun 2017, 7pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Our current system has delivered a banking crisis, recession, austerity, falling wages, rising government and personal debt, and increasing environmental threats. Simon Duffy (Centre for Welfare Reform), Natalie Bennett (Green Party) and Adrian Byrne (Positive Money) will put forward and debate changes they want to see in order to create a better, stronger and fairer economy. Tickets free. Organised by Positive Money as part of Festival of Debate, hosted by not-for-profit social enterprise Opus Independents (which also publishes a monthly Alt-Sheff column in Now Then magazine).
