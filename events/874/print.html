Summer Picnic - Sheffield Palestine Womens Scholarship Fund

Date, Time & Location Sun 22 Aug 2010, 11am-4pm at Padley Gorge, Derbyshire
Details Sheffield Womens Scholarship Fund invite everyone, children included, to join them at a Summer Picnic at Padley Gorge to celebrate sponsored students who have graduated this year and the fact that 10 women will be sponsored at university next year. Meet near the entrance from the road where the ice cream van parks (!) Look out for the Palestinian flags. Take your own food, drink and games. Relax, sunbathe or join in a game of rounders with other convivial folk. Donations welcome on the day. Padley Gorge is: Click here to see map. Bus 215 or 84 to Fox House (about 10-15 minute walk towards Grindleford). If the weather is poor on the day phone 07747 053109 or Sheff 2555550 to check whether the event is to proceed.
