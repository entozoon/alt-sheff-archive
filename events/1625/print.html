Party: Close of Summer of Sanctuary

Date, Time & Location Sun 11 Sep 2011, 6-9pm, Old Junior School, South View Rd, Sharrow S7 1DB
Details Sheffield Refugee Asylum Seeker Action Group and City Of Sanctuary would love the pleasure of your company today to end the summer of sanctuary 2011 by saying "Happy Eid to Everyone!" Guests will be treated to delicious halal food from around the world. RASAG members and special guests from DEWA will talk about their work in the community sector. A DJ will get guests into the party mood and then everyone's favourite Sheffield Kurdish band Yakam Jar Bu will get everyone up for a dance. Everyone is welcome. It's all free! Hope to see you there!
