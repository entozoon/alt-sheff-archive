Sheffield Action for Trans Health meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 5 Jul 2017, 6.30pm, Owen 920, Sheffield Hallam University S1 1WB
Details Come find out more about Action for Trans Health, get involved in planning campaigns and organise for upcoming events. This meeting is open to anyone whose gender identity is not wholly the same as the one they were assigned at birth. This includes people who identify as another gender, a combination of genders, no gender and people who are questioning their gender identity. The meeting space will be wheelchair accessible.
