Hillsborough Park Extravaganza

Date, Time & Location Sat 31 Jul 2010, 10.30-4pm at Hillsborough Park
Details Market stalls and entertainment, a fun day for all the family and a first in Hillsborough Park. The organisers are a local business but it's all to raise money for The Sheffield Childrens' Hospital Charity. FREE entry. Hillsborough Park is at Middlewood Road S6 4HD (Buses 20, 53, 77/78 etc.) Enquiries tel. 07922 411904 or by email at charityevents@live.co.uk
