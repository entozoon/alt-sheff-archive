Beyond Biodiversity to Bioabundance

Date, Time & Location Thu 14 Jun 2018, 6pm, ROCO Creative Co-op, 338-346 Glossop Rd. S10 2HW
Details Hugh Warwick, hedgehog-loving ecologist and author, with Natalie Bennett, former leader of the Green Party of England and Wales, discuss why we need to talk about the collapse in ecosystem mass and animal and plant numbers, not just the loss of species.
