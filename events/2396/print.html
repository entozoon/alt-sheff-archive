HOME GROWN ABUNDANCE CEILIDH

Date, Time & Location Sun 18 Nov 2012, 7.30-10:30pm, Broomhall Centre, Broomspring Lane, S10 2FB
Details Bring your dancing shoes for Abundances harvest season finale celebration at the Broomhall Centre! Shuffle your feet and swing your hips to music from The well Dressed band, win some lovely prizes in the raffle, enjoy a drink from the Abundance bar and bring a dish to share with your fellow Sheffielders. All to celebrate this years urban fruit harvest. Price: £5 adv. £6 on door. £3.50 concessions. This event will be the finale in this year`s Abundance Weekender. For a schedule of other activities/events going on check out www.sheffieldabundance.wordpress.com.
