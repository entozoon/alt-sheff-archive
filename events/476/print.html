The world is full of helplines - who needs Samaritans as well?

Date, Time & Location Wed 7 Apr 2010, 8pm at University Arms, 197 Brook Hill, Sheffield, S3 7HG (Sheffield University tram stop, or bus: 51 & 52 best; 94 & 95 close by)
Details The Samaritans offer support for anyone in emotional distress. Three Samaritans volunteers talk about the work they do and why it is so necessary. A talk open to the public, organised by the Sheffield Humanist Society. www.sheffieldhumanists.org.uk
