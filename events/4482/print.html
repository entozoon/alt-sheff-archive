Sheffield Esteli sponsored bike ride

Date, Time & Location Sun 15 May 2016
Details The Sheffield Esteli sponsored ride raises money for educational and environmental projects in Esteli in Nicaragua, one of the poorest countries in the world and with one of the largest gaps between rich and poor. The ride into Derbyshire starts from Endcliffe Park. There are 10, 20 and 50 mile options. Register and pay online here Sheffield Esteli Society
