Chile fundraiser for Valparaíso

Date, Time & Location Sat 17 May 2014, 7pm St. Andrew’s Church, Upper Hanover St. S3 7RQ
Details A fundraiser for the victims of the fire disaster in Valparaíso, when three days of forest fires swept into some of the city’s working-class neighbourhoods, leaving many people dead and thousands without homes. Expect live music and Chilean food, Latin rhythms and DJs. Organised by Chile SCDA and the Chilean Students and Hispanic and Latin American Society of Sheffield University and supported by El Paso and La Vaca restaurants, and Caznella jewellery. £5 on the door, £4 concessions/advance Chile SCDA
