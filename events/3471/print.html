Launch of Fusion Women's Group

Date, Time & Location Thu 19 Jun 2014, 10am-12pm
Details This group launches as part of Refugee Week and continues for 8 weeks, with an aim of becoming a weekly group at the Together Women Project: 106 Arundel Lane, Sheffield, S1 4RF. The group includes a weekly basic awareness session around issues such as health, confidence, money management and volunteering (delivered by professionals), followed by a conversation class with fun activities to improve English and integration. Children are welcome and refreshments are provided. If you have any questions please call Sheff 2758282.
