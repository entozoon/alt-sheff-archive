Greenpeace meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 8 Nov 2011, 8pm Red Lion, Charles St (Hubs end) Sheffield S1 2ND
Details Monthly meeting of Greenpeace's active South Yorkshire group, held on the 2nd Tuesday of each month. Newcomers always welcome. Current campaigns include one on the notorious Common Fisheries Policy, which has allowed 72% of European fish stocks to drop to unsustainable levels. Greenpeace volunteers are collecting personalised messages from the public for David Cameron. More details of meetings and current activities in the Get Active Supporters newsletter, see Greenpeace South Yorkshire website Upcoming event dates are shown on the home page.
