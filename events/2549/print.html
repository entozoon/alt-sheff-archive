  • The things you were taught don't make sense anymore?
  • Speaking the truth meant losing your community?
  • Following your heart meant doing the impossible?
Peaceable Kingdom explores the interconnected life journeys of farmed animals, former farmers, and animal rescuers struggling against an out-of-control industrial system. Breaking generations of silence in the farming community, Peaceable Kingdom weaves together themes of respect, forgiveness, commitment and healing, offering a vision of a more peaceful world that is well within our reach.
