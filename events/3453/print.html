SHEFFIELD ANARCHIST BOOKFAIR

Date, Time & Location Sat 12 Jul 2014, 10am-6pm Showroom Workstation, 15 Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details This 5th annual bookfair continues the anarchist tradition of reading, learning, organising and having fun. Come to find out more about this alternative way of understanding society, power and politics. There will be loads of stalls including books, comics, badges etc. A programme of films and workshops will run from 11am. Free entry, donation requested to cover costs. Sheffield Anarchist Bookfair
