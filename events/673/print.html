Stop Vivisection In Sheffield!

Date, Time & Location Sat 19 June 2010 12.30pm
Details The University of Sheffield experiments on 30 000 animals every year, one of the highest amounts for any UK university. Come and show your opposition to vivisection and speciesism. We'll meet at 12.30 outside City Hall, and march through the city and past the University. Join us for inspiring speeches, tasty cupcakes and (maybe) sunny weather! See http://saeis.wordpress.com/ for more information and email saeis@hushmail.com if you have any questions.
