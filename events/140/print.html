Soul, funk & disco for Cuban exchange visits

Date, Time & Location Sat 3 Oct 2009, 8pm til late at Millennium Hall, 520 Ecclesall Road AKA ‘the Polish Club’ (near Hunter’s Bar)
Details Please join the launch event for “Voces de Cuba” a fund to support a UK visit by a stunning Cuban choral group. Banish those blues and swing into winter with the Free Radicals, Sheffield Socialist Choir and The Free Radicals playing soul & funk classics. Plus Cuban disco - put on your dancing shoes… tell all your friends – come and let your hair down. No experience necessary £6.00 / £3.00 or donations Children welcome. BAR & soft drinks on sale. In November 2009 Sheffield Socialist Choir are taking part in the international Choral Festival in Santiago, Cuba, for a 2nd time. Keen to follow this trip with an exchange visit by one of the amazing Cuban choirs that take part in the event, they have organised this as a fundraiser not to be missed!
