Sheffield Against Fracking monthly meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 10 Sep 2019, 7.30 pm, Central United Reformed Church, Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details Sheffield Against Fracking are a group of people dedicated to raising awareness and publicly objecting to fracking. The group often collaborate with other groups to have a presence at many events and festivals, and hold monthly meetings on the first Tuesday of every month. If you would like to learn more about fracking or become involved in the fight against it, you are welcome to attend. Sheffield Against Fracking
