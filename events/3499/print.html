Sheffield Equality Group

Date, Time & Location Wed 2 Jul 2014, 7pm, 2014, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Sheffield Equality Group meetings aim to be an open space where people can discuss and plan actions around income equality. Currently they are discussing campaigns around welfare justice, the national income equality policy pledge, and the living wage. The group is an autonomous group affiliated to the national Equality Trust. Meetings are on the first Wednesday in every month. All welcome. Free, donations for biscuits. www.sheffieldequality.wordpress.com
