Allotment Soup

Date, Time & Location Sun 7 Oct 2012, 12-5pm, then evening social 5.30-7.30pm Morley Street allotments, Walkley Bank S6 2PL
Details Come and explore the Morley Street site where allotment holders are opening up their plots. They’ll be hosting artists and workshops featuring: apple pressing, soup, a willow tabernacle, harvest leaves, magic potions, Jack and the Beanstalk, Woodcraft Folk, Tom and Barbara, vegetable puppetry from Madame Zucchini, children’s activities, smoothie making, a herbalist walk, a Renga poem travelling workshop, artists, crafts, wood turner, drawing, poetry, stories and songs. Refreshments will be served at the ‘Treats and Beats’ cafe. Evening social featuring performances, acoustic music and plenty of ‘Allotment Soup’! www.growsheffield.com
