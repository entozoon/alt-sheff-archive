Film: "Canvas of Voices"

Date, Time & Location Mon 20 April 2015, 5.30pm Church of St James and St Christopher, Bellhouse Rd. Shiregreen S5
Details In association with Ignite Imaginations, a short film by Gemma Thorpe, Canvas of Voices is about young people in sheffield and their engagement in politics. Followed by a chaired debate about the topic. Have your say! Part of the Festival of Debate taking place in Sheffield in the run-up to the general election, during March and April. It provides opportunities to get together and discuss a range of issues which affect everyone. Informed decision making and strategic planning are essential for democracy, so come and get involved.
