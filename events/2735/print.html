One Step meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 5 Jun 2013, 8pm, Walkley/Upperthorpe
Details One Step is a small group based in the Walkley/Upperthorpe area which meets once a month to find practical ways to take one step a day, a week or a month to leave the world a better place than when they started. This month they are looking at the topic of "volunteering", as well as taking some time to look back over the first year of the group and plan out topics to cover in the future. Please email contact@one-step.org.uk for the venue of the meeting this month, or for more information about the group see www.one-step.org.uk
