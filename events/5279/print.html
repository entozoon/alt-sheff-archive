SHARROW FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sat 7 Jul 2018, Mount Pleasant Park, Sharrow Lane, nr London Rd. S11 8AE
Details If you’ve never been to Sharrow Festival, go. It’s a great annual multicultural community festival bringing people together with a focus on creativity and participation, for over two decades. It features great music, local groups and businesses, a worldwide variety of food, the local Sure Start team, Football Unites Racism Divides streetkick activities, Hip Hop zone, live grafitti art, the list goes on.
