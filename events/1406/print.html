Women's course: Basic Plumbing

Date, Time & Location Wed 27 Apr 2011, for 5 weeks
Details Re-opened for 2011 with great voluntary spirit, Women in Construction Arts and Technology (WICAT) offers courses for women (and some for men also). Prices are low, just to cover costs, with concessions for learners on various benefits. BASIC PLUMBING - 5 week starts Wed 27th April 2011, 6.00 – 9.00pm - £60. An introduction to household plumbing, to include understanding central heating systems, changing taps, washers, washing machine installation, soldering and copper connections. Women Only.
