Sheffield Vegan Bake Sale

Date, Time & Location Sat 27 Apr 2013, 10.30am, Lush, Fargate, Sheffield
Details The Vegan Approach will be holding this bake sale as part of the Worldwide Vegan Bake Sale﻿. Delicious vegan baked goods will be on sale from 10.30am, and all proceeds will go towards The Vegan Approach﻿ to help pay for future events that encourage and motivate people to go vegan. More info: info@theveganapproach.com
