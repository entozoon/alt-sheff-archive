"Resisting Occupation"

Date, Time & Location Tue 24 Feb 2015, 7.30pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity Campaign public meeting with Ruth Edmonds, an activist in the Israeli Committee Against House Demolitions and in Boycott from Within. The daughter of a Jewish mother and an English father she works in solidarity with the Palestinian resistance to occupation and ethnic cleansing. Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity Campaign
