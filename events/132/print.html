Thoughts for World Peace

Date, Time & Location Sun 25 Oct 2009, 2pm at the Multifaith Centre, 2nd Floor, Owen Building, Sheffield Hallam University, Howard Street, Sheffield S1 1WB
Details An event arranged by Sheffield Interfaith, with readings and meditations from a variety of faith traditions and beliefs ...or none, and music by Phil Croft. Based on the Week of Prayer for World Peace which had the following themes... 1.Caring for each other. 2.Taking Action. 3.Truth. 4.Hope. 5.Understanding and forgiveness. 6.Love and Kindness. 7.The natural world. 8.Peace makers. Contact Rev Geoffrey Usher tel. Sheff. 2767114
