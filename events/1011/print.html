Sheffield Committee To Defend Asylum Seekers (CDAS) meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 3 Nov 2010, 7pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James Street S1 2EW
Details Sheffield CDAS fights for asylum seekers who live in Sheffield to be allowed to stay in the UK. These people have become our friends, and contribute massively to the cultural identity of the city. Yet many live in fear of there lives, with the constant threat of deportation, torture, physical abuse or even death. Regular meeting, newcomers welcome. www.sheffieldcdas.org.uk
