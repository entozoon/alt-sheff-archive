Extinction Rebellion swarming event

Date, Time & Location Tue 19 Mar 2019, 8am
Details

Extinction Rebellion blocked the crossing outside the train station on Sheaf Street, Sheffield, in the top 10 most polluted places in Britain. Swarming onto the road for seven minutes at a time, they talked to drivers. The objective is pressure on the Council to act on climate crisis with a Climate Emergency Motion.
