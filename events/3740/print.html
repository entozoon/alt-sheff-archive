Climate Change General Election Hustings

Date, Time & Location Thu 11 Dec 2014, 7pm University of Sheffield, Hicks Building Lecture Theatre 1, Hounsfield Rd. S3 7RH
Details Candidates standing for election in Sheffield Central and Sheffield Hallam will form a panel ready to answer questions about their personal and party policies. The theme is climate change, so bring any questions you have regarding carbon reductions, fracking, energy security, green jobs, air pollution, renewable energy and other environmental concerns. Hosted by the University of Sheffield, and convened by Sheffield People and Planet and Sheffield Climate Alliance as a chance to find out what your candidates think, and to influence their own opinions and party policies. Confirmed attendees: Peter Garbutt – Green Party – Sheffield Hallam Oliver Coppard – Labour Party – Sheffield Hallam Jillian Creasy – Green Party – Sheffield Central Joe Otten – Liberal Democrats – Sheffield Central Others to be confirmed The event will kick off with an introduction from Sheffield City Council cabinet member for the environment, Jack Scott (Labour) who will outline the work going on in Sheffield to help combat climate change, and prepare us for the local effects.
