STANNINGTON CARNIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sat 13 Jul 2013, 12 to 4.30pm, Stannington Park, Oldfield Terrace, S6 6DP
Details The 22nd year of this great community-run carnival. Stannington Carnival Organisers & Festival Friends (SCOFF) plan fairground rides, clowns, games, birds of prey, food, bouncy castles, donkey rides, belly dancing, a variety of fabulous classic cars on display, and more. For information about stalls or general questions email the organisers mailto:scoff.carnival@live.co.uk or tel. 07967 872163
