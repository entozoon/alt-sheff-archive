NETHER EDGE FESTIVAL

Fri 16-Sun 25 Sep 2016

Nether Edge celebrates, with a family-friendly multi-cultural festival of music, arts, drama, social history, cycling, dancing, talks, street food and much more. Download the full programme of events from the website below, or pick up a paper copy at local shops.

Organised by a volunteer committee of local people and small businesses, supported by Nether Edge Neighbourhood Group and Nether Edge Farmers' Market. Many events are free, some require pre-booking.
