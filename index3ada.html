Tree protesters' court appearance

Thu 1 Dec 2016, Sheffield Magistrates Court, Castle St S3 8LU

UPDATE: The charges were NOT thrown out as expected; court date set - see full story on the News page.

After the City Council/AMEY chopping down of trees on Rustlings Road in the early hours of the morning, five people were charged. They appear in court accused of offences relating to preventing people from working, under legislation passed by the Conservative government designed to suppress trade union activity.

There will be a rally outside the court at 9am to support the protesters.

Dave Dillner and Helen McIlroy from Sheffield Tree Action Groups (STAG) were interviewed about the issue on Sheffield Live Radio

Stop Bombing Syria public meeting

Thu 1 Dec 2016, 7pm, Owen Building, Sheffield Hallam University, Howard St. S1 1WB

The War in the Middle East - Why more bombing won't help Syria. A public meeting hosted by Sheffield Stop the War Coalition, chaired by Lucinda Wakefield (Sheffield StWC). Come along to discuss the ever-growing crisis in the Middle East, and why it is vital to oppose intervention, the increased danger of escalation with the election of President Trump, and the anti-war movement in the UK and Sheffield.

Speakers:

Paul Rogers; Professor of Peace Studies, Bradford University
Piers Robinson; Professor of Politics & Journalism, University of Sheffield
Andrew Murray; National Officer, Stop the War Coalition
Maz Saleem; Anti-war & anti-racism campaigner
