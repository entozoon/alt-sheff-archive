Skeptics in the Pub: "An Honest Liar"

Tue 10 Jun 2014, 7.30pm, University Arms 197 Brook Hill S3 7HG

An Honest Liar is the brand new biography of leading magician, writer and skeptic James Randi. Randi saw his beloved magicians tricks being used by faith healers, fortune-tellers, and psychics not for entertainment, but to steal money from innocent people and destroy lives. Enraged by this, he dedicated his life to exposing those frauds, with the wit and style of a great showman. He even transformed his partner, Jose Alvarez, into a fake guru named Carlos to show how easily people can be fooled. A sudden and shocking truth in his personal life was brought to light when the tale of multiple identities took an unexpected and potentially devastating turn.

An Honest Liar is showing in Sheffield at the Odeon on the 10th June at 15.30 and the Showroom on the 11th June at 13.00.

Sheffield Anti Cuts Alliance AGM

Tue 10 Jun 2014, 7pm Central United Reformed Church, 60 Norfolk St. S1 2JB

A big decision at the AGM for Sheffield Anti Cuts Alliance: it has been agreed that a national network of non-party, anti-cuts groups is needed, and that the People's Assembly now fulfils that role. Several of those active in SACA have been working for some time with the local South Yorkshire People's Assembly, and some have become elected officers within it. There there is no point in running 2 parallel umbrella anti-cuts organisations, so the proposal is that SACA be dissolved, in accordance with the constitution, and remaining funds and activities be transferred to the South Yorkshire/Sheffield People's Assembly. This requires a vote at the AGM to dissolve SACA as a distinct organisation.
