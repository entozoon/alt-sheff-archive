PEAK DISTRICT CYCLING FESTIVAL

Sat 7-Sun 15 Sep 2013

Greenpeace meeting

Tue 10 Sep 2013, 8pm The Sheffield Tap, Platform 1B, Sheffield Station, Sheaf St S1 2BP

Monthly meeting of Greenpeace's active South Yorkshire group, held on the 2nd Tuesday of each month. Note the venue is The "Sheffield Tap" pub on platform 1 at Sheffield train station. It is accessible from a front door to the left of the station main entrance.

Newcomers are always welcome to Greenpeace meeings, more details of meetings and current activities in the Get Active Supporters newsletter, see Greenpeace South Yorkshire website

SAGE Greenfingers open day

Tue 10 Sep 2013, 11am-2pm, Grimesthorpe allotments, Grimesthorpe Rd. S4 8LE

SAGE Greenfingers promote the mental and physical well-being of adults experiencing mental health problems via the provision of creative therapeutic activities.

The Open Day programme includes viewing the year's work, poetry walk about and photo workshop and a performance by local poet Matt Black. There will be a buffet lunch and an opportunity to make a veggie portrait and join in a treasure hunt!

To help with catering numbers, please email enquiries@sagesheffield.org.uk

Chile film season

8 - 22 Sep 2013, Showroom Cinema, Paternoster Row S1 2BX

40 years ago a military coup plunged Chile into a brutal right-wing dictatorship, the first country in the world to experience the shock of "neoliberalism". Many fled the country, some arriving here to make Sheffield their new home. Now, in commemoration of the anniversary, there is a series of events including this festival of films at the Showroom. Further events are listed separately and on the websites below.

Chile 40 Years On website
South Yorkshire Chilean Solidarity website

Sheffield Anticuts Alliance AGM

Tue 10 Sep 2013, 7pm Central United Reform Church, Norfolk St. S1 2JB

Sheffield Anticuts Alliance (SACA) annual general meeting. SACA works to publicise the cuts and support the people of Sheffield to fight back and offer alternatives to the brutal attacks. Whether you are an individual or a campaign group who wants to get active, become part of SACA, all welcome

Any motions for constitutional amendments and nominations welcome for officer posts by Sunday September 8th to sheffieldanticutscampaign@gmail.com. Posts: Secretary, Chair, two Vice-Chairs, Treasurer, Publicity Officer and Membership Secretary. While some of the officers may be willing to stand again, Ben Morris, one of the vice-chairs, will be
leaving Sheffield to take up a new post on London this autumn, making at least one vacancy.

The current SACA constitution, which can be changed at an AGM, can be found online at
sheffieldanticuts.wordpress.com/about/constitution/
