NETHER EDGE FESTIVAL

15th-24th Sep 2017, various locations, Sheffield 7

Nether Edge holds its own festival for the third year running. Includes music, dancing, talks, street food and much more. Farmers' Market on Sunday 17th, (which donates profits to charity). The full programme of events can be downloaded from the website below, or pick up a paper copy at local shops.

Organised by a volunteer committee of local people and small businesses, supported by Nether Edge Neighbourhood Group and Nether Edge Farmers' Market. Many events are free, some require pre-booking.
