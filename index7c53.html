Repair Cafe at Heeley City Farm

Sat 19 Sep 2015, 10am-3pm, Heeley City Farm, Richards Rd. S2 3DT

Fix and reuse, don't throw away.

Repair Cafe is a regular workshop event hosted by Repair Sheffield. Bring along your broken household stuff and volunteer fixers will take a look at it and see if they can work their magic on it. Small electrical items, shoes, scales, toys, tools and wooden furniture are just some of the items that can be brought along, and that have been successfully fixed at previous cafes. Fixers will show you how to mend items and pass on valuable knowledge which will enable you to do repairs for yourself, or go in to a repair shop with enough know-how to feel confident of getting the job done properly.

Held in the Community Room by the pond at Heeley City Farm. This event is free but they gratefully welcome donations if people are able. All fixers are volunteers and the project has no funding. For more information contact Sarah Hardy on Sheff 2580482 option 5, or email s.hardy@heeleyfarm.org.uk

DIVERSITY FEST

Sat 19 Sep 2015, 2pm-midnight, Hagglers Corner, 586 Queens Rd S2 4DU

The first ever for Sheffield: a free celebration for people of every gender, sexuality, ethnicity, age and ability. A statement of unity and mutual respect between the many communities in Sheffield.

The programme includes events in the Hagglers Corner cafe, upstairs and outside. Talks, film, spoken word, bands, acoustic and solo musicians, dancers, DJs, films, kids activities, collaborative art work and creative mini-yurt.

Entry free.

"Time to Act" Conference

Sat 19 Sep 2015, 10.30am-4.45pm Sheffield College, Granville Rd. S2 2RL,

A national conference about divesting from fossil fuels, building the climate movement and investing in climate jobs to create warm, healthy homes, public transport with low fares and renewable energy infrastructure. Organised by the Trade Union Group of Campaign against Climate Change and Sheffield Climate Alliance.

Confirmed speakers include:

Louise Haigh, MP for Sheffield Heeley
Asad Rehman, International Campaigner from Friends of the Earth
Danielle Paffard, divestment co-ordinator at 350.org
Suzanne Jeffery, Chair of Campaign against Climate Change
