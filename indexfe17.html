5th June 2020

One Step meeting

Wed 5 Jun 2013, 8pm, Walkley/Upperthorpe

One Step is a small group based in the Walkley/Upperthorpe area which meets once a month to find practical ways to take one step a day, a week or a month to leave the world a better place than when they started.

This month they are looking at the topic of "volunteering", as well as taking some time to look back over the first year of the group and plan out topics to cover in the future.

Please email contact@one-step.org.uk for the venue of the meeting this month, or for more information about the group see www.one-step.org.uk

Full meeting of Sheffield City Council

Wed 5 Jun 2013, from 2pm

It should be an interesting Full Council meeting of Sheffield City Council on 5 June at 2.00, with various groups packing into the public gallery. To join them arrive before the start and register at the reception to say you would like to sit in the public gallery.

Sheffield Climate Alliance will be presenting an Anti-Fracking petition, calling on the council to oppose new fossil fuel extraction such as hydraulic fracturing (fracking), but also coal bed methane and coal bed gasification which are more likely in Yorkshire and Derbyshire, to avoid catastrophic climate chaos. The science of this is set out in a new American film "Do the Math" that can be viewed free of charge on Youtube.

Also, Sheffield Trades Union Council is encouraging support for resolutions indicating opposition to the Conservative-Liberal Democrat coalition government offensive and attempts to coerce democratically elected local government into doing its dirty business.
