Sheffield Feminist Network Women's Group

Mon 2 Nov 2015, 7.15pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW

"Vintage by the Kilo" pop-up clothes sale

Mon 2 Nov 2015, 11am-6pm, The Studio, University of Sheffield S10 2TD

Yorkshire legends "Vintage by the Kilo" have a 'weigh-and-pay' mega-collection of fashion which outsizes any charity shop, and all carefully chosen stuff, not just 'jumble'. No disrespect to charity or jumble sales, but this is mainly quality 60s, 70s, 80s and 90s clothing which would have been pricy first time round.

Every second-hand purchase you make is a nail in the coffin for the fashion industry which makes corporate shareholders obscenely rich by paying exploitation wages, highly polluting from manufacture to transportation, and leading vulnerable people into expensive, illusory lives as fashion-victims. Don't believe the hype; get yer grunge gear here.

South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG) meeting

Mon 2 Nov 2015, 6.30pm Theatre Delicatessen, 17 The Moor S1 4PF

SYMAAG unites refugee, asylum and migrant organisations with campaign groups and supporting individuals. It campaigns both for the rights of those seeking asylum and of those coming to work in this country. This month they will discuss anti-detention campaigning, ideas from new members about campaign priorities and how to continue the momentum of the Refugees Welcome protest in Sheffield.

Note the new venue and day: Theatre Delicatessen is the former Woolworths building on The Moor.
