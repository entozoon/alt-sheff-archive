Sheffield Seed Swap

Sat 28 Feb 2015, 12-3pm Heeley City Farm, Richards Rd. S2 3DT

Seeds are one of humanity's greatest resources. We rely on seeds for most of our food and much of our clothing and medicine. It's more important now than ever that we maintain our food sovereignty. One seed can produce a plant that can produce thousands of seeds. Saving seeds yourself is an important and rewarding aspect of local food growing.

This is Sheffield's annual seed swap, held at Heeley City Farm Community Classroom, by the duck pond. Pack up your home grown seeds and come along to swap them with like-minded growing enthusiasts. It's always very busy. There will be a large collection of flower, fruit and vegetable seeds; there should also be heritage seed varieties available, along with tips and information about other local organic growing projects.

Free, but please bring some donations of seeds to help the event along if you can, or a small cash donation in the collecting tins would be really appreciated. Heeley City Farm is 20 mins walk from Sheffield, or catch the No. 47 bus to Herdings from town - it drops you right outside the Farm. For more information please contact Darrell Maryon, tel: 07768 932613, email: darrell@heeleyfarm.org.uk or call Sarah at the Farm on Sheff 2505113

Hidden Perspectives LGBT Photo Exhibition

23-27 Feb 2015, 10am-6pm, Jessop West Foyer, University of Sheffield

Celebrating LGBT History Month, an exhibition of local amateur and professional photographers showcasing images submitted by volunteer contributors to celebrate LGBT lives in Sheffield, and diversity at the University. The exhibition seeks to give a platform to LGBT people to express themselves through an artistic process and will also include work by University of Sheffield students on the pioneering interdisciplinary LGBT Studies module. It continues the legacy of previous LGBT projects at the University of Sheffield, celebrating everyday life, milestones in equality and to highlight challenges that the LGBT community face.

Free admission, open to the public.
