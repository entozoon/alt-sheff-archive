"SheFest"

7-13 Mar 2016

Sheffield celebrates International Women's Day with a week of activities and workshops to smash the patriarchy and challenge everyday sexism.

The main event is at HUBS (Paternoster Row) on Saturday 12th.

City-wide "Fringe" events include a bike ride, fashion show, a "Market Hall" of female entrepreneurs, live performances, art, stalls, food and drink and much more.

Hugh Davies Project performance

Fri 11 Mar 2016, 7.30pm, Access Space, AVEC Building, 3-7 Sidney St. S1 4RG

An evening of sounds with Anton Mobin, Alex McLean, Benedict Taylor and Adrian Beentjes, covering several forms of the improvised music scene. This takes the totally DIY approach to music, using hand-made instruments and software.
