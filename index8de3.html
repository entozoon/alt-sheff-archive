Film: "Still the Enemy Within"

Thu 5 Mar-Fri 6 Mar 2015, Cast, Doncaster

This new documentary is being screened as we pass through the 30th anniversary of the historic 1984-85 Miners' Strike. South Yorkshire was heavily affected and the strike was widely supported. It was prompted by a deliberately savage mass closure of coal mines by the Thatcher government, deeply damaging communities dependent on them. The official reason was low profitability, but it was later proved to have been a pre-planned neoliberal attack on nationalised industry, and the power of the highly organised and left-wing National Union of Mineworkers. This moving documentary, by Bad Bonobo films, includes previously unreleased material from the time. It was the Audience Award Winner at Sheffield Docfest.

Doncaster is a short train ride from Sheffield, and Cast is an arts venue in the town centre.

Festival of Debate: "What is Freedom?"

Thu 5 Mar 2015, 7.30pm Harrisons 1854 15-29 Regent Terrace, S3 7QA

A debate on Marxism and Liberal Democracy in association with Sheffield Workers Liberty. What is freedom under liberal democracy, and who is it for? What is freedom of speech and do socialists mean the same thing by it as world leaders? With so much rhetoric from politicians across the world about free societies and human rights, this discussion will look at what value we place on liberal democracy around us and consider what freedom really means.

The Festival of Debate is taking place in Sheffield in the run-up to the General election, during March and April. It provides opportunities to get together and discuss a range of issues which affect everyone. Informed decision making and strategic planning are essential for democracy, so come and get involved. They also welcome volunteers, from attending events and taking part in discussions to volunteering time to help run an event, promote the festival and much more. Unless otherwise stated events are free to attend, but if possible please register via the appropriate Eventbrite link on the Festival of Debate website below to give organsisers an idea of numbers.
