Sheffield Committee to Defend Asylum Seekers (CDAS) meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 19 Nov 2018, 6.30pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details CDAS welcomes all to its monthly meeting, to fight for asylum seekers to be allowed to stay in the UK. These are people have become our friends, and contribute to the cultural identity of the city, yet many live in fear of deportation, torture, physical abuse or even death. CDAS
