Talk: Colonial Realities in Palestine

Date, Time & Location Mon 24 Feb 2014, 6pm, Sheffield University, Students Union Auditorium
Details Are there still colonial states in the 21st Century? As part of Israeli Apartheid Week 2014, Dr. Abdel Razzak Tekriti, Lecturer in The History Department, Sheffield University introduces a talk by Dr. Mazen Masri, Lecturer in Law at the City Law School, City University London. He received his PhD from Osgoode Hall Law School, York University in Toronto, Canada. Masri is also a qualified lawyer and served as legal advisor to the Negotiations Affairs Department of the Palestine Liberation Organization (PLO). Facebook event page
