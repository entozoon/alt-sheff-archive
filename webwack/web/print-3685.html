Arnold Freeman Conference

Date, Time & Location Fri 22 Jun-Sun 24 Jun 2012, Freeman College, 88 Arundel St. S1 2NG
Details A celebration of the life and work of socialist educator, scholar, anthroposophist and actor Arnold Freeman (1886-1972). He worked tirelessly to improve the life experience of Sheffield people, using education, theatre and social projects such as the Sheffield Educational Settlement. This weekend event includes a variety of introductory talks, performances and workshops.
