Queer Tango

Date, Time & Location Sat 5 June 2010, 4-6pm, Walkley Community Centre
Details Queer Tango welcomes everyone. It's a space to get together, enjoy tango music, socialize, exchange and learn new ways of communication through movement and dance. Queer Tango breaks with regimented tradition and bends the rules that apply to traditional tango. Sexual orientation is not an assumption, and gender does not restrict you to a role. It's in favour of exploring and embraces diversity, respects codes, style, roles, type of dress and shoes, how the dancing couples are conformed, sexuality, religion, ethnic background, age, gender, nationality, social class... Queer Tango nurtures and grows from exploring the possibilities that, in fact, we get from all being different. Beginners always welcome! Participation fee: £10/£8 conc. For details: Click to email
