Hiroshima Day Commemoration

Date, Time & Location Fri 6 Aug 2010, 11am in front of Sheffield Town Hall
Details All are welcome to witness this ceremony when The Lord Mayor, Councillor Alan Law, will give a speech accompanying the signing of the Mayors for Peace Declaration to commemorate the many victims of the atomic bomb dropped on Hiroshima in 1945. Organised by Sheffield CND. Contact sheffieldcnd@hotmail.com
