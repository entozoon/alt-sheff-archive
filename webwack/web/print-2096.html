World Development Movement (WDM) end of year social

Date, Time & Location Thu 4 Dec 2014, 7-9pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details WDM aims to change the actions, policies and institutions of governments, corporations and wealthy minorities, which are preventing an end to poverty and injustice. Part of a global movement, it works alongside people protesting in the developing world. The end of year social includes food and drinks, plus some letter writing to MPs on issues such as TTIP and Ghana seed laws. WDM Sheffield website
