Green Party meeting for Rotherham members

Date, Time & Location Wed 6 Mar 2013, 7.30pm-9pm, Whinny View Cottage, Oxford St, Rotherham, S65 2DJ
Details A social event and meeting for Rotherham Green Party. Rotherham and Sheffield joined together around 2006. Since then there has been activity including a campaign in Rawmarsh ward in the 2010 election. Discussion about how the party currently runs in Rotherham and the experience of Rotherham members views on building membership in Rotherham aiming to become a significant political voice in the borough. Hot and cold drinks, wine, cake and biscuits. Partners and anyone who may be interested in the party will be very welcome. Oxford Street is just over a mile from the town centre in Clifton between Cambridge St and Doncaster Road (close to the A630), go to the Doncaster Road end. There is on street parking but you may have to go further up towards Cambridge St to find a space. Buses from Corporation St. in Rotherham town centre with a short walk to venue: 19:10 Number 37 to Doncaster Rd/Rotherhill Close arriving 19:13, 19:25 Number 12 to Cambridge St/Lord St arriving 19:30. Lifts can be provided. Please contact if you intend to attend to help planning numbers. If you are unable to attend, they would welcome any views on the way the party operates in the borough and as a whole. Email eamonn.ward@btopenworld.com or tel Sheff 2311548
