Peace in the Park open meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 24 Oct 2012, 6pm-8pm, Regather, 57-59 Club Garden Rd. S11 8BU (near London Rd)
Details The volunteers are again organising Sheffield’s fantastic annual free festival, where profits go to good causes. Don‘t be shy if you want to get involved or have any ideas, come along to one of the open meetings, everybody welcome. (They also have ‘closed meetings’ for those already involved in planning, to reduce time spent on ‘introductions’ and increase time spent on getting things done). Peace in the Park website
