Day School on Latin America

Date, Time & Location Sat 21 Apr 2012, SADACCA (Sheffield & District Afro-Caribbean Community Association) 48 The Wicker S3 8JB
Details Annual event organised by Sheffield Cuba Solidarity Campaign. This year themes include Latin America and the global crisis, Chile and youth rebellion, advances in struggles against racism, sexism and social exclusion, and Cuba's revolution - continuity and change. Sessions:
  • Mexico's Drug Wars - Politics, Neoliberalism and Violence
  • Colombian social movements confront UK and US transnational corporations
  • Tackling gender based violence at different levels
  • Cuba's education internationalism
  • Cuba, the ALBA nations and the USA
  • Women's participation and empowerment: how can women be involved in changing the system?
  • Cuba & Human Rights
  • Cuba's economic changes
  • Chile's youth rebellion
Full details at www.chilescda.org
