Update on the Portland Works Campaign

Date, Time & Location Wed 18 Jul 2012, 7pm The Circle, Rockingham Lane, Sheffield
Details Derek Morton, Chair of the Portland Works Campaign will provide friends and supporters with an update on progress with this campaign to bring a large historic Sheffield building into mutual ownership. A social enterprise set up by an association of tenants and community members launched a community share issue in June 2011, with the intention of the Works belonging to the people of Sheffield, and continuing to offer low-cost accommodation to craftspeople and the creative industries. Entry is FREE. To reserve your place please book through the following link:- https://portlandworksupdate.eventbrite.com/?ref=estw
