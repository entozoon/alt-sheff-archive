AFC Unity Football for Food Campaign: Pop-Up Quiz

Date, Time & Location Fri 8 Jul 2016, 6-9pm Union Street, 18-20 Union St. S1 2JR
Details AFC Unity are a Sheffield based indie women's football club and also a not-for-profit community organisation. Their current Football for Food campaign collects food at home games and events to distribute via Sheffield Food Collective to local food banks. As part of the campaign this is a Football for Food general knowledge women-focussed pop-up quiz. You can enter by bringing food to donate at the event. All food collected will go via Sheffield Food Collective to local food banks. For more information and to book onto the quiz, please click onto Eventbrite link. Eventbrite page
