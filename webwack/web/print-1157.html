SYMAAG Executive Committee meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 9 Aug 2016, 6.30pm Victoria Hall, Norfolk St. S1 2JB (Chapel Walk entrance)
Details The Executive Committee meeting of South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG) which unites refugee, asylum and migrant organisations with campaign groups and supporting individuals. It campaigns both for the rights of those seeking asylum and of those coming to work in this country. www.symaag.org.uk
