Positive Money: "Money Creation and Parliament"

Date, Time & Location Mon 27 Apr 2015, 7.30pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details More and more people are meeting up around the country to learn more about our debt-based economic system. Mike Black gives this presentation, asking: * How does our money creation system impact on our democracy? * What have MPs (or parliamentary candidates) said about money creation? Talk, short videos, sharing information, questions and views. Positive Money is a campaign to create a money and banking system that works FOR society and not against it. They are positive, proactive people who want to work towards solving the debt crisis and reducing poverty, debt and economic chaos. The Sheffield group of Positive Money supporters meet monthly on the last Monday of each month. All welcome. Refreshments (50p) from 7.15, talk starts at 7.30.
