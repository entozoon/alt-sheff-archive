Course: Rustic Stool Making

Date, Time & Location Sat 29 Oct 2011, Sheffield
Details Learn how to make a rustic stool (which also works as a side table). This is a one-day course on working with green wood, freshly felled. This requires techniques based on understanding how wood reacts - both to the tools, and to the drying and shrinking process. Low-Impact Living Initiative (LILI) is a non-profit organisation to help people reduce their impact on the environment, improve their quality of life, gain new skills, live in a healthier and more satisfying way, have fun and save money.
