STANNINGTON CARNIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sat 22 Sep 2012, Stannington Park, Stannington S6 6DP
Details Re-scheduled following the unavoidable cancellation in July due to heavy rain, Stannington Carnival is back! Stannington Carnival Organisers & Festival Friends (SCOFF) are expecting up to 5000 visitors at this huge event of around 180 stalls, fairground rides, clowns, games, birds of prey, food, bouncy castles, donkey rides, belly dancing, a variety of fabulous classic cars on display, and more. For information about stalls or general questions email the organisers scoff.carnival@live.co.uk or tel. 07967 872163
