LaDIYFest meeting

Date, Time & Location Thu 5 Sep 2013, 6pm, Blue Moon Café, 2 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Meeting of LaDIYFest Sheffield, the DIY, anti-capitalist, community based, feminist festival group. Run by volunteers, led primarily by women, all are welcome to get involved. It aims to highlight female and LGBTQ art, projects and activism in the community, promoting social change in a non-discriminatory environment through music, performance art, spoken word, films, discussions, art exhibitions and practical workshops etc. http://ladiyfestsheffield.wordpress.com
