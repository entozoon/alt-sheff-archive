Cycle Forum Meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 17 Nov 2015, 4.30pm Sheffield Town Hall, Pinstone St. S1 2HH
Details The Cycle Forum is a bi-monthly meeting organised by Sheffield City Council. It discusses issues, shares information and makes recommendations about how cycling as a travel option can be improved. Representatives from CycleSheffield normally attend. If you would like to come along then you can find details here: www.sheffield.gov.uk/roads/about/getting-involved/cycle-forum.html. CycleSheffield Chair Matt Turner normally attends so get in touch with him at matt@cyclesheffield.org.uk if you have not been along before and would like a friendly face to go with.
