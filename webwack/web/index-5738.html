Job vacancy at E.D.E.N Film Productions

Closing date: 29th August 2016

Share on

  • Facebook

  • Digg

E.D.E.N Film Productions is a social enterprise based in Sheffield, South Yorkshire specialising in running filmmaking workshops specifically for the LGBT community and producing campaigns promoting LGBT equality and rights. They are recruiting for the E.D.E.N Shorts project, a Project Facilitator. This is a freelance project-based position, 42 hours in total, from September to November 2016 (approximately), paid at an hourly rate.

They are looking for an experienced filmmaker and project facilitator to facilitate filmmaking, planning and pitching workshops, mentor project participants through the filming and editing process, and coordinate and work with the other facilitators of the project. Full details at the link below. Closing date: 29th August. Interviews to be held later that week.
