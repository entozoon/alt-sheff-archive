Help make us "Frack Free"

August 2014

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Sheffield Climate Alliance and Frack Free South Yorkshire are requesting lots of help to raise awareness and build resistance to fracking in our area.

There will be a "Reclaim the Power" anti-fracking action camp to help people learn more about the issues, to be held near Blackpool from 14th-20th August, with a group from Sheffield attending, get in touch if you are interested; shared transport also available: info@sheffieldclimatealliance.net.

Fighting IGas in Bassetlaw: A large company called IGas will be applying for planning permission this September/October for shale gas drilling in North East Bassetlaw (not far from Sheffield). IGas aims to drill a vertical exploratory borehole to 3,000m early in 2015 to extract core shale samples and to do a pressure test. This will lead to an appraisal well with horizontal drilling and test fracking, followed in 2017 with full fracking production.

Whilst many actions are taking place to counter this, the key objective is to use 'people power' to demonstrate that IGas does not have a social licence and to put electorate pressure on local Councils - county, district and parish. This will start with a house-to-house survey taking place 9th-12th August, of the villages in the area (initially Everton and Misson) with the intention of declaring streets "Frack Free". If you can help deliver leaflets or chat to people, please get in contact, even a couple of hours would really help. Maps are being produced to plot interviews and responses. Leaflets, crib sheets, surveys and window posters are being produced. This will be a pivotal action to prevent fracking in our area - and send a clear message to the fossil fuel industry. Frack Free South Yorkshire calls on everyone to try to commit to some time on the streets to help; day or evenings or both; one day or more. This is an opportunity to come together to protect our environment, water and health. If anyone can car-share that will be useful. Please respond as soon as possible. Many thanks. If you have any questions don't hesitate to email David Burley david.burley@talktalk.net
