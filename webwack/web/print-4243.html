Sheffield Universal Education Collective: What kind of education are we fighting for?

Date, Time & Location Thu 30 Jun 2011, 4pm-6pm, Red Deer pub, 18 Pitt St. S1 4DD
Details Two panelists, plenty of food for thought, and a lively discussion and debate! Ansgar Allen (a lecturer at the University of Sheffield) will be talking about the history of meritocracy. John Rees (a lecturer at Sheffield Hallam University) will be exploring how learning is a transformative and liberating process that is manipulated and modified under the rule of capital.
