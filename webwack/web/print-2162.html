Meeting: Climate Change in the Philippines

Date, Time & Location Thu 9 Oct 2014, 7.30pm, Fulwood Room, Broomhill Methodist Church, Fulwood Road, Broomhill, S10 3BD
Details Public meeting with Voltaire Alferez, who is the National Co-ordinator of Aksyon Klima Pilipinas, a national network of more than 40 civil society organisations working on climate and development issues. Voltaire will speak about Typhoon Haiyan, its effect on the Philippines and work to rescue and rebuild, as well as the Climate Change campaigning he is involved in. The meeting has been arranged by Christian Aid. Door open and refreshments from 7pm.
