Cycle Sheffield meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 2 Sep 2013, 7.30pm, Heeley Development Trust, Ash Tree Yard, 62-68 Thirlwell Rd, S8 9TF
Details The monthly meeting of Cycle Sheffield, usually followed by pub social. This group is for all kinds of cyclists; it organises events, information and rides, and campaigns for cycling to be taken seriously as a means of transport, with the facilities it needs. The agenda this month includes the City Centre Master Plan, taxi driver behaviour and making one way streets contraflow. Full details at Sept 2nd meeting webpage www.cyclesheffield.org.uk
