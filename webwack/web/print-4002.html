Transition Sheffield Christmas Social

Date, Time & Location Tue 6 Dec 2011, Showroom Cinema, Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details Transition Sheffield is part of the growing Transition Towns Network, raising awareness about climate change, peak oil and starting local projects across the city. The organisation's Winter Social will be Showroom Cinema Cafe, a chance to share news with sustainability minded people and enjoy a great evening. www.transitionsheffield.org.uk
