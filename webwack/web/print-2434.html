Talk: "Everyday Sexism"

Date, Time & Location Wed 7 May 2014, 7.30pm University of Sheffield
Details A talk by Laura Bates, founder of the Everyday Sexism Project, one of the biggest social media success stories on the internet. Since 2012, her work has given a collective online voice to the protest against sexism. It proves sexism is endemic - socially, politically and economically, and is a call to arms in the new wave of feminism. Booking online, £7/£5 concessions University of Sheffield
