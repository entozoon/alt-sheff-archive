Ride For Eric

Date, Time & Location Sun 21 Sep 2014, start/end: Abbeydale Sports Club, Abbeydale Road South
Details Bike rides organised to mark the life of Eric Codling, an amazing man who was killed by a reckless driver in November 2013. There are three courses of various lengths. The rides will be to remember Eric, to highlight the need for safe space for cycling, and to raise funds for Cavendish Cancer Care. Ride For Eric
