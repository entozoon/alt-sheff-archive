"SheFest"

Date, Time & Location 7-13 Mar 2016
Details Sheffield celebrates International Women's Day with a week of activities and workshops to smash the patriarchy and challenge everyday sexism. The main event is at HUBS (Paternoster Row) on Saturday 12th. City-wide "Fringe" events include a bike ride, fashion show, a "Market Hall" of female entrepreneurs, live performances, art, stalls, food and drink and much more. Facebook event page
