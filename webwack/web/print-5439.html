Critical Mass Sheffield

Date, Time & Location Fri 27 Nov 2009 Meet 6pm outside the Town Hall (last Friday of every month)
Details Join a monthly cycle ride in celebration of the bicycle as the most efficient, peaceful and perfect mode of transport. Bring someone you know who's got a bike; bring EVERYONE you know who's got a bike. There are no organisers, no leaders; make some flyers and go around Sheffield sticking them on bikes or giving them to cyclists; speak to the person next to you at the traffic lights and tell them about it; do whatever you think will make more people come; come YOURSELF.
