An Evening With Estelí

Sat 16 Mar 2013, 6.30pm, The Old Junior School, Sharrow Community Forum , South View Rd S7 1DB

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

A family event to raise awareness and celebrate the long-standing cultural links between Sheffield and the city of Estelí in Nicaragua. As Sheffield Estelí week draws to a close, come along to a Nicaraguan fiesta. The evening will include live music from Jupiter in Jars, Cumbia Nena!, Los Angelitos, live dance groups: Son de America, INK Dance, Compañia del Son. Also free Nicaraguan dance lessons, raffle, piñatas, quiz, Nicaraguan craft sale and Nicaraguan snacks. There will even be the chance to Skype live with people from Estelí during live streamed performances from a simultaneous party in Estelí! Tickets £6, Concession £4.Facebook event by Sheffield Estelí Society
