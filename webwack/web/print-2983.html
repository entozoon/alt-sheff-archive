SHARROW FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sat 6 Jul 2013, Mount Pleasant Park, Sharrow Lane, nr London Rd. S11 8AE
Details Join in as the people of Sharrow come together for this great annual multicultural community festival, with a focus on creativity and participation. Sharrow Festival Group organises all sorts events throughout the year, to keep the flame of creativity burning, and fundraises for the £10k cost of putting Sharrow Festival on, with things like the Sharrow Club (membership £20 a year; more info email creativeactionnetwork@googlemail.com) Follow the festival action on Sheffield Live! radio, 93.2 FM. www.sharrowfestival.btck.co.uk
