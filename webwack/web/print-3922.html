Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change (SCACC) meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 21 Feb 2012, 7pm-9pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details This regular meeting is open to newcomers, all welcome, generally on the 3rd Tuesday of the month. If you would like to join some Sheffield Climate Alliance activists for food and informal discussion, they will be at the Blue Moon cafe nearby from 5.30pm before the SCACC meeting. This month SCACC's agenda will include an update on the Sheffield Climate Alliance campaign for 10,000 climate jobs for Sheffield, planning for the national Climate Jobs Caravan visiting Sheffield on Fri 18th May, planning for a conference on food to be held in September, and reports on other campaign work and events SCACC website
