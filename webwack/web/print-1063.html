ANTI-FRACKING WEEK

Date, Time & Location Tue 11-Sun 16 Oct 2016, Sheffield Students’ Union, Western Bank S10 2TG
Details Join Sheffield Students’ Union for a week of activities to oppose the nightmare of fracking. Includes a panel discussion, film screening and a craftivism event, before an action to protest fracking. All welcome. More information available at sheffieldsu.com/fracking.
