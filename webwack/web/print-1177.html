Sheffield Vegan Festival

Date, Time & Location 23 Jul 2016, from 10.30am, Cutlers Hall, Church St. S1 1HG
Details The first ever vegan festival to be held in Sheffield. It features stalls, information and loads of lovely food. Speakers include Kate E A Berridge on the spiritual consequences of mass animal abuse, and Sea Shepherd, which uses direct-action tactics to investigate, document and take action against illegal activities committed in our precious ocean ecosystems. Entry £2/£1 and under-5s free. Sheffield Vegan Festival on Facebook
