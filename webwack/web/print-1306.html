9 Pin #4 On the Edge Brewery Beer Festival

Date, Time & Location Fri 6 May 2016, 6-11pm The Old Junior School, South View Rd S7 1DB
Details Grow Sheffield open their exhibition "Grow" (from 7-9pm) in collaboration with Ignite Imaginations in an event from 6pm till the beer sells out! Celebrate spring with them and 9 of their unique beers! The beer list will be announced each week before the event. Why 9 Pin #4? On the Edge is a tiny craft brewery based in a house. The beer is sold in pins (35 pints), they have 9 of them, this is their 4th beer festival! They are looking for musicians to perform, there is a free beer in it! Pies and Peas available on the night (meat and vegetarian options). Grow Sheffield
