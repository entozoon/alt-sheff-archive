Protest against BNP rally in Derbyshire

Sat 15 Aug 2009, transport from Sheffield at 7.30am.

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Unite Against Fascism is calling on anti-fascists across the country to converge on Codnor, Derbyshire, at 9am on Saturday 15 August to protest against the British National Party rally taking place in the village that weekend. Transport is available from Sheffield, leaving Paternoster Row (next to Showroom Cinema) at 7.30am. £9 waged/£6 unwaged. Please ring 07590 044075 to buy a ticket.www.yhuaf.org.uk
