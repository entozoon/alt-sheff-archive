Permaculture Design Course

Date, Time & Location CAUTION: Starts EITHER 11 DEC OR 8 JAN: See website/to be confirmed
Details Permaculture is an approach to land use design based on the relationships found in natural ecologies. It combines social change with sustainability to maximise effect and minimise work. This non-residential training will cover the full design course syllabus, with a focus on urban resilience. Working in partnership with Hazelhurst CSA (Community Supported Agriculture) it will provide opportunities for practical design and implementation. Dates: 1 weekend a month till June/July 2011, each weekend is 2 full days, with optional evening social activities. Venue(s) in Sheffield to be confirmed.
