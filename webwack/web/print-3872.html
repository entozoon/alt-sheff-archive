Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change (SCACC) meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 20 Mar 2012, 7pm-9pm at Quaker Meeting House, 10 St. James St. S1 2EW
Details SCACC organises days of action and demonstrations, petitions to Sheffield Council, supports related national and local campaigns and runs film shows and talks for local groups. The regular meeting is open to newcomers, all welcome, generally on the 3rd Tuesday of the month. This month there will be a presentation by Chris Broome on his critique of the National Energy Policy. At 6pm the same evening there is an informal meeting of the Sheffield Climate Alliance in the Blue Moon cafe for anyone who cannot make the meeting from any other group to catch up or share important information or suggest a campaign etc. SCACC website
