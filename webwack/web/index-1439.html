Demonstration against Trident

Sat 27 Feb 2016, 10.30am, Sheffield Town Hall (& London)

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

A demonstration against the renewal of Trident nuclear missiles ahead of the vote, which will be happening across the country. Nuclear weapons are illegal in international law, immoral, unwanted by a majority of the population, and disgustingly expensive "toys for the boys". Bring placards, banners, etc. On the same day there will be a national demonstration in London, with a march and rally in Trafalgar Square to send a powerful message to the government. It has been called by CND (Campaign for Nuclear Disarmament) supported by the Stop the War Coalition, Muslim Association of Britain and the People's Assembly, with more organisations pledging support all the time. Transport to London will be available through Yorkshire CND, picking up from Meadowhall. For details on how to book a seat, contact scraptridentnow@gmail.comSheffield CND Pick up from Meadowhall Interchange 8.15am online booking (Contact by email: info@yorkshirecnd.org.uk)Pick up from Chesterfield Town Hall 8am online booking (Chesterfield contact moyra_jean@yahoo.co.uk or phone/text: 07732 128480)
