Inclusive Cycling Conference

Date, Time & Location Mon 23 Feb 2015, Hillsborough Arena, Sheffield, S6 4HA
Details A conference looking at ‘Inclusive Cycling: Achievements, Challenges and the Future’. Workshops Topics:
  • Delivering a range of outcomes - How Inclusive Cycling meets a broad range of outcomes across health, community, youth and volunteering
  • Visit an award winning and sustainable Inclusive Cycling Centre - Sheffield Cycling 4 All, Hillsborough Park sharing good practice ideas
  • Becoming a Sustainable Inclusive Cycling Centre - fundraising, business models, utilising volunteers and sharing best practice
  • The future support needs of the Inclusive Cycling Centres - tell us what you think
Free event with lunch and refreshments provided on the day, but spaces are limited, so if you have not received an invite by email, please call Jayne Rodgers on 07766 403220 and she will be happy to discuss availability. CTC - The National Cycling Charity
