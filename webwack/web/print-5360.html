Conference; Faith and the Environment

Date, Time & Location Wed 20 Jan 2010, 9.45am - 4pm at Quaker Meeting House, St James St., Sheffield, S1 2EW
Details What's the event about? One day conference by Sheffield Faiths Forum and SHEBEEN on 'The Crisis of Climate Change' Learn about, and discuss the views of faith communities on our relationship with the earth and the environment, based on traditional teachings and current action in response to climate change. Open to anyone interested in faith perspectives on the environment, common ground and differences in approach. Download the flyer at Flyer web page, or book online at www.offer.org.uk/events
