Wood Fair

Date, Time & Location Sat 26 Sep 2009, from 11am at Endcliffe Park, 2 miles south-west of Sheffield City Centre, off Rustlings Road S11
Details Join Sheffield City Council's Trees and Woodlands Team for Woodfair, Sheffield's favourite free family festival. See the amazing things that can be made out of wood as local crafts people and artists demonstrate their skills. Have a go at woodcrafts, art and woody games in the Ranger activity area or aerial rope walk. Get the lowdown on wood energy for your business and home. Discover fascinating tree facts and find out how important our woods were in the past and are for a sustainable future. Local produce and refreshments. FREE event tel Sheff 2356348
