Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change meeting

Date, Time & Location Tues 10 Aug 2010, 7.30-9.30pm at Highfield Library, London Rd. S2 4NF
Details SCACC organises days of action and demonstrations, petitions to Sheffield Council, supports related national and local campaigns and runs film shows and talks for local groups. The regular meeting is open to newcomers, all welcome. The agenda this time includes the AGM on 28 Sept, the proposed national coalition, meetings soon with Cllr Andrew Sangar, SCC cabinet lead for Climate Change and with Andy Nolan, SCC Director of Sustainable Development.
