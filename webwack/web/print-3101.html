Chesterfield May Day Gala

Date, Time & Location Mon 6 May 2013, 9am-4.15pm, Chesterfield
Details Organised by the Trade Union and Labour Movements with support and contributions from many organisations, Chesterfield May Day Gala is a great day out. May Day is traditionally a major working class celebration, and this is the largest and most popular May Day event in the region, if not the country. Details on the website below. www.chesterfieldmayday.org.uk
