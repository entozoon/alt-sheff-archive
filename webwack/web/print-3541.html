The Riverlution Festival

Date, Time & Location Sat 22 to Sun 30 Sep 2012
Details The Riverlution festival celebrates everything that is great about Sheffield waterways and the communities that surround them. Starting with a weekend of events and activities on the River Don - from Ball Street Bridge to Cobweb Bridge - and Victoria Quays, Then a week of fringe events spread across the wider Sheffield waterway network, Ends on 30th September: World Rivers Day. Loads of events, music, crafts, cycling, beer, BBQ, kayaking, animals, etc .... see website of The River Friends Network for details: www.riverlution.org.uk/festivals
