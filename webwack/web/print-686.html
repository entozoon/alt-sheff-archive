Caliban and the Witch Reading Group

Date, Time & Location Wed 4 Oct 2017, 7.30pm, The Bath Hotel, 66 Victoria St. S3 7QL
Details A group which welcomes new members interested in reading political books. The discussion is from a variety of left-wing/anti-capitalist viewpoints. Now reading "Caliban and the Witch: Women, the Body and Primitive Accumulation" by Silvia Federici. This is an informal discussion over drinks, which will cover chapters 1, 2 and 3 (mainly chapter 3) The meeting is in the small side room in the Bath Hotel, easily accessible.
