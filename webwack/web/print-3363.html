SAF Vegan Christmas Meal

Date, Time & Location Sun 9 Dec 2012, 12.30pm, Heeley Institute, 147 Gleadless Rd. S2 3AF
Details Join Sheffield Animal Friends for a delicious 3 course gourmet vegan meal. Menu Starter - Seasonal vegetable soup with garlic croutons Main course - Seitan roast or chestnut pie. Served with Yorkshire puddings, roast potatoes, roast vegetables, Brussels sprouts and onion gravy. Dessert - Chocolate yule log or sugar-free fruit cake. Served with ice cream, custard or cream. Followed by a mint chocolate and tea, coffee or caffeine-free alternative. All food is vegan. All proceeds will go to Sheffield Animal Friends to raise money for “The Vegan Approach 2013”, an event which supports people to adopt the vegan diet and lifestyle which means living without using animal products. £15 per head. Please bring your own alcohol or soft drinks. Full menu and details of how to pay on the website below. Pre-booking (deadline 2nd December) and payment in advance is essential.
