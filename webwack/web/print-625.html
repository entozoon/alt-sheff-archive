Postgrad Live: Sheffield Masters and PhD Study and Funding Fair

Date, Time & Location Wed 14 Feb 2018, 12pm–3pm, Sheffield Town Hall, S1 2HH
Details Are you considering postgraduate study? This is a chance to speak to over 40 top universities about Masters and PhD study. Talk through your options, get research proposal advice and find out what postgraduate study is really like by talking face-to-face with the people in the know. By attending you could also be in with a chance to win £1,000. The event is completely free to attend and will be accompanied by a programme of talks designed to help with the more complex issues of postgraduate study. Registration link - Findaphd.com
