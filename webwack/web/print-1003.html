Egyptian Party Night

Date, Time & Location Sat 19 Nov 2016, 7pm, Tapton Hall, Shore Lane, Fulwood, S10 3BU
Details Come and join the ultimate Arabic party night with live music from the Baladi Blues (Guy Schalom, Adam "Rhythmic Ginger" Warne), solo performances from the Tarab Dance Company (Nisha Lall, Anna "Loveday" Bisco, Heather Charlton, Joanne Miller, Siobhan Waring, Sandra Thompson) and lots and lots of boogie time! This special night will treat you to the singer Tarek Bitar joining the Baladi Blues all the way from Berlin! All profits will go to Just Because, a charity aiming to raise funds to promote awareness, early detection, treatment and after-care of breast cancer for women in Egypt. Advance tickets £14/£12 concessions (under 14s, 65 and over and NADA members) + 75p online booking fee. £16 on the door on the night. Facebook event page Booking online
