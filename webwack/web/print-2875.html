Rockerbellas Pop-Up Vegan Diner

Date, Time & Location Fri 20 Sep 2013, 7pm, Okeh Cafe, 116 Abbeydale Rd. S7 1FF
Details An evening of American diner food, rock n roll music and fun! Not to be missed. Rockerbellas Vegan Menu: Appetizer: Billy Bob’s corn on the cob served with a choice of maple syrup and peppercorn butter. Main: Rockerbellas’ ’chicken’ pot pie served with garlic mashers, glazed baby carrots and green beans drowning in herby gravy. Dessert: Key lime coconut cheesecake with a ginger snap biscuit base served with a dollop of soya cream. Food served at 7pm. Please arrive from 6.30pm onwards. £15 per person (non refundable, drinks not included but soft drinks are available). Limited places available.
