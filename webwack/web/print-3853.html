RotherFed AGM

Date, Time & Location Tue 27 Mar 2012, 2pm-4pm, Springwell Gardens Community Centre, ROTHERHAM
Details Annual general meeting of RotherFed, which unites and represents Rotherham tenants and residents. The new TARA (Tenants and Residents Association) Neighbourhood Mentoring Scheme to support residents 'break the mould' on anti-social behaviour will be officially launched. The delegates meeting will feature short presentations from various organisations including Neighbourhood Watch, Streetpride and Victim Support on topics such as drug and alcohol misuse, garden nuisance, intimidation, noise, pet and vehicle nuisance, litter and rubbish. Places are limited. To confirm your place please contact RotherFed on 01709 368515 or by email at info@rotherfed.org www.rotherfed.org.uk
