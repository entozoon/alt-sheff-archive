Transport: The Future For Sheffield - Workshop on Travel Policy & Carbon Emissions: Effect on our Health & Well-being

Date, Time & Location Tue 26 Oct 2010, 12.15pm for 1.50pm start, finish 5.30pm
Details An event run by Action For Involvement, for people interested in the subject of transport. Don't let the car users dominate it! OPEN TO EVERYONE - No previous knowledge or experience is required or assumed. Facilitators: Perry Walker, Head of Democracy and Participation, New Economics Foundation (NEF) and Wendy Stern, Founder and Chairman, Action for Involvement. Speakers:
  • John Bann, Head of Transport & Highways, Sheffield City Council
  • Professor Lenny Koh, Associate Dean, Management School, The University of Sheffield
  • Dr Jeremy Wight, Director of Public Health, Sheffield NHS & Sheffield City Council
Sheffield Travel Marketplace exhibitors:
  • Chesterfield Biogas City Car Club
  • East End Quality of Life Initiative
  • Get Walking, Keep Walking
  • Sheffield Campaign Against Climate Change
  • Walkit!
Action for Involvement is a not-for-profit, web-based think tank specialising in low cost workshops for people in our communities to meet key decision-makers and discuss topical issues with them. A grassroots organisation, focussed on value for money, governance and sustainability in the Public or Private Sector local and national service delivery.
