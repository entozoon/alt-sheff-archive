Art in the Park Villages Project 2012-13 Celebrations

Date, Time & Location Fri 15-Sat 16 Feb 2013, Bradfield, Penistone, Denby Dale
Details Art in the Park has been running a series of free art classes in Bradfield, Penistone and Denby Dale and will be running 3 events showcasing the artistic outputs. Theses events are all free and open to all. At Bradfield Village Hall on Friday 15th February from 7-9 pm there will be an exhibition of prints, paintings, drawings, decorative arts and group collaborations. https://www.facebook.com/events/334549519994279 At St’s John’s Church, Penistone from 1-5pm on Saturday 16th February there will be a Poetry and Craft Day. There will be a launch of a pamphlet of local poetry by this years Villages group with readings by Rob Hindle, Julie Mellor and the Penistone Poets. https://www.facebook.com/events/135527629944966 At Denby Dale Methodist Church, also on Saturday 16th February from 1 - 4 pm there is an Arts and Crafts Day with an exhibition of photography and textiles work with a photography walk lead by a photographer and the local group. https://www.facebook.com/events/518114871544869
