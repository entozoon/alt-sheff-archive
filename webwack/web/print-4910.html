Sheffield Friday Night Ride: The Northern Clemency

Date, Time & Location Fri 16 Jul 2010
Details Sheffield Friday Night Ride offers a fun monthly evening bike ride for everyone. Rides are prepared in advance on themes. This time it's a visit to Sheffield locations used in the recent novel The Northern Clemency by Philip Hensher. If you don't know this book it is an epic, set in Sheffield from 1975 to 2005, which was shortlisted for the 2008 Booker Prize. Philip Hensher was brought up and educated in Sheffield and attended Tapton School. Full details as always on the website FridayNightRide website
