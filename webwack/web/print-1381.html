"Principle 5" general meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 22 Mar 2016, 7pm, The Harlequin, 108 Nursery St. S3 8GG
Details The general meeting of "Principle 5" the Yorkshire Co-operative Resource Centre which provides study sessions, education and training on the development of co-operatives, and the nature and benefits of co-operation. Newcomers welcome. They are also looking for people with organising skills and office skills to help out at the Centre, and welcome ideas on how Principle 5 can best serve the Co-operative Movement.
