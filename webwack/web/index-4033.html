'You can't kill the Spirit': Greenham 30 years on

Sat 19 Nov 2011, 2pm to 5.45pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

An event celebrating the spirit of the Women's Peace Camp past, present and future. All women are welcome to come to this celebration and are asked to bring memories, memorabilia, stories, songs and food to share. Helen John, ex-Greenham and now setting up a new Peace Camp at RAF Waddington in Lincolnshire, where the British unmanned drones are to be based, will be speaking. Bring items to decorate the fence at RAF Waddington! Update: The Lord Mayor, Sylvia Dunkley, will also be visiting. Organised by Sheffield CND. Free. www.sheffieldcnd.org.uk
