Carbon Literacy Event

Date, Time & Location Thu 17 Oct 2019, 7.30pm, Lecture Theatre 5, Diamond Building, University of Sheffield, 32 Leavygreave Rd. S3 7RD
Details The Climate Communications Hub, in association with Sheffield Climate Alliance, ask how can we spread ‘carbon literacy’ across Sheffield’s universities, colleges and the city? This is an introduction to Manchester’s Carbon Literacy Project, followed by a public discussion about the way forward in Sheffield. Mark Jones, the Cabinet Member for Environment, Streetscene and Climate Change for Sheffield City Council is amongst the panel to contribute, alongside university staff from Sheffield Hallam and University of Sheffield.
