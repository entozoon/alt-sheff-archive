Divest, Disarm, Decolonise demonstration

Date, Time & Location Wed 20 Mar 2019, 1-2.30pm, University of Sheffield Concourse
Details Wednesday 20th marks the Day of Action for Palestine and Sheffield students are calling for the University to divest from fossil fuel companies, disarm from arms trade companies and to decolonise, taking Israeli apartheid off the campus. Come to show your support, to learn more about the cause, or to just have a chat. Facebook event page
