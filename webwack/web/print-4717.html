Rural Rotherham Ramble

Date, Time & Location Sun 24 Oct 2010, 9.30am, meet at Droppingwell pub, previously called Effingham Arms (top of Droppingwell Road/Upper Wortley Road)
Details A Sponsored Walk to raise funds for the Children's Projects in Gaza. 10 miles easy walking through the beautiful and historic countryside of South Yorkshire: Grange Park Woodlands, Thundercliffe Grange, Keppel's Column, Wentworth Woodhouse, Wentworth Village, Hoober Stand, the Needle's Eye, Elsecar Heritage Centre. Maps and guides provided. Plenty of places to eat and drink en route. Cost: £2/£1 (to cover postage, printing, refreshments) Full details and application forms from: Michael McColgan - Sheff 2685721 E-mail: michaelmccolgan@phonecoop.coop Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity Campaign website
