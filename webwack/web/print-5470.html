Talk: Human Guinea Pigs?

Date, Time & Location Wed 21 Oct 2009, 7.30pm. Quaker Meeting House 10, St James Street, Sheffield S1.
Details A talk about a little known aspect of Sheffield history - medical experiments on Conscientious Objectors during World War Two - and discussing other local stands 'of conscience' against militarism. Talk by Alice Collins, as part of Sheffield's 'Off the Shelf ' literature festival. Tickets £4/£3 from Sheffield Central Library Tel. Sheff. 2734737
