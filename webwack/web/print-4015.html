Voluntary Action Sheffield: Changing the World

Date, Time & Location Mon 28 Nov 2011, 1-4.30pm, Voluntary Action Sheffield, The Circle, 33 Rockingham Lane S1 4FW
Details Voluntary Action Sheffield (VAS) presents information, debate and workshops about using campaigning and influencing to make a positive difference. What works? A panel of people involved locally and nationally will share their ideas and experiences and take questions from the audience.
  • Harmit Kambo, Sheila McKechnie Foundation
  • Paul Hodgkin, Patient Opinion
  • Sarah Eldridge, City of Sanctuary
  • Simon Geller, Cycle Sheffield
  • Followed by the Annual General Meeting of VAS, including a short presentation by new Chief Executive, Sue White
A free event, but booking is compulsory, booking deadline Monday 21 November Booking information www.vas.org.uk
