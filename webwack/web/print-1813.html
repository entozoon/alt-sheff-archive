Sheffield Big Ride

Date, Time & Location Sat 25 Apr 2015, 10.30am assemble in Barkers Pool, city centre
Details You are welcome to bring your cycle and join hundreds of people of all ages riding together through the streets of Sheffield. A safe and fun mass ride, of about 6km. Cycle Sheffield volunteer marshals will ensure the group stays together and that the pace is suitable for even the youngest of riders. Expect music from a bike sound system and the ringing of hundreds of bicycle bells. If you like, bring a placard, decorate your bike, or wear red, the Space For Cycling colour. Timings: 10.30-11am assemble in Barkers Pool, 11am start, 12.30pm approximate finish time at Town Hall steps. Space For Cycling Sheffield Cycle Sheffield
