Morning Star South Yorkshire Conference

Date, Time & Location Sat 8 Jun 2013, 10am-4pm, St. Andrews Church, Hanover Way S3 7RQ
Details South Yorkshire Morning Star Readers and Supporters Group has organised this second annual conference in Sheffield following the very successful conference last year. The theme is "Challenges for the Future". In these challenging times there is a need for this wide ranging debate to inform future actions.
