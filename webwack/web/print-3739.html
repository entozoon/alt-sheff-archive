One Sheffield Many Cultures Festival

Date, Time & Location Sat 19 May 2012, 12-4pm, Barkers Pool, outside City Hall, S1 2HB
Details The annual One Sheffield Many Cultures festival shows our city at its best. This year showcasing the musical and dance talent of young people from schools across the city, featuring performances from 10 Sheffield secondary schools. Street-kick football with Football Unites, Racism Divides. Food stalls, and family fun. Opening by the Lord Mayor at 12 noon, entry free. www.onesheffieldmanycultures.org.uk
