Sharrow&#39;s Heritage

Date, Time & Location Mon 16 Mar 2015, 6-8pm The Old Junior School, South View Rd S7 1DB
Details Come for an evening exploring Sharrow's heritage. Starting with Mount Pleasant; a grade II* listed building 18th-century mansion situated on Sharrow Lane, this presentation will explore the site through a short film, exhibition of past photographs and letters written by the inhabitants, and a tour of the building. The aim for this event is to start a heritage group for Sharrow, supporting the promotion and care of these buildings and spaces in our community. Free, open to all, free refreshments.
