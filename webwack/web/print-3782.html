Residents against Station Closure meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 30 Apr 2012, 7pm, Victoria Centre, Victoria Methodist Church, Stafford Rd. S2 2SE
Details Sheffield railway station footbridge should remain open because it was built with public money, to be part of an integrated transport, bike and pedestrian network. RASC has has campaigned for 4 years against closure, and is unimpressed with the recent hurried, time-limited offer by the Secretary of State for a second bridge north of the station. To discuss these issues, everyone is welcome at this meeting. For further information contact Graham Wroe (Press Officer) on gswroe@yahoo.co.uk or 07752 473190 www.rasc-sheffield.com
