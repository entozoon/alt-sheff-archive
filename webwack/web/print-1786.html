Allotment 10th Birthday Family Fun Day

Date, Time & Location Sun 31 May 2015, 11am-2pm, Firth Park Community Allotment, Vickers Drive, S5 6TG
Details Firth Park Community Allotment is celebrating its 10th Anniversary this year! The community project has gone from strength to strength now spanning 4 allotments on the Brushes Allotments site. It is specially designed with the under 5’s in mind and welcomes regular visits from nursery, childminder and play groups. Come and help celebrate – the day will have:- Bug Hunts Grass Seed Head Making BIG Lunch Picnic – bring food to share VIP Grand Opening of the Potting Palace Tombola Site Tours Cake Stall Plant Stall Wooden Planter Making Demo & Heeley City Farm Animal Roadshow! Free Entry. Open to all – everyone from the community welcome! Enter by the big green gates on the junction of Vickers Drive and Road, just off Hucklow Road. Firth Park Community Allotment is one of Heeley City Farm’s family of food growing sites.
