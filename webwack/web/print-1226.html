Migration Matters Festival

Date, Time & Location Fri 24 Jun 2016, Theatre Delicatessen, 17 The Moor (old Woolworths shop) S1 3LR
Details Events for Friday, continuing the Migration Matters Festival. To Walk in Your Shoes, an exhibition by Rachael Munro-Fawcett continues 10.30am-6pm. 5.30pm-6.15pm, Scar Test, by Untold. “I came to England, scarred for life”. 6.30pm-9pm, Deaths by Rescue, by SYMAAG with Dr Simon Parker. Film & discussion on the refugee crisis. 9pm, Iftar with Open Kitchen. Food and conversation! Part of Refugee Week, celebrating sanctuary in Sheffield, which was the UK’s first City of Sanctuary. A partnership between Theatre Delicatessen and University of Sheffield supported by Arts Council England. All events are "Pay What You Decide". Migration Matters Festival continues through to 25th June 2016. Migration Matters Festival
