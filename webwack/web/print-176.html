Rite Trax on the Bole Hills

Date, Time & Location Sat 3 Aug 2019, 12-9pm, Bole Hill Recreational Ground, Northfield Avenue, S10 1QN
Details The 4th annual community market and festival in Bole Hills. Live performances from musicians, poets and artists, free Kids Zone & Workshop Village. Magicians, comedians, beatboxers, DJs, local food, drink, art and retail stalls and more. The first wave of announcements includes performances by (alphabetically): Adam Seymour Music, Benoit, Bloc2bloc Entertainment, Dave Viney, DJ Mike Pomranz, Doncaster Railway Social Club, Dr. Z, Frith, Harri Larkin, Heavy Lemo, InaVibe, Isaac Stovell, Leah Scorah, Liana Condor, LPB, Man of Steel, MOAN, NoSpace, Ragga Fanatics, Richard C. Bower, Riders Music, Rustique, Smiling Ivy, Spanish Caravan, Superlative, Them Sardines, Watermelon Maracas, Winston Hazel, Xybots. They are still looking for volunteers to join the team to help any time, from now through to Monday 5th August, to assist with various tasks. The festival event and RiteTrax as a business has been built with the help of countless volunteer hours from generous members of the community, If you want to add your help please contact them.
