Global Justice Sheffield meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 12 Jul 2017, 7pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St. James St. S1 2EW
Details Global Justice aims to change the actions, policies and institutions of governments, corporations and wealthy minorities, which are preventing an end to poverty and injustice. Part of a global movement, it works alongside people protesting in the developing world. This is the monthly meeting to discuss and plan upcoming events and campaigns. New members welcome.
