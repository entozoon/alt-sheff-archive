Torture: Why Britain Must Clean Up Its Act

Date, Time & Location Mon 20 May 2019, 6.30 to 7.30pm, Nice Neighbourhood, 338-346 Glossop Rd. S10 2HW
Details The UK in involved with torture as part of the "War on Terror". This is now a matter of public record. UK governments have denied this and tried to prevent independent investigation.
