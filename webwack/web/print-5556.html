Vestas day of action, Sheffield

Date, Time & Location Thurs 17 Sept 2009, from 4.30pm at Sheffield train station
Details Vestas have closed the UK's only wind turbine blade factories, on the Isle of Wight but workers who occupied the factory are still picketing. Leafletting at Sheffield train station (outside main entrance) from 4.30pm with a Vestas campaign broadsheets with details of their campaign, also collecting to raise more funds for the campaign to continue. The campaign calls for people to wear green ribbons to represent support for green energy and green jobs.
