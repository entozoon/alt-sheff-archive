Public meeting: How can Kashmir win freedom?

Date, Time & Location Thu 29 Aug 2019, 7pm, Sharrow Community Forum, Old Junior School, South View Rd. S7 1DB
Details A public meeting organised by Sheffield Socialist Workers Party arranged after Hindu nationalist President Modi of India cut off Kashmir, claiming it as a state of India and imprisoning activists. Indian administered Kashmir now has no telephone or internet access, there are rumours of killings and food shortages, and the Kashmiri communities in Sheffield and worldwide are worried and angry about the situation.
