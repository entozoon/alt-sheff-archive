"The Home Truth"

Date, Time & Location Thu 11 May 2017, 7-8pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Has privatisation of asylum housing worked? Should housing for the most vulnerable in society be run by a security company? Should asylum housing be traded like a commodity? ASSIST clients have no access to publicly funded housing. Join them to explore the impact this has on them, and the city. A Festival of Debate event, hosted by not-for-profit social enterprise Opus Independents (which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine).
