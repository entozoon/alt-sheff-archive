What are the Future Prospects for Co-operatives ?

Date, Time & Location Fri 7 Feb 2014, 2pm-4.30pm, Sheffield Town Hall
Details An invite to co-operatives across the City Region, to provoke thinking and consultation on the prospects and opportunities for co-operative developments, organised by The Social Economy Forum and stimulated by Ed Mayo, Secretary General Co-operatives UK and Meg Munn, Sheffield Heeley Labour & Co-operative MP. The aim is to stimulate discussion around what co-ops can offer across the Sheffield City Region, in terms of: 1. Growing economic prospects 2. Creating new job opportunities 3. Contributing to the social renewal If you would like to attend, or if you would like a briefing on what they are trying to achieve, please contact David Child, tel Sheff 2567400 or 07973 219431
