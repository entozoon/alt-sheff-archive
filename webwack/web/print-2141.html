Halloween Family Walk & Stories

Date, Time & Location Thu 30 Oct 2014, 6-7pm, Heeley City Farm, Richards Rd. S2 3DT
Details Meet outside the Farm Kitchen at 6pm, bring your pumpkin lantern and go on a Halloween walk! Starting at the cafe the walk will go around the Farm for some gruesome goings on and finish up at Alexandra Road Walled Garden for ghostly stories. Fill up on some free pumpkin soup and spooky treats in the cafe too. Come in fancy dress if you dare! Approx finish time 7pm. Children must be accompanied by parents/carers at all times and dogs must be on a lead. Ring Sheff 3039981 or email sharon.syec@heeleyfarm.org.uk for more info Heeley City Farm is 20 mins walk from Sheffield, or catch the No 47 bus to Herdings from town - it drops you right outside the Farm. email heeleycityfarm@live.co.uk
