Current Affairs discussion course

From Thur 22 Sep 2016, 10.30am-12.30pm, Central United Reformed Church, 60 Norfolk Str. S1 2JB

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

A Workers Educational Association discussion course looking at social, environmental, political and ethical issues, UK and world-wide. Topics to be agreed with the class. Runs for 11 sessions starting Sept 22nd till last meeting 8th Dec. (no meeting 27th Oct. - half-term). Enrolment is for the full term. Fees for 11 weeks £73 or FREE if on means-tested state benefits, or Working Tax Credit with income under £15,276. This Sheffield course is at a new venue. If you know anyone who might be interested, please do pass this on.The best way to guarantee a place is to enrol in advance online at www.wea.org.uk/yh, or by phone to WEA Regional Office (Leeds) 0113 2453304 or on the day - if so, please contact tutor first to say you are coming. Limited places available on the day in Sheffield. Courses need a minimum number of 12 participants in order to run and there is an upper limit of 16 for this course. If you cannot get to the first week, please make contact and the tutor will try to accommodate you later.
