ASSIST punk folk gig: Spiers and Boden

Date, Time & Location Tues 17 Nov 2009, 8pm at Library Theatre, Surrey Street, Sheffield S1 1XZ
Details John Spiers and Jon Boden, loud and proud, stories and dance music. Concert supported by The Bohemian Café in aid of Assist, supporting destitute asylum seekers sleeping rough in the city. Assist provides food, lodging, advocacy and encouragement to people who having fled human rights abuses and with often highly disturbing personal experiences find themselves turned away by a country that prides itself on its humanitarianism. Tickets £10/8 Contact Assist Sheff: 275 4960, email: assist_efa@hotmail.co.uk Tickets available online at www.wegottickets.com, and The Bohemian Café (53 Chesterfield Road, Sheffield S8 0RL)
