Green Party Leader speaks at public meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 11 Dec 2013, 6.45pm for 7pm, St Mary's, Bramall Lane S2 4QZ

Sheffield and Rotherham Green Party (SRGP) invite both members and non-members. Refreshments served from 6.45pm, the meeting will start promptly at 7pm.
