'Thank You Sheffield!'

Date, Time & Location Sat 17 Jul 2010, 10am-3pm
Details A Committee to Defend Asylum Seekers (CDAS) event for Summer of Sanctuary. A day of film, exhibition, food and one-to-one discussions to celebrate and acknowledge Sheffield's role in providing sanctuary, all taking place in a marquee on Barker's Pool (city centre). Attendance is free throughout the day, and all are welcome. For more information, go to www.cdas-sheffield.org.uk
