Peace in the Park planning meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 26 Oct 2011, 6pm, Highfield Library, London Rd S2 4NF
Details Sheffield's famous summer free festival is all organised by volunteers, giving the proceeds to charity. If you want to get involved or have any ideas, the open planning meetings are on the last Wednesday of each month, 6pm until 8pm (often followed by pub). All are welcome.
