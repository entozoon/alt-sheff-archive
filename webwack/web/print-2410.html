Sheffield Friday Night Ride (FNR)

Date, Time & Location Fri 16 May 2014, start 6.30pm Barkers Pool, city centre S1 2JA
Details Sheffield Friday Night Ride (FNR) offers a fun monthly evening bike ride for everyone. Rides are prepared in advance on themes. This month the theme is "Green Homes​ Sheffield"​. For Sheffield Environment Weeks they welcome you to cycle to see houses where residents are welcoming visitors and showing how their homes have used alternative technologies for a more sustainable lifestyle. Green Homes Sheffield Project is currently running a series of events. FridayNightRide website
