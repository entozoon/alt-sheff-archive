Sheffield Green Fair

Date, Time & Location Sat 26 June 2010, 11am-5pm at St. Mary's Community Centre, Bramall Lane, Sheffield
Details The 25th annual green fair, a great Sheffield day out and chance to find what's going on. Loads of different stalls including the latest green technology, food and drink, fun for kids of all ages, green living and wellbeing, all aimed at making the world a greener and fairer place. (Want to run a stall there? Booking deadline is 4 June). Website: Sheffield Green Fair
