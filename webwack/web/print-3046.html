Meeting: Stop Cuts to Legal Aid

Date, Time & Location Tue 11 Jun 2013, 6.30pm Exchange Brewery, 2 Bridge Street, Sheffield S3 8NS
Details Meeting discussing how cuts to legal aid and support affect migrants. Gina Clayton, immigration law expert, author, chair of ASSIST and South Yorkshire Refugee Law and Justice and (non-practising) immigration solicitor introduces the discussion. A South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group meeting, all welcome. For a summary of the legal changes see this briefing from the National Coalition of Anti-Deportation Campaigns www.symaag.org.uk
