Stand up for the planet

Date, Time & Location Sun 9 Jun 2019, 4-5pm Mam Tor Ridge, near Castleton, High Peak, Derbyshire
Details Peak District Against Fracking invite you to join them and stand together along Mam Tor and the Great Ridge in the heart of the Peak District on 9th June. Gather from 4pm at the Trig Point for a short walk and activities along the ridge. Bring friends and family, picnics and banners to make a day of it in this iconic landscape - a mass shout out for the environment. All the details including travel are on the link below.
