Postcapitalism Reading Group

Date, Time & Location Tue 28 Feb 2017, 7.30pm, The Bath Hotel, 66 Victoria St. S3 7QL
Details A group interested in political books. Now reading a recent book: "Inventing the Future: Postcapitalism and a World Without Work" by Nick Srnicek and Alex Williams [2015]. This meeting, in the small side room in the Bath Hotel, will cover Chapter 6, and focusing on Universal Basic Income. Discussion from a variety of left-wing/anti-capitalist viewpoints. Newcomers welcome.
