Rotherham Green Party Fundraiser and Action Day

Date, Time & Location Fri 13 Sep 2019, from 7pm, Dragons Tap pub, 477 Herringthorpe Valley Rd, Rotherham S65 3AD
Details Rotherham Greens supported by neighbouring local Green Parties in Doncaster and Barnsley are working hard to win their first Council seat and they need help (they don't accept large donations from large corporations so they rely on members and supporters to help).
