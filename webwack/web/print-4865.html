Introduction to Permaculture Course

Date, Time & Location Sat 7-Sun 8 Aug 2010
Details This is the second edition of Hazelhurst Community Supported Agriculture project's course for people to get involved in the project at Hazelhurst. It will be very high quality teaching by Joe Atkinson Learning Coordinator of the UK Permaculture Association. Trainees will learn Permaculture principles and focus on the design of the Community Supported Agriculture land. It includes camping on the 9 acre field.
  • 9.30am – 5.30pm with fabulous social event Saturday night.
  • Venue: Hazelhurst CSA Coop Ltd, Big Pond Field, Hazelhurst Lane S8 8BG
  • Cost £30-£120 based on income.
