St Mary’s Summer Fete

Date, Time & Location Sat 2 Jul 2016, 11am-5pm, St Mary’s Church, 1 Bramall Lane S2 4QZ
Details Summer Fete at St Mary’s Church, featuring live music, petting zoo, donkey rides, Pimms tent, alpacas, bouncy castle, brass band, vintage and craft stalls, and much more. Sheffield Green Party will be running fund raising activities: the live band stage, a cash bar, and stalls. St Mary’s (church, conference venue and community centre)
