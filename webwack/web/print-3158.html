The Hidden Perspectives launch event

Date, Time & Location Thu 11 Apr 2013, 6pm-8pm, Humanities Research Institute of Sheffield University, Gell Street
Details Introduction to a project "Bringing the Bible Out of the Closet". Hidden perspectives on gender, class, race and sexuality. Brief talks from Dr Katie Edwards, LaDIYfest Sheffield and students from the Biblical Studies department. There will be wine and nibbles on offer with a chance to meet the team and find out more about what’s going on – Everyone is welcome to attend!
