Film: "Thank You For The Rain"

Date, Time & Location Thu 28 Mar 2019, 7.30pm, University of Sheffield Students Union (Nelson Mandela Auditorium), Western Bank S10 2TG
Details The inspirational story of a Kenyan farmer who became an environmental activist after his livelihood was damaged by the effects of climate change. Tea and coffee from Coffee Revolution available before the event. Organised by Healthy Planet Sheffield, Students for Global Health Sheffield (SfGH), Greener Practice, and South Yorkshire GP Film Club. A free screening open to all: students, medical workers and the public. Tickets (free) at SignUpGenius
