Clap for our NHS

Date, Time & Location Thu 26 Mar 2020, 8pm, everywhere
Details Everyone is being encouraged to make a noise from their isolation, from doors, windows, gardens and balconies at 8pm. This is to give thanks for the NHS; the nurses, doctors, carers and all fighting Coronavirus. It might seem too little, it may seem childish, but why not do it - if only to show the NHS workers, the genuine gratitude for their sacrifices.
