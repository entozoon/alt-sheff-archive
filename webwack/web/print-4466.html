Sheffield Wildlife Trust Half Term Play Scheme: Free Activities for 5-13 Year Olds

Date, Time & Location Fri 25 Feb 2011, 1-3pm The Scout Hut, Welby Place, Norton Lees, Sheffield S8 9DA
Details THIS SESSION: making dream catchers and other things with willow, doing a treasure hunt, and doing our nature booklets. Sheffield Wildlife Trust's Half Term Play Scheme has 2 sessions each day with a variety of environmentally-themed activities; arts, crafts and games. 5-13 year olds are welcome to attend as many or as few sessions as they like. It's outdoors so wear appropriate clothing. Refreshments provided but if staying for the whole day bring a packed lunch. To book a place please contact Sheffield Wildlife Trust on Sheff 2634335 and ask for Jan or Stef. Or email j.breider@wildsheffield.com. They look forward to seeing you there!
