Repair Cafe

Date, Time & Location Sat 30 Nov 2013, 10-4pm, Heeley City Farm, Richards Rd. S2 3DT
Details A place for people who can mend stuff to bring their tools and get together with people with broken stuff and share skills and get stuff mended over a cuppa. Bring: Small Electrical appliances, toys, shoes, clothes, pots, kitchen equipment, computers, bikes, audio equipment, furniture…… They will see if they can help you mend it If you would like to volunteer as a repairer, please email info@repairsheffield.org to be registered. For more details ring Sheff 2580482 option 6 Heeley City Farm 20 mins walk from Sheffield, Bus 33 or 47.
