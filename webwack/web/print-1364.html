Sustainable Homes Discovery event

Date, Time & Location Wed 6 Apr 2016, 4-8pm, Central Atrium, Sheffield Hallam University
Details Green Homes Sheffield and Sheffield Hallam University have teamed up to create this event dedicated to sustainable homes. There will be a range of speakers, stalls and a chance to ask your questions. Hear about approaches to low-energy retrofit, the theories and issues involved, example case studies and how communities are organising. A free event but it is essential to book on the link below.
