Revolution Betrayed? What now for Egypt? Alliance for Workers Liberty public meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 6 Mar 2012, 7.30pm Victoria Hall, Chapel Walk S1 2PD
Details With Pete Radcliffe (UU activist from Derby). During his recent trip to Egypt Pete met up with various Labour Movement and other activists. Come and discuss the prospects of the Egyptian elections and the continuing strikes and workers struggles. For more info email: sheffield@workersliberty.org
