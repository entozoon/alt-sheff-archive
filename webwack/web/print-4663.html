Five Bad Reasons to Believe in God

Date, Time & Location Mon 15 Nov 2010, 7.30pm Showroom Cafe Bar, Paternoster Row, Sheffield
Details Skeptics in the Pub is about getting people together to have a relaxed and enjoyable evening with talks on a wide range of topics. This month Mike Hall, president of the Merseyside Skeptics Society, whose particular interests include alternative medicine, logic and Doctor Who, discusses the field of theology which seeks to present a rational basis for religious faith, and the claim that God exists. www.sheffield.skepticsinthepub.org
