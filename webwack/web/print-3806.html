Air Quality Symposium

Date, Time & Location Sat 21 Apr 2012, 9.30am-1.30pm, Medical School (next door to Hallamshire Hospital)
Details An Air Quality Symposium, organised by Broomhill Forum with sponsorship from Sheffield City Council through the Central Area Community Assembly and Sheffield Hospitals NHS Trust / University of Sheffield. Followed by free buffet lunch for participants who have registered on the website beforehand. Lots more detail on air pollution in Sheffield is also on the website. www.broomhillairqualitysymposium.org.uk
