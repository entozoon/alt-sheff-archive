Axe the Bedroom Tax Demonstration (Leeds)

Date, Time & Location Sat 5 Apr 2014 (Leeds - coaches from Sheffield)
Details This is a Yorkshire event, part of a nationwide series of demonstrations on April 5th against this vicious tax. Unite the Community is funding a free coach from Sheffield and Barnsley, so please come along: Leave Sheffield, Paternoster Row, 10.30am or Barnsley, White Bear (opposite Town Hall) 11am. Return 3pm.
