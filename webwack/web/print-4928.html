Abbeyfield Festival

Date, Time & Location Sun 11 July 2010, 1-6pm Abbeyfield Park
Details Performances and music from the whole community, local food and stalls, sports and circus skills, children's area, health zone, you name it, an annual day of fun in the sun. Plenty of buses along Barnsley Road: 47, 48, 75, 76 etc. No dogs this year at the festival please. Have you got the talent to perform in front of a festival crowd? Are you under 24? Get in touch and tell us what you can do, and you could be performing live in Osgathorpe or Abbeyfield Park. Contact Daniel at BCAF to get a form. For stalls, to perform or to volunteer, contact Burngreave Community Action Forum (BCAF) tel Sheff 2728008, email enquiries@bcaf.org.uk BCAF webpage about the festival
