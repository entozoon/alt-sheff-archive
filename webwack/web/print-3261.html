Stop the Closure of Stocksbridge Leisure Centre public meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 27 Feb 2013, 7.30pm, Stocksbridge Leisure Centre, Manchester Road, Stocksbridge
Details Public meeting called by 4SLC, a campaigning body open to anyone who accepts its aim of continued sport and leisure provision in the valley for the wider community, by keeping Stocksbridge Leisure Centre open as a vibrant facility. 4SLC developed out of the demonstration and earlier meetings and the "Working Group" established. It is constituted as fully independent, open and grass-roots, not dominated by any political party. Meetings are open to all members.
