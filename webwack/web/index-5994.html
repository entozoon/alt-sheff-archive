Portland Works under threat: Urgent support call-out!

Mar 2011

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Portland Works was the first place in the world to manufacture stainless steel cutlery. Current tenants and supporters, all volunteers, want to ensure the survival of one of Sheffield's great buildings by community purchase and social ownership, to continue with low-cost workshop space for small-scale manufacturing, independent artists and craftspeople.

And the forces of corporate power are against them. They need urgent action from the public to fight a Planning Application to convert Portland Works into flats, probably to be decided 11 April, 3 May or 23 May 2011. Please write to the Council objecting to the planning application: full details on the website.
