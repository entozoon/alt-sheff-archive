Public meeting: Asylum Seekers, Law and Justice

Date, Time & Location Thur 4 Mar 2010, 6-8.30pm at Lecture Theatre 5, Arts Tower, University of Sheffield Western Bank, S10 2TJ
Details A meeting for Sheffield activists and asylum seekers, lawyers and law students, to inform and involve them in local rights campaigning. Speaker Frances Webber - barrister, author and activist at the Institute of Race Relations. Followed by a discussion led by Gina Clayton on developing free legal services for asylum seekers in the city. Gina is author of 'Textbook on Immigration and Asylum Law' and a Trustee of City of Sanctuary. Organised by SYMAAG and Student Action for Refugees
