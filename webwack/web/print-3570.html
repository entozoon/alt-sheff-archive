ROTHERHAM SHOW

Date, Time & Location Sat 8 and Sun 9 Sep 2012, Clifton Park, Rotherham
Details Rotherham Show is the largest free land show in the North of England, a great day out. Entertainment, Horticultural Show, Vintage Vehicles, Fun Fair, Stalls and Refreshments, TomTillyTurnipsTomfoolery, music to suit all tastes, Main Arena, hosted by local radio station Rother FM. Additional events including a Fresh Produce and Craft Quarter and the "One Town, One Community Festival". Full list of events on website. RMBC Rotherham Show webpage
