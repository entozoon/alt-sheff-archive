Extinction Rebellion is working hard to grow and become more diverse and massive. When coronavirus eases, we still face climate chaos. The recent budget yet again doesn’t properly tackle the eco-crisis. A promised national net-zero infrastructure plan was delayed at the last minute. The hypocrisy of taxing renewables remains, while fossil fuels and airlines are subsidised, and huge sums are ready for building new roads. Despite hosting the COP26 later this year, our government is setting a terrible example to the world by not taking its own carbon emission pledges seriously.

Only ’people power’ will force the changes the planet needs. At the moment meetings and campaigns are continuing online. Fancy joining them? Contact XR Sheffield, or see the link below for newcomers.
