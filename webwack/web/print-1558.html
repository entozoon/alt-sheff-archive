PROTEST AGAINST THE BOMBINGS IN SYRIA

Date, Time & Location Sat 5 Dec 2015, 11am Sheffield Town Hall 11am,
Details As the Government blunders into war, opposition continues to grow. Sheffield Stop The War have called this demo at short notice. Bring your union banners, your friends and community groups. The campaign against the government war policies continues.
