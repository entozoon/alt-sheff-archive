UK Society for Co-operative Studies 51 st Annual Research Conferenc

Date, Time & Location Fri 31 Aug-Sun 2 Sep 2018, Sheffield Business School, Sheffield Hallam University S1 1WB
Details The UK Society for Co-operative Studies holds its Annual General meeting in Sheffield this year, hosted by FairShares Institute for Cooperative Social Entrepreneurship. The event title is "Diversity in Co-operation: People, place, and organisations", under the following themes: Co-operative History The Politics of Co-operation Co-operation in Public Service Co-operative Education Co-operative Retailing, Marketing and Sustainable Development New Approaches to Co-operation Leadership and Management in a Co-operative Context Co-operative Governance and Accounting There is a call-out for offers of papers, posters, panels and practical activities, click here.
