Cinema Syndicate - Foreigners out! Schlingensiefs Container

Date, Time & Location Tue 16 Nov 2010, 6.30pm Arts Tower Lecture Theatre 2, Western Bank S10 2TN
Details This week Cinema Syndicate takes a critical look at mainstream television; examining the seemingly ever-present and highly popular reality TV shows. Directed by Paul Poet the documentary follows the art project/television show of Christoph Schlingensief (previous underground filmmaker, film & theatre director, actor, artist and author) which he contributed to the Vienna's 2000 international festival of culture. The project, styled as a mockery of popular TV programme format Big Brother was critically aimed both at certain forms of television entertainment and at a latent xenophobia still thriving in the whole world.
