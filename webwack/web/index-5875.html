Feb 2014, deadline March

People of Sheffield - Peace in the Park needs your help to raise £12,000 by the end of March. There is £6308 in the bank, so just under £6000 is still needed to help make Peace in the Park 2014 the best ever! If everyone who came in 2012 was to donate £2 (or even better £2 a month) they could reach the target. Can you help?

Please donate now at: localgiving.com

By the way, the Peace in the Park radio show is on Sheffield Live! radio station (93.2fm), every Friday 5pm-6pm or catch the podcasts later.
