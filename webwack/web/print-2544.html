Benefit Dinner for Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity Campaign

Date, Time & Location Fri 14 Mar 2014, 7pm, The Umix Centre (FURD) 17 Asline Road, S2 4UJ
Details Come along enjoy this family event with delicious home made Palestinian food, as well as table tennis, pool, table football and many other activities for children. Proceeds will go to three children centres in Jabalia, Nussairat refugee camps and Khan Younis, for books, toys, and playground equipment as a modest contribution to empower Palestinian children who have been suffering long decades of Israeli occupation and illegitimate siege. £10 waged £7 concession.
