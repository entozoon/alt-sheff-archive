"Popular Protest in Palestine: The uncertain future of unarmed resistance"

Date, Time & Location Wed 24 Feb 2016, 7.30pm, United Reform Church, Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details Dr. Marwan Darweish is a Principal Lecturer who joined the Centre for Trust, Peace and Social Relations at Coventry University in 2009. He is the Course Director of the MA in Peace and Conflict Studies and Director of Post Graduate Certificate Course in Conflict Resolution Skills. Deeply concerned about the growing violent response to social, political and economic injustices in the world and the lack of constructive peaceful interventions, his current research focuses on peace processes, conflict transformation and nonviolence resistance. He will be speaking about his new book, "Popular Protest in Palestine: The uncertain future of unarmed resistance" Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign
