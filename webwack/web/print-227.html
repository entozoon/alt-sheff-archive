Sheffield Against Fracking monthly meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 4 Jun 2019, 7.30pm, Central United Reform Church, 60 Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details All are welcome to attend Sheffield Against Fracking meetings on the first Tuesday of each month, to support and plan campaigns and actions.
