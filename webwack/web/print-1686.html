NORTON SHOW

Date, Time & Location Sat 22 Aug 2015, from 10am, The Showground, off Bochum Parkway S8 8BA
Details Norton Show is an annual agricultural show, with a history going back over 100 years. Dog show, ploughing match, farmers market, horse riding, flower and produce competitions, etc. Entry charge, under 15s free. www.nortonshow.co.uk
