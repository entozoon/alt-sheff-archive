Public meeting 'Immigration - Blessing or Curse'

Date, Time & Location Tue 9 Nov 2010, 7-9pm, University of Sheffield, Lecture Room 3, Arts Tower, Western Bank, S10 2TN
Details A public meeting to discuss issues of migration under the Coalition Government, including the experience and rights of migrant workers. A South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG) evening event for the public. Confirmed speakers include
  • Andrew Geddes (migration writer, researcher and lecturer at Sheffield University Politics Department)
  • Marina Lewycka (Sheffield author of 'Two Caravans' and 'A Short History of Tractors in Ukrainian')
  • A trade union activist involved with organising migrant workers.
