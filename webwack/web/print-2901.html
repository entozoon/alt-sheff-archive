SAGE Greenfingers open day

Date, Time & Location Tue 10 Sep 2013, 11am-2pm, Grimesthorpe allotments, Grimesthorpe Rd. S4 8LE
Details SAGE Greenfingers promote the mental and physical well-being of adults experiencing mental health problems via the provision of creative therapeutic activities. The Open Day programme includes viewing the year's work, poetry walk about and photo workshop and a performance by local poet Matt Black. There will be a buffet lunch and an opportunity to make a veggie portrait and join in a treasure hunt! To help with catering numbers, please email enquiries@sagesheffield.org.uk
