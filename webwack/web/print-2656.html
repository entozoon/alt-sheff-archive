Sheffield Save Our NHS meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 20 Jan 2014, 7pm Victoria Hall Methodist Church, 60 Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details Sheffield Save Our NHS is fighting the appalling government plans for cuts, competition and privatization for the benefit of private companies. Get involved, show your support for the NHS. This meeting is an update on national and local developments including plans to recommission musculoskeletal services in Sheffield. www.sheffieldsaveournhs.co.uk
