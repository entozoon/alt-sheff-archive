South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG) meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 2 Nov 2015, 6.30pm Theatre Delicatessen, 17 The Moor S1 4PF
Details SYMAAG unites refugee, asylum and migrant organisations with campaign groups and supporting individuals. It campaigns both for the rights of those seeking asylum and of those coming to work in this country. This month they will discuss anti-detention campaigning, ideas from new members about campaign priorities and how to continue the momentum of the Refugees Welcome protest in Sheffield. Note the new venue and day: Theatre Delicatessen is the former Woolworths building on The Moor. www.symaag.org.uk ,
