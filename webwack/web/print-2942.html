Lowedges Festival,

Date, Time & Location Sun 11 Aug 2013, 12-5pm, Greenhill Park, Gresley Road, Lowedges S8 7HN
Details The popular Lowedges Festival returns to Greenhill Park. An annual community festival organised jointly by Greenhill and Bradway Tenants and Residents Association and the Friends of Greenhill Park. Music, dance, falconry display, dog show and agility activities (try your dog or see the experts), Escafeld Medieval show and history area, guinea pig competition and ferret exhibition, parrot display, tiny pony and donkey rides, mini zoo area, classic cars, vans and bikes, farmers market, car boot sale (please pre-book), funfair area, free full-size dodgems (sponsored by the Co-op), food from around the world, football and bike skills areas, organ grinders, mini steam train rides only 50p. Plus the usual face painting, Punch and Judy, and quiet area in the bowling area with cream teas. Wow! What a great free show. Details on the Sheffield Help Yourself website
