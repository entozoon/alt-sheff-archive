Curry night and auction for Cuba

Date, Time & Location Fri 12 Mar 2010, 7pm upstairs at the Rutland Arms, Brown St. S1 2BS
Details The Cuba Cycle challenge is 355 km ride over 6 days in Cuba in April/May. Sue Clarke and Ros Wollen are undertaking it, covering their own travel and accommodation costs. They aim to raise £2600 to be paid directly into the MUSIC FUND FOR CUBA to support two projects in Havana, for: (1) Essential educational and musical equipment for a school for visually impaired children. (2) The ongoing restoration of the Miramar community theatre. Join them for this fundraiser: 7pm Delicious homemade Asian Cuisine: inclusive meal for £10 9pm Auction. Come and buy skills and items you so badly need and want! Auction list will be circulated. If you don't want to eat, then come along for a drink and the auction only.
