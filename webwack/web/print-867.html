Nottingham Viva! Vegan Festival

Date, Time & Location Sat 6 May 2017, 10am-6pm, Nottingham Conference Centre, Burton St, Nottingham NG1 4BU
Details A vegan festival in a nearby city, well worth a day out. After Viva!´s Cardiff event another chance to meet some great like minded people, immersed in vegan food treats. International dishes, convenience foods, nutritional guidance, recipes and helpful factsheets, cookery demos and plenty of information stalls and talks. Vegan stuff from bags to chocolate, clothes to activism. Festival run by Viva! and sponsored by Hempish and Weleda. Standard entry £3, under 16´s free, ´VIP´ £13 including goody bag and no-queue entry. Free entry vegan beer festival from noon at The Peacock vegan pub, 1 Mansfield Road, Nottingham NG1 3FB, until 1am. Free entry vegan after-party from 7pm at Sumac Vegan Social Club, 245 Gladstone Street, Nottingham NG7 6HX. Nottingham Viva! Vegan Festival
