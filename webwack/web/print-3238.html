Global Grooves II

Date, Time & Location Thu 7 Mar 2013, 8.30pm, Penelopes, 53-55 Arundel Gate S1 2DN
Details Ashanti Beats and STAR (Student Action For Refugees) present a colourful collision of soulful reggae, Congolese harmonies and swing serenades, featuring:
  • Truly Apparent: Sheffield's finest reggae/lover's rock band
  • Umubano: Lovely harmonies via Congo
  • Satsuma: Fruity electro swing duo from exotic Notts
  • + a demo from Cordão de Ouro Capoeira
  • Followed by sets from Jah Reynolds (Frozac) and Yosemite Sam
Tickets £3 adv £4 on the door tel 07583 062717 Proceeds in aid of ASSIST and Conversation Club. Penelopes
