National March for Dignity

Sat 29 June 2013

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Sheffield joins in the National March for Dignity as part of Refugee Week, representing a call for dignity for asylum seekers and an end to destitution. 11am Meet at Ellesmere Green (Burngreave, at top of Spital Hill) to march through Sheffield12.30pm Arrival at City Hall for a gathering with speakers.1.00pm Leaving via The MoorDestination St Mary's, Bramall Lane, where refreshments will be available.This march is organised by refugee and asylum community groups in Sheffield, who encourage everyone to join in to show solidarity. Refugee Week is a UK-wide programme of arts, cultural and educational events that celebrates the contribution of refugees to the UK and promotes better understanding of why people seek sanctuary. Anyone can take part by organising, attending or taking part in activities.Refugee WeekASSIST Sheffield
