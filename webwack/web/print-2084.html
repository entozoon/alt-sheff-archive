Amnesty International December Meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 10 Dec 2014, 6pm-9pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Amnesty International December meeting falls on Human Rights Day so what better way than to spend it than mark the day with an extra long meeting. 6pm-7.15pm: Every December, Amnesty runs the Write for Rights campaign where cards are send with messages of hope. Come down and write a card! 7.15pm-9pm: Local solicitor and secretary of the National Council for Civil Liberties, Michael McColgan is to talk about human rights. Plus a Christmas quiz to celebrate the end of another year of campaigning! Sheffield Amnesty International on Facebook
