Sheffield Equality Group meeting

Date, Time & Location Thur 13 May 2010, 7pm, St Matthews, Carver St S1 4FE
Details Sheffield Equality Group meets on the second Thursday of the month and aims to campaign in a way that interests and excites people. If you have an idea about a campaign or something you want to do around income inequality, or just want to chat on the subject then come along. Donations for room hire, all welcome.
