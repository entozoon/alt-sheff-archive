Music and Socialism

Date, Time & Location 1 Aug 2010, 7.30-9.30pm at Rotherham Trades Club, S60 1RB
Details A talk about how music has always been part of young people's and working class resistance to oppression and inequality. Rotherham Trades Club is on Greasbrough Road, just past The Bridge pub, very close to Rotherham town centre and bus and railway stations.
