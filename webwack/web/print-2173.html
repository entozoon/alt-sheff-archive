Film: Occupation 101

Date, Time & Location Wed 1 Oct, 5pm The Void, Harmer Level 1, (Owen Building) Sheffield Hallam, Howard St. S1 1WB
Details Occupation 101 is one of the best documentaries to watch to introduce yourself to the Palestinian Struggle...from the rise of Zionism, through to the second Intifada and the conditions today. An award winning documentary which includes interviews from American, Palestinian and Israeli scholars, religious leaders, humanitarian workers and NGOs. Free entry with complementary snacks.
