Residents Against Station Closure protest

Date, Time & Location Fri 5 Nov 2010, 4.30pm, Railway Station
Details Another noisy protest to make a bonfire of East Midland Trains' ridiculous plan of closing the footbridge over the station. Limiting passage to ticket holders only would shut the vital access link between the town centre and the communities behind it. Residents Against Station Closure (RASC) website which has a 'hot line' so RASC can respond quickly to news of bridge blockages.
