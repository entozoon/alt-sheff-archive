Sheffield Anarchist Feminist Network meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 31 Aug 2010, 7pm upstairs in Rutland Arms, 86 Brown Street S1 2BS
Details A group of anarchist feminists which shares ideas and works together in direct action and solidarity. Anyone is welcome regardless of gender, whether they would class themselves as anarchist feminist or not, as long as respectful and rejecting all racist, sexist and homophobic views because the network is a liberated space.
