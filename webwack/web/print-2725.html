Porter Brook visioning event

Date, Time & Location Thu 28 Nov 2013, 2-5pm, Yorkshire Art Space, The Atrium, Persistence Works 21 Brown St. S1 2BS
Details An opportunity to have your say about the Porter Brook, and proposals for a "pocket park" to improve water quality, bio-diversity and green the built environment, reflecting the history, culture and heritage. Get an overview of the site, and give your views on what could be done. Limited places - why? - first come first served, to book a place contact: Sara.Parratt-Halbert@syforest.co.uk
