"Talking Politics"

Date, Time & Location Thu 18 Apr to 11 Jul 2013, 10am, St. Matthews Rooms, Carver St. S1 4FT.
Details A discussion-based course looking at current political developments and issues in the UK and world-wide. Topics are usually suggested by the class. Past topics include policing, the Euro, the NHS, immigration, the politics of food, the coalition government, nuclear energy, the "Arab Spring", overseas aid, Israel/Palestine, civil liberties, climate change, and many more. This unique course has just started and needs numbers to keep it running, if you think you may be interested please have a word with the tutor, Paul Boizot, see www.paulboizot.co.uk/courses.htm 12 sessions running through till July 11, no meeting May 30 (half-term). A WEA (Workers Educational Association) course. Cost £65, or FREE if on means-tested benefits, or Working Tax Credit and income under £15K.
