Sheffield Climate Alliance monthly meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 18 Aug 2015, 7pm, Shakespeare pub (upstairs), Gibraltar St. S3 8UB1 2EW
Details Note change of venue this month - normally at Quaker Meeting House This regular meeting of an alliance of Sheffield climate activists is open to newcomers, all welcome. The discussion will include the planned climate jobs conference in Sheffield on 19th September, and other activities including planning for a big event in Sheffield at the start of the Paris climate conference in November.
