Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change (SCACC) meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 19 Apr 2011, 7.30pm-9.30pm, Highfield Library, London Rd S2 4NF (buses 75, 76, 97, 98, 20, 20A +)
Details The first half hour - 7.30 till 8 - will be a Sheffield Climate Alliance meeting to discuss the campaign to achieve an Energy Descent Plan for Sheffield - if your group would like to get involved, please come along. At 8pm a 40 minute discussion led by Chris Broome who will share his critique of the the Council's 'Decentralised Energy City' document. The agenda will also include: - feedback on 'what SCACC stands for' - feedback on progress re Food event - info re Nick Clegg 'climate reconnection' - organising for stalls and petitions at summer festivals SCACC organises days of action and demonstrations, petitions to Sheffield Council, supports related national and local campaigns and runs film shows and talks for local groups. The regular meeting is open to newcomers, all welcome. SCACC website
