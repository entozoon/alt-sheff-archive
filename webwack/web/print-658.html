Public meeting: Stand Up To Islamophobia

Date, Time & Location Tue 28 Nov 2017, 7pm, Room 5, Showroom Cinema, 15 Paternoter Row S1 2BX
Details As part of Islamophobia Awareness Month, a public meeting hosted by Stand Up To Racism Sheffield on racist attacks, the "Prevent strategy", "Trojan Horse", the refugee crisis and attacks on Muslim organisations. Enough is enough! Join the fight back! Speakers: Nadeem Murtuja (Just Yorkshire) Amina Khan (MEND) Abdullah Wasim (Sheffield Hallam University SU BME rep) Shezana Hafiz (CAGE) Amy Murch (NEU) Includes screening of a short documentary of the Student Convoy to Calais in 2016. Details by email: sheffieldsutr@gmail.com Facebook event page - Stand Up To Racism Sheffield
