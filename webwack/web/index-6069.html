Peace in the Park FOOD fundraiser for Heeley City Farm

Sat 12 June 2010

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

The Farm Kitchen is joining forces with the ladies of Treats and Beats Cafe to provide savoury snacks, lunches, yummy cakes and refreshments as part of the Community Stage at the brilliant free festival Peace in the Park 2010. The menu will showcase locally available, seasonal produce. Profits will be donated to Heeley City Farm.

They are looking to recruit a few more volunteers for this event. Anyone interested in food preparation, decor making and/or helping on the stall should contact Helen on Sheff 2580244 or email: hcottle @ gmail.com

Website: www.heeleyfarm.org.uk
