Free film: Chasing Ice

Date, Time & Location Wed 1 May 2013, 7pm, Red Deer pub, 18 Pitt St. S1 4DD
Details The Green Film Festival 2013 runs on 4 Wednesday evenings from April into May. Sheffield Friends of the Earth invite you to FREE films with after-film discussions. Today: Chasing Ice. National Geographic photographer James Balog across the Arctic as he deploys time-lapse cameras designed for one purpose: to capture a multi-year record of the changing glaciers. The Red Deer is on Pitt Street, a small road off Mappin Street, just off West Street). Map at www.red-deer-sheffield.co.uk/location.htm The Green Film Festival 2013
