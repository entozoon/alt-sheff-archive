Public meeting: Why we should not be celebrating The Balfour Declaration – a Jewish perspective

Date, Time & Location Mon 23 Oct 2017, 7:30pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Green Party member Robert Cohen, blogger and dissident, talks about the impact of the Balfour Declaration in 1917 which offered British government support for the establishment of a "national home for the Jewish people" in Palestine, then an Ottoman region with a minority Jewish population.
