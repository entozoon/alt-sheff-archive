Small Publishers Book Fair

Date, Time & Location Sat 3 Nov 2012, from 10am, Bank Street Arts Centre, 32-40 Bank St. S1 2DS
Details A chance to see and buy books not usually to be found in your local bookshop. The fair will include hand made books, niche publishers of poetry, fine art and architecture along with a programme of talks, workshops and readings to support the event. Admission free. No need to book. For details Tel. 07812 167380. Part of Off the Shelf, Sheffield's 21-year old annual festival of words, with a great programme of over 200 events this year. www.offtheshelf.org.uk
