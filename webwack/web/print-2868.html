Peace in the Park 5-a-side fundraiser

Date, Time & Location Sun 22 Sep 2013, 11-5pm, Ponderosa Park, entrance via Oxford St. S10 1BA
Details With the help of friends at Action for Involvement and Regather, ‘Peace in the Park’ is putting on a family friendly 5-a-side football tournament to support Sheffield’s fantastic annual free festival. Additional proceeds go to the festival’s nominated causes for this year, local charities ASSIST and Roundabout. There are 22 team places available for adults and under-18 groups, with trophies and prizes to be won! Adult team entry is £25 and under 18′s team entry is £15. A deposit of £10 per team is required in advance before confirmation of booking is complete. Single sex or mixed teams are welcome. Under 18 teams will not be expected to play against 18+ teams. Register by online form, and invite your friends and family as loyal supporters. FREE entry for supporters, there will be plenty for everyone to do. Hot and cold food and refreshments will be on sale from the Regather Kitchen, and a range of family-friendly activities will be happening throughout the day, plus event sponsors ‘Action for Involvement’ are offering special FREE activity workshops. Event on the top field of the park, with entrance via Oxford Street. Please note, parking is limited on the roads around the park. 5-a-side website Map Peace in the Park website
