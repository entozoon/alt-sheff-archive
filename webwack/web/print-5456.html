Sheffield Doc/Fest's world premiere Moving To Mars

Date, Time & Location From Wed 4 Nov 2009, at Showroom Cinema, Paternoster Row, Sheffield S1 2BX (near the train station)
Details The long-running documentary film festival Sheffield Doc/Fest opens this year with the world premiere of Mat Whitecross' film Moving To Mars . The film follows a Burmese refugee family, who after 20 years in a Thai refugee camp, are offered a new life in Sheffield by a UN relocation scheme. Whitecross' previous film was the highly acclaimed The Road To Guantanamo which won a Silver Bear at 2006 Berlinale. For more information about the documentary festival see http://sheffdocfest.com
