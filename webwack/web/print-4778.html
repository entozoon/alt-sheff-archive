Sheffield Anarchist Feminist Network - Meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 22 Sep, 7-9pm Rutland Arms, 86 Brown Street, S1 2BS
Details Some anarchist feminists in Sheffield have created a network to share ideas, work together in direct action and join together in solidarity. They invite anyone who wishes to discuss anarchist feminist issues whether they would class themselves as anarchist feminist or not to join in at meetings or events, regardless of gender. They only require that people are respectful and reject all racist, sexist and homophobic views because this network is a liberated space.
