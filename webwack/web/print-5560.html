Sheffield Green Drinks: Fruit Juicing with Abundance

Date, Time & Location Tues 15 Sep 2009, 5pm - 7.30pm Sharrow Old Junior School, Southview Road, S7 1DB
Details Abundance is a project to harvest the seasonal glut of local fruit like apple, pear and plum. Each year hundreds of fruit trees go unpicked either because people donâ€™t notice them, may not be physically able to harvest them or there are just too many fruits at one time. Abundance is a team of volunteers who have been helping harvest city fruit and redistributing the surplus to the community on a non-profit basis - to community cafes, nurseries, Surestarts and individuals. Abundance also juices tonnes of fruit and make jams, pickles and preserves. Fruit juicing will be led by Daniele Rinaudo, an Abundance volunteer. A hand powered press, electrical juicer and other essential equipment will be available to use. Juicing will be done outside, but undercover - so whatever the weather we'll be juicing! They have a supply of apples, but if you can it would fantastic to bring a bag of locally harvested apples, pears, plums etc. to juice. www.sheffieldgreendrinks.org.uk
