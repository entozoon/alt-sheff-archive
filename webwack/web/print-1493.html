Amnesty International letter-writing meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 27 Jan 2016, 7.15pm University Arms, 197 Brook Hill S3 7HG
Details Every 4th Wednesday of the month Sheffield Amnesty International holds this informal get-together to write campaign letters, a vital activity in their opposition to human rights violations across the world. The University Arms pub is next to Sheffield University and Weston Park roundabout. Sheffield University tram stop, or several buses: 51 & 52 best; 94 & 95 close by. If you've never been to an event and would like to find out more, just send an email or Facebook message. Sheffield Amnesty International Facebook
