Don't Be A Dick: Etiquette for Atheists and Skeptics

Mon 11 Oct 2010, 7.30pm Showroom Cafe Bar, Paternoster Row, Sheffield

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

It's not easy being a rational person in what often seems to be in irrational world. How do you stay true to your skeptical standpoint without sounding like a tiresome know-it-all? Rebecca Watson presents helpful tips using real-life examples and pre-recorded reenactments starring Jon Ronson, Professor Chris French, and Dr Adam Rutherford of BBC's The Cell. Skeptics in the Pub is about getting people together to have a relaxed and enjoyable evening with talks on a wide range of topics.www.sheffield.skepticsinthepub.org
