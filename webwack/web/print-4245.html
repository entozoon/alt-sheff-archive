Skeptics in the Pub: The Psychopath Test

Date, Time & Location Mon 27 Jun 2011, 7.30pm, Showroom Cafe Bar, 15 Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details Jon Ronson, documentary maker and award-winning writer of bestsellers Them: Adventures with Extremists and The Men Who Stare at Goats will be talking about his latest book The Psychopath Test (due for publication June 2011). Skeptics in the Pub is about getting people together to have a relaxed and enjoyable evening while listening to talks on a wide range of topics, usually by a notable scientist or skeptic. Talks are typically about a common belief that can be demonstrated to be false or cannot be justified by the available evidence.
