BHS closed; Sir Philip Green got rich

Summer 2016

The BHS store on The Moor is now closed, as a Parliamentary report slams Sir Philip Green and his cronies for potentially fraudulent extraction of profit from the group. Paul Blomfield (Labour, Sheffield Central) is one of the MPs on the Business, Innovation and Skills Committee.
