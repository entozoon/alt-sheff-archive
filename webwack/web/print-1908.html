Sheffield Zine Fest

Date, Time & Location Sat 14 Mar 2015, 11am-6pm, Electric Works S1 2BJ
Details The third annual Sheffield Zine Fest, celebrating DIY publishing in all its forms. As well as stalls from all over the country, there is a full schedule of workshops throughout the day. Get in touch if you wish to book your table. Electric Works is near the Sheffield Interchange (bus station). Sheffield Zine Fest
