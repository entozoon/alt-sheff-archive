Big Boulder Music and Arts Festival

Date, Time & Location Sun 8 Sep 2013, 12noon-6pm, Heeley Millennium Park
Details The second annual Big Boulder Music and Arts Festival, a community celebration with live music in Heeley Millennium Park free for all to enjoy and share. Tandem The Hungarian Lanterns Rita Payne Maia Big Tent and the Gypsy Lantern Dave Formula and the Finks (former Magazine and Visage keyboardist) Food stalls, beer tent, free craft activities for children, kite-making, woodland crafts and nature trails and environment arts collective ResponsibleFishingUK will be providing stone balancing sessions, with members of the public encouraged to create seemingly impossible towers of stone. (Note: limited car-parking spaces) Bus services 20, 20A, 25, 25A, 43, 44 and 53 stopping at the Heeley Retail Park and the 47 and 48 get off at Spencer Road. Organised by the Heeley Development Trust (HDT), in partnership with local promoters Four Rivers. Facebook page or contact Bev Nunn, Events and Involvement Organiser for HDT on Sheff 2500613
