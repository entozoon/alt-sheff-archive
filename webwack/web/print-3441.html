Sheffield Cycle Jumble

Date, Time & Location Sat 3 Nov 2012 from 9.30am, St. Marys Community Centre, Bramall Lane S2 4QZ
Details Sheffield’s premier cycle jumble is to take place this Saturday, and the forecast is looking good. 40 tables now booked. Outside space is still available. Bring a table/sell from your boot. Come down and have a look at what’s on offer. Usually a good mix of old and new, road and mountain bike stuff. The cafe will be open too. Entry £1. www.sheffieldcyclejumble.wordpress.com
