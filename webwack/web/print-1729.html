STANNINGTON CARNIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sat 11 Jul 2015, 12-4.30pm, Stannington Park, Oldfield Terrace, S6 6DP
Details The 24th year of this great community-run carnival. Stannington Carnival Organisers & Festival Friends (SCOFF) plan fairground rides, clowns, games, birds of prey, food, bouncy castles, belly dancing, a variety of fabulous classic cars on display, stalls selling hot and cold food from around the world and beautiful handmade crafts and more. For information about stalls or general questions email Penny Simpson at stanningtoncarnival@gmail.com or tel. Sheff 2339646
