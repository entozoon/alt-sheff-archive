Memorial Day for the Earth

Date, Time & Location Fri 3 Dec 2010, 10.30-2.30pm Winter Gardens, Sheffield centre
Details The UN climate summit in Cancun, Mexico is marked in Sheffield, to remind people that climate change is a global emergency, but we can achieve a zero carbon Britain. From 10.30 there will be activities for passers by, to raise awareness: free gifts, messages of hope, etc. 12-1pm songs, poems, readings, candles and messages. 1.15pm assembling in Tudor Square for a procession, dressed in black (mourning) at the front, followed by a rainbow of alternatives... from Tudor Square, Norfolk Row, Fargate (up and down), Pinstone St, up and down Moor and back to Town Hall about 2.30pm Organised by Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change in conjunction with environment and faith networks.
