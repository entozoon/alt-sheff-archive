The EU Referendum: What does it mean for Sheffield?

Date, Time & Location Wed 20 Apr 2016, 6pm, St Marys Church, Bramall Lane S2 4QZ
Details The UK faces a momentous decision, so there is a need for more information and impartial analysis about the EU. This event will debate the main questions and members of the audience will be given the chance to pose questions. Panel featuring Dr Matthew Wood (Crick Centre), Susan White (Voluntary Action Sheffield), Gary Bell (ASSIST), Julie Kenny (Pyronix), Prof Simon Bulmer (University of Sheffield) and Prof Michael Keating (University of Aberdeen). Presented by the University of Sheffield in association with Festival of Debate, hosted by Opus Independents (which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine).
