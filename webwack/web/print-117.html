Global Justice Sheffield AGM and Film Screening

Date, Time & Location Wed 2 Oct 2019, 6.15pm, Central United Reformed Church, 60 Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details Global Justice Sheffield will be screening the multi-award winning documentary, "La Buena Vida", at 7pm following their AGM (Annual General Meeting) at 6.15pm. "La Buena Vida" is the inspiring story of a struggle of an indigenous community in northern Colombia, against coal mining on their land. Drinks and refreshments available from 6.45pm and guests are welcome, for both AGM and film or just the film. See you there! Global Justice Sheffield
