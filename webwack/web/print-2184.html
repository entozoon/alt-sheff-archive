Meeting: Against the Trans-Atlantic Trade and Investment Partnership (TTIP)

Date, Time & Location Wed 24 Sep 2014, 7pm, Central United Reformed Church, 60 Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details A meeting to discuss the secret talks between the governments of the UK and the US taking place on the Trans-Atlantic Trade and Investment Partnership (TTIP). This will result in big corporations holding tremendous power over 850 million lives. The speaker will be Linda Kaucher who is a trade treaty researcher. Linda is a campaigner against TTIP and lectures at the London School of Economics and London Metropolitan University. To learn more about the effects of this Treaty do come along to what will be a most informative evening. There will be the opportunity to ask questions and take part in the discussion. Organised by Sheffield Morning Star Supporters Group. More on TTIP at Think Left and Ethical Consumer
