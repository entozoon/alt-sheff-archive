Talk: “Fair Admissions”

Date, Time & Location Tue 1 Apr 2014, 8pm, The University Arms, 197 Brook Hill, S3 7HG
Details A talk by Mike Granville addressing the current unfair arrangements for admissions to faith schools, which are fully funded by general taxation. The information for this talk is provided by the Accord Coalition, an organisation that includes people from different faiths and none. Organised by the Sheffield Humanist Society, all welcome. www.sheffieldhumanists.org.uk
