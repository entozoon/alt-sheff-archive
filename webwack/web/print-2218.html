BARNSLEY GAY PRIDE

Date, Time & Location Sun 7 Sep 2014, Mandela Gardens, The Civic, Hanson Street, Barnsley S70 2HZ
Details Run by Barnsley LGBT Forum working in partnership with BMBC, Barnsley South Yorkshire Police, South Yorkshire Fire Service and many others, Barnsley Gay Pride (Barnsley's got pride!) was launched back in September 2009 to bring everyone a fun day and evening. Barnsley Gay Pride on Facebook
