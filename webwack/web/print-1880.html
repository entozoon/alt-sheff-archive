"AIDS Denialism...Yep That's A Thing"

Date, Time & Location Mon 23 Mar 2015, 7.30pm, Farm Road Sports & Social Club, Farm Rd. S2 2TP
Details Unbelievably there are a small number of people who are sceptical of the “official story” about AIDS. Although these people are small in numbers they are extremely well funded and can pose a very real threat to public health. A Skeptics in the Pub evening. Talks are typically about a common belief that can be demonstrated to be false or cannot be justified by the available evidence. The events are free though they ask for a £3 donation, and the meetings are open to all whatever your beliefs and views so please come along. Skeptics in the Pub
