Sheffield Friday Night Ride: The Christmas Jumper Ride

Date, Time & Location Fri 12 Dec 2014, meet 6pm, Giant bike store, Queens Rd/Asline Rd
Details Sheffield Friday Night Ride (FNR) offers a fun monthly evening bike ride for everyone. Rides are prepared in advance on themes, for this time of year get ready for The Christmas Jumper Ride! The real theme is enjoying and giving. This is a ride around Christmas lights, street parties and foodbanks, arriving at Bradway for refreshment at the Castle Inn. Finally descending Twentywell Lane back into the city centre along Abbeydale Road, stopping for chips. Meet 6.00pm for free mince pies and mulled wine courtesy of Andy and all who work at the Giant bike store, Queens Rd/Asline Road, Secure bike parking available. NOTE You will need to bring certain things, see website for details. Sheffield Friday Night Ride
