"Bin Laden: The One Man Show"

Date, Time & Location Thu 1 Jun 2017, 7.30pm, Theatre Delicatessen, 17 The Moor, S1 3LR
Details The world’s most notorious terrorist tells his story in this remarkable, provocative and multi-award-winning one-man show. This incendiary, intelligent debut from Knaïve Theatre provides a fresh perspective, creating within the unthinkable a space for debate and dialogue. Part of Festival of Debate, in association with Theatre Delicatessen, tickets £7-£10. Festival of Debate
