"The Chilean Coup 40 Years On: Sheffield Community and Academic Perspectives"

Date, Time & Location Fri 8 Nov 2013, University of Sheffield
Details 40 years ago a military coup plunged Chile into a brutal right-wing dictatorship, the first country in the world to experience the shock of "neoliberalism". Many fled the country, some arriving here to make Sheffield their new home. This study day at the University of Sheffield coincides with the last day of the exhibition "Arpilleras, Photographic and Political Caricatures" listed here separately.
