Dr Bike free check-up and tagging session

Date, Time & Location Tue 31 Jul 2012, 8:30-1:30pm Jessops West, Leavygreave Rd, Sheffield
Details Dr Bike offers a free check-up for your bike. You can also register your bike and other gadgets on a national register used by South Yorkshire Police and other Police forces across the country. Working in partnership with the University of Sheffield, police officers will be on hand to give advice on what types of lock to use and register your bike on www.immobilise.com. They can also show how to register things like laptops, smart phones, and MP3 players. www.cyclesheffield.org.uk
