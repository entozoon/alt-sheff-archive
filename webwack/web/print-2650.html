Sheffield Friday Night Ride

Date, Time & Location Fri 24 January 2014
Details A fun monthly evening bike ride for everyone. Rides are prepared in advance on themes. This time it is the London-Sheffield ride, around the many Sheffield streets and places with London-related names. Check details and starting point on the website. (Note this ride was previously mentioned as being on the 17th, but 24th Jan is confirmed as correct) FridayNightRide website
