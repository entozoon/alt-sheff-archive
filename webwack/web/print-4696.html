Sheffield Communist Forum - Defending the state? Welfare cuts and the social wage

Date, Time & Location Wed 3 Nov 2010, 7pm Upstairs at the Rutland Arms, Brown St. Sheffield
Details An open, independent forum to discuss class struggle and communist theory in Sheffield and South Yorkshire. Questions: Do we simply defend the "welfare state"? Will the cuts smash or restructure "welfare"? Are the cuts an economic necessity? Can Trade unions defend jobs and services? Reading: Issue 18 of The Commune, Oisin Macgiollamoir: Are the cuts necessary? And see linked debate from the article. Comments by Robert Kirby, Boffy and Bill Jeffries.
