&#39;Noche de Muertos&#39;

Date, Time & Location Sun 30 Oct2011, 7pm-late, Cubana, 34 Trippet Lane, S1 4EL
Details Steel City Presents a night of music and dance inspired by the Mexican and Nicaraguan celebrations for Day of the Dead. Traditional Mexican music and dance from Sheffield's very own 'Son de America'. Cumbia, bachata, merengue, salsa, reggaeton, hip-hop. Take a gamble on the 'Rifa de Fortuna' to win a spicy surprise. Plus mystery gift for every ticket holder. Dance the night away to Latin rhythms with DJs Chris Welch (Adelante/Brasilica) and Ruth Joy (Showroom/Phonetics). Tickets £3 full/£2 concessions. Advance tickets available at concession rate call: 07909 912753. All proceeds from this event will go to support JONAC, a community music project for young people in Sheffield's twin town of Esteli, Nicaragua via 'Sustainability Partners', Registered Charity No: 1119345 www.sustainability-partners.org.uk
