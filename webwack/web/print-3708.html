BROOMHILL FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location 9th - 24th Jun 2012, various locations around Broomhill
Details Broomhill Festival is a local community celebration, held every June since 1975, which brings Broomhill together in raising money for local charities. Events take place around Broomhill, including musical entertainment, art, readings, speakers and stuff for kids. www.broomhillfestival.org.uk
