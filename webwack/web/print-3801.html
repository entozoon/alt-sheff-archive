Palestine A Member of the United Nations?

Date, Time & Location Mon 23 Apr 2012, 2pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Oliver McTernan is the Director of Forward Thinking, a national charity which aims to promote a more inclusive peace process in the Middle East. At this meeting he will discuss recent developments, including Palestine's recent claim to be regarded as an independent state at the United Nations. Details from www.sheffieldpsc.org.uk.
