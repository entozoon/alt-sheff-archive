Sheffield Anticuts Alliance AGM

Date, Time & Location Tue 10 Sep 2013, 7pm Central United Reform Church, Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details Sheffield Anticuts Alliance (SACA) annual general meeting. SACA works to publicise the cuts and support the people of Sheffield to fight back and offer alternatives to the brutal attacks. Whether you are an individual or a campaign group who wants to get active, become part of SACA, all welcome Any motions for constitutional amendments and nominations welcome for officer posts by Sunday September 8th to sheffieldanticutscampaign@gmail.com. Posts: Secretary, Chair, two Vice-Chairs, Treasurer, Publicity Officer and Membership Secretary. While some of the officers may be willing to stand again, Ben Morris, one of the vice-chairs, will be leaving Sheffield to take up a new post on London this autumn, making at least one vacancy. The current SACA constitution, which can be changed at an AGM, can be found online at sheffieldanticuts.wordpress.com/about/constitution/ .
