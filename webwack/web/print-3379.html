World AIDS Day Candlelit Vigil

Date, Time & Location Sat 1 Dec 2012, 5.30pm Sheffield Winter Gardens
Details An open event to remember those who have been lost to HIV and AIDS both in the UK and globally, and to show our support for those living with and affected by HIV. There will be a collection at the event with all proceeds going to HIV support charities in the city. www.sexualhealthsheffield.nhs.uk
