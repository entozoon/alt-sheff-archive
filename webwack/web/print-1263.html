Upscribe book launch

Date, Time & Location Tue 7 Jun 2016, 7pm Hallam HUBS Building, Paternoster Row S1 2QQ
Details UpScribe Social Publishing celebrates the publication of their next collection of work - stories, scripts, poems and other writing from a group of asylum seekers and refugees living in Sheffield. Part of Festival of Debate 2016, hosted by Opus Independents (which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine). Free to attend, no ticket required.
