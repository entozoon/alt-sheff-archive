Animal Aid's Northern Compassionate Christmas Fayre

Date, Time & Location Sat 6 Nov 2010, 11am to 4pm Victoria Hall Methodist Church, Norfolk Street, Sheffield city centre S1 2JB
Details This event will be the first to be held in the north of England to copy the very popular annual Animal Aid Compassionate Christmas Fayres in London. Purchase all your Christmas gifts safe in the knowledge that no products have been tested on animals or include any animal ingredients and that your money will be going directly to campaign groups and ethical traders who are trying to make a positive difference in the world. Goods on sale include: *chocolates *cruelty-free toiletries and cosmetics *non-leather footwear *recycled and fair-trade goods * seasonal gifts and cards and more.... Animal Aid, Tanjero Soap, Vegan Organic Network, Artisan Preserves, Freerangers, Sea Shepherd and The Cake Scoffers amongst others will be holding stalls. Delicious, healthy vegan food and drinks will be available all day from local ethical caterers and there will be informative talks to enjoy. FREE ENTRY (Though voluntary donations would be very welcome). This event is sponsored by Lembas, Sheffield's original wholefood wholesaler and organised by Animal Aid with the help of Sheffield Animal Friends
