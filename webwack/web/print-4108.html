Course: Make Your Own Pole Lathe

Date, Time & Location Sat 15 - Sun 16 Oct 2011, Sheffield
Details Two-day course carefully guiding you through the construction and assembly of a pole lathe (to turn wood for woodwork without using electricity). Followed by the basics of green woodworking and pole lathe turning with an emphasis on using hand tools safely and accurately to produce small turned items. You will leave with a fully working pole lathe. Low-Impact Living Initiative (LILI) is a non-profit organisation whose mission is to help people reduce their impact on the environment, improve their quality of life, gain new skills, live in a healthier and more satisfying way, have fun and save money.
