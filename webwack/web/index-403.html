Surround Yarls Wood Immigration Detention Centre

Sat 1 Dec 2018, coaches 9am at Bar One, Sheffield University Students Union

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

A national demonstration against the injustice of Yarls Wood Immigration Detention Centre in Bedfordshire. Run by multinational corporation Serco, female refugees are indefinitely detained. There are multiple reports detailing human rights violations, including inadequate medical attention, harassment, rape, violence, torture, forced marriage, sexual abuse, FGM and persecution due to sexuality. Coach transport will be going from Sheffield. Free tickets are available to those who need them, no explanation needed (please email refugees@sheffield.ac.uk), but if you are able to pay, please buy a standard ticket. Organised by Student Action for Refugees (STAR), South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG), Sheffield Labour Students, Free University of Sheffield and Sheffield Socialist Students.
