Barnsley Pride 2009

Date, Time & Location Sun 13 Sep 2009, Barnsley
Details This year will see the first ever Pride event in Barnsley which promises to be a momentous occasion, bringing the gay community together in Barnsley to celebrate whilst promoting safety and cohesion. A huge amount of hard work has been undertaken to get the organisation & planning of the event underway and they now need support in turning the plans into reality.
