ROTHERHAM CARNIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sat 24 Sep 2016 12 noon
Details Carnival arts from around the country come together in a celebration of what makes Rotherham. A "Peoples Parade" will leave from Main Street at 12 noon, going through the town centre and ending up at Clifton Park where there will be stalls, music and the amazing Colourscape.
