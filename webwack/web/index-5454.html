Lecture: Energy and the Politics of the Future… "Political Realities"

Wed 11 Nov 2009, 7pm at Sheffield Cathedral

Share on

One of a series of lectures hosted by Sheffield Cathedral and Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change Political Realities: Professor Lord Anthony Giddens, formerly Director of the London School of Economics will draw on his recent influential book "The Politics of Climate Change". (Note change of date for this lecture)All lectures held in Sheffield Cathedral at 7pm. Tickets £2 (concessions £1) for single lecture, all three lectures £5 (concessions £2.50) available in advance by post from Julia Alder at Church House, 95-99 Effingham Street, Rotherham S65 1BL or from Blue Moon café (next to the cathedral) or on the door.Contact email Michael Bayley
