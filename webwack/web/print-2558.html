Cycle Sheffield meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 3 Mar 2014, 7pm, Heeley Development Trust, Ash Tree Yard, 62-68 Thirlwell Rd. S8 9TF
Details The monthly meeting of Cycle Sheffield, usually followed by pub social. This group is for all kinds of cyclists; it organises events, information and rides, and campaigns for cycling to be taken seriously as a means of transport, with the facilities it needs. Tea and coffee etc available. Followed by pub social, possibly at the Sheaf View. www.cyclesheffield.org.uk
