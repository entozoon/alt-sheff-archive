Sharrow Lantern Carnival

Date, Time & Location Sun 3 Apr 2011, meet 7.30pm
Details Sharrow Lantern Carnival is one of Sheffield's most colourful, fun events and a great annual festival to start the spring. It's a musical parade of colourful lanterns and people, of all shapes and sizes. This year it's on the theme of Elements.
