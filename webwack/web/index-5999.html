Sheffield waste import plan

December 2010

Share on

A proposal bring up to 50,000 tonnes of waste for Bernard Road Incinerator from surrounding towns and counties, has been made over the Christmas break. To check it out see application reference: 10/03861/FUL. It's asking for a temporary variation on the original controversial 2001 application reference 01/10135/FUL.
