Postcapitalism Reading Group

Date, Time & Location Tue 17 Jan 2017, 7.30pm, The Bath Hotel, 66 Victoria St. S3 7QL
Details An informal group, now reading "Inventing the Future: Postcapitalism and a World Without Work" by Nick Srnicek and Alex Williams [2015]. This meeting will be a discussion of Chapters 4 and 5. The group welcomes new members interested in reading and discussing political books, from a variety of left-wing/anti-capitalist viewpoints. Held in the small side room in the Bath Hotel (off West Street).
