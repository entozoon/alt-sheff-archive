Temporary Autonomous Arts (TAA) is about creating free, open access exhibition space by opening a disused building and making the space available to artists, to inspire creativity in all forms. TAA exhibitions exist outside the corporate world and established art elites. Not funded by the Council, the government, or by business. There will be no divide between artists, organisers, and visitors, in an atmosphere where everyone attending will hopefully be an active participant. To build community, to build cultures of resistance, encouraging everyone to take positive personal action in all areas of our lives.
