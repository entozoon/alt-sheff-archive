Chesterfield MAY DAY GALA AND DEMONSTRATION

Date, Time & Location Mon 7 May 2012, 9am onwards, Chesterfield
Details Chesterfield is staging a fantastic day of events for the traditional people's holiday. It runs from 9am and the main demonstration assembles at 10.30, at Chesterfield Town Hall.
