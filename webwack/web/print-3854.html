South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG) meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 27 March, 2012, 6.30pm Scotia Works, Leadmill Road S1 4SE
Details SYMAAG unites refugee, asylum and migrant organisations with campaign groups and supporting individuals for the rights of those seeking asylum and of those coming to work in this country. This month the main discussion will be about the privatisation of asylum housing in Yorkshire and Humberside. Although G4S (the company that usually locks up asylum seekers rather than house them) eventually won the contract, SYMAAG believes that asylum seeker tenants, with support, can monitor housing standards and challenge any cost-cutting reductions or abuse by G4S.
