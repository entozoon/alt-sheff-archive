"Verse Matters"

Date, Time & Location Thu 3 Mar 2016, 7.30pm, Theatre Delicatessen, The Moor S1 4PF
Details A feminist arts event to celebrate International Women´s Day. Verse Matters provides a platform for poetry, storytelling, puppetry, music and comedy, showcasing the work of talented people in a friendly, supportive environment. Some performers tell stories that have been erased by society: refugees, detainees, prisoners. Others will be speaking in public for the first time. Others are experienced poets, dramatists and performers. All are respected and appreciated. River Wolton (Poet) Carol Eades (Gorilla, Poet) Fi Helsing (Musician). Suzannah Evans (Bank St Arts, Poet) Marina Poppa (Firm of Poets) Ben Jackson (Poet) Laura Church Janine Smedley, and more! Cost: Pay what you can. £3 suggested donation. All door profits split between featured artists. Free entry for open mic participants. Verse Matters
