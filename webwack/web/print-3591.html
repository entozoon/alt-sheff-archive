Activism for a Future that Works

Date, Time & Location Fri 17 Aug 2012, 4pm Sheffield Railway Station
Details For the last two Fridays Sheffield activists and Remploy workers have leafleted Sheffield Railway station with great success to drum up support for the fight against the closure of the Remploy factories, as well as raising awareness of The TUC March for a Future that works, on October the 20th in London. They will be leafleting every Friday in the run up to the massive trade union demonstration in London on 0ctober 20th. Sheffield activists will gather at Sheffield station to sell travel tickets and hand out flyers and advice to the general public, raising awareness and filling the coaches at the same time.
