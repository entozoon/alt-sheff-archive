"Love Tax"

Date, Time & Location Sat 4 Apr 2015, 2pm, Fargate (Town Hall end), city centre
Details Sheffield Equality Group street protest about tax dodging, with songs and information about who is doing it, and talk about how taxes can make for a more equal society. You are invited to bring pots, pans, or instruments of any kind and join in with a sing song about lovely taxes, or a sign that says what you think about tax avoidance and creating a fairer society.
