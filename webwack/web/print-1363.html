Global Justice meeting

Date, Time & Location Thu 7 Apr 2016, 7-9pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Global Justice Now (formerly World Development Movement) aims to change the actions, policies and institutions of governments, corporations and wealthy minorities, which are preventing an end to poverty and injustice. Part of a global movement, it works alongside people protesting in the developing world. This month they will be particularly focusing on the International Food Justice action on 16th April and the AGM and public meeting with a film showing, on 25th May Global Justice Sheffield
