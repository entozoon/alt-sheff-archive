'We Want Our Buses Back' demonstration

Date, Time & Location Sat 9 Jan 2010, 12-30pm, Town Hall, Sheffield city centre
Details Protest at South Yorkshire bus companies' poor service, route cuts and price rises in the New Year, to send a message to the politicians and to First. Assemble outside the Town Hall for a short march at at 1pm round Arundel Gate and back to the Town Hall, for a rally with speakers invited from the council, bus drivers, campaign groups and others. Come and support or help petition, leaflet and organise, all welcome. To get an idea of the anger about Sheffield bus services look at Sheffield Forum where recent discussions have had literally hundreds of replies.
