Family Allotment Playschemes

Date, Time & Location Thu 7 Aug 2014, 1pm-3pm, Firth Park Community Allotment
Details Summer holidays are here! The regular toddler groups at Firth Park Community Allotment are making way for family playschemes for the school holidays, Thursdays 1pm-3pm on 31st July, 7th, 14th and 21st August. Activities on offer include bug hunts, flower dyes, face painting, fruit smoothies, potato day, plus the usual activities - sand tray, mud kitchen, dino park, crafts, etc. If you haven't visited before, the allotment site is specially designed for those with young families and is much more than a growing plot. And it has just been awarded its Green Flag status again! Firth Park Community Allotment is part of Heeley City Farm's family of growing sites. The allotment is at the meeting point of Vickers Road and Drive - just look for the big green allotment gates and follow the signs. Ring for more info: Dot 07985 421346.
