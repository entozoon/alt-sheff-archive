Free film: Grave of the Fireflies

Date, Time & Location Tue 18 May 2010, 6.30pm at Cinema Syndicate
Details This animated film set in Second World War Japan has been described as one of the most powerful anti-war films ever. Cinema Syndicate is an anarchist film collective, open to all, showing through political and activist film that we live in an unjust world, and it needs to change. Film shows are free, Tuesday nights at Lecture Theatre 2, Arts Tower, University of Sheffield. Full programme and details on Cinema Syndicate Facebook
