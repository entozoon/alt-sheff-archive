Menstrual Hygiene Day: Adventures in Menstruating

Date, Time & Location Thu 28 May 2015, Theatre Delicatessen, The Moor S1 4PF
Details For Menstrual Hygiene Day, a free info shop from 11am-4pm. Followed by Adventures in Menstruating from 7.30pm, a comedy show to break taboos and have a laugh, by Chella Quint. All genders welcome, suitable for ages 16+. Theatre Delicatessen is the former Woolworths shop on The Moor. Tickets £6/£4 concessions, fundraising for Periodpositive, Sexpression:UK and Irise International. www.periodpositive.com
