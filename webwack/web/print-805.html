Sheffield - hostility or sanctuary?

Date, Time & Location Tue 13 Jun 2017, 3pm, The Circle (VAS), 33 Rockingham Lane S1 4FW
Details A session run by ASSIST, aimed at anyone working with asylum seekers and refugees and anyone who might be affected by:
  • Hostile environment measures (Immigration Act 2014 & 2016)
  • Importance of immigration advice and AVR
  • Changes to asylum support and appeal rights
  • Withdrawal of Section 95 support to ARE families
  • Withdrawal of support to ARE care leavers
The session will be led by Gina Clayton, ASSIST trustee and legal practitioner at South Yorkshire Refugee Law & Justice, and John Donkersley, a specialist immigration adviser and the supervising solicitor at Sheffield Citizens Advice and Law Centre.
