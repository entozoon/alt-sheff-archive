One Step meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 6 Mar 2013, 8pm, Walkley/Upperthorpe
Details One Step is a small group that meets once a month to find practical ways to take one step a day, a week or a month to leave the world a better place than when they started. This month they are looking at the issues around asylum seekers, with a guest speaking about the work of Assist. Please email contact@one-step.org.uk for the location, or see www.one-step.org.uk for more information about the group.
