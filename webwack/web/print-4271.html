Women's course: Advanced Plumbing

Date, Time & Location Wed 8 Jun 2011, for 6 weeks
Details Re-opened for 2011 with great voluntary spirit, Women in Construction Arts and Technology (WICAT) offers courses for women (and some for men also). Prices are low, just to cover costs, with concessions for learners on various benefits. ADVANCED PLUMBING – 6 week starts Wed 8th June, 6-9pm, £72. For all women who have already undertaken a plumbing course. This course will look at more advanced techniques and learners shall gain and build on skills by installing new piping and radiators in a refurbished room at the WCC and connecting to an already existing central heating system. Women only WICAT: 21 Buckenham St. (above the Hindu Samaj) Pitsmoor. Call for details Sheff 2725360, text 07813 126388 or email wicat2011 {at} gmail.com
