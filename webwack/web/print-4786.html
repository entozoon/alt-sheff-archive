City of Sanctuary Talent Show

Date, Time & Location Sat 18 Sep 2010, 4pm-9pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW (near Anglican cathedral)
Details Have you got talent? City of Sanctuary is joining forces with Sheffield Quakers to hold an informal talent show and we need your help! It's an opportunity for all you Sheffielders and local refugees and asylum seekers to show off your musical, dancing or other skills and enjoy a social afternoon/evening with free food and drink provided. If you would like to take part please contact sarah@cityofsanctuary.org If you would just like to come along and watch, make a note in your diary.
