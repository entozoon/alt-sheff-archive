Talk: Che Guevara&#39;s daughter

Date, Time & Location Tue 2 Dec 2014, doors open at 6.30pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Sheffield Cuba Solidarity Campaign are delighted to host the great campaigner, Che Guevara's daughter, Dr Aleida Guevara, visiting Sheffield following her involvement at the "Latin America - Adelante" conference in London. She will be introducing her film "Hugo Chavez & the New Latin America", and discussing the Cuban revolution's continuing contribution to the world. Earlier the same day she will ALSO be speaking: 2pm in the Student Union Auditorium of Sheffield University. Sheffield Cuba Solidarity Campaign on Facebook
