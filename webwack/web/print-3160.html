Chalk Walk

Date, Time & Location Thu 11 Apr 2013, Meet 6pm at Devonshire Green next to The Forum
Details Hollaback! Sheffield chalk their way around the streets of Sheffield on an adventure to reclaim the streets where people have been followed, insulted, harassed or assaulted. One of several events for International Anti-Street Harassment Week. For full details see the large item above. Hollaback! Sheffield website
