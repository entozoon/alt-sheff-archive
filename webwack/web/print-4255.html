Heeley Festival

Date, Time & Location Sat 18 Jun 2011, 12-8pm
Details The annual summer festival centred around Heeley City Farm and The Energy House, and various venues around the Heeley area of Sheffield. Family fun with dancing, interesting stalls, activities and food, BBQ, beer tent and even a special 30th Birthday Brew made by Blue Bee Brewery. Stalls 12 noon till 5pm, and till 8pm music on the Farm Stage.
