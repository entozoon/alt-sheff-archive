National Debt - Debt or Delusion?

Date, Time & Location Mon 24 Feb 2014, 7.30pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Is the National Debt a curse or a blessing? Positive Money supporter Richard Bruce will be giving a talk on a number of topics relating to the way our money is created. No question the UK Government is in major debt but who do we owe? Will we run out of money? Is China going to take over? Positive Money is a movement to democratise money and banking so that it works for society and not against it. In Sheffield, they hold talks on the last Monday of every month (except bank holidays).
