Sheffield PSC fundraiser

Date, Time & Location Sat 18 Sep 2010, from 7pm at St. Mathews Church, Carver Street Sheffield
Details Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign is continuing, despite the inhuman and illegal Israeli blockade of the Gaza Strip, to support children projects in Jabalia, Nussairat refugee camps and in Khan Younis. Come along and support Children in Palestine who are trying hard to learn and play, challenging the ongoing Israeli violation of their basic human rights. Come along to this Sheffield PSC fundraiser featuring delicious palestinian food and a short film. £7.50 Waged/£6.00 Unwaged.
