Leeds Schumacher Conference 2010

Date, Time & Location Sat 23 Oct 2010 10am-5pm Left Bank Centre Cardigan Road, Leeds
Details OK it's not around Sheffield, but this is a free conference on the theme 'Beyond Consumerism': What would our world look like if a healthy, fulfilling and balanced way of life for all, instead of the pursuit of material growth and profit for some, became the primary goal? What creative and practical steps can we take now, communally and individually, to bring this possibility nearer to reality? More information at www.schumacher-north.co.uk
