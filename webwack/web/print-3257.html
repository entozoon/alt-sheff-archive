Free cycling film: "Janapar"

Date, Time & Location Thu 28 Feb 2013, 7pm, Russell's Bicycle Shed, 53 Mowbray Street, Neepsend, Sheffield, S3 8EN
Details A screening of the cycling film "Janapar", an adventure travelogue and love story on a journey through physically and emotionally challenging terrain. Entry is free but you will have to pay for drinks. There are limited numbers so if you are interested either email or call, details via the website below. www.russellsbicycleshed.co.uk
