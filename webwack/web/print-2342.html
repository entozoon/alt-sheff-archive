"Who makes the decisions? You or your brain?"

Date, Time & Location Mon 23 Jun 2014 7:30pm Farm Road Sports & Social Club Farm Rd. S2 2TP
Details Skeptics in the Pub talk with Peter Redgrave, Professor at the Department of Psychology at the University of Sheffield Peter will describe the hierarchical organisation of decision making showing that, over the course of brain evolution, behavioural options have evolved with the species, but the mechanism for selecting between them has been conserved from fish to reptile, from mammal to man. He will describe the structures that seems to act as the brain’s generic selection mechanism and what factors determine which option gets selected. He will consider our sense of agency (the subjective feeling that we have made a decision) and whether the concepts of free will and personal responsibility have any place in the neural networks of the basal ganglia.
