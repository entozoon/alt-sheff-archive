CELEBRATE HUMAN RIGHTS DAY IN SHEFFIELD

Date, Time & Location Thurs 10 Dec 2009
Details Thursday is International Human Rights Day. As part of celebrations going on across the globe, a coalition of Sheffield organisations are holding two main public events. At 12:30pm, a Rally in front of the City Hall (Barkers Pool). With readings, Street Theatre etc. Please being banners, placards etc. From 4:30pm to 6pm, a candlelit vigil will be held outside Sheffield Railway Station. Bring Placards, banners and warm clothes! www.cityofsanctuary.org
