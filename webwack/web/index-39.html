Trash Lab!

Sat 22 Feb 2020, 11am-5pm, Access Space, AVEC Building, 3-7 Sidney St. S1 4RG

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Come and get creative with e-waste, broken computers and consumer products. Create strange new things or bring something that no longer works and they will help you fix it.An event to help keep consumer products out of the waste stream, by giving extra life to your broken possessions. Access Space encourages a peer-based learning environment to tackling this problem with creative and tangential approaches. If you are concerned with material and electronic waste in contemporary society, Access Space can help with hacking electronics and repair practices, using appropriate technology for reusing and re/up-cycling materials. Free event all day, all welcome (adults and children over 10), you can reserve a place in advance. For more info please email jake@access-space.orgAccess Space
