Global Justice Sheffield meeting

Date, Time & Location Thu 3 Mar 2016, 7pm, Dog & Partridge, 56 Trippet Lane S1 4EL
Details Monthly meeting of Global Justice Sheffield, to discuss and plan upcoming events and campaigns including discussing contributions to the 16th April city centre action. Global Justice Now (formerly World Development Movement) aims to change the actions, policies and institutions of governments, corporations and wealthy minorities, which are preventing an end to poverty and injustice. Part of a global movement, it works alongside people protesting in the developing world. Meetings are usually followed by informal gathering in a nearby pub. Global Justice Sheffield
