Sheffield Anti-Cuts Alliance meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 11 Feb 2014, 7pm Central United Reformed Church, 60 Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details SACA has recently been working with the South Yorkshire People's Assembly. At this next SACA meeting, an update on developments with that, and plans for events in 2014. SACA works to publicise the cuts and support the people of Sheffield to fight back and offer alternatives to these brutal attacks. Get involved! Whether you are an individual or a campaign group who wants to get active, become part of Sheffield Anti-Cuts Alliance. It meets on the 2nd Tuesday of the month.
