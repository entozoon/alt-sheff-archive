How to graft your own fruit trees

Sun 14 Mar 2010, 11am-3pm

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter

Grow sheffield session with Niels Corfield from Leeds Permaculture network on how to graft your own fruit trees. 210 Shirebrook Road, Meersbrook S8 9RG (round the back!) If you have a successful graft you get to take home your tree ... you'll only know that in the spring when things start growing! Bring yer own lunch. Cost £10 for the day, concessions for unemployed £7.For more infomation contact Daniele 07507 686453 or Pete 07828 583561www.maxsalad.co.uk
