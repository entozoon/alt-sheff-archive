Futures North conference, in Leeds

Date, Time & Location Sat 11 Sep 2010 at Bodingon Hall, Leeds
Details Major conference about setting new directions. Themed around 5 broad topics of Education, Economy, Energy, International and Transition. The conference will combine keynote speakers with a wide range of workshops. It is organised by Co-operatives Yorkshire and the Humber and is aimed at members of workers co-operatives and social enterprises who might be interested in the co-operative model.
