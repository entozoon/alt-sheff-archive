Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change (SCACC) meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 16 Apr 2013, 7pm-9pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St. James St. S1 2EW
Details This is a chance for anyone interested in climate campaigning to connect with Sheffield Climate Alliance, the wider network that SCACC hosts. Some people meet beforehand to eat and chat in the Blue Moon cafe from 6pm, all welcome there also. This month the agenda will include following up from a meeting with Cllr Jack Scott, who holds the climate portfolio at the council, planning to take an anti-fracking petition to the Council, update about getting involved in Global Power Shift and planning for other events and stalls.
