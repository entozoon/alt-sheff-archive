Sheffield on the Move Question Time event

Date, Time & Location Thu 12 Jul 2012, from 4.30pm, Pavilion Room, St Marys Church and Conference Centre, Bramall Lane S2 4QZ .
Details Sheffield Bus Partnership says it is hoping to improve bus travel. It is made up of First Group, Stagecoach, TM Travel, Sheffield City Council, Sheffield Community Transport and South Yorkshire Passenger Transport Executive. They have a proposed new network and invite public opinions during a consultation period from just 18 June to 14 July 2012. They are also running this public event, but note that the deadline for submitting questions is Tuesday 10 July at 9am, and only up to 3 questions per person, by email to Greg Challis, Transport and Highways, Sheffield City Council, Greg.Challis@sheffield.gov.uk. Drop-in from 4.30pm, where feedback on specific routes may be offered. The panel event is at 5.30pm promptly. Details of the Sheffield Bus Partnership proposals at www.sheffieldbuspartnership.co.uk
