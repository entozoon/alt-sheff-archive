Palestinian Solidarity fundraiser with Hafez Omar

Date, Time & Location Sat 7 Feb 2015, 7pm St Andrews United Reformed Church Hall, Upper Hanover St. S3 7RQ
Details Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity Campaign first fundraiser of the year. An evening to emphasise support for Palestinian children on the basis of solidarity. A dinner with Hafez Omar, who has campaigned intensively for Palestinian political prisoners/hunger strikers. He has designed dozens of famous political posters. The simplicity and power of his posters have made some go viral worldwide. Hafez will talk about his work and the link between art and the Palestinian struggle for freedom, justice and return. Enjoy a selection of delicious homemade falafel, hommos, fool modammis, salads and Syrian style home made mannaeesh zatar. £8 Waged/£6 Concessions. All proceeds will go to SPSC partners, children's centres in Jabalia, Nussairat and Khan Younis. Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity Campaign
