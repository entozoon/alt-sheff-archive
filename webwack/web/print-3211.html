Home-Grown Ceilidh: Anarchist Bookfair fundraiser dance

Date, Time & Location Sun 17 Mar 2013, 7.30pm-10.30pm Broomhall Centre, Broomspring Lane S10 2FB.
Details Come and dance in the spring at the next HOME-GROWN CEILIDH. The event is a fundraiser for the 4th Sheffield Anarchist Bookfair (which will be taking place on 11th May - see: www.sheffieldbookfair.org) The band: THE WELL DRESSED BAND The caller: Lucy Nelson There will be a bar. Family-friendly event. Please bring food to share. Tickets £5 if reserved in advance, £6 on the night; concessions £3.50, under-16s free. To reserve a ticket in advance, email jerrysimon.grow@gmail.com.
