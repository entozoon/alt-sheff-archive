Global Justice meeting

Date, Time & Location Thu 7 Jan 2015, 7pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Global Justice (formerly World Development Movement) aims to change the actions, policies and institutions of governments, corporations and wealthy minorities, which are preventing an end to poverty and injustice. Part of a global movement, it works alongside people protesting in the developing world. Meetings are held on the first Thursday of each month, to discuss coming events and campaigns. Usually followed by informal gathering in a nearby pub. Global Justice Sheffield
