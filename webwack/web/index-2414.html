Food at the Third Sector Cafe: Grow, Give, Make, Sell

Thu 15 May 2014, 9.30am-11.30am, Jesus Centre, 93 Broomspring Lane (off Hanover Way) S10 2FB

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

The Third Sector Cafe discussion this month will be around food - growing it, giving it, making it and selling it. If food is important to the work of your not-for-profit organisation, please share your experiences during Sheffield Food Festival month, and hear more about what organisations are up to with food in Sheffield. People coming along to talk about their projects include:Dr Megan Blake of Sheffield University, on urban food, farming and justiceKim Swan, Victoria Community CafeJohn Le Corney, Heeley City FarmDr Caroline Hart, Sheffield Hallam University/Sheffield Food BoardLisette Baker, The Children's Food TrustDave Cates, Redemption MediaJo Densley, Relish MarketingKarina Tape, Buster's Coffee CompanyMore details and booking: Eventbrite
