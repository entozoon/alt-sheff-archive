Live music with Walkley Greens

Date, Time & Location Sat 5 Mar 2016, 7.30pm, Walkley Community Centre
Details Chris McMahon, Green Party election candidate for Walkley, is a renowned multi-instrumentalist in various Sheffield bands including Treebeard and Haze. Join him for a night of live music hosted by the Walkley Greens, a chance to relax and meet members from across the city. Please buy tickets in advance (£5/£2.50) and bring friends and family.
