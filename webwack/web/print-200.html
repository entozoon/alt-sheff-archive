SYMAAG Benefit Gig

Date, Time & Location Sat 6 Jul 2019, 7.30-10pm at the University Arms pub, 196 Brook Hill, S3 7HG.
Details South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG) present Mat Callahan and Yvonne Moore performing songs from their CD "Working Class Heroes, A History of Struggle in Song" as part of a UK tour. All proceeds to support the work of SYMAAG. Come along, enjoy the music, the company, and help support the work of SYMAAG. Tickets online here. Facebook event page
