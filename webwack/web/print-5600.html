John Ethridge and Sweet Chorus ASSIST gig

Date, Time & Location Sun 9 Aug 2009, 8pm at the Library Theatre, central Sheffield
Details One of the world's leading guitarists, last time John Etheridge played Sheffield he sold out the Crucible.
