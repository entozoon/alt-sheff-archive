Sheffield Fayre

Date, Time & Location Sun 25-Mon 26 Aug 2013, 10.30-5.30pm, Norfolk Heritage Park, Guildford Avenue S2 2PL
Details Sheffield Fayre is held in Norfolk Heritage Park every August bank holiday Sunday and Monday. It is described as the biggest free outdoor family show. Something for everyone including horticultural show, historical enactments and living history camp, trade stands, market stalls and traditional fairground attractions, community entertainment, funfair rides, community stalls, craft market, refreshments, volunteers welcome. Run by Sheffield City Council in partnership with The Friends of Norfolk Heritage Park and supported by The University of Sheffield and National Fairground Archive. Caution: large military involvement with potential for glamourisation of weapons; displays include military recruitment. Sheffield Fayre Eventplan website
