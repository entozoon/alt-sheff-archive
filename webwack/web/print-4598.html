Play: Sex & Docks & Rock'n'roll

Date, Time & Location Sat 4 Dec 2010, Greentop Circus Centre, Sheffield
Details Red Ladder Theatre Co. presents a play by Boff Whalley, of Chumbawamba, set in 1960s Liverpool. The story of one family, against a background of strikes, picketing and jailings. 'History with choruses you can sing along to' in the great tradition of radical, political and entertaining theatre. This is part of a UK tour, also in Barnsley on 16th Dec.
