First Sheffield Renewables 'One-to-One team' workshop

Date, Time & Location Wed 17 Mar 2010, 7pm-9pm at South Yorkshire Energy Centre, Heeley City Farm, Richards Rd. S2 3DT
Details Sheffield Renewables wants to spread the word about renewable energy and its plans, using the idea of a new One-to-One team. Do you like to keep up with the latest information on activities in your area whilst enjoying meeting and talking to people? If you have some spare time that you would like to use to benefit your local community and environment, find out at this first informal 'conversation' workshop. Find out more at: Sheffield Renewables volunteers webpage If you're interested in attending please email cariad@sheffieldrenewables.org.uk or tel Sheff 2508367
