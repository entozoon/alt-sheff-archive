Sheffield Bike Festival

Date, Time & Location Sat 19 June 2010 12-6pm on Devonshire Green
Details Sheffield's first ever bike festival !!
  • Rides coming from around the city to meet at the festival
  • Bike blinging competition! Prizes include a new bike
  • Bike polo - join up your own team!
  • Dr Bike - get your bike fixed up Bike amnesty to donate to the asylum seeker bike project
  • Loads of bike-related stuff to join in with
  • Displays, music and craft, food and fun!
