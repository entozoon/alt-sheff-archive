"On the commons: A world beyond state and market"

Date, Time & Location Wed 4 Dec 2013, 7pm at Sheffield Quaker Meeting House, St James St. S1 2EW
Details An introduction to the idea of a commons, through history and examples, looking towards the creation of a society that does not exploit others through the power of the state or the market. Informal talk followed by discussion, hosted by Sheffield Equality Group. Free, donations for tea and biscuits welcome. To learn more about the commons, see the excellent talk by David Bollier: www.youtube.com/watch?v=TCfreLenQC8
