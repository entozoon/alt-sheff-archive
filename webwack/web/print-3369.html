Firth Park Christmas Festival

Date, Time & Location Thu 6 Dec 2012, 3-7pm, Firth Park, off Bellhouse Road.
Details Friends of Firth Park run this annual community festival. Stalls will be in a large heated marquee with a floor. There may be stalls still available, enquiries to Chris Fox - 07814 719126 email chris@foxprom.co.uk Firth Park is on bus routes 75, 76, 47, 48 etc.
