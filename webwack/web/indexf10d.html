The Labour Party, Antisemitism and the Media

Wed 18 Mar 2020, 7pm, SHU Students Union HUBS Building, Paternoster Row S1 2QQ

SORRY, THIS EVENT HAS BEEN POSTPONED: SEE SPSC HOME PAGE.

As part of Israeli Apartheid Week, a talk by Dr Justin Schlosberg, media activist, researcher and senior lecturer in Journalism and Media at Birkbeck College, University of London.

Media news about the Labour Party and anti-semitism has inaccuracies and distortions, omission of essential context or right of reply, misquotations and false assertions consistent with a "disinformation paradigm", he argues.

Dr Schlosberg has written on political power and the media, and is a co-author of "Labour, Antisemitism and the News: A disinformation paradigm" (2018), and of "Bad News for Labour, Antisemitism, the Party and Public Belief" (2019).

Organised by Sheffield Hallam Students Society and Sheffield University Palestine Society, supported by Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign and Sheffield Labour Friends of Palestine.

Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign

Heading for Extinction talk

Wed 18 Mar 2020, 7pm, Union St. co-working space, 18-22 Union St. S1 2JP

SORRY, THIS EVENT HAS BEEN CANCELLED: SEE GREY NOTICE AT TOP OF THE FACEBOOK EVENT PAGE.

Find out about Extinction Rebellion, who they are, what it does, why it exists, what it is trying to achieve and how you can join. The planet is in ecological crisis: we are in the midst of the sixth mass extinction event this planet has experienced. Scientists believe we may have entered a period of abrupt climate breakdown. This is an unprecedented global emergency.

In this public talk, members of Extinction Rebellion Sheffield will share the latest climate science on where our planet is heading, discuss some of the current psychology around climate change, and offer solutions through the study of social movements. Everyone is welcome and there will be plenty of time to ask questions and discuss afterwards. Entry is free but please donate towards venue hire costs if you can.

Please aim to arrive at 6.45 for a prompt start at 7pm.
