Trees for Life

Date, Time & Location Wed 26 Feb 2020, 7pm, Showroom cinema, Paternoster Row, S1 2BX
Details To mark Fairtrade Fortnight, Taysir Arbasi and Mohammed Ruzz offer a talk about the work and vision of farming communities in Palestine. They will discuss the importance of building community resilience and their contribution to climate justice whilst living under the illegal Israeli occupation. Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign is hosting this meeting in partnership with a group of singers from Sheffield who will be visiting Palestine later this year. Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign
