Performance/film/photography from Palestine

Date, Time & Location Mon 12 Jul 2010 at Longley Park College
Details Young artists and dancers from Lajee Cultural Centre in Aida Refugee Camp, Bethlehem performing Dbaka, traditional Palestinian dancing, to bring their cultural resistance to the UK. 4pm Film by young film makers from Aida Camp 7pm Dance performance All Welcome. FREE. Longley Park College, is at Horninglow Road (just off Barnsley Road) S5 6SG, about 1/2 mile past Northern General Hospital, on loads of bus routes.
