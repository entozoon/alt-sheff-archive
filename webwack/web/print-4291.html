The Sharrow Pie Experiment

Date, Time & Location Thu 26 May 2011, 11.30am-2pm, St. Mary's Community Centre, Bramall Lane S2 4QZ
Details Getting people together starts ideas flowing; imagination and relationships flourish, loneliness and depression reduce and people feel energised - life gets more interesting. That's the idea, inspired by things like Pielab a Sharrow group are going to help people make life more interesting ... by eating pie together. They are running open events for everyone, whatever your age, background, class, faith, ethnicity: This event is an Up My Street Workshop, to be followed by another on 11th June. Call 07795 824887 for more information.
