Ugandan Art Stall

Date, Time & Location Sun 5 Dec 2010, 12 to 4pm, Nether Edge Road and Glen Road, Sheffield, S7 1RU
Details One of the stalls at today's Nether Edge Farmer's Market & Christmas Craft Fair will be sending all proceeds to the Dewe School of Art in Uganda. The art and crafts on sale have been hand made from natural materials and brought to the UK to raise vital funds for the school. To see a video about the school (which welcomes enquiries and volunteers) see http://vimeo.com/groups/15753/videos/16430625
