BIG BOULDER MUSIC & ARTS FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sun 6 Sep 2015, 12-6pm, Heeley People’s Park
Details Community festival with food stalls, beer tent, arts and crafts and family activities throughout the day. Music on the main stage from: The Early Cartographers The Angel Brothers The Cuckoo Clocks Dariush Kanani Eastern Seaboard Radio Station The Bronze Avital Raz The festival is free to all with funds being raised on the day for Heeley People’s Park.
