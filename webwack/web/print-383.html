Global Justice Sheffield Christmas Social

Date, Time & Location Thu 13 Dec 2018, 7-9pm, Dog and Partridge pub, 56 Trippet Lane S1 4EL.
Details Global Justice Sheffield Christmas social is a great chance for those who unable to normally attend meetings and events, but who would like to show their solidarity with their core team of activists.
