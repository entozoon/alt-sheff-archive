Sheffield Save Our NHS Public Meeting

Date, Time & Location Thu 2 Jul 2015 6.30 for 7pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Sheffield Save Our NHS is fighting against cuts and privatisation in the NHS. Keynote Speaker - Professor Ray Tallis Also speaking: Dr Graham Pettinger, Devonshire Green Surgery, S1 (threatened with closure) Nathan Walsh, UNISON senior representative on 7 day working in the NHS, Branch Secretary of UNISON National Blood Transfusion Service Speaker on Migrant access to healthcare invited Hosted by Sheffield Save Our NHS and supported by Sheffield 38 degrees, Peoples NHS and 999 call for the NHS. The meeting is planned as an open forum for people to put forward ideas and get involved. There will be a variety of campaign stalls and information available. The building will be open from 6.30 for people to view this information and start the informal discussion. Speakers will start at 7pm Sheffield Save Our NHS
