"Cabinet in the Community"

Date, Time & Location Wed 11 Feb 2015, 6pm-7.30pm, Terry Wright Community Hall, Gleadless Rd S2 2BT
Details Local residents will be able to ask Sheffield City Council leaders questions about issues affecting the south of the city at a ‘Question Time’ style session. Participants will be able to speak directly with individual cabinet members after the session, and can ask questions on the night or email in advance for a more detailed response.
