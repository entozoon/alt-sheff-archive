Independence FROM America Day

Date, Time & Location Sun 4 Jul 2010, 2pm-7pm
Details Sunday July 4th will see the annual Independence FROM America bash at Menwith Hill (US spy base on UK soil) in the Yorkshire Dales. This year Mark Thomas is headlining, together with Roy Bailey and Seize the Day. Organised by Campaign for the Accountability of American Bases (CAAB) www.caab.org.uk also details at Sheffield Indymedia website
