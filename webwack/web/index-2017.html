One Billion Rising - preparation dance workshops

Thu 5 Feb 2015, time and venue to be confirmed - Rotherham/Sheffield

Share on

One Billion Rising is holding a flashdance as part of the global movement to end violence against women (see Saturday 14th Feb below). You can organise your own event. Dancing can happen anywhere, at any time, with anyone and everyone. No corporation can own or control it. These events are Dance Workshops: Learn the "Break the Chain" dance with the help of experienced dance teachers, (and the practice video) in preparation for the Flash Dancing in Sheffield and Rotherham. Contact sarah.snowden@nrcentre.org.uk or msithule@yahoo.co.uk or tel. Sheff 384041 or mobile 07799 485588. All women welcome at dance workshops.
