Sheffield Student Hosuing Co-op Launch Party

Date, Time & Location Sat 23 Nov 2013, 7pm University Arms (upstairs) 197 Brook Hill, S3 7HG
Details The Sheffield Student Housing Co-op is having a launch party, aiming to take the great things housing co-ops offer, and transfer them into the student housing world. There will be discussion, speakers, music, co-op films and vegan cake! Come along for more info or if you'd like to offer your advice on housing co-ops, it would be much appreciated.
