Course: Basketmaking

Date, Time & Location Sat 3 Dec 2011, Sheffield
Details Sit by a wood stove in a woodland shelter, and learn about one of the oldest of all crafts. It pre-dates pottery and cloth weaving, and led to the invention of both. A course with Ray Lister, who has been making and teaching about English baskets for over 20 years, using colourful, locally-grown organic willow. Each student will craft a frame basket, even with no previous experience. Low-Impact Living Initiative (LILI) is a non-profit organisation helping people reduce their impact on the environment, improve their quality of life, gain new skills, live in a healthier and more satisfying way, have fun and save money.
