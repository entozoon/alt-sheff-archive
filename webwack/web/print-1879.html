Union Street Drop-In

Date, Time & Location Tue 24 Mar 2015, 10-1pm, 18-22 Union Street S1 2JP
Details As part of the Festival of Debate, Union Street Drop-Ins are informal sessions to host discussions around any topic suggested by members of the public. All are welcome. They will have computers and guidance available for online voter registration, as well as access to policy orientated websites like VoteForpolicies.org.uk. Festival of Debate
