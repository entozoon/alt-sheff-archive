"Bringing sanity into the world of finance"

Date, Time & Location Fri 15-Sat 16 Nov 2013, Freeman College, 88 Arundel St. S1 2NG
Details Do we as citizens have the power to act as central bankers? An exploration of money through the lens of some concepts of Rudolph Steiner. Presented by Arthur Edwards, MSc (Econ), editor of "Associate!", a journal on associative economics, ideas that gave rise to the creation of Triodos Bank, a social, ethical bank. An evening talk (Fri 15th, 7.30-9.30pm) and morning session (Sat 16th, 10am-1pm).
