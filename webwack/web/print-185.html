Stand up to Racism protest

Date, Time & Location Tue 23 Jul 2019, 5pm, outside the Town Hall, Pinstone St. S1 2HH
Details Sheffield Stand up to Racism is holding this protest outside the Town Hall to call for "Neither Johnson nor Hunt - We don’t want another racist in No 10". Sheffield Peoples Assembly will also have a stall on the day to support the event. They say NO to the Tory ’coronation’ of a new Prime Minister and YES to a General Election to finish them off! Please come along to support this event.
