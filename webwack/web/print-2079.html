Winter Fayre at Heeley City Farm

Date, Time & Location Sat 13 Dec 2014, 11am-3pm Heeley City Farm, Richards Rd. S2 3DT
Details Lots happening on site for a festive day out on the Farm! Santa's Grotto - come and tell Santa your Christmas Wishes and if you've been good he will give you an early pressie! Children's Craft Activities Face Painting Animal Petting in the Small Animal House Blacksmith's Demo - watch Iain metal working and creating toasting forks and rams head models Mince Pies & Warm Fruit Punch Tea Shop Candle Rolling Crafts, Gift, Food, Community Group and Bric-a-Brac Stalls Handmade Christmas Wreaths to buy Rent a Christmas Tree PLUS Entertainment from Boggarts Breakfast! Free admission to the Farm but for fundraising there will be games and tombolas to take part in. Please email s.hardy@heeleyfarm.org.uk or ring Sheff 2580482 option 5 for more info. Heeley City Farm is 20 mins walk from Sheffield, or catch the No 47 bus to Herdings from town - it drops you right outside the Farm.
