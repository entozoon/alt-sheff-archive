Are We Protected? Migrants Issue Around Criminal Justice

Tue 16 Feb 2010, 9.45am-2.30pm, run by South Yorkshire Local Criminal Justice Board (LCJB)

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Migrants, community leaders and people working within South Yorkshire's migrant communities are invited to this discussion day which aims to improve migrant communities' confidence in the criminal justice system, by listening to individuals' experiences as victims and offenders, then working together to identify problems and create solutions.To book a free place, (first come, first served basis) email wendy.whitehouse@southyorks.pnn.police.uk Tel Sheff 2197139 or Text 07976 264150For more details City of Sanctuary Events webpage Tel Sheff 2412780
