SHEFFIELD PRIDE

Date, Time & Location Sat 28 Jul 2018, Endcliffe Park, off Rustlings Rd. S11 7AB
Details For the 10th year running, Sheffield Pride annual community festival and parade celebrating lesbian, gay, bisexual, non-binary and trans life, and welcoming friends, family and everyone to join in the fun. Lineup stretching all through the day and night, something for everyone to enjoy. There are also fundraiser events at various venues. Endcliffe Park is 2 miles south-west of Sheffield city centre, at Rustlings Road junction with Ecclesall Road. Buses from city centre are very frequent. The main event will be free of charge, without age restrictions and community focused. Keep an eye on the website and social media pages to keep up-to-date with events and how you can support Pride Sheffield. Sheffield Pride
