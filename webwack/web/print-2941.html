Greenpeace meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 13 Aug 2013, 8pm, The Sheffield Tap, Platform 1, Sheffield Station
Details Monthly meeting of Greenpeace South Yorkshire group, held on the 2nd Tuesday of each month. Newcomers always welcome. Note the venue is The "Sheffield Tap" pub on platform 1 at Sheffield train station. It is accessible from a front door to the left of the station main entrance. More details of meetings and current activities in the Get Active Supporters newsletter, see Greenpeace South Yorkshire website Upcoming event dates are shown on the home page.
