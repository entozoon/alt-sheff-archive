In Conversation with Andrew Haldane

Date, Time & Location Thu 20 Feb 2014: 6-7.30pm, Firth Court, Western Bank, S10 2TN
Details Andrew Haldane has praised the 'Occupy' movement for questioning the banks. As Executive Director for Financial Stability at the Bank of England and a member of the Basel Committee, which oversees banking internationally, he is at the forefront of the global financial system. Professor Andrew Gamble will engage him in conversation about the financial crisis, and will take questions from the audience. This event is organised by the Sheffield Political Economy Research Institute (SPERI). See more information and register at Eventbrite booking .
