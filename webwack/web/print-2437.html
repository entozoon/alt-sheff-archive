Lecture by the Chief Constable of South Yorkshire Police

Date, Time & Location Tue 6 May 2014, 9am, Room DB13, Bartolome House, School of Law, University of Sheffield.
Details Chief Constable for South Yorkshire Police, David Crompton is giving a lecture on "What is the purpose of the police?". All are welcome to attend. David Crompton is the ACPO lead for Homicide Investigation and for Continuous Improvement. He holds an Honours Degree in Geography from Salford University and a Diploma in Criminology from the University of Cambridge. He is also responsible for Integrity and Transformation within the force.
