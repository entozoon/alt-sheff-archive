Film screening: "Families Like Ours"

Date, Time & Location Mon 22 Feb 2016, The Void Cinema, Room 123, L1 Owen Building. Sheffield Hallam University S1 1WB
Details Marking LGBT History Month, the screening of a film by E.D.E.N Film Productions. "Families Like Ours" was made with members of the LGBT community in and around Sheffield, highlighting their experiences of homophobia and transphobia, and raising awareness of LGBT+ identities to the wider community. The film was released to critical acclaim and has been screened at the Sheffield Odeon Cinema, Icons, The Royal Standard, Climax, and on Sheffield Live! TV.
