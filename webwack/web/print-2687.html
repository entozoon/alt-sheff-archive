"Miami Five" vigil/protest

Date, Time & Location Sat 14 Dec 2013, 12-1.30pm, outside Sheffield Town Hall
Details A city centre event by Sheffield Cuba Solidarity Campaign, to highlight the plight of the "Miami Five" to the wider Sheffield public.
