Sheffield Equality Group meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 2 Nov 2011, 7pm to 9pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Sheffield Equality Group meetings aim to be an open space where people can discuss and plan actions around income equality. Meetings are now on the first Wednesday in every month. This month the agenda includes the Occupy movement, anti-greed campaigning, and general chat about income equality. All welcome. Sheffield Equality Group website Tel: 07956 384142 Facebook
