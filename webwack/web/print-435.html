Sheffield FridayNightRide planning session

Date, Time & Location Wed 14 Nov 2018, 6pm for 6.30pm, Gardeners Rest, (upstairs room), 105 Neepsend Lane S3 8AT
Details Sheffield Friday Night Ride (FNR) offers a fun monthly evening bike ride for everyone. Rides are prepared in advance on themes, and it has been riding for years, attracting dozens of cyclists. This is a brainstorming session to discuss and propose future rides, which will start again hopefully at the end of March 2019. FridayNightRide
