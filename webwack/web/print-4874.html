Sheffield Animal Friends meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 2 Aug 2010, 6.30-8pm Fat Cat pub, 23 Alma Street, Sheffield S3 8SA
Details Sheffield Animal Friends meetings are held on the first Monday of each month to plan upcoming animal rights and vegan outreach/education actions. Newcomers are always welcome! The venue is near the Kelham Island Industrial Museum, if you're not sure how to get there or would just like someone to accompany you to the pub, you can be met outside the Blue Moon cafe by the Cathedral at 6.15pm, if you email a request beforehand to sheffieldanimalfriends@gmail.com
