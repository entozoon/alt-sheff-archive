"Why Sheffield Should Go Dutch"

Date, Time & Location Wed 24 Feb 2016, 6pm, Showroom, Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details A fun and friendly CycleSheffield event, open to all. Includes an excellent talk on "Designing Quality Of Life Into The City: Why Sheffield Should Go Dutch", explaining how the Dutch approach of designing freedom to move into their urban spaces affords families a quality of life and freedom which is the envy of Europe. Followed by a short selection of Streetfilms showing how smart transportation design and policy can result in better places to live, work and play. Prepare to be educated, inspired and ready to push for change in Sheffield! Food and drink available from the bar throughout the evening. Stay to chat and hang out with CycleSheffield members afterwards. Facebook event page
