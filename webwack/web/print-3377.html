Yorkshire & Humber Unite Against Fascism regional conference

Date, Time & Location Sat 1 Dec 2012, 10am to 2pm, Swarthmore Centre, LEEDS, LS3 1AD
Details Unite Against Fascism aims to build a united movement to defend multiculturalism and oppose racism, get Andrew Brons MEP out, and make our region a fascist free zone by 2014. The conference will also reflect on the successes and lessons of the campaign to defeat the attempts by the EDL to intimidate and divide communities across Britain, as shown recently in Rotherham where UAF organised a peaceful multicultural celebration of over 400 people, addressed by local MPs, councillors, trade unionists, faith leaders and many others. Conference Chair: John Campbell, Chair Yorkshire & Humber UAF. Speakers include: Jon Trickett MP; Ratna Lachman, Director, JUST West Yorkshire; Kevin Donnelly, Vice-chair,Yorkshire & Humber TUC; Elizabeth Lawrence, UCU regional secretary; Ashiq Hussain, Chair, We Are Bradford; David Eales, PCS and Chair, Yorkshire & Humber TUC LGBT Froum; Weyman Bennett, Joint national Secretary UAF and Sabby Dhalu, Joint national Secretary UAF Please see for more details: www.yhuaf.org.uk
