Save Sheffield Sure Start Children's Centres

Date, Time & Location Mon 28 Feb 2011, 4-5pm. Broomhall Centre, Broomspring Lane
Details Sure Start Centres all over the country are under threat of closure and budget cuts. In Sheffield the Council may cut funding to all Sure Starts by 15% when they set the new budget on 4th March. This is a last chance before the meeting to talk to talk about the proposed cuts, with Councillor Mohammad Maroof (Labour Party Central Ward) Phone: 07415 428696 Email: defendoursurestart@gmail.com Facebook group: Defend Sheffield Surestart Childrens' Centres PLEASE sign the online petition on Sheffield City Council's website (if 5000 people sign the Council will have to have a full debate about cuts to Sure Start on 2nd March (two days before the budget is set). Lots of people have signed paper copies of the petition already but they need a lot more.
