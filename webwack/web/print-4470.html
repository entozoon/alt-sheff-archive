March and picnic : Save Sheffield Sure Start Children's Centres

Date, Time & Location Wed 23 Feb 2011, 12 noon from Devonshire Green (to the Town Hall)
Details Bring and share food. March from Devonshire Green to the Town Hall. A lovely picnic demonstration for Sure Start Centres, under threat of closure and budget cuts. In Sheffield the Council may cut funding to all Sure Starts by 15% when they set the new budget on 4th March, leading to a dramatic service reductions. Phone: 07415 428696 Email: defendoursurestart@gmail.com Facebook group: Defend Sheffield Surestart Childrens' Centres PLEASE sign the online petition on Sheffield City Council's website (if 5000 people sign the Council will have to have a full debate about cuts to Sure Start on 2nd March (two days before the budget is set). Lots of people have signed paper copies of the petition already but they need a lot more.
