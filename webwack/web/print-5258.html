Free show: Leeds Surrealist Group

Date, Time & Location Tue 9 Mar 2010, 6.30pm at Cinema Syndicate (University of Sheffield)
Details Cinema Syndicate is an anarchist film collective, open to all, showing through political and activist film that we live in an unjust world, and it needs to change. Film shows are free, every Tuesday night at Lecture Theatre 2, Arts Tower, University of Sheffield. 9th March is a special guest showing from the Leeds Surrealist Group, one not to be missed!
