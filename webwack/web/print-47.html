Flash dance practice for One Billion Rising

Date, Time & Location Mon 10 Feb 2020, 5.30-7pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Want a chance to rehearse ‘Break the Chain’ or ‘Un Violador En Tu Camino’ ready for 15th Feb? One Billion Rising is a global campaign to stop violence against women and girls. They are adding a new dance in solidarity with women in Chile who have taken to the streets to protest against human rights violations during the current political unrest.
