General Election Hustings: Central Constituency

Date, Time & Location Thu 23 Apr 2015, 6pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Hustings, in Central Constituency, on Thursday 23 April (not Wednesday as announced in Sheffield Telegraph!) at 6.00pm. "Sheffield for Democracy" is hosting this meeting to give voters the opportunity to question candidates in person Present will be: Paul Blomfield, Labour Jillian Creasy, Green Joe Otten, Lib Dem Conservative candidate Stephanie Roe will be sending a representative, Stephen Carstens Dominic Cook, UKIP Chair: Professor Sylvia Johnson. All welcome. Sheffield for Democracy will be taking a collection to pay for the room.
