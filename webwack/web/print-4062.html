The Media - From the Miners to Murdochgate

Date, Time & Location Tue 8 Nov 2011, 7pm, Wortley Hall, Sheffield S35 7DB
Details A meeting on the power of the media, with speakers Granville Williams of the Campaign for Press and Broadcasting Freedom and editor of "Shafted: the Media, the Miners Strike and the Aftermath", and Peter Lazenby, Industrial Correspondent of the Yorkshire Evening Post, who himself was arrested during the Miners' Strike. An event running alongside Ashes and Diamonds exhibition, a tribute to the mining industry and those who fought so long and hard to defend it, raising awareness and debate on relevant issues for today. Organised by the National Justice for Mineworkers Campaign and supported by Derbyshire NUM Venue details: www.wortleyhall.org.uk
