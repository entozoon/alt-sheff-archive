Sheffield Hallam Amnesty Society pubcrawl

Date, Time & Location Mon 6 Dec 2010
Details A charity pubcrawl in collaboration with University of Sheffield Amnesty Society, to raise funds for Amnesty International and awareness of censorship and political prisoners in Burma. Petition signing along the way, and distribution of informative beermats, as well as asking for donations from the general public. Meet at the Harley Hotel, Glossop Road, at 7pm, going down Division Street, no specific route plan, but if anyone is interested in going and can't make it till later, contact 07762 865906. Sheffield Amnesty International
