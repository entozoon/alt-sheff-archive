SPRING FAIR at Heeley City Farm

Date, Time & Location Sat 12 Apr 2014, 12-4pm Heeley City Farm, 25 Alexandra Road, S2 3EE
Details Spring is in the air, hooray! Come out and support Heeley City Farm and other worthy causes at the annual Spring Fair. Stalls to browse and buy from. Cuddly critters to hold in the Small Animal House PLUS a sale of cages and other pet housing. Blacksmiths demonstration in the Roundhouse Woodland area. Andy's Birds of Prey. Children's Crafts and Family Fuel Busters advice in the Energy Centre PLUS tours of the centre as part of the Green Homes open day. Lots of special offers in the Garden Centre for those who want to get their garden in shape. Cakes in the Farm Kitchen. Come and see the lovely lambs and goat kids - they are so cute! FREE admission PLUS raffle to raise a bit of money for the Farm. See you all there. Catch the No 47 to Herdings from outside Wilkinsons or the bottom of the Moor - it will drop you right outside the Farm. Heeley City Farm
