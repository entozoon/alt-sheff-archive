Sheffield 'Clone Town' debate

Date, Time & Location Tue 21 July 2009, 7.30pm at Electric Works, Digital Campus (opposite Railway Station)
Details 'Slack space ... A debate about avoiding a clone city'. Come and share ideas on how Sheffield can avoid looking like every other UK town during the recesssion. New ideas welcome by a panel of council and other speakers along with organisers Sheffield Civic Trust. Free for their members, £5/£2 concessions for the rest of us. Email: info@sheffieldcivictrust.org.uk
