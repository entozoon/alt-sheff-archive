World Development Movement (WDM) campaigning meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 11 Apr 2012, 7-9pm, Central United Reformed Church, 60 Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details The World Development Movement (WDM) is a UK-based anti-poverty campaigning organisation with actions to debate, understand and change things.
