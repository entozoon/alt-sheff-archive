Banner Cross Family Festival

Date, Time & Location Sat 17 Jul 2010 11am-4pm
Details Banner Cross's first Family Festival with lots for kids and adults. The festival is on Glenalmond Road and in the grounds and inside Banner Cross Methodist Church on Ecclesall Road South. It's a brand new venture for the Banner Cross Neighbourhood Group with over 50 stalls, a magician and a circus workshop and face painting. All welcome to drop by and support this community event.
