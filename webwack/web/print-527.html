Stories from Chatsworth Servants’ Hall

Date, Time & Location Tue 25 Sep 2018, 12-12.45pm, The Spiegeltent, Barkers Pool S1 2JA
Details "From Servants to Staff" research project is a collaboration between the University of Sheffield and the nearby Chatsworth Estate, a huge house and grounds still owned by a minor branch of the ’nobility’ (not by the people). Hear stories of dairy maids, gardeners, upholsterers and governesses through the eyes of the people who worked for the ’landed gentry’, who are probably very nice people, not parasites.
Free to attend, part of the 2018 Festival of the Mind, with Off the Shelf festival of words.
