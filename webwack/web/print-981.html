Day of action against Hewlett Packard

Date, Time & Location Sat 26 Nov 2016, outside John Lewis, Barkers Pool, S1 2JA
Details Hewlett Packard (HP) is one of the world’s largest IT companies. It’s one of the main suppliers to the Israeli military and security sectors, and to some settlements, playing a key role in the occupation and the violation of Palestinian rights. From the 25th November to 3rd December there is a global week of action to hold HP accountable. (See this video on YouTube). In Britain there is a national day of action on Saturday 26th November. This is also a celebration of the UN Day of solidarity with Palestinian people. Join Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign in the city centre outside John Lewis, a provider of HP products, to let the public know what HP are really like. After the action they will be joining a rally outside the Town Hall. The campaign is also asking people to sign a pledge (here) saying that they will not buy HP products in the future. Please sign yourself, and pass the link on to your friends. Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign
