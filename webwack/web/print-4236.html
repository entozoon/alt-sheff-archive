Woodseats Festival

Date, Time & Location CONTINUING Mon 4-Sun 10 Jul 2011
Details An annual festival held in various venues around Woodseats, all run by enthusiastic volunteers from the local community. Arts and crafts, music, performance, sport and leisure activities over a whole week during July. There's even a car boot sale. And a dog show called Woof-seats! What more could you ask? Full programme and details on the website. www.woodseatsfestival.org.uk
