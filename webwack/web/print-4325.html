Save Our NHS

Date, Time & Location Tue 3 May 2011, 7-9 pm; Victoria Hall, Chapel Walk, Sheffield
Details Sheffield Save Our NHS has invited Professor Wendy Savage to speak about the Coalition's NHS reforms and to open a broader discussion about how to make the NHS more effective without compromising its basic principles. The meeting will also seek to identify current and planned cuts in Sheffield's NHS services and how best to campaign against them. Wendy Savage is an internationally respected campaigner for women's rights in health and is co-chair of the national Keep Our NHS Public organisation Sheffield Save Our NHS came together through 38 degrees and is working with other groups including local trade unions, Sheffield MedAct and Sheffield Medsin who will also be speaking. www.sheffieldsaveournhs.co.uk http://ssonhs.blogspot.com/ email: team@sheffieldsaveournhs.co.uk
