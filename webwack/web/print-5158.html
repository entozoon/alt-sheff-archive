Asylum, Law and Justice

Date, Time & Location Tues 13 Apr 2010, 6pm Lecture Theatre 1, Arts Tower, University of Sheffield, Western Bank
Details Asylum, Law and Justice – follow-up meeting to set up a free legal support group for asylum seekers in South Yorkshire. A meeting of 60 people, law students, people seeking asylum, legal practitioners and activists met last month and agreed to set up a legal support group for people seeking asylum. This meeting will begin the work of this new organisation. Organised by: · South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG). Contact: dignitynotdetention@yahoo.co.uk · Student Action for Refugees (STAR). Contact: refugees@sheffield.ac.uk Stuart Crosthwaite, Secretary, South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG)
