QUEER: Exhibition

Date, Time & Location Fri 24-Sun 26 Feb 2012, The Black Rose Centre, 268 Verdon St. S3 9QS
Details An exhibition of local artists' work in response to LGBT History Month. Runs for 3 days, followed each evening by drinks at the nearby Riverside pub. Opening event Friday 6-10pm, then Saturday 6-9.30pm and Sunday 12-9pm. Facebook page: www.facebook.com/BlackRoseArt Email: Blackroseart@riseup.net Venue Website: www.sheffieldsocialcentre.org.uk
