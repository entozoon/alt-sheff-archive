AGM of Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change (SCACC)

Date, Time & Location Tues 28 Sep 2010, 7-9.30pm at Victoria Hall Methodist Church, 60 Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details The annual general meeting of SCACC, which organises days of action, film shows, talks and demonstrations on climate change. Speaker Councillor Andrew Sangar will present the Sheffield City Council vision on climate change and respond to proposals outlined in the Green New Deal - followed by questions. Suzanne Dhaliwal, speaker for UK Tar Sands Network will present the UK based campaign work against the extraction of oil by BP from the Canadian Tar Sands in Alberta including the links with First Nations struggle to regain control over their land. The Royal Bank of Scotland, which now owns NatWest Bank and is 84% owned by the British tax payer, is a major investor in the vast Tar Sands project which could eventually cover a land mass the size of the England. Note venue is Committee Room, entrance on Chapel Walk S1 2JB SCACC website
