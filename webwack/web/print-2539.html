Energy Use and Empowerment sessions

Date, Time & Location Mon 17 Mar 2014, 3-4.30pm & 6-7.30pm, Sheffield Central Library Archives & Info, Surrey St. S1 1XZ
Details David Garlovsky, US-born campaigner, long experienced in Sheffield as an activist and teacher of alternative technologies, demystifies energy use, efficiency and fuel-saving. Guides on energy and cost saving will be available. Special sessions will cover causes of feeling cold, drying clothes with limited ventialtion, radiator layout, air exchange, insulation, in-home renewable energy, green tax/levies, fuel bills and responsibilities of energy suppliers, and various tips including low-cost/no-cost fuels. Part of the National Science and Engineering Week and Sheffield Festival of Science and Engineering. To reserve your place, email info@inno-therm.com, or tel.
