Anti-Fracking near Doncaster

Date, Time & Location Sat 22 Feb 2014, 4pm, Daneshill Rd., Lound
Details A protest at the proposed site for exploratory drilling at Lound, a few miles south of Doncaster in one of the areas scheduled for fracking. Local residents and various campaign groups will be assembling, all support is welcome. www.frackfreesouthyorkshire.co.uk
