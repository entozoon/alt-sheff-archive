PAYP: Free environmental activities for 8-13 year olds

Date, Time & Location Thurs 28 Oct 2010, 11-4, Meersbrook Park, Sheffield S8 9FL
Details Positive Activities for Young People: This Session: Printing with natural dyes. Each session is a different activity ranging from den building and bug hunting to arts and crafts projects and sports activities. The sessions are open access and the young people are free to come and go as they please. All activities lasts between 2 and 2.5 hours, outdoors so please wear suitable clothing and sensible shoes. Refreshments: hot choc in the cold winter months! Sheffield Wildlife Trust is not a child care provider and under 8’s are welcome to attend but must be accompanied and supervised by a parent or guardian. Under 8’s activity box with treasure trails and bug hunting activities. You don’t need to book but if you are bringing a group please contact so they can bring sufficient resources. Got an idea for a session? Email us and they’ll try and include it in planning. For more info call Sheff 2634335 Email j.breider@wildsheffield.com
