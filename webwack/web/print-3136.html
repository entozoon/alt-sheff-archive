Fancy Dress Pub Crawl for Mental Illness

Date, Time & Location Fri 19 Apr 2013, meet 8.30pm outside FUBAR, Rotherham
Details Mental health charities are severly under-funded, yet 1 in 4 people experience a mental illness throughout one year. This pub crawl is to raise money for Depression Awareness Week and other mental illnesses. All money raised will go to the charity 'Mind'. If you see them during your night out, please give generously, or you can donate at http://uk.virginmoneygiving.com/raise-for-mind Facebook event www.mind.org.uk
