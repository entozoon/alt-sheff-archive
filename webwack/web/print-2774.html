Sheffield Equality Group monthly meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 6 Nov 2013, 7pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Sheffield Equality Group meetings is an open space where people can discuss and plan actions around income equality, an autonomous group affiliated to the national Equality Trust. All welcome. This time, launch of the Basic Income survey, with practice and discussion for those interested in interviewing friends, family, associates and the anonymous! Contributing to a wider discussion about welfare and benefits in Sheffield. www.sheffieldequality.wordpress.com
