Sheffield International Workers' Memorial Day

Date, Time & Location Thu 28 Apr 2011, 12.30pm outside the Town Hall
Details An important trade union event and opportunity to show respect for those workers who lost their lives while at work, while still fighting for the living. Ceremony at the Workers Memorial Tree which is situated in front of the Sheffield Town Hall. Councillor Alan Law, the Lord Mayor of Sheffield, has been invited to open the ceremony and a number of local trade unionists and citizens have been invited to speak.
