'Welcome' film show and discussion

Date, Time & Location Thur 7 Jan 2010, 6.10pm Showroom Cinema, Paternoster Row, Sheffield S1 2BX
Details Tonight's 6.10pm screening of this controversial French film about immigration is followed by a discussion of the film in Screen 5 from 8pm to 9pm, with speakers from French asylum/migrants' rights groups. See Showroom Cinema website for more information.
