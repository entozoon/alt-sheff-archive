DIVERSITY FEST

Date, Time & Location Sat 19 Sep 2015, 2pm-midnight, Hagglers Corner, 586 Queens Rd S2 4DU
Details The first ever for Sheffield: a free celebration for people of every gender, sexuality, ethnicity, age and ability. A statement of unity and mutual respect between the many communities in Sheffield. The programme includes events in the Hagglers Corner cafe, upstairs and outside. Talks, film, spoken word, bands, acoustic and solo musicians, dancers, DJs, films, kids activities, collaborative art work and creative mini-yurt. Entry free. Diversity Fest
