Food at the Third Sector Cafe: Grow, Give, Make, Sell

Date, Time & Location Thu 15 May 2014, 9.30am-11.30am, Jesus Centre, 93 Broomspring Lane (off Hanover Way) S10 2FB
Details The Third Sector Cafe discussion this month will be around food - growing it, giving it, making it and selling it. If food is important to the work of your not-for-profit organisation, please share your experiences during Sheffield Food Festival month, and hear more about what organisations are up to with food in Sheffield. People coming along to talk about their projects include: Dr Megan Blake of Sheffield University, on urban food, farming and justice Kim Swan, Victoria Community Cafe John Le Corney, Heeley City Farm Dr Caroline Hart, Sheffield Hallam University/Sheffield Food Board Lisette Baker, The Children’s Food Trust Dave Cates, Redemption Media Jo Densley, Relish Marketing Karina Tape, Buster’s Coffee Company More details and booking: Eventbrite
