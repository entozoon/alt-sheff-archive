Singing and cycling in harmony

News February 2014, planning for July

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Sheffield Harmony plans to run a singing workshop as part of the cultural festival supporting the Tour de France to celebrate and promote singing in harmony, social cycling, participation, fun, social harmony, creativity, green credentials, sustainability, new skills, new experiences, broadening horizons and stuff. One aim of the event is to raise awareness of different kinds of bikes and approaches to cycling and emphasise accessibility. Participants will learn a cycling/celebration themed song, and the workshop will culminate in a glorious performance on wheels! Those more comfortable on foot will provide back-up accompaniment. Unusual bikes very welcome.

Preparations will probably start on 27th-30th March, for a "Singing and Cycling in Harmony" performance on 5th/6th July. Would you be interested? Cyclists, non-cyclists, less confident cyclists welcome, and they would particularly like to hear from tandem riders.
