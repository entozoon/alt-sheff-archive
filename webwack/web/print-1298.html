Date, Time & Location Fri 13 May 2016, 8pm, The Audacious Art Experiment, 107A Harwood St. S2 4SE
Details A fundraiser gig run by LaDIYFest Sheffield, a DIY, anti-capitalist, community based, feminist festival group run by volunteers, led primarily by women but all are welcome to get involved.
