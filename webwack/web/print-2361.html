Cycle to the Cinema: Bamford

Date, Time & Location Sat 14 Jun 2014, Bamford Village Institute, Main Road, Bamford, S33 0DY
Details Cycle to the Cinema offers inspiring films at great venues ... and you cycle to them. This is a programme of 3 mountain biking films including ‘Roam’ starring the world’s top riders including Sheffield legend Steve Peat. Tickets: Adults £5, under 16 £2. Tickets are available on the door, and online. Starts 7.30 (please arrive 7pm). Bamford Village Institute is opposite The Anglers Rest, and it has bike racks. www.cycletothecinema.co.uk
