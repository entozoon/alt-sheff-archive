Sheffield Doc/Fest

Date, Time & Location 10-15 Jun 2016
Details The annual festival of the art and business of documentary making. Workshops, screenings, pitching opportunities, discussion panels and more. This year it features 160 films on themes including Instigators & Agitators, Women in Docs, Queer Screen, and Behind the Beats. Venues: cinemas across Sheffield and outdoor screens on Howard Street and Tudor Square. www.sheffdocfest.com
