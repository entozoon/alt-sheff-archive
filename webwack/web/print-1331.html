Talk: Steve Bell

Date, Time & Location Thu 21 Apr 2016, 7.30pm, Auditorium, Sheffield Students’ Union, Western Bank S10 2TG
Details Why penguins? Whose underpants? Which mad eye? Steve Bell reveals the deep dark secrets of the world of political cartooning and his ongoing mission to tear holes in the clothing of the political establishment. Across four decades, Steve Bell has lampooned just about every prominent figure in the world of politics. Witness how he does it, in a vividly illustrated political history of our times with accompanying voice and sound effects. Part of Festival of Debate 2016, hosted by Opus Independents which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine. Tickets £8/£6.
