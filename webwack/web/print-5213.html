'Spring into 2010' cycle rides

Date, Time & Location Sun 21 Mar 2010
Details Meet at the Cathedral tram stop before 10am for a tram-assisted ride out of Sheffield to Waterthorpe at £5 return for bike plus rider, £10 for tandem and riders, £2 for child with own bike. (NB This is a specially commissioned tram, not for public use, so if you miss it sorry there isn't another one you can board!) Cycle through the housing estate on off-road cycle track to Rother Valley Country Park (where you can join in if you live at that side of town). Toilets and cafe for a picnic if you've not food. Then separate to either easy, intermediate or longer routes. A ride to suit all preferences. Return about 3.45pm to town centre. Come on, help fill the tram and beat the record of 34 bikes and riders last year. Cycle Sheffield website
