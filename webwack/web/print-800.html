Standard: Elite

Date, Time & Location Wed 14-Thu 15 Jun 2017, 7.30pm, Theatre Delicatessen, 17 The Moor S1 3LR
Details An interactive theatre show exploring class and social mobility by splitting its audience in two. ‘Elites’ decide the outcome of the show. ‘Standards’ get to play games. Game winners get to become Elites. So that’s fair for everyone. Right? Part of Festival of Debate, in association with Theatre Delicatessen. Ticket prices from £7-£10 Festival of Debate
