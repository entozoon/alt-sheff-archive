Sheffield Vigil for Climate Justice !!

Date, Time & Location Fri 11 Dec 2009, start 5pm outside Nick Clegg MP's office 85 Nethergreen Rd, Sheffield, S11 7EH
Details In response to the call-out for climate action around the world, join a candlelit walk up to Bole Hill Park in Crookes, where Chinese lanterns will be released to display support. Reducing carbon dioxide in the air down to 350 parts per million is simply a matter of survival. Protest on the streets and radical action can make the Copenhagen summit do this. If not, what else?
