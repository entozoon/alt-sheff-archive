"Art for All in Sheffield?"

Date, Time & Location Sat 12 Oct 2013 9.30 - 2.30 Millennium Gallery, city centre
Details Sheffield Visual Arts Group is asking "Are the City's Art Galleries weathering the Storm?" Hear what is going on and share your opinions. Free to all. Email on sheffvisarts@gmail.com if you are coming. They will provide tea and coffee but bring your own lunch. Speakers: Gordon Bridge, Isobel Bowler, Janet Murray, Amanda Wade, Kim Street, Paul Allen, Sian Brown. The day is dedicated to a dear colleague Keith Hayman. Millennium Gallery
