Paths to Climate Justice: Positive feedback from Copenhagen

Date, Time & Location Mon 25 Jan 2010, 6.30-9.15pm at Quaker Meeting House, St James St. S1 2EW (near Cathedral tramstop)
Details Copenhagen: What happened, and what next? Come and hear from Sheffield people who were present in Copenhagen about the new coalition for Climate Justice, in the face of the failure of the COP15 talks. Join with others to plan what we in Sheffield can do to develop resilient communities and work in solidarity with people from the Global South Organised by Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change http://sheffield.campaigncc.org
