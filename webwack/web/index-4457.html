An Evening with Moazzam Begg: Former Guantánamo Prisoner

Wed 2 Mar 2011, 6.30pm Darnall Education Centre, Starworks Youthclub Building, Darnall Rd S9 5AJ

A free event, everyone welcome as Yorkshire Muslim Writers presents An Evening with Moazzam Begg, who for many years was imprisoned by the USA in their witch-hunt following 9/11. A British citizen, Moazzam and his family campaigned his innocence, and after his release he continues to speak out against injustice.
