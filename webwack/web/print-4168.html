ROTHERHAM SHOW & DIVERSITY FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sat 10 and Sun 11 Sep 2011, 10am-6pm, Clifton Park, Rotherham
Details The biggest free show in the north returns to Clifton Park, close to Rotherham town centre. It's massive. Enough stalls to get lost in, Diversity Festival, animals, horticulture, music and dancing and food from every corner of the world. A great day out and showcase for Rotherham's community activism.
