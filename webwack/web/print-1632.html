Sheffield Students Housing Co-operative launch

Date, Time & Location Sat 3 Oct 2015, 131 Northfield Rd. Crookes
Details The launch party and general meeting of the new housing co-operative run by and for students and graduates. The profit gets re-invested into the co-op, rather than going to a landlord. Occupants share the real costs and joys of house maintenance, democratically making all the decisions about finances and management. 11am Annual General Meeting 4pm Party 6pm food; bring a dish to share. Badge making and screen printing to try as well. Sheffield Students Housing Co-operative
