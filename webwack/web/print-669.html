Green Party meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 6 Nov 2017, 7pm, Central United Reformed Church, 60 Norfolk Row S1 2JB
Details Non-member supporters and members of Sheffield Green Party (SGP) are welcome to attend this meeting to discuss city-wide policies and actions. There will be a report from the tree protest injunction court cases. The main agenda item will be a discussion and decision on whether or not SGP should stand a candidate in the Sheffield City Region Mayoral Election (probably in May 2018). Sheffield Green party usually meets on the first Monday of the month, 7pm for 7.15pm start. Central United Reformed Church is at the end of Chapel Walk opposite the Crucible. Sheffield Green Party
