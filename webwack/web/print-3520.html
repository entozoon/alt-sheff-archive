Growing Fruit: A course of 10 evening classes

Date, Time & Location Mon 1 Oct 2012, 7pm-9pm, Central United Reform Church, Chapel Walk, S1 2JB
Details An introduction to growing perennial foods. First of 10 Monday evening sessions for beginners and experienced learners. Tutor Richard Clare was the founder of the Abundance project, and Sheffield Organic Food Initiative. He has been actively involved in organic cultivation for many years and has put his extensive knowledge into this course. (Note, to avoid confusion, there are also Wed evening classes on more general food growing from 3rd Oct.)
