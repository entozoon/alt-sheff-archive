AGM of Sheffield Green Party

Date, Time & Location Wed 8 Dec 2010, 7.15 for 7.30pm St Matthews, Carver St, Sheffield, S1 4FT
Details Sheffield Green Party's annual general meeting, a great celebration of the rising tide of green activism. Speaker Caroline Dowd (President Sheffield Hallam University Students Union) plus panel Q & A on the anti-cuts campaign including tuition fee proposals. Speaker & Q & A from 7.30pm with AGM after interval at 8.50 pm. Refreshments will be served before the meeting and in the interval. All members, friends and prospective members are welcome to attend. If you would like a copy of the accounts for the last financial year before the meeting please contact. PLEASE NOTE THE AGM IS NOT AT THE USUAL MONTHLY MEETING VENUE. Sheffield Green Party website
