The 24 Hour Inspire

Date, Time & Location Thu 27-Fri 28 Mar 2014, Firth Hall, University of Sheffield
Details 24 hours of lectures on a wide range of topics - from physics to philosophy, from law to literature, from maths to medicine - all for non-specialist audiences, by speakers from across the University of Sheffield and beyond, in celebration of Dr Tim Richardson (1964-2013) and his charity Inspiration for Life, and raising funds for local cancer charities. See Inspiration for Life website for updates as the event approaches, and for general enquires email c.j.annabel@sheffield.ac.uk
