Cycle To The Cinema: "Tour de Cinema"

Sat 21-Sun 22 Jun 2014, Cliffhanger, Millhouses Park, Abbeydale Road South S7 2QQ

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

A one-hour loop of the best short films from across all the sessions at this year's Sheffield Adventure Film Festival, screened continually in a dedicated "Cycle To The Cinema" marquee at Cliffhanger. If you arrive part-way through the loop you can simply watch until the loop finishes and continue watching the next loop until you catch up!Note the Tour de Cinema is free once you've paid to enter the Cliffhanger event which is £6 for adults and £2 for under 16s.Programme:10 Things Every Mountain Biker Should Have Done (3mins)Restless (7mins)Duct Tape Surfing (5mins)Just Jumpy The Dog (2mins)Nine Knights – Bike 2012 (5mins)Sarah Ridgeway – Mountain Runner (3mins)Frame Running (2mins)The Burn (7mins)The Epic Urban One-Shot (4mins)In The Frame – Little Chamonix (10mins)Sportlife: Water (8mins)www.cycletothecinema.co.uk
