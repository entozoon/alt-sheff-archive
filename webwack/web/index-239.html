Edible and Medicinal Plant Walk

Sun 26 May 2019, 1.30 to 3pm, Heeley People’s Park, View Rd. S2 3DJ

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Meet opposite the Brothers Arms pub (106 Well Road, Heeley S8 9TZ) and take a gentle walk around the park while identifying plants and learning practical ways of using them as food or medicine. You'll never look at a 'weed' in the same way again. No need to book, just turn up. Suggested donation £5-£15.Part of Festival of Debate 2019, hosted by not-for-profit social enterprise Opus Independents (which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine). Our Planet strand kindly sponsored by Sheffield Climate Alliance and Regather, in association with Unity Edible Gardens.
