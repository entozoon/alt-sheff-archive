Course: Political Values and Ideas for Activists

From May 2011

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

A new course by the WEA (Workers' Educational Association) offers you the chance to take stock of where we are today and to explore how political values and ideas have developed and changed.

The course will consider political ideas from the 1940s, will explore how participants developed their own ideas and values and will focus on dominant values in society today.

Starts Wednesday 4 May 10.30am-1pm and will run on 8 consecutive Wednesdays at WEA, Sheffield Learning Centre, 3 Vicarage Road, Attercliffe, S9 3RH. Book your place now by contacting cmakison@wea.org.uk or tel Sheff. 2423609
