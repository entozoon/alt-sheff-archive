People’s Assembly Saturday Information Point

Date, Time & Location Sat 19 Jul 2014, 11.30am-2.30pm, Moor Market main entrance, The Moor
Details Every month, South Yorkshire People’s Assembly has been running this information point, to campaign and raise awareness among people in the city centre. SYPA is an umbrella organisation (part of the National People’s Assembly) bringing together and supporting individuals, organisations, community campaigns and trade unions as a collective force for positive social change and united resistance against ’austerity’. All anti-austerity organisations are welcome to join in. Call by and find out what’s really going on. South Yorkshire People’s Assembly
