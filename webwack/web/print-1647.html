"Time to Act" Conference

Date, Time & Location Sat 19 Sep 2015, 10.30am-4.45pm Sheffield College, Granville Rd. S2 2RL,
Details A national conference about divesting from fossil fuels, building the climate movement and investing in climate jobs to create warm, healthy homes, public transport with low fares and renewable energy infrastructure. Organised by the Trade Union Group of Campaign against Climate Change and Sheffield Climate Alliance. Confirmed speakers include: Louise Haigh, MP for Sheffield Heeley Asad Rehman, International Campaigner from Friends of the Earth Danielle Paffard, divestment co-ordinator at 350.org Suzanne Jeffery, Chair of Campaign against Climate Change Sheffield Climate Alliance
