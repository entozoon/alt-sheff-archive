“Why Does God Hate Women?”

Date, Time & Location Tue 4 Mar 2014, 8pm, The University Arms, 197 Brook Hill, S3 7HG
Details Brian Quinn of The Humanist Society of West Yorkshire will address the question of misogyny in religion, and consider why it is that women tolerate the discrimination practiced by many world religions. A talk open to the public, organised by the Sheffield Humanist Society, all welcome. www.sheffieldhumanists.org.uk
