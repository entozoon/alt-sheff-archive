Sheffield Friday Night Ride

Date, Time & Location Fri 22 Jun 2012, start 6.30pm Devonshire Green, Sheffield S3 7SB
Details Sheffield Friday Night Ride (FNR) offers a fun monthly evening bike ride for everyone. Rides are prepared in advance on themes. This time: Redmires and Stanage Pole. (Off-road bikes can get on to Stanage Edge). Why? Beautiful route and place to get to, longest Friday of the year so catch the sunset, SFNR’s 4th birthday – celebrate 4 yrs and over 50 different SFNRs. More details on the website.
