Lifting the Lid: Ongoing Adventures in the World of Pseudoscience

Date, Time & Location Mon 28 Apr 2014, 7.30pm Farm Road Sports & Social Club, Farm Rd. S2 2TP
Details Skeptics in the Pub is about getting people together to have a relaxed and enjoyable evening while listening to talks on a wide range of topics, usually by a notable scientist or skeptic. Talks are typically about a common belief that can be demonstrated to be false or cannot be justified by the available evidence. This month, Michael Marshall will show what happens when you begin to crack the surface of the pseudosciences that surround us – revealing the surprising, sometimes-shocking and often-comic adventures that lie beneath. Free event, although they ask for a £3 donation. http://sheffield.skepticsinthepub.org
