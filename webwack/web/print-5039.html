Run for ALL Life

Date, Time & Location Sat 22 May 2010
Details Please support this off-road 5km and 10km run held in Ecclesall Woods. It will fund the Dr Hadwen Trust for Humane Research, the leading medical research charity funding exclusively non-animal techniques to replace animal experiments, benefiting both humans and animals. You can take part, sponsor someone else or make a donation. More information at website: www.drhadwentrust.org/get-active/events or email: dawn.biram @ virgin.net
