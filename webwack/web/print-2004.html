One Billion Rising - preparation dance workshop: Sheffield 50+

Date, Time & Location Wed 11 Feb 2015, 12pm City-Limits Dance Studios, 607-613 Penistone Rd S6 2GA
Details One Billion Rising is holding a flashdance as part of the global movement to end violence against women (see Saturday 14th Feb below). These events are Dance Workshops: Learn the "Break the Chain" dance with the help of experienced dance teachers, (and the practice video) in preparation for the flashdances in Sheffield and Rotherham city centres.
