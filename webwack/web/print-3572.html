Hollaback! Sheffield launch party

Date, Time & Location Fri 7 Sep 2012, from 8pm, Redhouse, 168 Solly St. S1 4BB
Details Hollaback! is a movement dedicated to ending street harassment and we believe everyone should feel confident and safe at all times on the streets of Sheffield. Come along to find out more about the project, mark the online launch and share your experiences! Together we have the power to end street harassment! LaDIYfest are helping throw this party, all are welcome to celebrate, become part of and party with the new branch of Hollaback!
