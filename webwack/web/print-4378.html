Lantern-making workshop for Sharrow Lantern Carnival

Date, Time & Location Sat 2 Apr 2011, 12-4pm
Details Sharrow Lantern Carnival is one of Sheffield's most colourful, fun events and a great annual festival start to the spring. This year it's on the evening of Sunday 3rd April on the theme of Elements, so lantern-making workshops in preparation run on Saturdays through February, March until this final one on 2nd April.
