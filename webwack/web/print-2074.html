Sheffield Friends of the Earth meeting and meal

Date, Time & Location Mon 15 Dec 2014, 7pm Red Deer, 18 Pitt St. S1 4DD, off West St.
Details Instead of the usual meeting at 8pm, Friends of the Earth are celebrating another successful year of campaigning with a festive meal in the upstairs room at the Red Deer. There will be a short meeting at 7pm to get the party started and discuss any important business before the food arrives at 7.30pm. If you want to join in for dinner please see menu choices and make contact by 1st of December via the website (see below). Sheffield FOE website
