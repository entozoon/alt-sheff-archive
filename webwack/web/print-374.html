FOODHALL PUBLIC CHRISTMAS

Date, Time & Location Tue 25 Dec 2018, 10am-5pm, Foodhall, 121 Eyre St. S1 4QW
Details Foodhall offers its big public Christmas day. Free opening all day. Lots of food, crackers, games, tree, etc etc. All year round, Foodhall promotes community, skill sharing, education, participation, equality and inclusion, from the dining room and kitchen at the heart of Sheffield city centre, using food waste streams from local traders. All welcome, come along if you're free and want some fun on Christmas day! Foodhall
