'How the vote was won'

Date, Time & Location Fri 13 Aug 2010, 7pm at Wortley Hall, Sheffield S35 7DB
Details Find out how votes for women were achieved in the early part of last century. The 8th annual Sylvia Pankhurst Memorial Lecture is given by Irene Cockcroft, exhibition curator, author and lecturer. This lecture is sponsored by the National Assembly of Women, the Sylvia Pankhurst Memorial Committee and Wortley Hall and will be followed by light refreshments. If you require accommodation at Wortley Hall (the "workers' stately hall") for the night you should contact them on Sheff 2882100, Wortley Hall website
