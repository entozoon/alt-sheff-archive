"Building with Bales"

Date, Time & Location Sat 23 Nov 2013 in Todmorden
Details An essential one day seminar for all strawbale self builders with Barbara Jones, FRSA, author of the best selling self build manual The seminar focusses on the project management of your strawbale build, explaining techniques you can use to bring your project in on quality, on budget and on time.
