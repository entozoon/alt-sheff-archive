New Takes on Fallen Women in Global Cinema

Date, Time & Location Tue 16 Oct 2018, 5.45pm, Waterstones Bookshop cafe, Orchard Square S1 2FB
Details A talk by Dr Kate Taylor Jones of the School of East Asian Studies at the University of Sheffield. She explores global cinematic representations of the image of the prostitute, and the debate on repressive and empowering depictions. Free to attend, no need to book, but spaces are limited and allocated on a first-come, first-served basis. Book tickets at the link below. Part of the 2018 Off the Shelf festival of words.
