Film: Closing the Loop

Date, Time & Location Thu 7 Mar 2019, 7pm, Regather, 57-59 Club Garden Rd. S11 8BU
Details Guest presenters Unwrapped (zero waste shopping in Sheffield) host this film night on global sustainability. Going “circular” refers to the necessary change from our current take-make-waste linear economy to a borrow-use-return circular economy (sometimes also referred to as the zero-waste or cradle-to-cradle economy). The conclusion; "Unless we go to Circular it’s game over for the planet; it’s game over for society". Tickets £5. Regather
