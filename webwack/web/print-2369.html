South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 10 Jun 2014, 6.30pm Exchange Brewery, 2 Bridge St. S3 8NS
Details SYMAAG unites refugee, asylum and migrant organisations with campaign groups and supporting individuals. It campaigns both for the rights of those seeking asylum and of those coming to work in this country. They will be discussing the future of asylum advice services, given that a company called Migrant Help (www.migranthelp.org) was recently given the contract.
