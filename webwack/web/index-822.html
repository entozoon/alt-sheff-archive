Drag power couple, Adam All and Apple Derrieres return to Walkley Commmunity Centre to host Andro and Eve´s celebration of male impersonation in a special Holiday Camp Edition. The line-up has some of the UK´s most talented Drag Kings including Luke Warm, Romeo De La Cruz, Oliver Assets and Oedipussi. Put on your holiday best, because we´re all going on a summer holigay! Gender bending costumes encouraged! It will be surreal. It will be fabulous. There will be cake. All hail the King. Tickets £12 Advance, available from Tickets for Good or Beeches of Walkley. Everyone very welcome. Doors open from 7.30pm. Cash bar available on the night. Andro and Eve
