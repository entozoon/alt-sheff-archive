"Spirit of 1868"

Date, Time & Location Mon 24 Sep 2018, 7.30pm, Trades & Labour Club, 200 Duke St. S2 5QQ
Details Banner Theatre presents a stirring dramatisation of some of the struggles, protests and moments of resistance that shaped our past and our present. Featuring folk, blues, punk, reggae and stories of workers standing together in solidarity over 150 years. Doors and bar open from 6.45pm.
