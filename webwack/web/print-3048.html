PEACE IN THE PARK is ***NOT*** GOING AHEAD THIS YEAR

Date, Time & Location Sat 8 Jun 2013
Details Sadly, the Peace in the Park festival has been postponed until June 2014. This is due to the problems of fundraising in the current tough economic climate, central government cuts, the increased costs of living and declining incomes. This tough decision will be a disappointment for the city, but with your support Peace in the Park will be back! The next Peace in the Park festival is now planned for next year (June 14th 2014). All money already raised will go towards this 2014 festival. Read the full statement at: Peace in the Park website
