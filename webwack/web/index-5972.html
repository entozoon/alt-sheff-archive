Credit union moves into new shop

October 2011

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Sheffield Credit Union has, for many years, run its excellent member-owned service of loans, savings and financial help. Profits go to members not shareholders, and the management committee is voluntary, elected by members, so they can offer great value and proper service.

After a long time tucked out of the way above the market, they are now based in splendid new shop premises, at 16 Commercial Street S1 2AT (near the tram stop, opposite Jessops Cameras). Opening times are 10am-1pm Mon, Tue, Wed, Fri. Tel Sheff 2760787, email admin@fisy1.co.uk
