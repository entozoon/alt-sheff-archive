Westfield: Creative community planning in an era of austerity

Date, Time & Location Thu 25 Jun 2015, 9.30-5.30, Shortbrook Primary School & Com.Unity, Westfield S20 8FB
Details A full day conference in and about Westfield (south-east of Sheffield), one of many places and communities dramatically impoverished under the current economic system. Full title: Creative community planning in an era of austerity: the Westfield International Planning Conference. Organised by The Department of Town and Regional Planning at the University of Sheffield and the Town and Country Planning Association, in association with Westfield Big Local, in partnership with residents of Westfield. Speakers include Pam Warhurst (Incredible Edible Todmorden) and Mandy Wilson (on Big Local). Full details and booking at: Eventbrite page
