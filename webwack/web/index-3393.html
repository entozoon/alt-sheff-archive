"Drones: The Future of War": AGM of Sheffield CND

Wed 21 Nov 2012, 7.15pm at Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James Street S1 2EW

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

The annual general meeting, open to the public, of Sheffield Campaign for Nuclear Disarmament. Chris Coles of the Drones Campaign Network will be speaking and answering questions about the horrifying new reality of this lethal military technology, some of which is built, tested and operated by the UK. Come and join CND for this important event.For more information see www.dronewars.net, a source of information on the growing use of armed drones and www.dronecampaignnetwork.org.ukSheffield CND meets on the third Wednesday of each month, except August, newcomers are welcome. www.sheffieldcnd.org.uk
