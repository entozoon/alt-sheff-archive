South Yorkshire Wildlife Action Day

Date, Time & Location Sat 29 Jan 2011, 10am to 4pm, Kelham Island Industrial Museum
Details Action Day for conservation and wildlife groups from across the Sheffield region. Chance to discuss some of the key conservation issues, meet others, exchange ideas and find out more about a variety of projects, and promote the biodiversity and wildlife / nature conservation cause at the grassroots level.
