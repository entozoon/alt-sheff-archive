No More Rich or Poor? Building a more equal Sheffield

Date, Time & Location Tues 20 Apr 2010, 7pm The Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW (near Anglican cathedral)
Details Kate Pickett, co-author of The Spirit Level - Why Equal Societies Almost Always Do Better and Danny Dorling, author of Injustice: Why Social Inequality Persists, will discuss why the gap between rich and poor matters and how we can change. Reception and exhibition from 6pm, donations on the door, all welcome. www.sheffieldequalitytrust.org.uk Phone 07956 384142
