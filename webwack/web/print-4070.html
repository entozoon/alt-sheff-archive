Introduction to Permaculture course

Date, Time & Location Sat 5 and Sun 6 Nov 2011, 9.30am-5pm Sharrow Old Junior School, South View Rd. S7 1DB
Details The permaculture design group within Transition Sheffield presents an introduction to permaculture, exploring how to design our world in harmony with nature. Learn from ecosystems how to solve problems and reap a harvest in our homes, gardens, communities and city. Make connections with people and projects in Sheffield. The course runs Saturday and Sunday, plus optional Saturday evening social. Taught by Joe Atkinson and Jenny Patient. Cost: £45 (conc) or £75 (standard) or £120 (supporting). To book, send a £20 deposit payable to ‘Leeds Permaculture Network’ by 31st Oct to: 49 Steade Road, S7 1DS. Call Sheff 2587073 or email jenny . patient at blueyonder .co.uk for more info.
