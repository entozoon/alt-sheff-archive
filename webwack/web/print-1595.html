Greenpeace meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 10 Nov 2015, 8pm, Red Lion 109 Charles St., corner of Charles St./Eyre Lane
Details South Yorkshire Greenpeace highlights local, national and international environmental issues and discusses how to effectively campaign in a positive way, as a local group, whilst supporting the Greenpeace organisation as a whole. They welcome all people from the South Yorkshire area who would like to get involved. Meetings are fun and informal, with lots of laughs and perhaps a few pints of beer to enjoy the evening with too, so please do come along. South Yorkshire Greenpeace
