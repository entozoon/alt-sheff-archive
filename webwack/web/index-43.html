Extinction Rebellion Sheffield open meeting

Mon 17 Feb 2020, Union St. co-working space, 18-22 Union St. S1 2JP

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

On Monday evenings the Union St Cafe is open til 6.30pm for anyone to relax after work and meet up before the XR meeting. From 6pm hot vegan food is served for anyone coming to the XR meeting, Pay As You Feel.From 6.45pm there is an induction meeting if anyone's attending for their first time on the ground floor. And also at 6.45pm the One Space (1st floor; lift available) is open for the main meeting, to start at 7pm.More info on the national movement at: rebellion.earthSheffield group website: xrsheffield.org Sheffield Extinction Rebellion on Facebook
