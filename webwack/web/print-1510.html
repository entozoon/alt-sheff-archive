Public debate: Trident and Labour

Date, Time & Location Sat 16 Jan 2016, 2pm Central United Reformed Church, Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details The Government will bring the decision on the renewal of the Trident nuclear weapons system to Parliament in early 2016. In preparation, Nether Edge and Central Labour Party branches will host this mmeeting to take a balanced look at arguments for and against renewal and the consequences for employment. A public debate hosted by Nether Edge and Central Labour Party branches. Speakers and further details to follow. Wheelchair accessible venue. (Note: CND hope to have a meeting with Sheffield MPs in March. We will update you on these events as information becomes available.)
