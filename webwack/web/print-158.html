Extinction Rebellion NVDA training

Date, Time & Location Wed 4 Sep 2019, 7pm-9pm, Union St, 18-20 Union St. S1 2JP
Details Non Violent Direct Action (NVDA) training for Extinction Rebellion, etc. in the peaceful movement addressing the climate crisis. Covers the practice of non-violent techniques: how to de-escalate tense situations, how to interact with the police, what to do if you get arrested and much more. This training is designed to give you confidence when on protests and is essential for anyone who wishes to get more info on their rights, generally and in non-violent direct action. Please note, this is not just for those who are willing to get arrested, it is for everyone with an interest in the cause. Training is free, but small donations to cover room hire costs are appreciated. Please reserve a space so they can keep track of numbers. Facebook event page
