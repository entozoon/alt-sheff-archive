Sheffield Friday Night Ride

Date, Time & Location Fri 23 Apr 2010, meet 6.30 pm in Waitrose car park by ATM machines
Details Sheffield Friday Night Ride (FNR) offers a fun monthly evening bike ride for everyone. Rides are prepared in advance on themes. This month's theme is Sheffield's 5 remaining Vestry Halls, which apparently were administrative and social centres before the Victorian local government reforms and creation of towns, boroughs, cities etc. FridayNightRide website
