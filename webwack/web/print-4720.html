Regather opening day

Date, Time & Location Sat 23 Oct 2010, 10am-4pm, 57-59 Club Garden Road
Details The opening day of a new co-operative for Sheffield, trading locally made products and services, with a local shop, a van, a trike and a website, and opportunities for local volunteers, students and community organisations. Membership offers the benefits of mutual trading, share dividends and voting rights. Like all co-operatives it promotes the values of fairness, co-operation and common wealth www.regather.net
