Meeting: "Fools paradise? How tax dodging harms us all"

Date, Time & Location Wed 1 Apr 2015, 7pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details A Sheffield Equality Group meeting, open to all. Informal talk around the impact tax dodging by individuals and companies has on our society. There will then be a discussion on the taxes we all pay, redistribution, and how we might like to do tax in the future. Free, donations requested for tea and biscuits. Sheffield Equality Group
