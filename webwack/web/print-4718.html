Anti-Cuts Rally

Date, Time & Location 23 Oct 2010, 12.30-3pm, Sheffield Town Hall
Details Deep, rapid spending cuts will hit the vulnerable, undermine vital services and threaten both private and public sector jobs. Join in the regional protest and rally being organised by the Yorkshire and Humber TUC and let your voice be heard.  Brendan Barber and Ed Milliband will be speaking, among others. A 'feeder march' leaves Sheff University at 11.30am Further details: Yorkshire and Humber TUC: 0113 2429696  UK website (not very helpful, Sheffield mentioned near the bottom) Local coverage on Sheffield Indymedia
