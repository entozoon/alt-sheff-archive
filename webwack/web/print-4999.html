Sheffield Anarchist Feminist Network

Date, Time & Location Fri 11 June 2010, 7pm Rutland Arms, 86 Brown Street S1 2BS
Details First meeting of a network to share ideas and work together in direct action in a liberated space, rejecting racist, sexist and homophobic views. Open to anyone who wishes to discuss anarchist feminist issues whether they would class themselves as anarchist feminists or not, regardless of gender. There are intentions to host a conference/dayschool in the future and the group will be looking for contributions to these events. Email: shefanarfem @ gmail.com
