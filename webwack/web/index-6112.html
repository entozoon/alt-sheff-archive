Abbeydale School closure fight continues

Nov/Dec 2009

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Abbeydale Grange School makes a unique contribution to Sheffield's education, but it faces closure despite massive opposition. The council makes the decision on 9th December 2009.

The protest group website is at www.saveabbeydalegrange.co.uk

There is a good political analysis of the planned closure and need for protest at WSWS socialist website article

Also a supporters' video at Demonstration video

And various parents and pupils' opinions at Parents and pupils speaking out
