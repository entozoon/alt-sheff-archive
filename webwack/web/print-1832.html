Festival of Debate: George Monbiot

Date, Time & Location Thu 16 Apr 2015, 7pm, SU Auditorium, Sheffield Students’ Union, S10 2TG
Details Award-winning author and investigative journalist, George Monbiot’s writing spans topics such as the environment, political corruption and corporate misdeeds. A tireless campaigner, advocate for human rights and never one to shy away from a controversial topic. Tickets £7.50 (£6 concessions) from www.su.sheffield.ac.uk. The Festival of Debate is taking place in Sheffield in the run-up to the General election, during March and April. It provides opportunities to get together and discuss a range of issues which affect everyone. Informed decision making and strategic planning are essential for democracy, so come and get involved.
