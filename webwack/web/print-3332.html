Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change/Sheffield Climate Alliance meeting

Date, Time & Location Tues 15 Jan 2013, venue to be confirmed
Details Meeting of Sheffield climate campaigners, following the cancellation of the December meeting. Meetings are open to newcomers, all welcome, generally on the 3rd Tuesday of the month. The agenda will probably include the proposed coal "washing" development in Chapeltown, see www.cowleyrag.com and attendance at an international gathering of 500 climate activists in June next year, see www.globalpowershift.org and how to make the most of it to build the climate change movement in Sheffield.
