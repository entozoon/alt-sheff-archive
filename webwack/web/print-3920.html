South Yorkshire Migration & Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG) meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 21 Feb 2012, 6.30pm Scotia Works, Leadmill Rd S1 4SE
Details SYMAAG unites refugee, asylum and migrant organisations with campaign groups and supporting individuals. It campaigns both for the rights of those seeking asylum and of those coming to work in this country. If you want to come to any SYMAAG meeting but find it hard to afford transport do not be put off, bring your ticket or a receipt and SYMAAG can pay you back at the meeting. Or check in advance.
