8th March 2020

SheFest

5 - 15 Mar 2020, various venues

SheFest is back to Celebrate International Women’s Day across South Yorkshire, 10 jam packed days full of empowering and inclusive events across Sheffield, Rotherham, Barnsley and Doncaster.

Including, theatre, art, comedy and celebrity guest speakers.

The programme includes partnerships with Museums Sheffield, Theatre Deli, The Leadmill, BBC Radio Sheffield, Showroom Cinema, The Tuesday Club, She Productions and so many more. Tickets for all events are on sale now via Tickets for Good. Festival brochure available in print and online (link below).

Women’s Free Taster Sessions in DIY

Sun 8 Mar 2020, 10am-4pm, Endeavour Centre, Earl Marshal Rd. S4 8FB

For International Women’s Day, a celebration event by Women in Construction Arts and Technology - WiCAT - offering FREE DIY taster sessions to women and girls for the day!

You will have a chance to take part in a range of different activities, trying your hand at Joinery; Tiling; Electrics etc. Using power drills and hand tools, picking up skills in Painting & Decorating and lots more.

There will be a vegetarian lunch provided on the day and it will be a great opportunity to get to see some of the things WiCAT can offer, and meet some of their highly skilled tutors and volunteers. If you would like to take part please let WiCAT know by registering through their website below.
