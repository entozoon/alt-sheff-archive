Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity Campaign Open Forum - October session

Date, Time & Location Wed 31 Oct 2012, 7pm-9pm, Showroom Cinema cafe, Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details The final of four monthly sessions in 2012 looking at different areas of the work of Sheffield PSC. The agenda will include the history of Palestine, the BDS campaign (Boycott, Divestment and Sanctions), women and Palestine, feedback & panel session for questions on the current situation, and an introduction to the work of SPSC, which includes a women’s scholarship fund, and children’s projects. Followed by an optional extra hour for coffee/drinks from the bar and informal discussion till 10pm. Please email openforum@sheffieldpsc.org.uk to register your interest.
