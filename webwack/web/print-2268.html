GAZA SLAUGHTER: DEMONSTRATIONS IN SHEFFIELD AND LONDON

Date, Time & Location Sat 9 Aug 2014
Details At the time of writing, the numbers slaughtered in Gaza by Israel are close to 1900, 80% of them civilians, including over 300 children. Over 9000 Palestinians have been injured. Thousands of buildings have been turned to rubble, including hospitals, schools, power stations and UN centres sheltering refugees. If you believe the BBC and other 'mainstream' media you will not know how massive is the international wave of revulsion and protest.
