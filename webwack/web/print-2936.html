Jumble/Nearly New Sale Fundraiser

Date, Time & Location Sat 17 Aug 2013, 12noon-2pm, Heeley City Farm, Richards Road, S2 3DT
Details The next Heeley City Farm fund raising event – please come along and help to support the farm and raise money for the animals and other projects. Ladies’, men’s and children’s clothes for sale as well as toys, books and bric a brac. The vast majority will be jumble but a couple of rails of nearly new. It is a clear so things will be from 10/20p. Come and bag a bargain! Donations of good quality clothes and bric a brac welcome and appreciated. Ring the farm on Sheff 2580482 or email s.hardy@heeleyfarm.org.uk for more details or to arrange to drop off any donations. Sale held in the Reception Classroom near the Duck Pond. 50p entrance fee.
