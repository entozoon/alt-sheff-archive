Sheffield 'Yes' Campaign for Fairer Votes

Date, Time & Location Wed 4 May 2011, Sheffield Hallam University 12 noon - 2pm
Details This is the day before the referendum on the Alternative Vote, so Sheffield 'Yes' Campaign for Fairer Votes want to make a big splash as they remind people to vote, and why the Alternative vote, whilst a modest change, has significant advantages over our worn-out first-past-the-post system Come and join in! In front of the University main entrance, off Arundel Gate, opposite Millennium Gallery www.fairervotessheffield.org.uk
