'Question Time' style debate

Date, Time & Location Mon 15 Mar 2010, 6.30-8pm, Richard Roberts Lecture Theatre, University of Sheffield
Details Debate in the style of the BBC Question Time programme. Panellists will address a range of questions related to issues such as youth radicalisation, terrorism and civil liberties, belief and politics, surveillance and privacy, as well as broader issues relating to the state of politics in the UK.
