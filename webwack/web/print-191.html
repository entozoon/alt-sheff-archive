Talk: Dr. Mona El-Farra - the situation in Gaza

Date, Time & Location Thu 18 Jul 2019, 7.30pm, Quaker Meeting House, James St. S1 2EW
Details Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign welcomes Dr. Mona El-Farra to Sheffield. She will be updating everyone on the current situation in Gaza, the meeting is open to all. Dr. Mona El-Farra is a grassroots activist and the driving force in numerous community projects in Gaza. She is a founder member of the Union of Health Workers Committee, director of Middle East Children's Alliance and a former vice-chair of Gaza Red Crescent. Mona will discuss the initiatives being taken in the face of the Gaza humanitarian crisis. Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign
