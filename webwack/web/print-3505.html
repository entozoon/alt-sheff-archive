Refugee Asylum Seeker Action Group IT club

Date, Time & Location Mon 8 Oct 2012, 11am-3pm, Old Junior School, South View Rd S7 1DB
Details Every Monday, Sheffield's Refugee Asylum Seeker Action Group (RASAG) runs this IT Club providing a friendly, warm environment with high speed internet access and ESOL, literacy and IT support. They always need help with paired reading and IT support. Donations are also welcome, so feel free to bring along a packet of biscuits or other items that may be of use to members. RASAG was formed in April 2010. Apart from one development worker the group is made up of refugees and asylum seekers. For more info please email katelyn_mckeown@yahoo.co.uk http://sheffieldrasag.wordpress.com
