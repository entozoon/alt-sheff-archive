Sheffield Racial Equality Council (SREC) Public Meeting

Date, Time & Location Thu 22 Jul 2010, 6pm.
Details Meeting discussing recent developments and consulting on plans for the future, with particular emphasis on how to survive the public sector cuts and funding withdrawal. Sheffield Racial Equality Council is the only organisation in Sheffield with such a longstanding track record for providing an excellent quality anti-racial discrimination casework service, information, legal advice and representation to victims of racial discrimination, harassment and victimisation. SREC also leads in building good race relations between people from different cultural backgrounds and working closely with others to create successful community cohesion and safety. Guest speakers and a panel for questions. All welcome to attend, refreshments available. Venue: Ellesmere Suite, 2nd Floor Sorby House, Spital Hill, Burngreave S4 7LG (Loads of buses 75/76, 83, 88, 97, 33, 45/46, 20) Email howa.srec@hotmail.co.uk Website: www.sheffieldrec.co.uk
