Langdon Street Summer BBQ and Games

Date, Time & Location Sun 8 Jun 2014, 3-6pm, Langdon Street garden
Details Bring your community spirit and food/blankets to share if you live around Langdon Street neighbourhood. Come rain or shine, they will be having fun and games, and planting and gardening. Halal meat and vegetarian food barbeque. Organised by Langdon Street residents, with support from Community Games.
