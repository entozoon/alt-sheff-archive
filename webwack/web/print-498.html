Steel City: An Industrial History

Date, Time & Location Tue 9 Oct 2018 7pm, Cadman Room, Millennium Gallery, Arundel Gate, S1 2PP
Details Environmentalist, historian and author Professor Ian Rotherham of Sheffield Hallam University talks about Sheffield and the manufacturing history of one of Britain’s great industrial centres. In collaboration with Blackwell’s Bookshop at University of Sheffield, part of the 2018 Off the Shelf festival of words.
