Falafel and Film night: Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity

Date, Time & Location Fri 19 Apr 2013, 7pm
Details An evening with Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity Campaign that promises to keep up the tradition of good food, good company and a good cause. A short film on what has happened to Shuhada Street, Hebron. This major West Bank city was invaded by a small number of Zionist fanatics, come and see the results of that invasion. £8/£5 entry, food served until 8pm. Children also welcome. Venue is not 100% confirmed - SPSC website shows Friends Meeing House AND St. Matthews Rooms, Alt-Sheff will list the correct one as soon as this is confirmed.
