Open Generation: Our Say On Migration

Date, Time & Location Thu 3 Apr 2014, 6.30pm-9pm, SU Auditorium, Western Bank, Sheffield S10 2TN
Details An evening of debate on immigration ahead of the EU elections this May, run by University of Sheffield. Joined with London and other locations via a live stream to discuss the challenges and rewards of immigration and the EU. You will also be able to tweet comments and ask the panel your own questions. Speakers will include: Owen Jones - Columnist and author Dom Anderson - NUS Kiri Kankhwende - Media Diversified Marc Kidson - British Youth Council Eliza Galos - Researcher on East European communities Dan Goodwin - UNISON Simon Mole and Deanna Rodger - Spoken word artists Additionally, the event will feature some short films on this topic and a discussion on the WeAreInternational campaign. More information at: University of Sheffield
