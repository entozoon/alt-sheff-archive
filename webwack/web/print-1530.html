Critical Mass Sheffield

Date, Time & Location Fri 25 Dec 2015, meet 6pm outside the Town Hall
Details An international monthly cycle ride in celebration of the bicycle as the most perfect mode of transport, bringing peace and joy to all the world. Critical mass is a self-organised movement, on the last Friday of each month, around the world. In other words it happens with whoever turns up, but wrap up in warm clothes!
