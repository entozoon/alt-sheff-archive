Co-Operation for Positive Social Change in Sheffield

Date, Time & Location Sat 9 Nov 2013, 12-4pm Regather Co-Operative, 57-59 Club Garden Rd. S11 8BU
Details A one day exhibition celebrating co-operation for positive social change in Sheffield. The event will feature guided tours, presentations, workshops, food, drink and entertainment - provided by Sheffield based co-operatives and located at the Regather Works - a community centre for co-operation in Sharrow, Sheffield. Free registration at: http://cooperateforchange.eventbrite.co.uk Regather
