ROTHERHAM PRIDE

Date, Time & Location Sat 12 Jul 2014, 11am-6pm, Clifton Park, Rotherham S65 2AA
Details Rotherham Pride returns to Clifton Park, close to the centre of Rotherham, to promote and celebrate the LGB & T community that lives in or visits Rotherham. Live local bands in the bandstand area, refreshment areas, children's entertainment, info and market stalls. Live music also on the day in the William Fry pub. Rotherham Pride
