Get Growing!

Date, Time & Location Sat 8 Mar 2014, 10-3.30pm, Meersbrook Park Community Walled Garden
Details A one day course – Making the most of your allotment. For beginners or those with limited gardening experience – includes advice about designing growing spaces, clearing ground, good soil structure, composting, how to control pests and diseases and much more! £25 per person. Concessions available. Please book in advance. Contact Darrell Maryon for more info: 07768 932613 email: darrell@heeleyfarm.org.uk Held at Meersbrook Park Community Walled Garden site, owned by Heeley City Farm. Entrance: Brook Road.
