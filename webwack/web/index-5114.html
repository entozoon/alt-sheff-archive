People's Conference for Climate Justice

Sat 24 Apr 2010, 10am–4pm St Mary's Community Centre, Bramall Lane S2 4QZ

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

After Copenhagen, what next? The countries of the global South, least responsible for climate change, and hardest hit, are taking the lead. President of Bolivia, Evo Morales, has called a "Peoples' Conference on Climate Change and Mother Earth Rights" in Cochabamba, Bolivia, 19-22 Apr. SCACC is holding this parallel event in Sheffield to link climate justice, financial crisis, learning from the global South, green jobs, refugees, and our relationship with the Earth. Workshop/sessions include:

  • Climate Crisis, System Crisis
  • Green Jobs and Trade Union Perspectives
  • Rights for the Planet in International Law
  • Women & Climate Change
  • Prosperity Without Growth
  • Non Violent Direct Action
  • PLUS A Live link to Bolivia to hear about the World Conference and what it achieved
  • FILMS from the global struggle for Climate Justice
  • Stalls, and St Mary's Wholefood Cafe
Celebrate and share current local stuff, activities for all ages at a very creative event involving the broadest range of people and perspectives. Can you help to make this a vibrant, inclusive, creative and skill-sharing day of action? Please email: email ajmiller to register and/or with offers of workshops, displays, stalls, creative input and involvement of children and young people.Admission - free for young people; £1 for student/unemployed; £5 low wage; £10 high wageSCACC website
