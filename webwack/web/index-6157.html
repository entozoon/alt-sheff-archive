Afghanistan: The war is over ....

July 2009

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

There is now a full scale crisis over the occupation in Afghanistan. The death toll mounts, there's no strategy or sensible explanation for why the troops are there. Despite the media spin, most people here and in Afghanistan are opposed.

Stop the War is collecting signatures towards a mega-petition to Gordon Brown. The petition can be signed online and downloaded for printing on Stop the War website.

Also see leaflet "10 reasons to get the troops out of Afghanistan" and watch the powerful Rethink Afghanistan video
