Free Film "Perspectives on Poverty"

Date, Time & Location Thu 10 Jan 2013, 6pm & 8pm, Lantern Theatre, Kenwood Park Rd S7 1NF
Details Kat Budd, a young Woodcraft Folk volunteer, spent some months last year working in Senegal as part of an IFM (International Falcon Movement) project. She and people from sister organisations have produced a film about their experiences and this is its UK launch with two showings and discussion slots at 6:00 and 8:00p.m. Free entry, but its helpful if you let them know you are going, email katherine.budd (at) gmail.com
