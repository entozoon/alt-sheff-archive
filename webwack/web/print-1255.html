"Willing the Possible" and "Necessary Frames"

Date, Time & Location Sat 11 Jun 2016, 6pm, Bloc Projects, 71 Eyre Lane, S1 4RB
Details Films on conflict from present day Palestine to historic Scotland, allowing the possibility of collective narrative plotting as an open-ended process – a way of thinking through conflicting positions. Followed at 8pm by Necessary Frames Directed exclusively by women, the films in this programme adopt different strategies towards issues surrounding the use value of documentary film. Part of Sheffield Fringe 2016: Object Documentary. The biennial Sheffield Fringe 2016 is held at Bloc Projects, in Sheffield’s Cultural Industries Quarter, coinciding with Sheffield International Documentary Film Festival (Doc/Fest). A nine-day programme of film screenings, talks and performances. Many of the short documentaries and artists’ films have very interesting politically and socially engaged content. All events are free and booking is not required. Bloc Projects
