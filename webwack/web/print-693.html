SYMAAG Open Planning Meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 19 Sep 2017, 6.30pm, Welcome Centre, Chapel Walk S1 2PD
Details South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG) Open Planning Meeting on what should be the campaigning priorities for 2017-18 and reviewing the goals set last year (see Annual Report 2016-2017). (Note this meeting is not 26th as arranged earlier.) All welcome, other refugee-rights, anti-racist, community and campaigning groups invited. SYMAAG unites refugee, asylum and migrant organisations with campaign groups and supporting individuals. It campaigns both for the rights of those seeking asylum and of those coming to work in this country. See SYMAAG events page for details of forthcoming events www.symaag.org.uk
