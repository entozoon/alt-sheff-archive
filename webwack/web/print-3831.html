How to be a Troublemaker: A workers guide to fighting back

Date, Time & Location Thu 5 Apr 2012, 7.30pm, Palin Room, University Arms, 197 Brook Hill S3 7HG
Details Unhappy with the way the world is run? The best way to fight back is to be a troublemaker at work. Work is where exploitation happens where profit is made and where capitalism works. Troublemakers in history have built the first industrial unions, organised strikes, worked to rule, controlled their bosses and won in many different ways. An Alliance for Workers Liberty public meeting. For more info contact: sheffield@workersliberty.org
