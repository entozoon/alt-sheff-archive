Freedom Ride Campaign picnic and lobby of councillors

Date, Time & Location Wed 3 Sept 2014, 12 noon, Peace Gardens, Sheffield city centre
Details The Freedom Ride Campaign is still going strong 6 months on after forcing a u-turn and concessions from the transport authority and Northern Rail! Bring your own food and join the public transport campaigners for a picnic in the Peace Gardens at 12 noon, and then lobby of Sheffield City Council meeting at 1.30pm.
