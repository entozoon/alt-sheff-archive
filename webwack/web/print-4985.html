CND meeting (Campaign for Nuclear Disarmament)

Date, Time & Location Wed 16 Jun 2010, 7.15pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW (near Anglican cathedral)
Details All are welcome to this monthly meeting at which the new coalition government's response to the issue of Trident nuclear weapons replacement and to the commissioning of new nuclear power plants - and CND response to that - will be the main topics of discussion. Sheffield CND meets on the third Wednesday of each month, except August. Sheffield CND website
