Bring Your Own Band (BYOB), Vegan BBQ and Clothes Swap

Date, Time & Location Sat 11 Aug 2012, Red House, 168 Solly St. S1 4BB
Details Bedroom singers and established performers are invited to come together and play some covers by women artists in a non-judgmental and dance-friendly environment. Vegan barbecue and clothes swap from 6pm, you can bring clothes you are ready to pass on, and find something new you fancy. Music starts at 8pm, DJ Sam and Mo til late. Sheffield is a DIY, anti-capitalist, community based, feminist group promoting social change in a non-discriminatory environment. It is led primarily by women but all are welcome to get involved. £3 Suggested donation, this is a fund-raiser for Survivors of Depression in Transit (SODIT) and the Young Women’s Housing Project (YWHP). http://ladiyfestsheffield.wordpress.com
