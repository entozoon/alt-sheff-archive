SHARROW FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sat 6 Jul 2019, 12pm-5pm, Mount Pleasant Park, Sharrow Lane, off Abbeydale Rd S11 8AE
Details Join in as the people of Sharrow come together for this great annual multicultural community festival, with a focus on creativity and participation. Community Stage, drumming and street dance workshops, sports, global foods to try, a huge variety of stalls and activities for kids. Sharrow Festival on Facebook
