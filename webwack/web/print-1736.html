Camelot: The Shining City

Date, Time & Location Thu 9-Sat 18 Jul 2015, 7.30pm
Details It is said that King Arthur will return when the nation most needs a leader. That time has come. It’s time for the Revolution. Join theatrical innovators Slung Low and Sheffield People’s Theatre for a unique, unforgettable adventure as they reinvent one of the greatest legends of all time. Beginning in the Crucible Theatre, before venturing out to Tudor Square and on to the Peace Gardens, Camelot: The Shining City is an epic production, perfect for anyone with an adventurous spirit. Sheffield People’s Theatre is the intergenerational theatre company of Sheffield Theatres. By engaging with local people aged 12 plus within the community who have a passion for creating theatre they aim to stage exciting, bold and challenging work. Dress for the weather! Age guidance 12+.
