Fundraising event: Sheffield Live! buffet

Date, Time & Location Thur 4 Mar 2010, 7pm at The Wick at Both Ends, 149-151 West St. Sheffield S1 4EW
Details Radio folk put on aprons and take to the stove. Harry’s prawn curry, Irini’s spinach pie and Dale’s burritos meet Simon’s lentil stew, Paul’s chili and Jill’s wakes cakes on a night set up to share our favourite dishes with you! Join Sheffield Live! to meet the faces behind the voices and find out what they've been cooking. On the decks will be
  • Graham Marshall (Spirit of the Wapentake)
  • Chris Welch (Sounds Like this)
  • Kevin Resley (Walk a mile in my shoes)
  • S. Lee Price (Vancouver Manouever)
Tickets £10 from the venue. All proceeds go to Sheffield Live! 93.2 FM.
