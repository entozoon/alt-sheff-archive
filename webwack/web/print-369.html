Global Justice Sheffield meeting

Date, Time & Location Thu 10 Jan 2018, 7-9pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St. James St. S1 2EW.
Details Global Justice is an anti-poverty campaigning organisation. Their welcoming meetings are held every month. Not just talk, but action and projects. A chance to debate and understand issues and generate creative ideas about how to change things. The January meeting of Global Justice Sheffield moves to the 2nd Thursday, after the excitement of New Year, note the venue is also different; the Friends Meeting House. The group will be discussing and planning campaigns and activities for 2019. Global Justice Sheffield
