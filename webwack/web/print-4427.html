Who runs the internet? Wikileaks, piracy and censorship

Date, Time & Location Sat 12 Mar 2011, 8pm-9pm Meeting Room 6, Mercure St Paul's Hotel, 119 Norfolk Street S1 2JE
Details Libel law reform campaigner and former MP Evan Harris, website pioneer Mary Reid, James Blessing of the Internet Service Providers' Association (ISPA) and Jim Killock of the digital rights champions Open Rights Group debate recent issues about free speech and the internet with chair Mark Pack.
