Putting Humanism Into Practice

Date, Time & Location Tue 3 Mar 2015, 7.30pm, Farm Road Sports and Social Club, S2 2TP
Details What does it mean to be a Humanist? How can the principles of Humanism be applied? In this talk Chris Worfolk, co-founder of the Humanist Action Group (and Treasurer of the West Yorkshire Humanists), will speak about why Humanists should get involved in volunteering and his experiences doing homeless outreach work. A talk open to the public, organised by the Sheffield Humanist Society, all welcome. Sheffield Humanists Facebook page
