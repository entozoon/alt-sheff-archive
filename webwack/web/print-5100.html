'Keeping the Lights on in Sheffield - is our future nuclear free or nuclear?'

Date, Time & Location Wed 28 April 2010, 7pm-8.30pm Sheffield Hallam University, Furnival Building room 9130
Details Hear the facts, figures and arguments on nuclear energy presented by 6 speakers covering the case for and against, then add your own questions to the debate. Introduced by Dr Malcolm Denman, SHU, and moderated by David Garlovsky of Schools and Homes Energy Education Project. This Sheffield-based registered charity and not-for-profit company assists schools, professionals and the general public to make informed decisions about energy use and resources at home, work and play.
