Cycle Sheffield meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 15 Apr 2013, 7.30-9pm Broomhall Centre, Broomspring Lane S10 2FD
Details The monthly meeting of Cycle Sheffield, usually followed by pub social. This group is for all kinds of cyclists; it organises events, information and rides, and campaigns for cycling to be taken seriously as a means of transport, with the facilities it needs. www.cyclesheffield.org.uk
