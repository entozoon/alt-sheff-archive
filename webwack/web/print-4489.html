'Women Not Sex Objects' campaign launch

Date, Time & Location Sat 12 Feb 2011, 1.45-4.30pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Sheffield Feminist Women's Group presentation and workshop to start a local campaign for a community free of 'sex object' culture, and the harmful attitudes this engenders. All self-identified women are welcome to this event. www.object.org.uk
