Day workshop: Introduction to Woodcarving

Date, Time & Location Sun 17 Aug 2014, 9.30-4pm, Woodland Discovery Centre, Ecclesall Woods, Abbey Lane S7 2QZ
Details Learn the fundamentals of fine woodcarving techniques, how to design, mark out and carve incised and relief patterns, including how to select, sharpen and use a range of tools. Run by Low Impact Living Initiative (LILI) which offers publications, free factsheets, a huge database and forum, products, services and courses in everything from co-operatives to wildfood camping. In Sheffield, courses are often held at the Woodland Discovery Centre, an educational facility in the largest ancient woodland in South Yorkshire. Full course details are on the LILI website. Although not free, they are run by experts, giving in-depth tuition in less common skills. Low-impact Living Initiative (LILI) Ecclesall Woods Craft Courses
