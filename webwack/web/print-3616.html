Sheffield Pride AGM

Date, Time & Location Mon 30 Jul 2012, 6.15pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Annual general meeting of Shefield's LGBT festival organisation. It will include feedback on the year's activities and election to the various roles. Then, as a big step this year, following the AGM Sheffield Pride will be incorporating both as a limited company and a registered charity, so these roles will carry more legal and moral responsibilities.
