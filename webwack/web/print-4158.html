Sheffield Friday Night Ride

Date, Time & Location Fri 16 Sep 2011, meet 6.30pm at Barkers Pool, city centre S1 2HB
Details Sheffield Friday Night Ride (FNR) offers a fun monthly evening bike ride for everyone. Rides are prepared in advance on themes - this time it is "Pulchritudinous Pitsmoor"! Their motto is: We have nothing to lose but our chains! (But bring locks and lights too, just in case). FridayNightRide website
