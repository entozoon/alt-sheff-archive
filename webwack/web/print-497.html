"Rise" – Gina Miller

Date, Time & Location Tue 9 Oct 2018, 7pm, Pennine Theatre, Owen Building, Sheffield Hallam University, Howard St. S1 1WB
Details A talk by Gina Miller who speaks out on injustice in personal, political and professional arenas. She was targeted with extreme verbal abuse, sexism and racism after challenging in the courts the government’s right to implement Brexit without approval from parliament, and was named as the country’s most influential black person. Part of the 2018 Off the Shelf festival of words. Tickets on the door £9/£8 concessions, or in advance £8/£7 concessions, at the link below. Off the Shelf
