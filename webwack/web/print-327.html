Cube of Truth

Date, Time & Location Sat 9 Mar 2019, 8am-8pm, Fargate, Sheffield centre
Details Anonymous for the Voiceless is an activist organisation for animal rights, health, veganism and the environment, now spread to in nearly 1,000 cities across the world. The Sheffield group offer this 12-hour public "Cube of Truth". It is a pioneering method of raising public curiosity, discussion and awareness. Come and see, and have a word if you can.
