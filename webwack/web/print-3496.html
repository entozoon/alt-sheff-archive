Sheffield Friday Night Ride visits street art by Phlegm

Date, Time & Location Fri 12 Oct 2012, Start 6.30pm
Details Sheffield Friday Night Ride (FNR) offers a fun monthly evening bike ride for everyone. Rides are prepared in advance on themes, and this month it is the work of the Sheffield based, street artist called Phlegm - for examples see www.phlegmcomicnews.blogspot.com. FridayNightRide website
