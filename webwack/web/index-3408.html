Discussion: involvement of UN peacekeepers in the sex trade

Thu 15 Nov 2012, 7.15 pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St. James St. S1 2EW

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

A follow-up discussion about the issues raised in the film "The Whistleblower" shown last month, exposing the involvement of UN peacekeepers in the sex trade during the Bosnian War in the 1990s. Even after the release of the information about the rape, torture and exploitation of the women caught up in the sex trade, Amnesty International produced a report, "Hopes Betrayed", pointing to corruption as the basis of many of the problems....Organised by The Women's International League For Peace & Freedom (Sheffield) and Amnesty International UK (Sheffield) For more information tel. Sheff. 2353127
