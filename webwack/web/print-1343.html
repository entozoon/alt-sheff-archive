How to Feed the World!

Date, Time & Location Sat 16 Apr 2016, 11am-4pm, top of Fargate, city centre
Details A celebration of local food-growing worldwide. Held in solidarity with the International Day of Peasant Struggle, working for small-scale farmers to have control of their own land and water, and for everyone to have affordable healthy food. Join them in demanding an end to global business attempts to impose land-grabs and unaffordable patented seeds. Jointly organised by Global Justice Sheffield, Friends of the Earth, Grow Sheffield, Regather, Sheffield Climate Alliance, War on Want, Village Aid and The Food Assembly.
