BRUCE KENT VISITS SHEFFIELD

Date, Time & Location Tue 29 Apr 2014, 6.30 for 7pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Elder statesman Bruce Kent is to visit Sheffield as part of his Anti Trident Faith Tour. Long-time political activist and former Roman Catholic priest, he has been active in the Campaign for Nuclear Disarmament (CND) for over three decades. He now holds the honorary title of vice-president. Sheffield Campaign for Nuclear Disarmament has an active campaigning presence in the city, concentrating on the militarization of university campuses, Scrap Trident Campaign and local connections with the US Missile Defence System and drones.
