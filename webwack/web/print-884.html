Space for Cycling: The Big Ride

Date, Time & Location Sat 22 Apr 2017, 11am, Devonshire Green S1
Details Join this big family-friendly bike ride to say roads and streets in Sheffield need to be made people-friendly and include safe space for cycling. Gather from 10am, the ride will begin at 11am. The route this year will be a fun, family-friendly route of approximately 5km. Come and make some noise, dress in Space-for-Cycling red (or anything you like!) and have a great time. Facebook event page
