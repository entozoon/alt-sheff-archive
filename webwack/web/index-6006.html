IMPORTANT: Message to all Community Organisations and Volunteers

Through till Mon 6 Dec 2010

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Council message to all Community Organisations - your help is needed in order to ensure that vulnerable adults do not suffer during the current weather conditions, a Local Area Crisis Team has been set up in the Town Hall which will run until the end of Monday. This team will prioritise and coordinate the provision of services across the city. Provision of sufficient 4x4s has been arranged so transport across the city will not be an issue.

Do you know of any vulnerable people who may require help. If you do please could you contact the Local Area Crisis Team (LACT) on the following telephone numbers -

Where a person needs assistance over the next few days (including the weekend) immediately contact the LACT on tel - Sheff 2735822 or fax details Sheff 2735583.

If a person may need assistance after the weekend if the adverse weather continues then please highlight this when you contact the LACT.

In addition, we are trying to identify people who can volunteer to help out in their local area - please contact annemarie.johnston@sheffield.gov.uk
