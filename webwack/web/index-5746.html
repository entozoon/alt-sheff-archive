Wanted: Future film-makers to tell trans stories

Starts 7 September 2016

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

A new free film course starts soon, featuring a masterclass by Genderation star Fox Fisher. Are you Transgender, Queer, Lesbian, Gay or Bisexual? Ever dreamt of making your own short film? Want to challenge the lack of transgender representation in mainstream media? Now you can, E.D.E.N Film Production is launching a new, FREE, filmmaking course for the LGBTQ+ community. Called E.D.E.N SHORTS, the project will teach you how to make short films that tell transgender stories. It will teach you all the skills you need: camera, sound, light, equipment, storytelling, interviewing, editing, publishing, and will provide one-to-one support to help develop your skills.

Four films produced with E.D.E.N. SHORTS will be screened at Transforming Cinema Film Festival 19-20 November 2016.

E.D.E.N SHORTS launches 7 September and places are limited. Email info@edenfilm.co.uk or phone 07725 911736 to book your place.
