Temporary Autonomous Arts (TAA) event

Date, Time & Location Thu 19 to Sat 21 May 2011, Unit 3, Bowdon Street, S1 4JP
Details Sheffield's Art and Soul Kollective is is now back in action to create out of nothing another 'Temporary Autonomous Arts' event. Reclaiming and reusing derelict urban spaces Art, they believe in building fleeting pirate utopia's, using DIY culture tactics to create space for art and expression outside of the establish artworld elites. Music, film, performance, kids area and workshops. All this and loads more, full list of activities is on the website. Bowdon Street is near the Corporation nightclub.
