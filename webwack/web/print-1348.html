Media Coverage and Women Athletes: "Derby Crazy Love"

Date, Time & Location Thu 14 Apr 2016, 7.30pm, Film Unit, University of Sheffield Students Union, Western Bank S10 2TG
Details A film screening which peeks inside the adrenaline-filled world of roller derby. Learn how these skaters are changing how we look at women in sport and femininity, one block at a time. The screening will be followed by a panel discussion led by members of the local sports community. Part of a series of events exploring gender politics in the modern sports world. Activities include films, panel debates and workshops. Presented by Sheffield Steel Rollergirls in association with Festival of Debate, hosted by Opus Independents (which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine).
