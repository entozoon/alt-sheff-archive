Alternatives to Austerity

Date, Time & Location Mon 26 Oct 2015, 7.30-9pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Public Talk and Discussion, hosted by Positive Money (Sheffield) Speaker : Adrian Cruden – former Green Party campaigns manager for Yorkshire and Humber region, and Parliamentary candidate for Dewsbury. Austerity is a political choice not an economic necessity, so what are the alternatives? For decades, western governments have worked to the ideal of Margaret Thatcher's "housewife's shopping basket" analogy. That might make sense for an individual or a household, but maybe the opposite is true for a national or international economy. * how do we make fair and sustainable use of our world's resources? * what should be the role of politics in the economy? * and what alternatives are there that can deliver sustained prosperity for all without busting the planet or destroying our children's future?
