Poverty in Sheffield Today - Finding a way forward

Date, Time & Location Sat 29 Nov 2014, 10am–12.30pm Victoria Hall Methodist Church, 60 Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details This meeting will include presentations on LISTEN UP interviews with people in poverty, impact of benefit sanctions, food banks, and the Sheffield Fairness Commission. There will be opportunity to contribute for those attending, as well as a range of stalls. Organised by the People’s Assembly for Sheffield, Church Action on Poverty in Sheffield, and Sheffield Equality Group. Doors open 9.30am, free. All welcome. Facebook event page
