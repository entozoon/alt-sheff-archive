Are we going to be fracked? – Dronfield

Date, Time & Location Thu 8 Dec 2016, 7.30pm Dronfield Civic Hall, Dronfield Civic Centre S18 1PD
Details In nearby Dronfield, seismic surveying is due to begin, a sign that fracking is planned. This free presentation by Frack Free South Yorkshire explains fracking - what it is, what the risks are and why they oppose it. Followed by question and answer session.
