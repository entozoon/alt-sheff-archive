Stop Bombing Syria public meeting

Date, Time & Location Thu 1 Dec 2016, 7pm, Owen Building, Sheffield Hallam University, Howard St. S1 1WB
Details The War in the Middle East - Why more bombing won't help Syria. A public meeting hosted by Sheffield Stop the War Coalition, chaired by Lucinda Wakefield (Sheffield StWC). Come along to discuss the ever-growing crisis in the Middle East, and why it is vital to oppose intervention, the increased danger of escalation with the election of President Trump, and the anti-war movement in the UK and Sheffield. Speakers: Paul Rogers; Professor of Peace Studies, Bradford University Piers Robinson; Professor of Politics & Journalism, University of Sheffield Andrew Murray; National Officer, Stop the War Coalition Maz Saleem; Anti-war & anti-racism campaigner Stop the War
