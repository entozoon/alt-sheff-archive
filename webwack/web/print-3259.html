Peace in the Park open meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 27 Feb 2013, 6pm-8pm, Regather, 57-59 Club Garden Rd. S11 8BU (near London Rd)
Details The volunteers are again organising Sheffield’s annual free festival for June 2013. Profits go to good causes and it’s a fantastic event. Don’t be shy if you want to get involved or have any ideas, come along to one of the open meetings, last Wednesday of each month, these are open to everybody. (They also sensibly have closed meetings for those already involved in planning, to reduce time spent on introductions and increase time spent on getting things done). Peace in the Park website
