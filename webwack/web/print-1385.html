Banking on Land Vs. Prosperity

Date, Time & Location Mon 21 Mar 2016, 7.30pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Our prosperity problems are systemic - there is a remedy! Through film animations from ´The Taxing Question of Land´ and a new game called ´A Tale of 2 Cities´, this workshop will give you a helpful grounding in all things Land Value Tax and explore how this remedy helps to create a helpful banking system with positive prosperity cycles. Presented by Yoni Higgsmith, A Land Value Tax activist and filmmaker, and active officer for the Labour Land Campaign and Professional Land Reform Group. Positive Money Sheffield in association with Festival of Debate 2016, hosted by Opus Independents which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine.
