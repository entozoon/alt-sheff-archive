World Development Movement (WDM) meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 14 Nov 2011, 8-10pm, Red Deer pub, 18 Pitt St. S1 4DD
Details The World Development Movement (WDM) is a UK-based anti-poverty campaigning organisation. Their welcoming pub meetings are held on the 2nd Monday of every month, upstairs at the Red Deer pub, (just off West St. It's not just talk, but action and projects. A chance to debate and understand issues and generate creative ideas about how to change things. WDM Sheffield website
