Could you ASSIST with a tea party?

Preparations autumn 2013, various venues

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Sheffield charity ASSIST is ten years old this year. That means ten years of helping asylum seekers who have no place to stay, no right to work and no other form of support. ASSIST provides accommodation, food and small grants to those in most need.

To mark its birthday, ASSIST is holding a Big Tea Party in October. ASSIST volunteers and supporters are invited to join in by hosting a Tea Party at work, at home or in a community venue, on a day of their choice. Each Tea Party host will be provided with a pack of invitations, posters and information about ASSIST, and there will be an opportunity to share ideas, recipes and tips to help the Big Tea Party go with a swing.

The Big Tea Party will be launched on Saturday 5 October, with a special birthday Tea Party for ASSIST clients, volunteers and supporters.

Anyone wishing to host a Tea Party for ASSIST can sign up now via this website: www.assistsheffield.org.uk
