Workshop: "Your Parliament: An introduction"

Date, Time & Location Thu 5 May 2016, 6-7pm Boardroom, Hallam HUBS building, Paternoster Row S1 2QQ
Details Increase your knowledge of what Parliament is and does, and learn about the differences between Parliament and Government. Get an overview of a variety of the parliamentary processes that the public can engage with, and find out how to access further information. In association with Houses of Parliament Outreach Service, part of Festival of Debate 2016, hosted by Opus Independents (which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine). Free to attend, but please register so they can get an idea of numbers. Festival of Debate - Twitter: @FestOfDebate - #FofD - Facebook
