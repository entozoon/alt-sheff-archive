Migration Matters Festival Launch Party!

Date, Time & Location Fri 17 Jun 2016, 5.30pm til late, Theatre Delicatessen, 17 The Moor (old Woolworths shop) S1 3LR
Details Part of Refugee Week, celebrating sanctuary in the steel city. Sheffield was the UK’s first City of Sanctuary. The Migration Matters Festival continues through to 25th June. A rich and soulful programme that brings the diverse and global mix of Sheffield’s communities together with artists from all over the country in a celebration of food, culture and performance. Featuring established companies, emerging artists, community arts groups and charitable organisations. Side by Side Theatre, Umoja African Dance, live music and One Way Belonging. With food from the Open Kitchen Social Club. A partnership between Theatre Delicatessen and University of Sheffield supported by Arts Council England. All events are "Pay What You Decide".
