RASAG IT club

Date, Time & Location Mon 28 May 2012, 11am-3pm, Old Junior School, South View Rd S7 1DB
Details Sheffield Refugee Asylum Seeker Action Group (RASAG) runs this IT Club providing a friendly, warm environment with high speed internet access and ESOL, literacy and IT support every Monday. They welome help with paired reading and IT support. RASAG was formed in April 2010. Apart from one development worker the group is made up of refugees and asylum seekers.
