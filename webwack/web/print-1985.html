Green Party Leader Natalie Bennett speaks in Sheffield

Date, Time & Location Tues 17 Feb 2015, 7-9pm St Georges Church, St Georges Terrace S1 4DP
Details Natalie Bennett, leader of the Green Party of England and Wales, speaking in Sheffield. She is joined by Jillian Creasy, highly respected local Councillor, GP and national Green Party spokesperson for Health, who is running for Parliament in the May General Election. Fresh from winning the challenge to be included in the national leaders debates and with Green Party membership surging past UKIP and the Liberal Democrats, towards 60,000 in the UK, Natalie Bennett will be making a major speech in Sheffield. A Green Party event, open to all. Sheffield and Rotherham Green Party
