LOWEDGES FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sun 14 Aug 2016, 12-5pm, Greenhill Park, Gresley Rd, Lowedges S8 7HN
Details The popular Lowedges Festival in Greenhill Park is an annual community event. Music, dance, stalls and food, dog show, dog agility trials, medieval re-enactment show, living history, funfair, farmers market, carboot, crafts, mini steam, car boot and much more. A great free show. Lowedges & Greenhill community on Facebook
