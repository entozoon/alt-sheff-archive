"In Maggie’s Shadow: Thatcher’s Enduring Legacy"

Date, Time & Location Tue 6 Nov 2018, 6pm–7pm, The Council Room, Firth Court, University of Sheffield S10 2TN
Details To what extent has Margaret Thatcher shaped our society? Based on ten years’ research into the long term impact of Thatcherism, this talk by Professor Stephen Farrall focuses on the issues of crime, housing and education. Part of Understanding Society, the festival of social sciences, organised by the universities (Sheffield and Hallam), funded by the Economic and Social Research Council. Free to attend, pre-booking a place is recommended. Understanding Society
