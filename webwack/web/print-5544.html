"King Coal" film series for the 25th anniversary of the 1984-85 UK miners' strike

Date, Time & Location Through Sept until 15 Oct 2009, at Showroom Cinema, Paternoster Row, Sheffield S1 2BX
Details The Showroom Cinema is showing a series of films about the experience of being a coal miner, life in mining communities and the miners' strike at the time of the disastrous Thatcher government, events which made a big impact on local mining communities.
