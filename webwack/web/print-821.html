A Hidden History of the Cuban Revolution

Date, Time & Location Mon 5 Jun 2017, 7.30pm, Red Deer, Pitt St. S1
Details The Cuban revolution is almost universally understood as the victory of a guerrilla army, in which workers and their organisations were passive onlookers. Stephen Cushion presents a talk about his 2016 book "A Hidden History of the Cuban Revolution: How the Working Class Shaped the Guerrillas’ Victory" which turns this conventional wisdom on its head. Based on years of research he argues that the Cuban working class played a much more decisive role in the Revolution’s outcome than previously understood. An open meeting, if you need any further details please email Sheffield Cuba Solidarity Campaign at cscsheffield@btinternet.com
