Women-only bike ride

Date, Time & Location Sun 9 Mar 2014, 9.30am from Barkers Pool, Sheffield
Details An opportunity to celebrate International Women's Day while cycling. This a very easy ride along the Trans Pennine Trail link route (NCN6) to Oughtibridge via Hillsborough Park through beautiful Beeley Woods, with flapjack and a cafe stop in Oughtibridge before returning around 1pm. The riders will take a very gentle pace to enjoy the friendly company and the scenery. Contact via email if you wish to join in: polytone@doctors.org.uk
