"Iterative Cities: Renewal and Revitalisation From The Bottom Up"

Date, Time & Location Tue 11 Feb 2014, 6pm, Octagon Centre, University of Sheffield, Western Bank S10 2TQ
Details A public lecture at the University of Sheffield with Marcus Westbury, Founder of Renew Newcastle (Australia) and Renew Australia, on low budget DIY urban renewal schemes which lead to major transformation, as social, cultural and technological factors drive a great surge in creative and economic life at the small scale, in cities and towns around the world. Entry is free of charge. For more information and to book a place see www.sheffield.ac.uk/whatson/iterativecities
