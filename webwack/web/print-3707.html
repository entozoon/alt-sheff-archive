PEACE IN THE PARK

Date, Time & Location Sat 9 Jun 2012, 12-8pm, Ponderosa Park Crookes Valley Rd. S10 1BA
Details A Sheffield highlight, with 6 stages of music, cabaret, arts and crafts, kids tents, great food, real ale, graffiti displays, yoga and dance classes, drum circles and more. Enjoy a beautiful relaxed atmosphere at this free festival, run by volunteers. Donations go to charities chosen by an open community group. www.peaceinthepark.org.uk
