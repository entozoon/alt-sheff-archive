Women's course: Tiling

Date, Time & Location Tue 7 Jun 2011, for 7 weeks
Details Re-opened for 2011 with great voluntary spirit, Women in Construction Arts and Technology (WICAT) offers courses for women (and some for men also). Prices are low, just to cover costs, with concessions for learners on various benefits. TILING  - 7 week starts Tues 7th June, 6–9pm, £84. An introduction to ceramic wall and floor tiling to enable you to tile your bathroom, kitchen, wet room, utility room or conservatory. The course will cover estimating, floor levelling, cutting tiles using electric tile cutters, adhesives and grouting. Women only WICAT: 21 Buckenham St. (above the Hindu Samaj) Pitsmoor.
