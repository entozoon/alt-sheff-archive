Talk: Public health and changes to the NHS

Date, Time & Location Wed 19 Jun 2113, 1pm-2pm, St Mark's Church, Broomfield Road, Broomhill
Details Dr Jeremy Wight, Sheffield Director of Public Health, will offer a factual explanation of the NHS changes and what they may mean for public health. The changes are controversial but this meeting is intended solely to increase knowledge and to answer factual questions. Admission: Donations please. One of many events which are part of Broomhill Festival. www.broomhillfestival.org.uk
