Sheffield Anti Cuts Alliance public rally

Date, Time & Location Thu 10 Nov 2011, 6.30pm, Ponds Forge Sports Hall
Details Sheffield Public rally to build for 30th November, when up to 3 million public sector workers will strike against attacks on their pensions, among the vicious cuts planned by the coalition government Speakers:
  • Mark Serwotka (General Secretary PCS)
  • Matt Wrack (General Secretary Fire Brigades Union)
  • Karen Reahy (Regional Sec, Unite)
  • Peter Davies (Sheffield Organiser, GMB)
  • Sally Kincaid (NUT, Wakefield Branch Secretary)
Sheffield Anti Cuts Alliance is a grass roots working class anti cuts organisation bringing together trade unionists, campaigners, students, the unemployed, pensioners and disabled people against the cuts. www.sheffieldanticuts.wordpress.com
