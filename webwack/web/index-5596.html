Vesta workers day of action

Wed 12 Aug 2009, 7pm Showroom 5, Showroom Cinema, Paternoster Row, Sheffield S1 2BX

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Meeting to support Vestas workers - objecting to closure of one of the only factories in the UK that manufacture wind turbines. The campaign argues that if the government believes in creating green jobs and renewable energy then they should step in to save the factory.As part of the day of action a worker from Vestas will be coming to Sheffield to speak about the campaignOrganised at short notice by the Right to Work Campaign. All interested groups and individuals welcome.www.campaigncc.orghttp://savevestas.wordpress.com
