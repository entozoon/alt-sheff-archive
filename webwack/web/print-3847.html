International Day of Co-operatives meeting

Date, Time & Location Fri 30 Mar 2012, 6pm-7.30pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details The International Day of Co-operatives is celebrated on the first Saturday of July every year. Its aim is to increase awareness on co-operatives and promote promote the successes and ideals of international solidarity, economic efficiency, equality, and world peace. Light refreshments available from 5.30pm
