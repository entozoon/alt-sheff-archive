Wortley Hall Walled Garden Heritage Open Day

Date, Time & Location Sun 6 June 2010, 10am-4pm
Details Wortley hall Walled Garden is a 3 acre organic kitchen garden managed by Heeley City Farm. The open day will feature local history displays and information, sound recordings of local people's memories, heritage trail around the garden, heritage craft maker, organic plants and produce for sale, and children's activities.
