2nd Annual Sheffield Anarchist Book Fair

Date, Time & Location Sat 21 May 2011, 10am-6pm, Bank Street Arts, 32-40 Bank St. S1 2DS
Details It's that time again! Sheffield's second annual anarchist book fair. Events like this happen all round the world, introducing the ideas, activism, ethics, creativity and history of the contemporary anarchist movement, for a world outside greedy capitalism and state dictatorships. Publishers, comix, zines, art, food and fun stuff, presentations, film screenings and a social/fund raiser in the evening. Proposals for meetings, workshops or presentations from groups and individuals welcome. Rates for stalls £15 for groups/campaigns and £20 for book stalls/distro's (half price on 2nd tables). Please contact in good time to reserve your stall. Space will be allocated on a first come, first served basis. Email: sheffield(at)bookfair.org.uk www.bookfair.org.uk
