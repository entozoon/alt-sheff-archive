Fighting for the Right to Stay: Anti-Deportation Training

Date, Time & Location Mon 10 Oct 2011, 7pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Training on defending asylum seekers from deportation, and the risk of torture or death. Organised by Sheffield Committee to Defend Asylum Seekers (CDAS) with a guest speaker (and expert campaigner) Michael Collins, from the National Coalition of Anti-Deportation Campaigns (NCADC). Come to the meeting, bring your questions and your expertise, and tell other people about this event. www.cdas-sheffield.org.uk
