Skeptics in the Pub: Use Your Imagination

Date, Time & Location Mon 25 Jan 2015, 7.30pm, Farm Road Sports & Social Club, Farm Rd. S2 2TP
Details A Skeptics in the Pub get-together; a relaxed and enjoyable evening while listening to a talk, usually by a notable scientist or skeptic. Talks are typically about a common belief that can be demonstrated to be false or cannot be justified by the available evidence. This month, Sarah Beck, Reader in Cognitive Development at the University of Birmingham, explains why children are great pretenders, poor problem solvers, and sometimes less clever than crows. Children are excellent imaginers, coming up with all kinds of creative and weird worlds. But what is the imagination really for?
