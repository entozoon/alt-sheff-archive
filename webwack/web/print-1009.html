A Better Sheffield: City of Makers

Date, Time & Location Wed 16 Nov 2016, 5.30pm, Workstation Creative Lounge, Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details A Better Sheffield offers a spotlight on a new wave of activists building local economies and reviving its reputation as a city of makers. Against a backdrop of austerity and uncertainty, these social, environmental and cultural innovators are searching out new spaces in Sheffield where changes can happen. Speakers will include Prof Vanessa Toulmin (University of Sheffield) & Laura Bennett (TechCity). Part of Festival of Debate 2016, in association with CIQ Agency & Urban Pollinators. Two related "Better Sheffield" events were on 28th Sept and 19th Oct. Please register using the Eventbrite link on the Festival of Debate website. Tickets £1 for My Showroom members or £4 for non-members. My Showroom is free to join at www.showroomworkstation.org.uk/my. Festival of Debate
