"Three Acres & a Cow" A History of Land Rights and Protest in Folk Song & Story

Sat 5 Dec 2015, Christ Church, Pitsmoor, Nottingham St. S3 9AQ

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Performance by activist musicians Robin Grey, Tim Ralphs, Kate Thomas and Sally Goldsmith. Part talk, part history lecture, part folk club sing-a-long, part poetry slam, part storytelling. "Three Acres & a Cow" connects the Norman Conquest and Peasants' Revolt with current issues of fracking and the housing crisis via the Enclosures, English Civil War, Irish Land League and Industrial Revolution; the radical people's history of Britain. Come and share in these tales as they have been shared for generations.Includes a simple shared meal from 6pm by donation, show starts at 7pm. £12/£5 concessions, tickets from New Roots shops at Glossop Road and Burngreave."Three Acres & a Cow"
