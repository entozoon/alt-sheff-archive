George Galloway and Ken Livingstone - "The Outsiders"

Date, Time & Location Sun 11 Nov 2018, 7pm, The Montgomery, Surrey St S1 2LG
Details Veteran MP, writer and broadcaster George Galloway and Ken Livingstone MP live on stage on Remembrance Day. They will be discussing their life and times, and their plans and ideas for the future, and a better Britain. Followed by questions and answers (Q&A). Arrive from 6pm-7pm, or 9pm-10pm if you want a book signing. Please note there is no disabled access at present. The Montgomery
