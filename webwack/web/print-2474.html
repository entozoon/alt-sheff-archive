Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 15 Apr 2014, 7pm-9pm at Quaker Meeting House, 10 St. James St. S1 2EW
Details This regular meeting of an alliance of climate activists is open to newcomers, all welcome. It is on the 3rd Tuesday of each month. Currently campaigning with trade unions on climate jobs and fracking, divestment from the fossil fuels industry, and Sheffield City Council and the Green Commission. Anyone interested in climate campaigning and linking their organisation to Sheffield Climate Alliance is welcome to attend.
