Sheffield Committee to Defend Asylum Seekers (CDAS) meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 14 Mar 2011, 7pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details CDAS fights for asylum seekers who live in Sheffield to be allowed to stay in the UK. These people have become our friends, and contribute to the cultural identity of the city, yet many live in fear of deportation, torture, physical abuse or even death. CDAS website
