Sheffield Estelí Society Ceilidh

Date, Time & Location Sun 6 Mar 2016, 7.30pm, Broomhall Centre, Broomspring Lane S10 2FB
Details Sheffield Estelí Society is holding this Ceilidh (traditional British dance) with the fabulous "Well Dressed Band". Lots of great dancing with caller Sue Coe to explain the steps. You do not need to know the dances to enjoy them! All welcome, it looks set to be a good evening with a great band, bar, and lots of information on the Sheffield Estelí Society sponsored bike ride on May 15th 2016, supporting groups and people of Estelí in Nicaragua. Tickets £7 on the night; £6 if booked in advance, concessions £5, accompanied under-16s FREE. To book tickets in advance, email jerrysimon.grow@gmail.com or text/phone 07976 313642) All are welcome, family-friendly event, please bring food to share. Sheffield Esteli Society
