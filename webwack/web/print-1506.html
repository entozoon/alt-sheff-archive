Exhibition for Holocaust Memorial Day

Date, Time & Location Winter Gardens, Surrey St. Mon 18 Jan to Wed 27 Jan 2016
Details An exhibition of posters leading up to Holocaust Memorial Day (HMD) on 27th January. The world marks this day we share the memory of the millions who have been murdered in the Holocaust and subsequent genocides in Cambodia, Rwanda, Bosnia and Darfur, in order to challenge hatred and persecution in the UK today.
