Family Fuel Buster Fun Days

Date, Time & Location Wed 11 & Thu 12 Aug 2010, 11am-3pm
Details One of a series of free activity sessions for families on recycling and energy-saving. Staff will advise on ways to reduce and manage fuel bills and increase home energy efficiency. Become a "Fuel Buster" by making your energy saving promise, completing the Energy House trail and designing your very own "Fuel Buster" badge. FREE.
