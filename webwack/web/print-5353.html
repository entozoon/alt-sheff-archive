Public meeting: Remember Gaza: Never again: for anyone

Date, Time & Location Sun 24 Jan 2010, 7 pm at Showroom cinema
Details Last December, Israel began a 23-day bombardment of Gaza, killing around 1,400 people. This public meeting brings together Hajo Meyer Haider Eid, who survived Auschwitz and Sheffield Palestine Solidarity, united in a commitment to Palestinian freedom. Sheffield Palestine Solidarity website
