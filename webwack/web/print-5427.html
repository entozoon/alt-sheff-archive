Sheffield FridayNightRide on the theme of Dissent

Date, Time & Location Fri 4 Dec 2009, meet 6.30pm at Barkers Pool, city centre
Details A cycle ride out to places associated with political and social dissent, about 15 miles. Sheffield Friday Night Ride offers a monthly evening bike ride for everyone. Their motto? "We have nothing to lose but our chains!" (But bring locks and lights too, just in case).
