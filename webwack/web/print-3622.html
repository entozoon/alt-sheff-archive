Sheffield Local Television public consultation event

Date, Time & Location Wed 25 July 2012, 6pm, Workstation Creative Lounge, 15 Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details Sheffield may soon get local television, via Freeview, cable and the internet. Sheffield Local Television consortium is inviting ideas on what the proposed new local TV channel should cover to ensure a wide cross-section of Sheffield views are reflected in its plans, before making a formal application for licensing in August. A short online survey which takes 2 minutes to complete is at http://survey.sheffieldlocaltv.co.uk All are welcome to attend this public consultation event, registration at http://sheffieldlocaltv.eventbrite.com Sheffield Local TV on Facebook
