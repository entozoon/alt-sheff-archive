Quick! Object online to East Midlands Trains planning application

Deadline for objections 9 Oct 2009

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

East Midlands Trains have applied for planning permission (listed building consent) for their crazy plan to gate off the station overbridge. You can object online before 9th October at Sheffield Planning. The planning reference is 09/02887/LBC, and if you'd like some suggestions of what to say in your objection, visit sheffieldgreenparty.org.uk details
