No Need for Cuts!

Date, Time & Location Thu 16 Mar 2017, 6.30pm, Central United Reform Church, Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details Councils were cut earlier and harder than the rest of the public sector. Councils are already becoming financially unviable, says Peter Latham in ’Who Stole the Town Hall’ (Policy Press, 2017). So what do we need to do? A discussion with panel members: Barry Kushner, Liverpool Councillor, author of ‘Who Needs the Cuts’ (Hesperus Press, 2013) Dave Berry, GMB, Walkley Labour Party – Standing Up for Sheffield Manifesto Olivia Blake, Sheffield Walkley Councillor A public meeting organised by the South Yorks Morning Star Supporters’ Group. Everyone welcome. Please note the early start-time
