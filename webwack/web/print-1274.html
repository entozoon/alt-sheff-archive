Workshop: Parliament and legislation

Date, Time & Location Thu 26 May 2016, 6pm-8pm Boardroom, Hallam HUBS Building, Paternoster Row S1 2QQ
Details An event in association with Houses of Parliament Outreach Service. Attendees will leave with the ability to describe and understand the legislative process, as well as having knowledge of how to influence legislation effectively and how to use the website to track bills before Parliament. Part of Festival of Debate 2016, hosted by Opus Independents (which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine). Free to attend, but please register so they can get an idea of numbers. Festival of Debate - Twitter: @FestOfDebate - #FofD - Facebook
