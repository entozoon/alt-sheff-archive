Women's course: DIY in the community

Date, Time & Location Wed 27 Apr 2011, for 5 weeks, at Firth Park Pavillion
Details Re-opened for 2011 with a great voluntary community spirit, Women in Construction Arts and Technology (WICAT) offers courses for women (and some for men also). Prices are kept low, just to cover costs, and there are concessions for learners in receipt of various benefits. DIY IN THE COMMUNITY - 5 weeks starts Wed 27th April 2011, 9.30am – 12 noon - £50. This course is suitable for learners who have some or no DIY skills. Learners will gain skills in joinery, fixing to walls and tiling. Women only. (COURSE TO BE HELD AT FIRTH PARK PAVILLON) WICAT: 21 Buckenham St. (above the Hindu Samaj) Pitsmoor.
