Protest cuts to the justice system

Date, Time & Location Mon 16 Jun 2014, 12.15pm outside Sheffield Town Hall, Pinstone St. S1 2HH
Details Cuts to the justice system affect probation, court staff and legal aid - soon justice will be for the rich only. Come and protest this and hold off one more attack on the welfare state! Protestors We will be outside Sheffield Town Hall for an hour with leaflets, Justice Alliance petitions and invitations to do a “justice selfie”. Join them! Bring colleagues, friends, family and pass the message on. Banners and placards welcomed – e.g. “Justice is not for sale”, “People over profit: Justice for All”, or anything you want to say.
