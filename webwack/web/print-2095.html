Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity final fundraiser of the year

Date, Time & Location Sat 6 Dec 2014, 7pm-10pm, St Andrew’s Hall, Upper Hanover Street S3 7RQ
Details Enjoy a delicious homemade Palestinian meal; meat, vegetarian or vegan, with music from fantastic Egyptian musician Ahmad Adnan on oud and guitar. All profits are in support of the childrens’ projects in Jabalia, Nussairat and Khan Younis which Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity Campaign has a long relationship with. It has pledged never to abandon those children, and with your valuable help, it will fulfil that pledge. Your support is needed more than ever in view of the latest Israeli brutal attacks which left 100s of thousands of children severely traumatised. £10 Waged/£7 Concessions. St Andrew’s Hall is close to the Sheffield University Tram Stop Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity Campaign
