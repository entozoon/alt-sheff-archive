Film: "La Yuma"

Date, Time & Location Fri 28 Feb 2014, 6pm The Void Film Studio. Sheffield Hallam University, Owen Building, Room 123 (Level 2 ) City Campus S1 1WB
Details Sheffield Esteli Society (SES) is proud to present a showing of La Yuma. Made in 2009, it is about a young Nicaragua woman who wants to be a boxer and challenges world wide gender stereotypes. With fantastic footage of every day life in Nicaragua and the challenges the young woman faces in trying to get away from gang culture. Donations to SES £5/£3 Directed by a woman, Florence Jaugey, the film was selected as the Nicaraguan entry for the Best Foreign Language Film at the 83rd Academy Awards. Lead actor Alma Blanco has won many awards. Sheffield Esteli Society
