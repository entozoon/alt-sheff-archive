Film: How To Change The World (hosted by Greenpeace)

Date, Time & Location Thu 28 Feb 2019, 7pm, Regather, 57-59 Club Garden Rd. S11 8BU
Details How To Change The World is a feature documentary, the story of Greenpeace, by Jerry Rothwell. It tells the adventures of an eclectic group of young pioneers who set out to stop Richard Nixon’s nuclear bomb tests in Amchitka, Alaska, and ended up creating the worldwide green movement with the birth of Greenpeace. Tickets £5. Regather
