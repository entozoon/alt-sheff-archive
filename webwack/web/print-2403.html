Space for Cycling ride

Date, Time & Location Sat 17 May 2014, 10.30am assemble Barkers Pool, city centre S1 2HB
Details As part of the Space4cycling campaign for safe space for cycling, for everyone, everywhere, this is a special ride, everyone welcome. It should be a big event in the city, and similar rides will be taking place on the same day in London, Leeds, Bristol, Manchester, Newcastle, etc. Organised by Cycle Sheffield, the ride will move off about 11am, ending at 12.30 on the Town Hall steps.
