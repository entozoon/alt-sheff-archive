Women's course: Introduction to DIY

Date, Time & Location Wed 27 Apr 2011, for 5 weeks
Details Re-opened for 2011 with a great voluntary community spirit, Women in Construction Arts and Technology (WICAT) offers courses for women (and some for men also). Prices are kept low, just to cover costs, and there are concessions for learners in receipt of various benefits. INTRODUCTION TO DIY  - 5 week starts Wed 27th April 2011, 12.45- 3.15pm - £50. This course is suitable for learners who have some or no Do-It-Yourself skills. Learners will gain skills in tiling, plastering, decorative paint effects, fixing to walls and joinery. Women only. WICAT: 21 Buckenham St. (above the Hindu Samaj) Pitsmoor. Call for details Sheff 2725360, text 07813 126388 or email wicat2011 {at} gmail.com www.wicat.org.uk
