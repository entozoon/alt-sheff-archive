Is Money a Psychological Problem?

Date, Time & Location Wed 4 May 2016, 4.30-6:30pm Lecture Theatre 2, The Diamond, 32 Leavygreave Rd. S3 7RD
Details A talk by Stephen Lea, expert in economic psychology, a field that explicitly unites the two disciplines. He is the author of over 150 peer-reviewed academic articles and has done extensive research on personal debt and its psychological consequences. An event by the Alternative Economics Society and Institute for Economic Analysis of Decision-Making (InstEAD) in association with Festival of Debate, hosted by Opus Independents (which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine).
