RASAG IT club

Date, Time & Location Mon 19 Mar 2012, 11am-3pm, Old Junior School, South View Rd S7 1DB
Details Sheffield Refugee Asylum Seeker Action Group (RASAG) runs this IT Club every Monday, providing a friendly, warm environment with high speed internet access and ESOL, literacy and IT support. They always welcome help with paired reading and IT support. Donations are also great, so feel free to bring along a packet of biscuits or other items that may be of use to members. http://sheffieldrasag.wordpress.com
