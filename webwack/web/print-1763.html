SHARROW FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sat 13 Jun 2015, till 8pm, Mount Pleasant Park, Sharrow Lane, nr London Rd. S11 8AE
Details Sharrow's free multicultural festival is back earlier this year, with a special covered Community Stage, and a hip-hop jam in the all-weather pitch with Chillfoo Front (sounds) and Marco (paint jam). Loads more to see and do, including a climbing wall, drumming and street dance workshops, five-a-side football with Football Unites Racism Divides, global foods to try, a huge variety of stalls and kids' activities. This year, Peace In The Park bring their own event, 'Little Peaces'. Music line-up (full details on the website below) includes: Bell Hagg Orkestar Black Thorn Bongo Bert & Sofia Brave New Storm Clara Pandy Early Cartographers Hot Diamond Aces Isembard's Wheel Kath Auto Malarkey Affair Mulembas D'Africa Dancers Nycha Dance Romano Jilo Son De America Steel City Rhythm The Mango Rescue Team The Soul Organisation The Unscene Truly Apparent 5-minute performances from some talented local people. Follow the festival action in a live broadcast by Sheffield Live! radio, 93.2 FM, and join the after-party at the Cremorne pub (see below). www.sharrowfestival.btck.co.uk
