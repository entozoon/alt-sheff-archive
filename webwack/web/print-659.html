Sheffield Against Islamophobia

Date, Time & Location Sat 25 Nov 2017, 2-4pm, Broomhall Community Centre, Broomspring Lane S10 2FD
Details An event hosted by MEND (Muslim Engagement and Development) to bring people together in solidarity and to learn more about the menace of Islamophobia, empowering local communities and creating changemakers. Also featuring their landmark causes and cures presentation. Guest speakers include Paul Blomfield MP (Sheffield Central) Free event, refreshments will be provided. Facebook event page
