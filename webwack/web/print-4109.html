The REALLY Free School

Date, Time & Location Sat 15 Oct 2011, CADS, (Creative Arts Development Space) Snow Lane Entrance, Shalesmoor, S3 7AL (5 mins from city centre, next to “KitLocker”).
Details A great day out run by Sheffield Social Centre group, which is forming a co-operative to open a permanent space in the city, run on anti-capitalist, non-hierarchical, non-discriminatory principles. This event is open to everyone, giving a taste of what a social centre can be like. A space for the free sharing of skills, ideas and knowledge. A full line-up of activities is planned, including:
  • YOU’VE BEEN FARMED: SOCIAL MEDIA AND SECURE/NON-CORPORATE ONLINE COMMUNICATIONS
  • CO-COUNSELLING
  • HUNT SABOTEURING
  • WORKPLACE RIGHTS
  • LaDIYFEST PRESENTS: RIOT GRRL RETROSPECTIVE FILM ‘DON’T NEED YOU’
  • YOGA
  • GROW OUR OWN
  • ANARCHA-FEMINISM
  • RECYCLED ART & PUPPET-MAKING FOR ALL
  • TOOL-SHARPENING
  • BIKE MAINTENANCE
  • FILM: THE PIPE, WITH DISCUSSION
  • LUDDITE UPRISINGS: TECHNOLOGY POLITICS THEN AND NOW
  • SQUATTING AND AUTONOMOUS SPACES
  • FREE SOFTWARE CLINIC
  • CIDER AND WINE-MAKING
  • CREATIVE WRITING
  • DISCUSSION: CUTS, CRISIS, CAPITALISM
... and there will be food available and kids activities. Followed by an evening benefit gig from 8pm at Red House, 168 Solly St. S1 4BB Evening line-up: Toein’ In The Dark Christine Moon Skiprat DJs Full details at: http://sheffieldsocialcentre.org.uk
