Free event: Zanzibar and its music

Date, Time & Location Tues 27 Apr 2010, 2pm till 2.50pm Showroom Cinema
Details Mim Suleiman (singer-songwriter and performer, Swahili cultural artist) will be presenting her live musical performance with a film of her experience in her native land Zanzibar. The film is produced by Sandra Thomas. The event is free!!! But you must collect you ticket at the showroom cinema box office in advance.
