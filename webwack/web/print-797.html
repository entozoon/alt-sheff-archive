Interested in volunteering with ASSIST? Volunteer info session

Date, Time & Location Thu 15 Jun 2017, 12-2pm, ASSIST Office, Victoria Hall S1 2JB
Details Refused asylum seekers are not allowed to work, to register themselves as homeless or to be placed in local authority night shelters. Many become destitute, struggling daily for food, accommodation and clothing. ASSIST works with asylum seekers in this situation.
  • Find out more about the different volunteer roles, people supported, and the organisation
  • Chat about what might suit you
  • Meet current volunteers
  • Get information on how to apply to be a volunteer with ASSIST
The ASSIST office, Victoria Hall has its entrance on Chapel Walk, opposite the Superdrug store. Please note: the same presentation will be repeated twice, at 10 minutes past each hour, so choose the time that is best for you. To find out more, contact Sheff 2754960 or email volunteer@assistsheffield.org.uk ASSIST Sheffield
