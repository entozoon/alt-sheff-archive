Cinema Syndicate Presents: Free films

Date, Time & Location Tue 1 Mar 2011, 7pm, University Arts Tower Lecture Theatre 1
Details Showings of political and activist film for free every Tuesday night. Cinema Syndicate is an anarchist film collective that is open to all, to actually offer something to people rather than just expect them to take an interest in politics. Through film, skip the banal opening debates about direct democracy, socialism or capitalism and get straight to what most people know and understand ...
