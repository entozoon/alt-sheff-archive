Freegle gets connected

News for 2013

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Freegle, the scheme to help people give things away rather than putting them into the rubbish, is using a new app to raise donations. The "Give as You Live" app donates a small amount to Freegle when you do online shopping without any extra cost to yourself. The you can download for free from www.giveasyoulive.com. Currently this only works on a PC or laptop, although Android and iPhone/iPad apps will soon be available.

Funding is vital for Freegle nationally, although all of the work done is voluntary they still need funding to help pay for the things they don't get for free such as the internet servers needed to help run the groups and to run Freegle Direct.

Also Freegle would like to ask for your votes to help Freegle UK get a small amount of funding, please see the following link www.hotgalaxywarmheart.com/fund/freegle

Your help would be greatly appreciated for one or both. The funding from both of the above goes to Freegle nationally, to help maintain such things as the servers, and not to an individual group or person.
