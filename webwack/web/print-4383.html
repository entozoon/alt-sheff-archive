Benefit Dinner for Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign

Date, Time & Location Sat 2 Apr 2011, 5-9pm, Hadfield Institute, 43 Attercliffe Common S9 2AE Opposite Attercliffe Police Station
Details Benefit Dinner in Aid of Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign, with 'Salam' Band featuring fantastic Egyptian singer Ahmad Adnan. Games and Presents for Children. Enjoy a delicious selection of home-made Palestinian dishes, suitable for carnivores, vegetarians and vegans. All you can eat. An Egyptian themed event in the aftermath of the Egyptian Revolution. Funds raised will support Palestinian children to cope in the face of ongoing Israeli atrocities. Food will be served between 5:30 and 7pm. £10 Waged / £6 Unwaged For more Information/Tickets phone 07715 048821 www.sheffieldpsc.org.uk
