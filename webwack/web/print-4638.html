Sheffield Anti-Cuts Campaign meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 24 Nov 2010, 6pm Furnival Suite, Novotel, Arundel Gate S1 2PR
Details Initial meeting to bring together Sheffield community groups, trade unions and students to discuss how best to organise and campaign against the cuts. Add your voice to help ensure the people who paid for the bank bailout don't pay for it again with their jobs and service. All welcome to attend.
