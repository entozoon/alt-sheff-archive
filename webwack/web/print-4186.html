Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change (SCACC) meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 16 Aug 2011, 7.30pm-9.30pm at Highfield Library, London Rd. S2 4NF
Details SCACC organises days of action and demonstrations, petitions to Sheffield Council, supports related national and local campaigns and runs film shows and talks for local groups. The first half-hour of this meeting will be spent on business of the Sheffield Climate Alliance - any organisations wanting to collaborate on climate campaigning are welcome to send a rep. The agenda will also include progress report and further plans for the petition on 10,000 climate jobs for Sheffield; planning for meetings/events this autumn; reports on developments around food, energy, transport and jobs in Sheffield The library can be reached by buses along London Road: 75, 76, 97, 98, 20, 20A, 25A etc. All are welcome to attend.
