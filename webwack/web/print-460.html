Stop and Scrap Universal Credit Rally

Date, Time & Location Sat 27 Oct 2018, 11.30am-1.30pm, outside the Town Hall, Pinstone St. S1 2HH
Details Rally called by Sheffield Labour Party in the continuing protest about Universal Credit. It was due to be implemented in Sheffield this November, meaning increased problems for all those dependent on benefits because of cuts to benefit levels and long delays in payment. However, there have been reports in the media that further roll-out might be stopped or slowed down by the Tories. (Leafletting for the rally by Sheffield Disabled People Against the Cuts on Wednesday 24th Oct 10.30 - 11.30 outside Cavendish Court Job Centre, Bank St. S1 2DR) Facebook event page
