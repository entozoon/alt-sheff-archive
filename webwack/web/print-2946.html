Sheffield Equality Group monthly meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 7 Aug 2013, 7pm St Matthew's Meeting Rooms, Carver St, Sheffield S1 4FE
Details Discussion of the proposed mythbusting survey around basic income, and other ongoing activities. The group is an autonomous group affiliated to the national Equality Trust and meets on the first Wednesday of every month. Free, donations for biscuits, all welcome. For more on the campaign about social support see the website. Note venue is NOT Quaker Meeting House this time, www.sheffieldequality.wordpress.com
