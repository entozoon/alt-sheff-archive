Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change AGM (SCACC)

Date, Time & Location Tue 22 Sep 2009, 7 – 9.30pm at Quaker Meeting House, St James Street, Sheffield S1 2EW (near the cathedral)
Details SCACC organises days of action and demonstrations, petitions to Sheffield Council, supports related national and local campaigns and runs film shows and talks for local groups. The Annual General Meeting is followed by Sheffield’s Climate Emergency Parliament! An emergency sitting of the People’s Parliament, to demand real action on Climate Change. The Parliament will consider Bills to: Make 10% reductions in UK Greenhouse gases by 2010; Create a million Green Jobs and an emergency insulation program; Ban all domestic flights by the end of 2010; Introduce a 55 mph national speed limit, halve the cost of public transport and terminate the roads program. If you’d like to propose one of these Bills, contact Jenny Patient, or just come along to join in! SCACC website
