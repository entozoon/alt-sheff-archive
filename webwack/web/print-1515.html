Sheffield Amnesty International meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 13 Jan 2016, 7.15pm at Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Amnesty International is a worldwide organisation of nearly 3 million people all working to protect Human Rights. The Sheffield branch meets monthly on the 2nd Wed of every month (except August). Actions on human rights abuses, contacts, successes and updates from various campaigns. Newcomers welcome. At the first meeting if the year, they also offer the opportunity to volunteer to assist with the running of the group, in one of the roles available. The roles include chairperson, secretary, media officer, event planner, treasurer and country co-ordinator. If you would like to volunteer please email or join them at the meeting. If more than one person wants a role, they take a vote. Any assistance you could provide would be appreciated. Similarly if you have any ideas for what they should focus on in 2016 or events the group could run please get in touch. Sheffield Amnesty International Facebook
