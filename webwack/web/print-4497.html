Books For A Better Future

Date, Time & Location Wed 9 Feb 2011, 6pm, Sheffield Central Library
Details February's meet-up will be a discussion of 'Sustainability without the hot air' by David MacKay. 'Books For A Better Future' is a reading group based at Sheffield Central Library, that aims to read and discuss books that will inspire us to build a better future. Visit the blog http://booksforabetterfuture.blogspot.com or contact Dan booksforabetterfuture (at) gmail.com for more information.
