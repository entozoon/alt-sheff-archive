Repair cafe

Date, Time & Location Sat 16 Nov 2019, 10am-4pm, Community Hall, Heeley City Farm, Richards Rd. S2 3DT
Details Forget the throw-away culture, many broken things can be repaired. Repair cafes are run by Repair Sheffield, on one Saturday every alternating month. A group of dedicated volunteers spend free time repairing everyday items such as vacuum cleaners, sewing machines, radios, and toys and gadgets. They are joined by BitFIXit, a group of volunteers and professionals which has been fixing computers since 2005 on a not-for-profit ’Pay-As-You-Can’ basis. BitFIXit Repair Cafe
