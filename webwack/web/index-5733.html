Book reviewers needed

November 2016

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

"Principle 5", the co-operative resource centre based in Sheffield, needs people to write short reviews of books to put online. They are building a nice collection of literature about co-operatives and related issues. The Books section of their website contains a listing with cover photos, but additional information is not yet available on some of the books. If you might like to write a review of any of the books, please contact Principle 5 for details via the website.

Alternatively come and have a chat before or after the Principle 5 AGM on Tuesday 22nd November, 3:00pm at Union Street co-working co-op.
