Grenfell Memorial Rally Sheffield

Date, Time & Location Fri 14 Jun 2019, 5-6pm, Outside Sheffield Town Hall, Pinstone St. S1 2HH
Details Friday 14th June marks the 2nd anniversary of the Grenfell Tower Fire. As the inquiry continues, Sheffield People`s Assembly have called this rally to remember the terrible event and consider whether and how fire safety has improved. Speakers invited include some from the Fire Brigades Union, Council, and MPs. Sheffield People`s Assembly Sheffield People`s Assembly on Facebook
