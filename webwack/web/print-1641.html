Film screening: "Sell-Off: the Abolition of your NHS"

Date, Time & Location Tue 22 Sep 2015, 7pm, Theatre Delicatessen, The Moor (old Woolworths shop) S1 3LR
Details A screening of a documentary about privatisation in the NHS featuring a performance by Heather Morgan, and a discussion led by one of the London GPs who appears in the film. Organised by Sheffield Save Our NHS, which is fighting the appalling government plans for cuts, competition and privatization for the benefit of corporate power.
