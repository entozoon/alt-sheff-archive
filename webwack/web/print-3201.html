Farm Kitchen Gourmet Evening

Date, Time & Location Fri 22 Mar 2013, Heeley City Farm, Richards Road, S2 3DT
Details Time for another gourmet evening at Heeley City Farm. The Farm Kitchen is hosting its first pop up restaurant event of the year, the community cafe will be transformed into an evening venue serving up delicious vegetarian and vegan gourmet food. They recommend booking a table without delay as every event the cafe has hosted so far has sold out in advance. Full details and a nice write-up at "Its happening in Heeley" Heeley City Farm
