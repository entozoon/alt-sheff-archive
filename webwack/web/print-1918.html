Festival of Debate: Shami Chakrabarti - On Liberty

Date, Time & Location Tue 10 Mar 2015, 6.30pm SU Auditorium, Sheffield Students’ Union, S10 2TG
Details Shami Chakrabarti is director of Liberty, the UK’s most influential civil rights organisation. This talk will draw inspiration from her recent book, On Liberty, in which she warns of the threats that currently face our democratic institutions. Talk followed by a Questions and Answers session and book signing. In association with The Exchange. The Festival of Debate is taking place in Sheffield in the run-up to the General election, during March and April. It provides opportunities to get together and discuss a range of issues which affect everyone. Informed decision making and strategic planning are essential for democracy, so come and get involved. They also welcome volunteers, from attending events and taking part in discussions to volunteering time to help run an event, promote the festival and much more. Unless otherwise stated events are free to attend, but if possible please register via the appropriate Eventbrite link on the Festival of Debate website below to give organsisers an idea of numbers.
