Wildflower and Plant ID for Tortoise Owners

Date, Time & Location Wed 17 Sep 2014, 6pm, Heeley City Farm, Richards Rd. S2 3DT
Details A fully qualified herbalist will guide tortoise owners through the identification of approx 20 common wildflowers and plants which can be eaten safely by their pets (as they would eat in the wild and help to save owners a fortune!). The workshop will consist of a classroom session with an introduction by Helen Smith of The Tortoise Table, showing examples of plants and a walk around the Farm to identify plants. It will also cover dangerous and commonly confused specimens. Session includes a handout to take away.
