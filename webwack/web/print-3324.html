Sheffield Friends of the Earth meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 21 Jan 2013, 8pm Red Deer pub, 18 Pitt St. S1 4DD, off West Street (upstairs room)
Details Friends of the Earth Sheffield Group, currently campaigning on many issues including the application by Veolia (the operators of Sheffield Incinerator) to increase the amount of waste it imports by another 15,000 tonnes annually and to extend the catchment area to as far as Mansfield and Newark.
