World Development Movement AGM & Food Sovereignty talk

Date, Time & Location Thu 28 Jun 2012, Central United Reformed Church, 60 Norfolk St, S1 2JB.
Details The annual general meeting of the WDM starts at 7pm, followed by an excellent speaker on Food Sovereignty, 7.30 till 9.30pm. Patrick Mulvany is senior policy adviser to the charity Practical Action (previously ITDG). He is also Chair of the UK Food Group, which works on global food and farming issues including promotion of ecological food provision in the framework of food sovereignty. WDM Sheffield website
