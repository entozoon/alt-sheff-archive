What kind of NHS do we really want?

Date, Time & Location Tue 26 Jun 2018, 7-9pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details A Socialist Health Association event asking, if we all want to protect the NHS, what kind of NHS? We want it to be national, but surely some decisions need to be local and personal? Many campaigners argue that the new Accountable Care Organisations are a route to privatisation. But what do we want instead? Join us for this thoughtful seminar.
