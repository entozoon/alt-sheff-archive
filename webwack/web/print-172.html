LOWEDGES FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sun 11 Aug 2019, 12-5pm, Greenhill Park, Gresley Road, Lowedges S8 7HN
Details The popular Lowedges Festival returns to Greenhill Park, and it is big. Funfair, dog show, over 300 classic cars and bikes, free climbing wall, kids bungee jump, rides, mediaeval re-enactments, bar, or bring your own beer, food from around the world and loads of stalls, etc. Huge non-stop reggae marquee, approximate times: Irie White 1-2pm PJ's One Drop 2-3pm Abelwell Foundation 3-4pm Macka B & the Roots Ragga Band 4-5pm Free entry. An annual community festival organised by volunteers. For details by email: stephen.rich@hotmail.co.uk Facebook event page
