Free Fruit Tree Grafting Workshops

Date, Time & Location Sat 19 and Sun 20 Mar 2011, 11 - 4pm, 61 Carfield Avenue, S8 9HZ
Details Abundance's Pete Hodge and Stephen Watts teach how to graft fruit trees for a whole day! Grafting local varieties of apples onto mm106 rootstock, and local pears onto quince rootstock. You get to take home a free fruit tree from the workshop! Part of the Abundance Project. Thanks to a south area community assembly grant, the grafting workshops are now free of charge, to who ever wants to come on them. Another next weekend ...
