Spring Out 2012

Date, Time & Location Sat 12 May 2012, 9.30am-6pm, St.Mary's Conference Centre, Bramall Lane S2 4QZ
Details The 10th year of this celebration event promoting healthy lesbian, gay and bisexual communities in Sheffield. Discussions, workshops, food, even zumba and self-defence.
