Free Love and Socialism Cycle Ride

Date, Time & Location Fri 17 Jun 2011, start 6.30pm meet at Barkers Pool, S1 2HB
Details On the longest Friday of the year, Friday Night Ride (FNR) will be kicking off Bike Week for CycleSheffield. The theme of this ride is Free Love and Socialism: a ride to see the Millthorpe house where Edward Carpenter (nationally and internationally renowned radical, socialist activist, writer and thinker, advocate of free love, early gay activist, 'bohemian' and sandalmaker) lived from the 1880s to the 1920s.
