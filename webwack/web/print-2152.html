Sheffield Friends of the Earth meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 20 Oct 2014, 7pm Red Deer, 18 Pitt St. S1 4DD, off West St.
Details Friends of the Earth is a powerful force of people from all walks of life pressing for environmental protection and positive change. There are many different ways to help, depending on the time or skills you have. Membership of Friends of the Earth Sheffield group is free if you subscribe to the electronic newsletter. The meetings are free and welcoming to newcomers. Sheffield FOE website
