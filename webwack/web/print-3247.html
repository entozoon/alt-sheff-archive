Film: "How Long is Indefinite?"

Date, Time & Location Tue 5 Mar 2013, 6pm, University Arts Tower LT2, Western Bank S10 2TN
Details As part of a series of films for Refugee Week, a documentary about the impact of being detained indefinitely, focusing on the growing number of detainees who are incarcerated for the purposes of removal even when the Home Office cannot in fact remove them. Facebook event
