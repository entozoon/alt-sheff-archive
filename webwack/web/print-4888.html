Edward Carpenter: a walk with Sally Goldsmith and Rony Robinson to hear about this Victorian writer, anarcho-socialist, sandal wearer and simple-lifer who lived openly with his male lover at Millthorpe near Sheffield and Chesterfield. This is a walk around some of the places associated with Carpenter and his circle, especially Millthorpe where he entertained many of the prominent writers and thinkers of his day including E M Forster, George Bernard Shaw and Olive Schreiner. We will eat our packed lunch in the garden of Carpenter House. Approx 5 miles with stops along the way. Meet 11am at Holmesfield Church carpark (on right, next to Angel pub).
