Sheffield Live! free basic computer lessons

Date, Time & Location Wed 28 Sep 2011, from 2pm, 15 Paternoster Row, Sheffield S1 2DD
Details Community radio station Sheffield Live! 93.2 FM is running free basic computer lessons every Wednesday afternoon from 2pm. If you know someone who has never used a computer before, wants help with setting up an email account or wants to learn how to search online then just get them to pop in. www.sheffieldlive.org/
