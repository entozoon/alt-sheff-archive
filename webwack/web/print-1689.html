Gig in aid of ASSIST

Date, Time & Location Thu 20 Aug 2015, 7.30pm The Shakespeare, 146-148 Gibraltar St. S3 8UB
Details A night of intimate acoustic music in aid of ASSIST Sheffield. Great music in aid of a great cause. Full details on the link below. Line up: Jupiter In Jars - original harmonic folk ditties and the odd tasty cover Ash Gray - of “Punkgrass” band High Class Family Butchers, with toe-tapping country and Bluegrass Robbie Thompson - of Americana band The Buffalo Skinners, delicate melancholic and smooth finger-picking Sarah Morrey - of Sheffield folk duo Captives on the Carousel, Sarah´s songs are dark, captivating and enchanting Mari-Liis Tamm - ASSIST volunteer and one of the few Estonian folk singers in the People´s Republic of South Yorkshire. Refused asylum seekers are not allowed to work, to register themselves as homeless or to be placed in local authority night shelters. Many become destitute, struggling daily for food, accommodation and clothing. ASSIST works with asylum seekers in this situation. Tickets £5 on the door ASSIST Sheffield
