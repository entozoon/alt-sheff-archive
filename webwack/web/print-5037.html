Guided Wildlife Walk

Date, Time & Location Sun 23 May 2010, 10am
Details Guided walk led by Michael Senkans, Sheffield City Council Ecology team, along Tongue Gutter in Parson Cross. This walk will feature on different species of plants and animals that make the area their home. Meet at Parson Cross Pavilion, Parson Cross Park, off Buchanan Road, S5 6FL. The walk will take approximately 2 hours and then will return to the pavilion for a cup of tea and a chat. Just turn up on the day with suitable clothes and shoes.
