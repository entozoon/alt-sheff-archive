Insight into the Syrian Culture

Date, Time & Location Wed 14 Sep 2016, 9.15am-12.15, The Circle, Rockingham Lane S1 4FW
Details An event to share information and discuss innovative ways for a successful protection and integration of Syrian refugees in South Yorkshire, to increase awareness about the Syrian relocation scheme, and give specific knowledge about the Syrian culture, integration challenges and expectations of Syrian refugees. The session will increase the ability of partner organisations to work effectively with Syrian refugees after their 12 months support ends. Organised by The Refugee Council in collaboration with the Syrian Community of Sheffield, open to agencies, volunteers, community groups, voluntary and community organisations.
