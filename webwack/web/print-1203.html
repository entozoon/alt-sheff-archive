STANNINGTON CARNIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sat 9 Jul 2016 from 12 noon, Stannington
Details Annual carnival, run by Stannington Carnival Organisation and Festival Friends (SCOFF) held in Stannington Park and Lomas Hall, S6. Stalls, parade, displays, fairground organ, and a chance for local organisations to raise funds and promote themselves. The usual fun stuff for a day out, all in a good cause. Volunteers welcome as well. Email for details stanningtoncarnival@gmail.com Stannington Carnival on Facebook
