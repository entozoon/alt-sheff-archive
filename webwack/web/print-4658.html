Campaign for Nuclear Disarmament (CND) Annual General Meeting and presentation

Date, Time & Location Wed 17 Nov 2010, 7.15pm at Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James Street, Sheffield S1 (next to the Anglican cathedral)
Details All are welcome to the (brief) AGM which will be followed by a talk by Stuart Parkinson, executive director Scientists for Global Responsibility, 'Arms Conversion for a Low Carbon Economy'. Arms conversion is a particularly pertinent topic given the current controversy about whether the Trident nuclear weapons renewal programme should be cancelled and concern about whether this would put people out of work.
