"Before Now" - Childrens' workshops

Feb 16, 18 & 21, Jordanthorpe Library, 15 Jordanthorpe Centre S8 8DX

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

FREE Art workshops aimed at 8-12 year olds, working with a professional artist. Explore what Sheffield was like before now, and create your own artworks based on our local history. This is an open access session on a first come first serve basis. Maximum of 15 places, so if you would like to book on to secure your place email admin@igniteimaginations.org.uk or call Sheff 2686813, or Jordanthorpe Library, tel: Sheff 2037701. Note if you do not arrive within 15 mins of the start of the workshop, your place may be given away. Children under 8 years must be accompanied by an adult.Sessions: Monday February 16th: 10am - 1pm, Wednesday February 18th: 3 - 6pm, Friday February 20th: 3 - 6pm, Saturday February 21st: 10am - 1pm
