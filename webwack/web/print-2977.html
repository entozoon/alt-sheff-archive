Public meeting: Sharrow responds to racist violence

Date, Time & Location Mon 8 Jul 2013, 7.30pm, Hagglers Corner, 586 Queens Rd. S2 4DU
Details Following the upsurge in racist activity like street harassment, an attack on a Muslim information stall, and on anti-fascist protesters, Sharrow residents have set up this public meeting to decide how best to respond. All local residents are welcome to come and discuss experiences of recent events, and community responses. More details of this and similar events in other areas from sheffantifascist@gmail.com
