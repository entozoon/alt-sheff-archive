Performance: Act 38: We are all Equal

Date, Time & Location Sat 10 Dec 2011, 7pm-8.30, Mandela Room, Sheffield Town Hall, Pinstone Street, S1 2HH
Details Artist Monica Ross’ poignant and stirring performance of Act 38: We are all Equal, focusing on the significance and relevance of the Universal Declaration of Human Rights. The performance takes place on the 63rd anniversary of the Declaration and on International Human Rights day. Local citizens will recite from memory articles of the Declaration and the Sheffield Socialist Choir will perform a song to celebrate justice and freedom. Presented in partnership with Site Gallery and Sheffield Socialist Choir as part of Monica Ross’ 2011-13 UK tour ‘Anniversary – an act of memory’ which is funded by Arts Council England. FREE admission but booking advisable: www.actofmemorysheffield.eventbrite.com Further information: www.actsofmemory.net
