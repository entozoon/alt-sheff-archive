Behind the Wire

Date, Time & Location Fri 13 May 2011, 7pm, Bridge Inn, Chantry Bridge, Rotherham (centre)
Details Rotherham Red Choir and Amnesty International present 'Behind the Wire'. Songs, stories, activities to support prisoners of conscience across the world. Plus Rotherham band Toein' in the Dark, raffle, real ale, and Amnesty's 50th birthday cake! Tickets £5/£3. Pay on the door or tel. 01709 379945
