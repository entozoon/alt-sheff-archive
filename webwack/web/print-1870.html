Festival of Debate: Word Life

Date, Time & Location Fri 27 Mar 2015, 7.30pm Shakespeares 146-148 Gibraltar St. S3 8UB
Details Sheffield’s leading live literature night returns for a politics special, featuring a headline set from Matt Black’s Vote For Someone campaign and a politically themed open mic. To sign up for the open mic, email wordlife@nowthenmagazine.com. Recommended donation of £5 on the door. All welcome. The Festival of Debate is taking place in Sheffield in the run-up to the General election, during March and April. It provides opportunities to get together and discuss a range of issues which affect everyone. Informed decision making and strategic planning are essential for democracy, so come and get involved.
