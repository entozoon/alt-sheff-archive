Climate Change Hearing

Date, Time & Location Fri 27 Nov 2009, 5pm to 8pm (welcome & buffet from 4.15pm) at Bentley Pavillion, Bentley, Doncaster DN5 0HU
Details Oxfam event to hear from local people how climate change is impacting on them, to capture these local stories on film, in the media and on blogs, to help influence the government. This is a free event. If you want to find out more, register your attendance, or share your story at the event, please email ssaunter@oxfam.org.uk Tel. 01302 343300
