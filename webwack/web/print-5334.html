Workers' Climate Action (WCA) meeting

Date, Time & Location Mon 8 Feb 2010, 7.30pm Upstairs at The Bohemian Cafe, 53 Chesterfield Road, Sheffield S8 0RL
Details Meeting to discuss how bus workers’ struggles against privatisation and pay cuts can be linked to our collective struggle against climate change. Luke Evans of Liverpool WCA will talk on potentials for alternative fuels, and local labour movement and environmental movement activists will discuss possibilities for building practical solidarity with bus workers’ struggles.
