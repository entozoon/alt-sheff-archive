Peace and Justice Public Meeting for Heeley

Date, Time & Location Thu 30 Apr 2015, 7pm Carfield School, Argyle Close S8 9HJ
Details Four organisations have joined together to put the issues of peace and justice before the Heeley Constituency General Election candidates. The issues are the wars abroad, the Middle East, Palestine, Trident, and Islamophobia. Candidates attending: Stephen Castens (Conservative) Simon Clement-Jones (Liberal Democrats) Louise Haigh (Labour) Rita Wilcock (Green) Organised by Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign, Sheffield CND, Sheffield Stop the War Coalition, and Sheffield Yemeni Community Association.
