Lecture: Energy and the Politics of the Future… "The Figures"

Date, Time & Location Wed 21 Oct 2009, 7pm at Sheffield Cathedral
Details One of a series of lectures hosted by Sheffield Cathedral and Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change The Figures: Ann Pettifor, Executive Director of Advocacy International and Operation Noah, formerly with Jubilee 2000, Member of the Green New Deal Group. An experienced international economist takes a hard but hopeful look. All lectures held in Sheffield Cathedral at 7pm. Tickets £2 (concessions £1) for single lecture, all three lectures £5 (concessions £2.50) available in advance by post from Julia Alder at Church House, 95-99 Effingham Street, Rotherham S65 1BL or from Blue Moon café (next to the cathedral) or on the door.
