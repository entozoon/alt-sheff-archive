Transition Sheffield Christmas Social

Date, Time & Location 16 Dec 2010, 7pm til late Riverside Live Pub, 1 Mowbray St, S3 8EN
Details Following great demand for more city-wide Transition get-togethers, a just-for-fun evening with an open mic session, including a performance by local vegetable entertainer Madame Zucchini and possibly a film show. Transition Sheffield raises awareness about climate change, peak oil and starting local projects across the city. www.transitionsheffield.org.uk
