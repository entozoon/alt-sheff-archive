SHEFFIELD PRIDE

Date, Time & Location Sat 28 Jun 2014, Sheffield City Centre
Details The annual parade and festival promoting and supporting the LGBT community in Sheffield, one of the safest and most welcoming cities in the UK. Join with over 12,000 others to show your colours and show your pride. Live entertainment in the main arena in Devonshire Green, 2 stages and Gaydio Dance Arena. Street entertainers, dancers, musicians and drag performers will be making an appearance throughout the city and event site. Bars, food and marketplace with a fantastic selection of local organisations, and activities for children running in partnership with Hallam FM. Performance lineup: Carol Havana Anthony Rewind Sour Cherry Polkadodge GTF Red Faces Kelly Wilde Sam Solace.
