Allotment Soup!

Date, Time & Location Sun 23 Oct 2011, 12-7pm, LEAF Community Allotment, Norwood Allotments, Herries Rd, S5 7HA
Details An annual creative and artistic harvest celebration of food growing and allotment culture. For one day only allotment holders at the Norwood allotment will be opening up their plot to host an installation, artwork and workshops. This year celebrating the change of seasons with the theme SEASONING! The day will feature: Amazing allotments! Art installations! Greenhouse galleries! Performances and poetry! Art workshops! Recipe swapping! Food workshops! Cider pressing! Cycle powered smoothie making! SOUP! Singing! Fruit and veg! Tea and cake! Growers! Artists! You! Me! Mitsi the Cat! 12-5pm Art trail around the allotments, activities and events starting from the LEAF allotment 5-7pm Soup social celebration at the LEAF allotment More details on Allotment Soup 2011: http://allotmentsoup2011.blogspot.com For more information about previous Allotment Soup events: www.growsheffield.com/pages/groshefallothm.html And to read about the amazing LEAF allotments please see: www.leafsheffield.org.uk
