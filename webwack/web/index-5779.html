Sheffield Climate Alliance growing

January 2016

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

With climate change at a crucial point, Sheffield Climate Alliance is in top gear and has 1200 subscribers to its newsletter. They would like to build a team from the universities, local businesses and societies to look at what a zero carbon future could mean, and how we get there - fast! Get in touch if you want to be involved.

They are also looking for people who want to volunteer with things like updates to their website, communicating with people who want to know more, joining in campaigns or running a fundraising event such as a benefit gig.

Please get in touch (email info@sheffieldclimatealliance.net) if you can spare a couple of hours once a month or so - all enquiries welcome and they would love to help you get involved. Computer skills appreciated, but plenty of chance to learn and build your CV.
