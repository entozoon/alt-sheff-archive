YES to Co-operation, Equality and Individual Creativity

Date, Time & Location Fri 23 Sep 2016, 7.30-9.30pm Freeman College, 88 Arundel St. S1 2NG
Details Dr John Carlisle puts forward these principles as workable alternatives to the malign neoliberal practices of monetising and commodifying everything in the public and private realms. Dr Carlisle is a former visiting professor at Sheffield Business School. Such a shame that business schools do not put these principles at the heart of all their teaching. Part of Sheffield Festival of Debate 2016 Autumn Season, hosted by Opus Independents (which also publishes an Alt-Sheff column in the free monthly Now Then magazine). Free to attend, donations welcome, but please register via the appropriate Eventbrite link on the website so they can get an idea of numbers. Festival of Debate
