Mayday, Justice Day!

Sat 3 May 2014

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

12 noon assemble at Devonshire Green for a march and rally led by Janet Alder and Carole Duggan from family justice campaigns, Orgreave Truth and Justice Campaign, NAPO trade union who are fighting privatisation and solicitors, fighting legal aid cuts, and strikers from Care UK and SOAS.1pm-4pm Plug (nightclub), Matilda Street, city centre. Unite the resistance: organise to win - discussion, free food, music, film. Themes include NHS pay, racism, austerity and the 30th anniversary of the miners': struggle against Thatcher. Registration £3/£1 concessions.Unite the Resistance
