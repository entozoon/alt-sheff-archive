Sharrow Spring Market and Fashion Show

Date, Time & Location Sat 30 Apr 2016, Old Junior School, South View Rd. S7 1DB
Details Sharrow Festival will be holding their first indoor and outdoor market with global food and enticing stalls, followed by their first ever fashion show, featuring the talents of local designers specialising in a range of clothing and accessories in different multicultural styles. Designs by El Addo, Mhacagi, Dread Funky, Khimaar, Araneus T-Shirts, Araneus ADW and Team Sunrise, the hair styling of Alicia Nang Hair & Beauty and make-up artistry of Fern Strawson MUA and Hayleigh Louise. Vinyl/CD music sellers are welcome to come down to the market, call Alan on 07903 949931 if you need more info. To book a stall please contact mark_a_cohen@hotmail.com Market: 12-6pm Fashion show: 8pm-midnight Facebook events page Sharrow Festival
