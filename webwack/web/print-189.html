Dabke Dance Group

Date, Time & Location Sat 20 Jul 2019, 12.30pm, Devonshire Green, Sheffield
Details The Dabke Dance group are young people from the Dar al Fonon Centre in Nablus in Palestine. This performance at Devonshire Green is part of Tramlines Fringe Festival. Tickets are free but a donation to the travel costs of the young people would be welcome. Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign
