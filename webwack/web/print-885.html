Why Sheffield Needs a Radical Bookshop

Date, Time & Location Thu 20 Apr 2017, 7pm, Theatre Deli, 17 The Moor S1 4PF
Details If you’re interested in feminism, anarchism, Marx, anti-racist struggles or LGBT rights, join Jepp’s Books, Sheffield’s new radical book emporium, for an evening of music, poetry, ranting and drinks. Jepp’s Books, newly established the month before, will be based in Theatre Deli until June. It is open Tuesday to Saturday, 11am to 4pm. Jepp’s Books
