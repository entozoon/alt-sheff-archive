New climate jobs for Sheffield?

New report August 2013

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

New academic research concludes that investment in climate solutions could create over 6000 jobs and add £300 million per year to our local economy.

"The Economics of Low Carbon Cities" asks what are the best ways to "decarbonise" Sheffield City Region. Reviewing the hundreds of low carbon options available, it looks at the political, business and social cases for such investment. It explores the scope for their deployment in households, industry, commerce and transport, investment needs, financial returns and carbon savings. Benefits include long-term secure jobs in the low carbon sector, as opposed to risky dirty short-term jobs in fossil fuels.

The full 34-page report can be downloaded as a pdf file from:
www.lowcarbonfutures.org/sites/default/files/SCR%20Mini%20Stern%20Final%20Report.pdf
