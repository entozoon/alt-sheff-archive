Hiroshima/Nagasaki Peace Picnic

Date, Time & Location Sun 9 Aug 2015, 1pm Meersbrook Park Community Walled Garden
Details Anti-nuclear activist Bruce Kent comes to Sheffield for this commemoration organised by CND. It marks the 70th anniversary of the dropping of the first nuclear bombs on Japan. Also present will be the Deputy Lord Mayor, signing the Mayors for Peace Declaration, along with many artists and groups including SCRAP bringing their beautiful peace quilt to the event. As the 70th anniversary it will be quite special. Bring your picnic with some extra to share. If you can come early to help set up that would great. Meersbrook Park is 4km south of the city centre, off A61 Chesterfield Road S8 9FL. Entrance: Brook Road. Buses 53 from Pond St. or 20, 20A, 25, 25A from the Peace Gardens to Beeton Rd./Chesterfield Rd.
