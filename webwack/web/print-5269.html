International Women’s Day celebration benefit

Date, Time & Location Sat 6 Mar 2010, 7pm Broomhall Centre, Broomspring Lane, S10 2 FD
Details Sheffield Palestine Women’s Scholarship Fund and Body of Sound celebrate the first 3 Palestinian scholarship women to gain their degrees. Women only event (children welcome): Women’s choir, dancing, disco and great food. £5/£2 on the door (free to asylum seekers). Soft drink bar. Accessible venue. All proceeds to the scholarship fund supporting women in Gaza to access education.
