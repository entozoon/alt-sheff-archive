Macrocosm

Date, Time & Location On now till 26 Mar 2014, 10am-4pm Kiac studios, 40 Ball Street, S3 8DB
Details Exhibition raising funds for Peace in the Park Festival. The latest work exploring the very far from sight, above, beyond and internal, created by Cassie Limb, includes free artwork available in exchange for wishes, this is a new insight into taking steps into our futures creatively. The "System" is not working for "you". A evening of discussion exploring various topic around who "YOU" are and your "Legal NAME" Sharing fun stories of taking our power back against "fines/penalties" and government threats/bullying tactics. We are existing in a system which charges you for living. Listen to Outside the Box Open Forum with Kate of Gaia for additional insights. Facebook event page
