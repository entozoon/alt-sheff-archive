Protest: No More Tory Austerity

Date, Time & Location Wed 5 Dec 2012, 5pm, Sheffield Town Hall
Details There will be protests in London and across the UK, including Sheffield, on the day of Osborne's Autumn Statement. £10bn further cuts in Welfare spending are predicted, as Osborne reports on the failure of his economic policy over the past 2 years. Sheffield Council expects to make £50 million additional funding cuts, further damaging local services, it is predicted that 600 further job losses at Sheffield Council on top of 1400 already lost. Meet outside Sheffield Town Hall for a protest supported by Sheffield Trades Council, Sheffield NUT (union) and Sheffield Anti Cuts Alliance.
