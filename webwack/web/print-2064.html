Sheffield Feminist Network Women‘s Group

Date, Time & Location Mon 5 Jan 2015, 7.15pm Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Monthly meetings for (self-defined) women only, working together politically to campaign for women‘s rights, in a safe and supportive space. This month: current charity projects, Sheffield Working Women‘s Opportunities Project (SWWOP) and discussion of the ‘Nordic Model‘, (an approach to preventing trafficking and exploitation also known as the ‘Swedish model’ that penalizes demand for commercial sex while decriminalizing individuals in prostitution and providing support services, including help for those who wish to exit prostitution.)
