Transforming Cinema film festival

19-20 Nov 2016, Sheffield

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Transforming Cinema is a national film festival, with a focus on the transgender, intersex, gender nonconforming, non binary and genderqueer community. Giving people a platform to broadcast their voices and stories is important because, despite press coverage, there is still a lack of understanding in society at large. Mainstream films rarely touch upon this subject and trans people are suffering because of the widespread ignorance and negativity that often surrounds their depiction in the media. Depression, self-harm and suicide are some of the issues that affect trans people to a much higher rate than non-trans people. Media representation is low and the messages we do hear are negative, unhelpful and even harmful. Presenters include: Rebecca RootFox FisherSarah O'Connell Kate Adair Trans Media WatchMore to be announced.Sunday 20 November - honouring Transgender Day of RemembranceThe festival is organised by E.D.E.N Film Productions, following their popular film-making workshops, and E.D.E.N Action! and Trashing Transphobia projects. If you want to get involved, there are opportunities for volunteers to gain experience by working at the film festival. Transforming Cinema
