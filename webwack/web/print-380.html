Diversity Fest 2019: planning meeting

Date, Time & Location Sun 16 Dec 2018, 3pm-4pm, Hagglers Kaffihaus, Hagglers Corner, 586 Queens Rd. S2 4DU
Details All welcome to this, the third planning meeting for Diversity Fest 2019. There is lots to do over the next 6 months including fundraising, developing the organisation, publicity, recruiting volunteers etc. Then in April 2019, start booking performers for the festival. If you want to get involved with this fabulous festival, come along to one of these planning meetings monthly (previous one was 22nd November). Diversity Fest
