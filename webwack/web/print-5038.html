Sheffield Anarchist Bookfair

Date, Time & Location Saturday 22 May, 10am-4pm, Corporation Nightclub, Corner of Eldon Street and Milton Street
Details The first Sheffield Anarchist Bookfair will feature stalls from a number of activist groups, distros and magazines.
