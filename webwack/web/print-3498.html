Tax Justice Hearing

Date, Time & Location Wed 10 Oct 2012, 7.30pm-9pm, (bus from 4pm) Sheffield Cathedral
Details Dean Peter Bradley (Dean of Sheffield Cathedral), Dr Dereje Alemayehu (Tax Justice Network), and David Price (Church Action on Poverty) speaking on how tax dodging is harming the poor locally and globally. The Tax Justice Bus will be outside the Cathedral from 4pm. Over seven weeks, it is going to 80 locations across the UK and Ireland, with one goal: tax justice for the poorest communities. www.christian-aid.org/tax-bus
