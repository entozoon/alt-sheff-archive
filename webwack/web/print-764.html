SHARROW FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sat 1 Jul 2017, Mount Pleasant Park, Sharrow Lane, nr London Rd. S11 8AE
Details If you’ve never been to Sharrow Festival, try to go. It’s a great annual multicultural community festival bringing people together with a focus on creativity and participation. Performances, local groups and businesses, great music, dance, a worldwide variety of food, the local Sure Start team, sports activities and more. There’s also the Sharrow Fringe Festival on the days surrounding the main festival event. Sharrow Festival
