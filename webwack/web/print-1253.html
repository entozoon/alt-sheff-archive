PEACE IN THE PARK FESTIVAL 2016

Date, Time & Location Sat 11 Jun 2016, 12-8pm, Ponderosa Park, Crookes Valley Rd. S10 1BA
Details Peace in the Park is an excellent Sheffield festival, with multiple music stages, food, dozens of stalls, entertainment and fun for all ages. All the local activist and community groups are there and it is a great day out. Run entirely by volunteers, and funded by gigs all year round, the proceeds are given to different charities every time. Tell your friends and come along, see you in the park! To book a stall use this online form, or contact the organisers. Peace in the Park
