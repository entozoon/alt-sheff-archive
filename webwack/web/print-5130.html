Free film: Globalisation Tapes

Date, Time & Location Tue 20 Apr 2010, 6.30pm at Cinema Syndicate
Details Cinema Syndicate is an anarchist film collective, open to all, showing through political and activist film that we live in an unjust world, and it needs to change. Globalisation Tapes is about the rise on contemporary globalisation through the 'modernisation' of Indonesia Film shows are free, Tuesday nights at Lecture Theatre 2, Arts Tower, University of Sheffield.
