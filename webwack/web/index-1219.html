Migration Matters Festival

Sat 25 Jun 2016, Theatre Delicatessen, 17 The Moor (old Woolworths shop) S1 3LR

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Events for Saturday, concluding the Migration Matters Festival.12pm-2pm, The Art of Migration Showcase, featuring Bashar Farhat (poet), Mina Salama + Special Guest. Performances from a vibrant mix of talented regional artists with migration and refugee backgrounds. Commissioned by Arts on the Run.2pm-4pm, Puppetry Worskhop, by Vertebra Theatre. Exploring immigration through puppetry!4pm-5pm, Silk Road, by Incomplete Collaboration. Migration explored through poetry, sound and art.6pm-7pm, Eastern European for Dummies, by There There. A brutal beginners guide to Eastern European.7.30pm, Tanja, by Strawberry Blonde Curls. Locked up. Shipped around. Sold as sex...Migration Matters Festival is part of Refugee Week, celebrating sanctuary in Sheffield, which was the UK's first City of Sanctuary. A partnership between Theatre Delicatessen and University of Sheffield supported by Arts Council England. All events are "Pay What You Decide". Migration Matters Festival
