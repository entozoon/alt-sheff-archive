Protest Against the Demolition of Devonshire Street

Date, Time & Location Tue 24 Mar 2015, 1.15pm Town Hall
Details Meet at Sheffield Town Hall to protest against the plans for "development" (demolition) of Devonshire Street, home to unique independent local shops like Rare & Racy, Syd and Mallory and Natural Bed Company. This is in spite of the biggest online petition of its kind ever seen for Sheffield of 19,300 signatures, and 20,000 objections on the council planning website, the support of local MPs and famous Sheffield musicians Facebook event page
