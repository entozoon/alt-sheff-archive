Karen New Year Celebration

Date, Time & Location Sat 12 Dec 2009, 11am-2pm at Abbeydale Grange School, Hasting Road, Sheffield S7 2GU
Details The Karen Community Association UK would like to invite you to the celebration of Karen New Year 2749 (2010). The Karen are an ethnic group of Burma who have been persecuted by the Burmese military dictatorship for decades. Most of the 500 Karen people in the UK live in Sheffield.
