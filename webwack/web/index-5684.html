Food Works Not Food Waste Campaign

Deadline 4pm on 24th December 2018

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

The Real Junk Food Project has been working for the last three years as collecting and redistributing perfectly good food that would have gone to waste, turning over 300 tonnes into thousands of meals. Their strong community of volunteers, suppliers and sponsors could do more if they had a long term lease on a warehouse with proper big fridge/freezer storage and a kitchen on site, so they are running a Crowdfunding appeal for £50,000. They´re already getting towards that target; can you help? Please take a moment to take some action, and share the campaign with people you know.

The campaign closes on Christmas Eve at 4pm. They´ll be open well into the night at the Sharehouse Market and all are welcome to come down and join them for mince pies. Full details including video on this website.
