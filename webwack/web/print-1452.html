LGBT Pop-Up Café

Date, Time & Location Tue 23 Feb 2016 10.30am-5.30pm, 18-20 Union St. S1 2JR
Details LGBT Café opens every Tuesday, all welcome. This week (during LGBT History Month) from 5.30pm onwards come and help put Sheffield’s LGBTQ history on the map at Sheffield’s first pinning party. Which places and spaces are important to you?Spaces are a vital and fascinating part of LGBTQ heritage and include everything from bars and nightclubs and houses lived in by lesbian, gay and trans people to meeting places for LGBTQ activists.
