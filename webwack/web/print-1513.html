“Devolution: the future for Sheffield: Understanding the opportunities and challenges for our city”

Date, Time & Location Thu 14 Jan, 9am-12.30, Electric Works, Sheffield Digital Campus S1 2BJ
Details “Sheffield First” is hosting this event to offer an opportunity to hear more about the case for devolution, learn more about the detail of the Sheffield City Region Devolution "Deal", and hear about some the challenges and opportunities devolution and decentralisation could present for Sheffield. As well as being informative, it is also an opportunity for "city leaders" to discuss developing a shared city approach to future devolution opportunities. Booking is necessary and only a few places remain. Details and booking form at Sheffield First
