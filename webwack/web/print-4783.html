Black Rope Walking Group Walk

Date, Time & Location Sun 19 Sep 2010
Details Get your boots out and come along for a walk with other libertarians and anarchists. Meet at Sheffield interchange at 10.45am to catch the Castleton bus 272 at 11am to Fox House. This can also be boarded at 11.11 at Hunters Bar, Hunter House Hotel. Arrives at Fox House at 11.28. The walk goes from Fox House down to Padley Gorge, down through the woods to Grindleford station and cafe. Then to Longshaw estate and back to Fox House (pub). Buses come back into Sheffield from Fox House at 15.28, 16.28, 17.28, 18.43. Walk with lunch stop takes about 4 - 4-and-a-half hours at leisurely pace and is child friendly but not suitable for wheelchairs or pushchairs and can be muddy in bits.
