Cycling culture films and display at Chesterfield

Date, Time & Location Sat 19 June 2010, Chesterfield Library Lecture Theatre
Details Cycle displays in meeting rooms adjacent to the cafe open from 12.00 until 4.30pm. There will be two showings of a one hour programme of short films of cycling culture at 1.30pm and 3.00pm. Tickets are needed for the film showings which are free and available from the Chesterfield Cycle Campaign, Chesterfield Library and Chesterfield Tourist Office, or ring 01246 520820.
