Talk: "A Long Ride Home"

Date, Time & Location Thu 5 Sep 2013, 7pm, Amici and Bici, 220 Abbeydale Rd. S7 1FL
Details James Kemp, an architect from Dronfield, cycled across Europe from Dronfield to Gibraltar all in aid of Cancer Research UK. From everyday cyclist to long distance tourer he talks about his inspiration and efforts in undertaking such an adventure.
