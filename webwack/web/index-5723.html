Sheffield Green Business Group planned

March 2017

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Sheffield Green Party members who own or run a business are invited to a meeting discussing policies for Sheffield Green Party which are friendly to local business, rather than remote, faceless and profit-sucking corporations.

Please contact Councillor Rob Murphy at robmurphy.sheffield@virgin.net or call 07580 498392 if you would like to come to the meeting and/or be involved in the group.
