"Disco is Back!" fundraiser

Date, Time & Location Fri 18 Oct 2019, 8.30pm-2am, Hagglers Corner, 586 Queens Rd. S2 4DU
Details A fundraiser disco with the cream of the Sheffield disco DJs. Organised by Broomhill and Sharrow Vale Labour Party, to help asylum-related work in the city. Funds will go to SYMAAG (South Yorkshire Migration & Asylum Action Group, ASSIST (for homeless "refused" asylum seekers), and South Yorkshire Refugee Law and Justice. Entry: pay what you can. SYMAAG.
