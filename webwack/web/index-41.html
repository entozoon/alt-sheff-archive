Non-Violent Direct Action Training

Wed 19 Feb 2020, 6.30pm, Union St. co-working space, 18-22 Union St. S1 2JP

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

This session is an essential training on how to take part in non-violent direct action. It guides you through your rights whilst protesting, when you should speak to the police and what you should tell them and how to remain calm and confident during an action.Whether you are intending to break the law or not, this is a great opportunity for Extinction Rebellion training, whether you are new or experienced. Training hosted by Extinction Rebellion Sheffield and Union St on two dates in Feb (19th at 6.30pm, and 29th at 1pm). (And monthly with sessions every third Wednesday and every last Saturday of each month).Facebook event page
