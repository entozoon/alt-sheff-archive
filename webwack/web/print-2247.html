Workshop: Woodland Pyrography

Date, Time & Location Sun 17 Aug 2014, Woodland Discovery Centre, Ecclesall Woods, Abbey Lane S7 2QZ
Details Pyrography – the art of writing with fire! An introduction to the traditional folk art of burning designs into a natural surface such as wood, experimenting with different lines, textures and shading. Take home with you your own coaster, keyring, necklace, wooden sign or piece of art. Workshops runs 9.30-12.30 and 1pm-4pm, (£45 half-day, £80 full). Run by Low Impact Living Initiative (LILI) which offers publications, free factsheets, a huge database and forum, products, services and courses in everything from co-operatives to wildfood camping. In Sheffield, courses are often held at the Woodland Discovery Centre, an educational facility in the largest ancient woodland in South Yorkshire. Full course details are on the LILI website. Although not free, they are run by experts, giving in-depth tuition in less common skills. Low-impact Living Initiative (LILI) Ecclesall Woods Craft Courses
