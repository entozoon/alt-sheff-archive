Extinction Rebellion at the Town Hall

Date, Time & Location Wed 6 Feb 2019, 1pm-4pm, Sheffield Town Hall, S1 2HH
Details Various local councils have declared a Climate Emergency, but not Sheffield City Council. Extinction Rebellion are putting pressure on to make sure they take it seriously, by attending the Full Council meeting, (starts 2pm, agenda online here). It seems this idea is taking off; Labour Councillors are asking for a review of the current zero-carbon date (2050) and the Green Party want it to be 2030, plus a renewable electricity contract, so it could be a useful debate. If you can make it, please join in. Facebook event page Extinction Rebellion Extinction Rebellion Sheffield group on Facebook
