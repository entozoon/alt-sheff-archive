Sheffield Book Fair

Date, Time & Location Sun 28 Oct 2012, 10am-4pm, The Workstation, Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details Mostly second-hand books from around 15 established sellers based in Sheffield, South Yorkshire and beyond. Antiquarian and collectables, art and design, local studies, fiction, poetry, history, transport, topography, natural history, children’s and much more. To book a stall please contact David Granville at Books on the Park. Tel. Sheff 2666364. Tickets £1 on the door. Children free. Part of Off the Shelf festival. www.offtheshelf.org.uk
