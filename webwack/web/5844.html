Workshop: Oppression, Movement Building and Our Relationships as Activists

Sat 7 Mar 2020, 2.30pm-6.30pm, Theatre Deli, 202 Eyre Street, S1 4QZ

Share on

An Extinction Rebellion workshop which will look at how movements can be divided when oppression goes unaddressed, offering practical tools to heal these divisions, which affect our relationships as activists.
