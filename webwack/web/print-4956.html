Falafel and Film Fundraiser

Date, Time & Location Sat 26 Jun 2010, 6.30pm Pitsmoor Methodist Church
Details Food and film showing of 'Hebron Home Eviction', filmed by Deacon Dave showing the Israeli army evicting a family from the Old City of Hebron. The family eventually break open their house and move back in. Deacon Dave is planning to go back to Hebron, Palestine as a peacemaker and to film interviews. He has DVD films available from previous visits. If you would like a film or to make a donation please contact Dave on 272 9695.
