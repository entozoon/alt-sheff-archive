Growing Food: A course of evening classes

Date, Time & Location Wed 10 Oct 2012, 7pm-9pm, Central United Reform Church, Chapel Walk, S1 2JB
Details Second session of 10 Monday evening sessions in an introduction to "edible-culture" for beginners and experienced learners. Tutor Richard Clare was the founder of the Abundance project, and Sheffield Organic Food Initiative. He has been actively involved in organic cultivation for many years and has put his extensive knowledge into this course. (Note, to avoid confusion, there are also Monday evening classes running on perennial food growing.)
