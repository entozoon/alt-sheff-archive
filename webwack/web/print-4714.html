Cinema Syndicate - WACO

Date, Time & Location Tue 26 Oct 6:30pm - 9pm - Sheffield University Arts Tower Lecture Theatre 2
Details This week Cinema Sydnicate features WACO: The Rules of Engagement. A controversial documentary investigating the events between a the religious group the Branch Davidians and the United states Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms, and Explosives (ATF) occuring on Febrauary 28th 1993 and ending 50 days later with the death of 74 civilians including 25 children. The documentary suggests a different view to that of the government and media; interviewing various technical, psychological, religious experts and studying video evidence from the event. There will be chance for discussion following the film. Cinema Syndicate group page
