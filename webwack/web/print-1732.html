WOODSEATS COMMUNITY FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Fri 10 - Sun 12 Jul 2015
Details Woodseats Community Festival is now in its tenth year, held in various venues around Woodseats, all run by enthusiastic volunteers from the local community. Arts and crafts, music, performance, sport and leisure activities. There's even a car boot sale. Full programme and details will be on the website. Woodseats Community Festival
