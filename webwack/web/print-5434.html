CND annual general meeting and talk

Date, Time & Location Mon 30 Nov 2009, 7.30pm at The Quaker Meeting House, St James Street, Sheffield S1 2EW (near the Anglican cathedral)
Details Sheffield Campaign for Nuclear Disarmament (CND) Annual General Meeting followed by a talk 'NUCLEAR POWER & NUCLEAR WEAPONS: CAN THEY BE SEPARATED?' with speaker Stewart Kemp. A free event, all welcome to come and join in the discussion - and find out more about CND too. Sheffield CND website
