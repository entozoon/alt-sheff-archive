Public talk: Mike Prysner

Date, Time & Location Thur 27 Jan 2011, 7.30pm (tea/coffee from 7) Quaker Meeting House, St. James Street, near the cathedral
Details Mike Prysner, US war veteran and the founder of the anti-war organisation March Forward has recently returned from a visit to Gaza. He will speak about US and UK foreign policy in the region. A powerful speaker providing a perspective from the US on anti-war struggles and the centrality of the struggle for justice in Palestine. Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign are hosting this meeting alongside Sheffield Stop the War and the campaign against the cuts in Sheffield. Everyone welcome. www.sheffieldpsc.org.uk
