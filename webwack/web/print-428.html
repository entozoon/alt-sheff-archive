Film Show and Salsa Fundraiser for Hallam CLP

Date, Time & Location Sat 18 Nov 2018, 4pm - 9pm, Crookes Social Club, Mulehouse Rd. Crookes S10 1TD
Details Hallam Constituency Labour Party Fundraiser to celebrate the 60th Anniversary of the Cuban Revolution (in 2019); to commemorate the 51st anniversary of the murder of Che Guevara, and to raise money for Hallam CLP campaigning fund. Hallam CLP are applying for a licence to show the film "Che" (part 1) at 4pm. Salsa class for beginners at about 7pm. The evening will end with a couple of hours of mojitos and salsa dancing. There may be some street food available. Tickets £10 on the door, £8 in advance, £4 low/unwaged from tonyomercer@btinternet.com. All welcome.
