Demo and March for Kashmir

Date, Time & Location Sat 31 Aug 2019, 12.30pm, Peace Gardens, Pinstone St. S1 2HH
Details Demonstration to demand freedom for Kashmir. Hindu nationalist President Modi of India has removed its autonomous status, claiming it as a state of India. Telephone and internet connections were cut and activists imprisoned. With rumours of killings and food shortages, the Kashmiri communities in Sheffield and worldwide are worried and angry about the repression. Facebook event page
