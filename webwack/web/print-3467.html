Talk: The Need to be Seen as Authentic And How it Influences Our Behaviour, Attitudes and Beliefs

Date, Time & Location Mon 22 Oct 2012, 7.30pm, Farm Road Sports & Social Club, Farm Rd. S2 2TP
Details Michael Heap presents a Skeptics in the Pub get-together about how behaviour, attitudes, beliefs, and even the way we experience events are highly influenced by the demands and expectations of the various roles that we occupy in the course of our everyday life. This may lead us to behave and think in ways that are irrational, questionable and counterproductive. Michael Heap is a clinical and forensic psychologist with over forty years of experience working both with people with mental health and neurological problems and with criminal offenders. Skeptics in the Pub is about getting people together to have a relaxed and enjoyable evening while listening to talks on a wide range of topics.
