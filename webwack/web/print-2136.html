PEACE AND CRAFT FAIR

Date, Time & Location Sat 1 Nov 2014, 11am-4.30pm, Sheffield Town Hall, Pinstone St. S1 2HH
Details Sheffield's annual Peace and Craft Fair brings together campaigning groups, and local crafters selling artisan items which are not normally available on the high street. All organised by a group of volunteers on a not-for-profit basis. • Ethical and affordable festival gifts • Quality crafts direct from the makers – clothes, accessories, jewellery, cards, textiles, food, bath products, toys, homeware and more • Campaigning and voluntary groups • Live music and singing • Pop-up cafe serving refreshments Something for everyone in a vibrant family friendly atmosphere. Entry fee only £1 for adults, children free. www.peaceandcraftfair.btck.co.uk
