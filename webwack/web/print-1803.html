General Election Hustings: Hallam Constituency

Date, Time & Location Wed 29 Apr 2015, 7.30pm Wesley Hall, Crookes S10 1UD
Details "Crookes Forum" and "Sheffield for Democracy" are jointly hosting this event, to give voters in the Hallam Constituency the opportunity to question candidates in person. Present will be: Peter Garbutt, Green Oliver Coppard, Labour Joe Otten representing Nick Clegg, Lib Dem Ian Walker, Conservative Joe Jenkins, UKIP Chair: Professor Leni Oglesby. All Hallam constituents are welcome. There will be a collection to pay for the room.
