Sheffield Climate Alliance meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 18 Mar 2014, 7pm-9pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St. James St. S1 2EW
Details This regular meeting of an alliance of climate activists is open to newcomers, all welcome. Currently campaigning with trade unions on climate jobs and fracking, divestment from the fossil fuels industry, and Sheffield City Council and the Green Commission. This meeting will focus on: - report back on discussions about Climate Jobs with Sheffield City Council - anti-fracking and divestment updates - lobbying Nick Clegg about EU carbon targets SCACC website
