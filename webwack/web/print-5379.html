Women in Black vigil

Date, Time & Location Sat 2 Jan 2010, at Barker's Pool, Sheffield city centre
Details All women welcome at this vigil. Women in Black vigils are held on the first Saturday of every month at the War Memorial at Barker's Pool in Sheffield city centre (in front of the John Lewis store) from 1-2.30pm, campaigning against all forms of violence against women and for justice and equality. Any enquiries phone Sheff 2660434.
