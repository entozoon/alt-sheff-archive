In Conversation with Julian Assange

Date, Time & Location Thu 12 May 2016, 6.30-9pm Foundry, Sheffield Students Union, S10 2TG
Details Julian Assange is a journalist, publisher and internet activist. He is the editor-in-chief and founder of Wikileaks, which since 2006 has worked to support whistleblowers, promote governmental transparency and hold power to account across the globe, including through well-known leaks initiated by Chelsea Manning and Edward Snowden. In this ‘in conversation’ event, Julian will discuss his work via video link from the Ecuadorian embassy in London, where he is currently claiming asylum. Part of Festival of Debate 2016, hosted by Opus Independents (which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine).
