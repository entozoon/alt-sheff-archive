Family-friendly Salsa and Zumba fundraiser

Date, Time & Location Fri 27 Sep 2013, 6.30pm, Crookes Social Club, Mulehouse Rd. S10 1TD
Details This event will be a celebration and shared learning of the diverse cultures and communities in Sheffield. Trained speakers will be there to talk about their experiences and what Sheffield ASSIST conversation club and drop-in service mean to them. Confirmed artists so far: Holly Tara Knight (Zumba) Ieva Dubova (Bokwa) Ivan Rodrigo Garcia (Salsa/Zumba) Jodie Marshall and team from ‘A Mind Apart’ (Facepainting) Prices: Suggested donation: £5 adults/£1 for children/free to those seeking sanctuary. A fundraiser for City of Sanctuary. www.cityofsanctuary.org
