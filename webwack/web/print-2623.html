"Life Gamification"

Date, Time & Location Sat 8 Feb 2014, 10.30-5.30pm, 35 Chapel Walk, S1 2PD
Details Free event, part of the week of external "pop-up" activities by Access Space. Performer Paul Newman will be in the the gallery all day engaging people with what he calls "life gamification" - making your life better by having fun! At 2pm-4pm there will be a Grand Tea Party. All welcome, come and find out what it's all about. The Chapel Walk space will be open 10.30am-5.30pm with more than ninety artworks on display, and designs and commercialised products made by participants in Sheffield. Venue details: www.35chapelwalk.com Access Space
