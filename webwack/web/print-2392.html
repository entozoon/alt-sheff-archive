Basic Pole Lathe Turning course

Date, Time & Location Sun 25 May 2014 9.30am-4pm, Woodland Discovery Centre, Ecclesall Woods, Abbey Lane S7 2QZ
Details A one day course at the J G Graves Woodland Discovery Centre, in beautiful Ecclesall Woods. Learn the basics of turning green wood on a pole lathe, preparing timber to make useful materials for hand crafts, tool choice and maintenance and safe tool use. Make either a garden dibber, spurtle or spatula. www.ecclesallwoodscraftcourses.co.uk
