Films: Fragments of a Lost Palestine, & Bethlehem Checkpoint

Date, Time & Location Sat 27 Nov 2010, Nov 2010, 6pm, Showroom Cinema, Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details When filmmaker Norma Marcos returns home to Palestine she is keen to show her friend Stefan how normal people try to live normal lives outside of the occupation. Through a series of conversations with friends, family and strangers she shows that despite the tense political environment, life goes on. The film is followed by a questions and answers session with director Norma Marcos, and shown alongside short film Bethlehem Checkpoint. Part of the Cinema Palestino season. Entry £5. The Showroom are doing a £20 offer to see all Cinema Palestino films over the weekend, the Showroom restaurant is putting on Palestinian food all the weekend with a £25 deal for a meal for two including a bottle of wine and two tickets to a film. 50% of the net profits from the season will be donated back to children's projects sponsored by Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign
