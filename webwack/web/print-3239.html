Film "Climate Refugees"

Date, Time & Location Tue 7 Mar 2013, 6.30pm, University Arts Tower LT03, Western Bank S10 2TN
Details As part of a series of films for Refugee Week,the University Ethical and Environmental Committee will be screening a documentary about people who have lost their homes due to droughts, desertification, extreme weather and sea-level rising, and how global warming is affecting the most vulnerable people in the world.
