Orgreave Rally 2018

Date, Time & Location Sat 16 Jun 2018, 1pm, Orgreave Lane, S13 9NE
Details The Orgreave Truth & Justice Campaign (OTJC) will not be silenced in pressing for a public inquiry on police aggression against striking miners in June 1984. Bring your banners, whistles, drums, family and friends. The rally will be led through Orgreave by the Unite Brass Band. Speakers: Gill Furniss, MP Joe Whitworth, Durham Miners Assoc. Helen Steel, Spycops and social justice campaigner Matt Abbott, poet Kate Flannery, OTJC Tim Roache, GMB union Chris Williamson, MP Orgreave Truth & Justice Campaign
