Global Justice Now AGM and film

Date, Time & Location Wed 25 May 2016, 6.30pm Central United Reformed Church, Norfolk St. S1 2JB
Details Global Justice Now is holding a short AGM meeting prior to the public filming showing of "Dirt! The movie", at 7.15pm. Narrated by Jamie Lee Curtis, this brings to life the environmental, economic, social and political impact that the soil has. It shares the stories of experts from all over the world who study and are able to harness the beauty and power of a respectful and mutually beneficial relationship with soil. Newcomers welcome.
