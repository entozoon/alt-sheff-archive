Talk: The Nakba revisited: past, present and future course of the Israeli - Palestinian conflict

Date, Time & Location Tue 17 May 2011, 5pm, Auditorium, Sheffield University Students Union building, Western Bank
Details Ilan Pappe, reknowned Israeli historian and political activist will be in Sheffield. If you have never had the chance to hear Ilan Pappe speak before this is a not to be missed event. He is one of the most illuminating and sharpest analysts of the Palestinian Catastrophe, the 1948 Nakba, and he is also a humanitarian, with a deep commitment to justice for the Palestinian people. Students Union auditorium is in the Sheffield University Students Union building, Western Bank, to the left of the dual carriageway as you travel towards Broomhill out of Sheffield city centre, about 5 minutes walk from the large university roundabout. Free. Non students welcome. Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity Campaign and Sheffield Students Palestine Society.
