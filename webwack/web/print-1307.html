LaDIY Presents: Try The Pie, Two White Cranes, Be Still My Heart, AMereKat

Date, Time & Location Thu 5 May 2016, 8pm, The Audacious Art Experiment, 107A Harwood St. S2 4SE
Details A gig run by LaDIYFest Sheffield, a DIY, anti-capitalist, community based, feminist festival group run by volunteers, led primarily by women but all are welcome to get involved. It aims to highlight female and LGBTQ art, projects and activism in the community, promoting social change in a non-discriminatory environment through music, performance art, spoken word, films, discussions, art exhibitions and practical workshops etc. Try The Pie Two White Cranes Be Still My Heart AMereKat An evening of acousticy loveliness, with vegan treats! £5 donation on the door, no one will be turned away for lack of funds. LaDIY and TAAE operate a safe spaces policy, don’t be a douche or you will be asked to leave. Please remember to bring donations for the fookbank drive. BYOB. Facebook event page LaDIYFest
