The Social Progressivity Index

Date, Time & Location Wed 20 Apr 2016, 5pm, Lecture Theatre 3, The Diamond, 32 Leavygreave Rd. S3 7RD
Details Please note the date change from the printed brochure, 20th April is the correct date. A talk by Michael Green, author of Philanthrocapitalism, and CEO of the Social Progress Imperative. He is part of the team that has created the Social Progress Index, a standard to rank societies based on how they meet the needs of citizens. This event is the Alternative Economics Annual Lecture, presented in association with Festival of Debate, hosted by Opus Independents (which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine).
