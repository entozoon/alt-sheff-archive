Ceilidh dance: Transition Porter Valley

Sun 10th Oct 2010, Millennium Hall, Ecclesall Road, 6.30pm till 10.30pm

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

A family-friendly fund-raising, foot-stomping ceilidh dance, with the amazing Well Dressed Band. All the dances will be taught so don't worry if you haven't done ceilidh dancing before. Transition Porter Valley are asking people to bring food to share for a pot-luck supper. Bar and raffle. Tickets £5 in advance, £6 on the door, and half-price for children under 16. Tickets: tel. Sheff 2682483 or email stephanie.howlett@btinternet.com
