Pedlars Corner Car Boot Sale

Date, Time & Location Sun 6 Apr 2014, 11am start, Abbeydale Picture House, Abbeydale Road
Details Local social enterprise The Monocle Magpie CIC has started a new and regular car boot sale in the Sheffield Antiques Quarter to help the locals of Nether Edge, Sharrow, Heeley and the Abbeydale Corridor to off-load themselves of hoarded items and so clear the dust from their attics.
