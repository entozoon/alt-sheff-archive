Trees protest: surround the Town Hall

Date, Time & Location Sat 7 Jan 2017, 11.45am at Town Hall, Surrey St. S1 2HH
Details Sheffield Trees Action Groups (STAG) are organising a human chain to surround the Town Hall. People are invited to assemble at 11.45am, dressed in yellow if possible to reflect the yellow ribbons tied around the 20,000 trees threatened with the axe under Sheffield City Council/Amey's PFI contract.
