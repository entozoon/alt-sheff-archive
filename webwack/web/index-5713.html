The new situation with Bramleymoor Lane - more objections please!

INEOS are trying to be very clever by filing a new Planning Application with Derbyshire County Council for exploratory drilling at Bramleymoor Lane. Because it is going to be treated effectively as a new application, new objections to this must be sent to Derbyshire County Council, ideally by Thursday 1st February.
