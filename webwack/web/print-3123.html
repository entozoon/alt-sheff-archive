Free film: Blood of the Amazon

Date, Time & Location Wed 24 Apr 2013, 7pm, Red Deer pub, 18 Pitt St. S1 4DD
Details The Green Film Festival 2013 runs on 4 Wednesday evenings from April into May. Sheffield Friends of the Earth invite you to FREE films with after-film discussions. Today: Blood of the Amazon covers the story of one woman who travels down the Amazon researching the effects of the oil industry on the environment and indigenous people who live there. The Red Deer is on Pitt Street, a small road off Mappin Street, just off West Street). Map at www.red-deer-sheffield.co.uk/location.htm The Green Film Festival 2013
