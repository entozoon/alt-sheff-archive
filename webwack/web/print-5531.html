NO2ID meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 30 Sep 2009, 7.30pm at The Harlequin pub, 108 Nursery St. Sheffield S3 8GG (close to city centre)
Details NO2ID campaigns on the threat to liberty and privacy posed by the rapid growth of the database state, of which "ID cards" are the most visible part. The Sheffield group meets monthly. Everybody is welcome. The meeting room is upstairs, congregate in the bar before the meeting or ask the bar staff for directions. Website: www.no2id.net Sheffield email: sheffield@no2id.net
