Performance: &#39;Libres&#39; - fundraiser for the Sheffield Anarchist Bookfair

Date, Time & Location Thu 21 Mar 2012, 7.30pm Harland Cafe, 72 John St. S2 4QU
Details The 2nd fundraiser for this year‘s Sheffield Anarchist Bookfair, which will be held on Saturday 11th and Sunday 12th May 2013. “Libres” is a performance event by a London-based activist coming up specially to support the bookfair so it would be great if to give her a warm and enthusiastic audience for what promises to be an inspiring night. Please bring your mates! In 1936, Spain witnessed a short-lived but inspiring transformation of society when the anarchist unions of the CNT seized control of the factories and land and experimented with new ways of organising production and distribution of social goods, co-operation and challenging poverty and inequality. Drawing inspiration from the amazing times of the Spanish Social Revolution of 1936, sharing the beauty and relevance of those events and making links with what’s currently happening in Spain, “Libres” is a historical presentation which includes folk songs written and sung in those days, as well as those of the artist. Entry £3/£5, tickets available in advance from Harland Cafe or can be purchased on the door. www.harlandcafe.co.uk/2013/02/libres
