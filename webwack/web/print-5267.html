Women in Black vigil

Date, Time & Location Sat 6 Mar 2010,1-2.30pm at the War Memorial (in front of John Lewis store), Barker's Pool, Sheffield S1
Details Women in Black vigil for International Women's Day calling on the Minister for Women and the Foreign Secretary to support the establishment of a powerful and effective United Nations Agency for Women. All women welcome to join in for all or part of this vigil. There will also be leaflets to distribute celebrating the achievements of women worldwide as well as letters and a petition to sign. Women in Black website Email: sheffieldwomeninblack@hotmail.co.uk
