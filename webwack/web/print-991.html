Basic Income: Money for Nothing?

Date, Time & Location Wed 23 Nov 2016, 7pm, DINA, 32 Cambridge St. S1 4HP
Details Is a no-strings ‘basic income’ a good idea? Would it re-balance our economy and create a generation of entrepreneurs, or would we choose not to work and become dependent on handouts? This open discussion brings together a panel of experts, including from Basic Income UK and the Green Party, to explore this groundbreaking idea. Audience members encouraged to participate in the discussion. Book tickets (free) here Part of Festival of Debate 2016, hosted by not-for-profit social enterprise Opus Independents (which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine).
