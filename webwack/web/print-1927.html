Benefit evening for International Women’s Day

Date, Time & Location Sat 7 Mar 2015, 7.30-11.30pm, St Andrews United Reform Church Hall, Upper Hanover St. S3 7RQ
Details An evening benefit full of fun to celebrate International Women’s Day and help One Billion Rising, the global movement to end violence against women. Come and see Bella Shimmy Bellydance Hafla performances, join-in dances and disco, souk, raffle and more. Bring-and-share buffet, bring your own beverages. All genders and ages welcome. Tickets: £7 adult, £6.50 performers / concession, £3 for 14 years and under. All proceeds will go to One Billion Rising and local organisations working to address violence against women and girls. Contact for details: bellashimmy@gmail.com tel 07786 868369.
