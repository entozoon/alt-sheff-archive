Free film: End of Suburbia

Date, Time & Location Tue 11 May 2010, 6.30pm
Details Documentary film on peak oil and what this means for everyday lives. Cinema Syndicate is an anarchist film collective, open to all, showing through political and activist film that we live in an unjust world, and it needs to change. Film shows are free, Tuesday nights at Lecture Theatre 2, Arts Tower, University of Sheffield. Full programme and details on Cinema Syndicate Facebook
