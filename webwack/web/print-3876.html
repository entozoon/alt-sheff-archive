Traveller Solidarity Network on tour

Date, Time & Location Mon 19 Mar 2012, 7pm, Black Rose Centre, 268 Verdon St. Burngreave S3 9QS
Details Traveller Solidarity Network (TSN) works with travellers, Roma and Gypsies in fighting discrimination, through action including direct action. There are lots of local TSN groups getting set up all over the country. Throughout the month of March, traveller and solidarity activists will be touring the UK for a series of talks and info-nights, join them as they reach Sheffield. http://travellersolidarity.org
