Latin America Adelante

Sat 1 Dec 2018, 10am, London

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Sheffield Cuba Solidarity Campaign will be involved in this major Latin America conference, and encourages all those interested in Latin America to attend. The Trump Presidency has overseen a worrying rise in US interventions in Latin America with the blockade of Cuba being tightened, sanctions threatened in Nicaragua, and Trump pushing for military intervention in Venezuela. Contributions from over 50 campaigners, politicians, trade unionists and academics from Argentina, Bolivia, Brazil, Colombia, Cuba, Ecuador, Mexico, Nicaragua, Venezuela and the UK. (Speakers have now been announced).Admission £10 waged, £8 unwaged in advance, £12/£10 on the door. Venue: Congress House, Great Russell Street, London WC1B 3LS. For full details and advance registration see the website. Latin America Conference
