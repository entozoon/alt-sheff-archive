Pop Up Art Club Exhibition

Date, Time & Location Wed 20 Feb 2013, 1-4pm, Burngreave Vestry Hall, 2 Burngreave Rd, S3 9DD
Details Over the past six months, Art in the Park has been running a series of free art sessions for the children of Burngreave. Now the children of Burngreave will be holding an exhibition of their art works with the chance to join in with some of their activities and with free food. Facebook event
