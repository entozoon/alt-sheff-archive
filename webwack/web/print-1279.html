Radical Walk in Sheffield City Centre

Date, Time & Location Sun 22 May 2016, meet 12 midday at Sheffield Town Hall
Details A Radical Walk in Sheffield City Centre led by Steve Andrew. A stroll through some landmarks of Sheffield’s radical history. Followed by a drink in a local pub and a quiz. £5 per person. A fundraiser for Cuba Solidarity Campaign to bring the Cuban Five to the UK. For more information: cscsheffield@btinternet.com
