Akala: Hip Hop & Politics

Date, Time & Location Sun 23 Jul 2017, 12.30pm, Pennine Lecture Theatre, Sheffield Hallam University, Howard St. S1 1WB
Details MOBO award-winning hip hop artist, writer, poet and educator Akala returns to Sheffield to tell the history of hip hop. Akala provides a passionate overview of the oral traditions, musical, cultural and political movements that have contributed to the evolution of MCing so far. Followed by an open Q & A (questions and answers session) facilitated by Hassun El Zafar. Event open to everyone, space limited. so please book early to avoid disappointment.
