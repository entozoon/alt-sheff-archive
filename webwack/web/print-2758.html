Jazz gig in aid of ASSIST

Date, Time & Location Fri 15 Nov 2013, 8pm, White Lion pub, Chesterfield Road
Details ASSIST is thrilled to have the Beeline quartet playing mellow jazz classics in aid of ASSIST at the lovely real ale pub, the White Lion. The band includes Yo Tozer-Loft whose beautiful vocals have been heard in several great Sheffield bands. The last event she and her band put on was packed, so don't miss out. Entry free with donations to ASSIST, in aid of its 10th year helping asylum seekers in Sheffield. A flyer can be downloaded from here: www.assistsheffield.org.uk/events/232/jazz-gig-beeline-quartet
