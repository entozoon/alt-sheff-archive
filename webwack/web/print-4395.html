Assist ceilidh

Date, Time & Location Sun 27 Mar 2011, 7.30-10.30pm Broomhall Centre, Broomspring Lane S10 2FD
Details Calling all ceilidh lovers .... The popular series of 'home grown' ceilidhs run by the fantastic Well Dressed Band continues with an event in aid of Assist Sheffield. This inimitable crew will keep you up on your feet with their music all night, led by one of the favourite local callers, John Brown. Food is a bring and share but beer and cider will be on sale. There will also be a raffle in aid of Assist. The last Assist ceilidh was sold out - so make sure you book your tickets now! Tickets £5 in advance or £6 on the door. To book in advance please call 0797 6313642 or email jerry.simon@hotmail.co.uk. For any more information, please go to: www.welldressedband.org.uk www.assistsheffield.org.uk or call 07791 450620
