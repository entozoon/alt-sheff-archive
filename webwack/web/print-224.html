“Solidarity” world premiere (film)

Date, Time & Location Sat 8/Sun 9 Jun 2019, The Light Cinema, The Moor S1 4PD
Details A film about the experiences of trade unionists who have been blacklisted in the UK construction industry and their fight for justice. Part of Sheffield Doc Fest. The film features members of Blacklist Support Group, core participants in Undercover Policing Inquiry, and members of other campaigning groups including: Voice of Domestic Workers, Cleaners and Allied Independent Workers Union, Independent Workers of Great Britain, GMB, RMT, Unite British Airways Mixed Fleet, County Durham Teaching Assistants, BECTU Picturehouse and many individual trade unionists. Saturday screening 11.15am; Sunday 9th June at 11.30am. There will be a Q&A (questions and answers discussion) at both screenings.
