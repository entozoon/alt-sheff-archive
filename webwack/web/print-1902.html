"Don’t let them drown!"

Date, Time & Location Tue 17 Mar 2015, 12.00 outside the Moor Market
Details A march protesting against the terrible conditions and deaths of desperate people crossing the Mediterranean into Europe. Meet at 12 noon outside the Moor Market to proceed to the Town Hall for speeches at 1pm, and then end up at Vulcan House. Anyone is welcome. Organised by South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG). www.symaag.org.uk Twitter: @SYMAAG Tel: Sheff 2729332 extension 332
