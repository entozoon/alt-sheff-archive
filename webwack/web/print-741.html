ROTHERHAM PRIDE

Date, Time & Location Sat 15 Jul 2017, Clifton Park, Rotherham
Details Rotherham Pride is now in the fifth year of promoting and celebrating the LGBT+ community that lives in or visits Rotherham. Live local bands in the bandstand area, refreshment areas, children's entertainment, info and market stalls. A family-friendly, inclusive event. Clifton Park is quite close to Rotherham town centre.
