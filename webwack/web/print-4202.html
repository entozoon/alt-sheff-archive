Sheffield Humanist Society annual general meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 3 Aug 2011, 8pm University Arms, 197 Brook Hill, S3 7HG (Sheffield University tram stop)
Details The SHS AGM is open to everyone (but only members can stand for election to the committee or vote). Discussion on activities over the next year. Your ideas and comments will be very welcome. Among other items for discussion is giving support to the Uganda Humanist Schools Trust. A speaker from it is planned for later this year www.sheffieldhumanists.org.uk
