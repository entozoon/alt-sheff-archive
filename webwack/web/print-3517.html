Sheffield Equality Group meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 3 Oct 2012, 7pm to 9pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Sheffield Equality Group meeting on the gap between high and low income, and planning actions to address it. The group is an autonomous group affiliated to the national Equality Trust. Meetings are on the first Wednesday in every month. All welcome. Their recent submission to Sheffield Fairness Commission is reported on the News tab. www.sheffieldequalitytrust.org.uk
