Brandalism and Subvertising Workshop

Date, Time & Location Tue 13 Feb 2018, 6-7.30pm, Sheffield Students Union, Western Bank S10 2TG.
Details "Subvertising" (the art of subverting advertising) is growing in popularity as a tactic for social movement campaigns. In this workshop, organised by Sheffield People and Planet, there will be an examination of history of subvertising in the UK and beyond - as well as learning practical techniques in how to install your own campaign posters in different types of spaces.
