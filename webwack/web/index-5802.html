Transition Westfield?

Summer 2015

Transition Sheffield announced (28th July) a study group is being discussed, to explore examples of communities building up resilience, rising to the challenge of the world changing at an unprecedented rate.

Westfield (in Sheffield S20) is known to be in a particularly severe struggle to protect local community resources. Apparently an agreement has been reached to secure the future of the Westfield Community Centre, and maybe a multi-stakeholder co-operative will be set up.
