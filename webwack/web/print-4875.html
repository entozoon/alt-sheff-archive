'Tea for tribes'

Date, Time & Location Sun 1 Aug 2010, 2pm-5pm
Details Have tea, dress up and support tribal people endangered by western exploitative invasion in a fundraiser for Survival International. Dress to express your tribal affiliations, who you are beyond the corporate machine. Let your indigenous roots show! This is a gathering of the tribes to support those whose survival is threatened. Bring food, drink, local produce. Bring donations. Bring yourself. If you're unable to attend but wanting to support, visit Survival International to donate, or call to add to group donation on the venue/details number: 07768 757108.
