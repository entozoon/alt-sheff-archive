Hackenthorpe Gala

Date, Time & Location Sat 21 Aug 2010, 1pm, old Miners Welfare Field on Church Lane / Beighton Rd
Details Hackenthorpe Gala is being revived after a 33 year absence, to bring all the community together for a day of fun. Includes funfair, dance display, circus skills, martial arts display, brass band and more. Organised by the events committee of the Tenants and Community Association.
