Hagglers Corner charity cycle event

Date, Time & Location Sun 8 Sep 2013, 2-9 pm, Hagglers Corner, 586 Queens Rd. S2 4DU
Details Hagglers Corner hosts a load of fun cycling activities. Plans include "Race the Best - 70s turbo trainer challenge" (how fast can YOU go?), smoothie bike, best dressed 70s cyclist fancy dress competition, classic cycling film clips, bike advice and maintenance, evening BBQ, bar and 70s film screening. Proceeds to local charity SAFE@LAST (www.safeatlast.org.uk), which helps children at risk through running away. www.hagglerscorner.co.uk/
