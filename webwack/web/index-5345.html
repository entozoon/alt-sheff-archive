Fundraising meal for Haiti

Fri 5 Feb 2010, 7.30pm at Ethio-Cubano Restaurant, 15-23 Arundel Gate, Sheffield City Centre, Sheffield, S1 2PN

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Cost: £13. £3 from each entrance price and all other proceeds and donations will be sent to help supply Cuban medical teams working in Haiti. "We send doctors, not soldiers" (Fidel Castro on Haiti)A Cuban bank account has now been opened to help sustain their medical teams working in Haiti. Donations via Cuba Solidarity Campaign, which will gather all donations and forward them in lump sums to Cuba to avoid multiple transfer fees. All donations will be acknowledged. You can donate by Credit Card at 0208 800 0155 or by sending a cheque (payable to 'CSC', with 'Cuban doctors in Haiti' on the back) to CSC c/o UNITE – Woodberry, 218 Green Lanes, London N4 2HB.
