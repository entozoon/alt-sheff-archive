LOWEDGES FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sun 10 Aug 2014, 12-5pm, Greenhill Park, Gresley Road, Lowedges S8 7HN
Details The popular Lowedges Festival returns to Greenhill Park. An annual community festival which promises to be big. Along with music, dance, stalls and food are things like a dog show, dog agility trials, dog flyball, medieval re-enactment show, living history village, plus funfair, farmers market, crafts, mini steam, car boot and much more. A great free show. Details on the Sheffield Help Yourself website
