Film screening: "Trashing Transphobia"

Date, Time & Location Fri 20 Nov 2015, 6.30pm Odeon Cinema, Sheffield S1
Details Part of the many activities to mark Transgender Day of Remembrance, 20th November. "Trashing Transphobia" is the latest film by E.D.E.N. Films, which advocates for LGBT+ equality worldwide, by producing films with the aim of promoting equality and raising awareness of issues concerning the lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgender community. This new film aims to tackle the discrimination that the transgender community often faces. It follows Laura Holt, speaking with members of the transgender community, alongside charitable organisations. Full programme of the events all day online/to download: E.D.E.N. Film
