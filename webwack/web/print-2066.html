Palestine Pantomime of Peace workshop

Date, Time & Location Mon 5 Jan 2015
Details Sheffield Palestinian Solidarity Campaign is producing a Pantomime of Peace: after two planning sessions they now have opportunities for people to get involved. Rehearsals start on the 31st, but today is a prop making workshop; if you like to paint or sew, they could use your skill and enthusiasm. Email for further details: j.fortune@shu.ac.uk.
