Housing for All? Prospects for a National Movement

Date, Time & Location Wed 31 May 2017, 6.30-8pm, Brood Cafe Bar, Roco, 338-346 Glossop Rd. S10 2HW
Details A private rented sector with high rents and poor conditions, the demolition of public housing in cities like London and a lack of imagination around resolving economic and housing conditions in the North tells us something needs to be done about UK housing. This event is an opportunity to hear ideas about how public housing might offer a way forward. In association with the Property and Social Justice Network & Inclusive Societies. A Festival of Debate event, hosted by not-for-profit social enterprise Opus Independents (which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine).
