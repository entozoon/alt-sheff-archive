Amnesty International events meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 20 Jan 2016, 6pm upstairs at Blue Moon Café, 2 St James St. S1 2EW
Details Amnesty International is a worldwide organisation of nearly 3 million people all working to protect Human Rights. Actions on human rights abuses, contacts, successes and updates from various campaigns. This is an events planning meeting. Bring any ideas you have for events to discuss and plan a timetable for the year ahead. If you have an idea for an event and cannot make the meeting please email details beforehand to amnestysheffieldchair@gmail.com. If you have never been to an event and would like to find out more, just send an email or Facebook message. Sheffield Amnesty International Facebook
