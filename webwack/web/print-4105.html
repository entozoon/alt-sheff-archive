Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change (SCACC) meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 18 Oct 2011, 7.30-9.30, Highfield Library, London Rd. S2 4NF
Details Meeting open to newcomers, all welcome. On the agenda this time is feedback from meetings with Sheffield City Council, and the final push for signatures on Sheffield Climate Alliance petition for 10,000 climate jobs for Sheffield. Also plans for future events/campaigns including campaigning in the run-up to the December climate talks in South Africa; in Sheffield there will be a meeting on Tues 15th Nov with speakers from South Africa and from the Campaign against Climate Change TU group to make the link between Climate Jobs and Climate Justice globally ahead of the Durban talks.
