Falafel and Film Fundraiser

Sat 26 Jun 2010, 6.30pm Pitsmoor Methodist Church

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Food and film showing of 'Hebron Home Eviction', filmed by Deacon Dave showing the Israeli army evicting a family from the Old City of Hebron. The family eventually break open their house and move back in. Deacon Dave is planning to go back to Hebron, Palestine as a peacemaker and to film interviews. He has DVD films available from previous visits. If you would like a film or to make a donation please contact Dave on 272 9695.For good daily news reports on Palestine see: www.maannews.net/engFor reports and interviews from Palestinian women: www.youtube.com/user/SleeplessinGazaFor the latest from the Free Gaza Flotilla: www.freegaza.orgFor reports from the International Solidarity Movement see: http://palsolidarity.orgFor photos and more info see webpage: www.deacondave.burngreave.netfor videos see: DeaconDave777 on YouTube
