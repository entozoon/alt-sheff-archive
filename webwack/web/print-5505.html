"Food, Fashion, Famine and Fear" Workers Educational Association course

Date, Time & Location Starts Wed 7 Oct 2009, 10am-1pm at Broomhall Community Centre, Broomspring Lane, Sheffield S10 2FB
Details This 4 session course is ideal for those who want to begin to understand today’s world and make the changes, says tutor Graham Birkin. "Globalisation? What’s New?" Check the shelves in the supermarket, the migrant labour in the Lincolnshire fields, the labels on your T-shirts and find out that what you eat, what you wear and how you feel are all part of globalisation. The course is FREE. For details graham@birkin194.fsnet.co.uk or tel. Sheff 2423609.
