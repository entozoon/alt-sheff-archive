Workshop/Consultation: "Refreshing the Sheffield Food Strategy"

Date, Time & Location Wed 6 Nov, morning, at City Hall.
Details There have been many changes in Sheffield since Sheffield produced its first Food Plan in 2011. Some changes have been positive, for example the continuing success of the Food Festival, action to improve School Food and the soon to be opened new Market on the Moor. Unfortunately there have also been cuts in welfare and the appearance of Food Banks in the city. The time is right for a refresh of the Food Plan - (now draft Food Strategy) for Sheffield. Please contact Jill Lancaster Jill.Lancaster@sheffield.gcsx.gov.uk tel Sheff 2057469 for further information about this event or if you wish to be part of a virtual network of people who have an interest in this area.
