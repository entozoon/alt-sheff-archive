Palestine film: "Off Frame aka Revolution until Victory"

Date, Time & Location Tue 11 Jul 2017, 6.15pm, Showroom Cinema, Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details A film about the Palestinian people’s struggle to represent themselves on film, along with work by international filmmakers from Syria, Italy, UK, Lebanon, France, Germany and Argentina. It presents a historical narrative of struggle recorded on camera, but without forgetting those ’off frame’. Followed by Q&A (question and answer session) with Director Mohanad Yaqubi. Screening supported by Sheffield Hallam University, Arts and Humanities Research Council, Sheffield Palestine Cultural Exchange, Cinema Palestino Manchester. Showroom Cinema
