Debate: Gender and Poverty

Date, Time & Location Thu 18 May 2017, 7-9pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St S1 2EW
Details Panel discussion with Sophie Walker, leader of the Women’s Equality Party, and guest speakers, looking at women’s poverty in Sheffield and the UK. Is there gender bias in the way traditional economic thinking addresses poverty? Will business development plans be ‘more of the same’ or a lasting solution to poverty? A Festival of Debate event, hosted by not-for-profit social enterprise Opus Independents (which also publishes the Alt-Sheff "Localcheck" column in "Now Then" magazine).
