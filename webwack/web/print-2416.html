Palestinian Knesset member talks about what Nakba day means

Date, Time & Location Thu 15 May 2014, Sheffield University Council Chamber, Firth Court
Details Dr Jamal Zahalka is a Palestinian member of the Knesset (Israeli parliament) and chair of the Balad Party. Sheffield University Palestine Society and Sheffield Palestine Solidarity Campaign are putting on this joint event where Dr Zahalka will talk about what the Nakba means to Palestinian people.
