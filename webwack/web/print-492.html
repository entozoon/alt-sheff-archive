Lantern Procession

Date, Time & Location Sat 13 Oct 2018, 5.45pm, from Cooks Wood Road, Burngreave S5 8XB
Details The Friends of Parkwood Springs present this seasonal community event, it includes food stalls and drinks available. The procession with big illuminated lanterns sets off from the car park on Cooks Wood Road. Free to attend. Bring a torch and warm clothes - and bring a friend! There are lantern-making workshops preceding this up to 11th October (details online here).
