A Basic Income for refugees

Wed 1 Apr 2020, 6.30pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

SORRY THIS EVENT IS POSTPONEDOver the last few years, the media's increasingly hostile rhetoric towards migrants and refugees has led to the demonisation and 'othering' of people born outside the UK. Could a UBI (Universal Basic Income) form part of the solution, giving refugees a real stake in the UK as well as an economic platform from which to build a new life?Join UBI Lab Sheffield's Tchiyiwe T Chihana (Migration Matters Festival, African Voices Platform) for an informal talk followed by a discussion. (Followed by ongoing project updates and planning from 7.30pm)All welcome, free. UBI Lab Sheffield
