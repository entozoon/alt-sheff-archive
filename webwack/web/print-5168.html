Asda Protest... Handsworth

Date, Time & Location Sat 10 Apr 2010, 10am
Details East End Quality of Life Initiative and the Handsworth Community Forum are mounting a Community Protest against the planned expansion of the Handsworth-based Asda Superstore, which includes 80 extra car park spaces. Say no to extra traffic, worse air quality, more CO2 and the destruction of local shops - join the protest at the bus stop outside Asda. More info email neil@sheffieldct.co.uk
