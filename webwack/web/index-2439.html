Chesterfield May Day 2014

Mon 5 May 2014, Chesterfield (near Sheffield)

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

The 37th annual Chesterfield May Day is held on the first Monday after the beginning of May, one of the biggest celebrations of working class solidarity in the country. 10.30am Assembly outside Chesterfield Town Hall on Rose Hill.11.00am The march moves off through the town arriving at New Square for the Rally. The afternoon is full of activities and entertainment celebrating international workers day. Music, dancing and stalls of community and campaigning organisations.Speakers: Toby Perkins, MPMatt Wrack, FBU unionClara Osagiede, RMT unionAngela Sabino, Democratic Women's Movement of PortugalMusic:Bleeding HeartsDucieMaiaChesterfield May Day
