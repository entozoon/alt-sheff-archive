Walking Politics for the Over Fifties

Date, Time & Location Fri 1 Dec 2017, meet 10.30am Hunters Bar entrance to Endcliffe Park, S11
Details A walking group for socialists, feminists, environmentalists and other political progressive people over fifty. Come along for a walk to Forge Dam and back, chatting with like-minded people. The plan is to meet on the 1st and 3rd Friday each month. To confirm, email sheffieldwalkingpoli­tics@gmail.com or just turn up. Please wear suitable footwear for a walk beside the river.
