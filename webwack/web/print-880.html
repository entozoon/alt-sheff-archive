Health and Wellbeing AGMs of Recovery Insulation and Solar-Active

Date, Time & Location Thu 27 Apr 2017, 6.30-8.30pm, Sheffield Hallam University, Howard 5540 , Howard St. S1 1WB
Details An evening with AGMs of two longstanding and innovative Sheffield sustainable energy organisations, (Recovery Insulation and Solar-Active), and discussions on the theme of "Health and Wellbeing in our Homes and Neighbouroods". Presentations, panel discussion and Q&A (questions and answers). a. LEDs with blue-rich spectra - street light effects on human and wildlife circadian rhythms, road safety and the view of our night sky. - i. On flowering plants in front gardens - ii. On trees in streets - iii. Urban tree care and planting b. Urban food production and food waste c. Air quality in the home and on our streets Refreshments and raffle.
