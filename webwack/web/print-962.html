Cuba Solidarity Campaign

Date, Time & Location Mon 5 Dec 2016, 8pm Red Deer, Pitt St. S1 4DD
Details A talk on Cuba: The right wing revolt in Latin America, with speaker Francisco Dominguez. All welcome to discuss the political crises of the left in Latin America and the impact on Cuba. Francisco Dominguez is Head of Latin American Studies at Middlesex University, National Secretary of the Venezuela Solidarity Campaign, and a member of the National Executive of the Cuba Solidarity Campaign. Entrance is free. A collection will be taken at the end of the meeting. Sheffield Cuba Solidarity Campaign on Facebook
