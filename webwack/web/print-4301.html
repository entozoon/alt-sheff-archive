South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group: Annual General Meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 17 May 2011, 7pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St James St. S1 2EW
Details SYMAAG's AGM will include a review of last year's work and a look forward to the next. The guest speaker is Keith Best of the Medical Foundation for the Care of Victims of Torture who will be leading a discussion on whether it is possible to eradicate torture given the current troubled state of the world. All those who support the aim of fighting for asylum and migrant rights are welcome to come to the meeting. For more information contact SYMAAG at dignitynotdetention@yahoo.co.uk
