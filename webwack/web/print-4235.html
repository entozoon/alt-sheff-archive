After The Apocalypse, with Q & A

Date, Time & Location Tue 5 Jul 2011, 6.30pm, Showroom Cinema, Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details 456 nuclear experiments were conducted in Kazakhstan during the Cold War. Today the local population struggle to cope with the resulting birth defects. UK director Anthony Butts follows a young pregnant woman battling to keep her baby. This is the untold story of a tragedy, leaving viewers disquieted about what should be done. The screening will be followed by a question and answer session with director Antony Butts. www.showroomworkstation.org.uk/afterapocalypse sheffield.indymedia.org.uk/2011/06/481255.html
