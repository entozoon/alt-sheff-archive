Global Justice Sheffield monthly meeting

Date, Time & Location Thu 12 Sep 2019, 7pm, Quaker Meeting House, 10 St. James St. S1 2EW.
Details Monthly meeting of Global Justice, which aims to change the actions, policies and institutions of governments, corporations and wealthy minorities which are preventing an end to poverty and injustice. The meeting is for discussing and planning upcoming campaigns and activities. Newcomers always welcome. Global Justice Sheffield
