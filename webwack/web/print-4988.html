Skeptics in the Pub get-together

Date, Time & Location Mon 14 Jun 2010, 7.30pm Lescar Hotel, 303 Sharrow Vale Rd, S11 8ZF
Details Skeptics in the Pub is about getting people together to have a relaxed and enjoyable evening while listening to talks on a wide range of topics. Talks are typically about a common belief that can be demonstrated to be false or cannot be justified by the available evidence. This month The Psychology of Anomalous Experiences, the study of extraordinary phenomena of behaviour and experience in an attempt to provide non-paranormal explanations in terms of known psychological and physical factors.
