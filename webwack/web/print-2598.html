Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 18 Feb 2014, 7-9pm Quaker Meeting House, St James St. S1 2EW
Details The next meeting of Sheffield Campaign against Climate Change, hosting the wider Sheffield Climate Alliance. Everyone is welcome to attend the meeting. Some people will be meeting at the Blue Moon cafe to eat beforehand from 6pm, anyone can also join them there for an informal get-together. The meeting will discuss: - campaigning with Trade unions on climate jobs and fracking - joint campaigning with churches, universities and pension funds on fossil fuel divestment - involvement in Sheffield Green Commission up till May - plans for recruitment of a climate campaign worker
