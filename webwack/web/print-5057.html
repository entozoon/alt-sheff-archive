Sheffield Amnesty annual street collection

Date, Time & Location Sat 15 May 2010 11am - 4pm
Details Annual Ecclesall Road street collection. Amnesty International's main fundraiser which usually gets a good team of tin shakers out. Please consider providing your local group with some practical support by helping out for an hour or two on the day. Please email toby.mallinson@googlemail.com if you can help giving the time you can help (between 11 and 4) and your phone/mobile number so you can be contacted in the event of cancellation.
