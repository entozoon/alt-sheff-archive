WOODSEATS FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Fri 14-Sun 16 Jul 2017, various venues
Details An annual festival held in various venues around Woodseats, all run by enthusiastic volunteers from the local community. Parade, arts and crafts, music, performance, sport and leisure activities, even a car boot sale. This year the theme is "The Garden of Woodseats". Full programme and details on the website.
