Caroline Lucas MP: "Why Sheffield needs more elected Greens"

Fri 9 Feb 2018, 7pm, Central United Reformed Church, 60 Norfolk Row S1 2JB

Share on
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Co-Leader of the Green Party, Caroline Lucas MP visits Sheffield, to speak at this public meeting on "A fairer, greener future - why Sheffield needs more elected Greens". There will be plenty of time for questions and contributions from the floor. Refreshments served from 6.45pm, meeting at 7pm. Entrance free but there will be a collection for room hire. Please reserve a free ticket as the hall capacity is limited to 120 people. All welcome.Eventbrite tickets (free)
