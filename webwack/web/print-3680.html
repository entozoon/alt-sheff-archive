HEELEY FESTIVAL

Date, Time & Location Sat 23 Jun 2012
Details The 2012 Heeley Festival returns with loads of stalls on the Heeley Millennium Park and Heeley City Farm sites, and also a car boot sale on the Anns Grove School car park. The event is organised by the Heeley Events & Activities Team (HEAT) which is made up of local residents and workers, they welcome new members on board to help with the festival, from helping with stall bookings and entertainment through to publicity and help on the day to set up and close down.
