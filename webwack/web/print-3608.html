DONCASTER PRIDE

Date, Time & Location Sun 5 Aug 2012, at Doncaster
Details Doncaster Pride starts with the "Walk of Unity", this year as visible as possible and even bigger and better than last year. It will again be walking through the town centre starting and finishing in the Market Place. Come and get dressed up in your best carnival attire and show Doncaster what you are made of. Gather at the Market Place outside Crystals, from 11.30am, walking starts at 12.30pm. This year the walk is approximately 3 miles and will take around 30/40 minutes from start to finish. The Pride festival itself follows at the Keepmoat Stadium, Stadium Way DN4 5JW, 2pm to 8pm (gates open from 1pm) www.doncasterpride.co.uk
