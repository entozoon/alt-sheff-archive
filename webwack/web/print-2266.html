"Cut off" - an exhibition of photos

Date, Time & Location All of Aug 2014, Weds-Sat 11am-4pm, Bank St. Arts
Details "Cut off" is an exhibition of photos taken by people who have been refused asylum, exploring their circumstances, experiences, hopes and frustrations. It forms part of a general exhibition on the theme "Cut", at Bank Street Arts, 32-40 Bank Street, Sheffield, S1 2DS. www.bankstreetarts.com
