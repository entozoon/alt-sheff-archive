CycleSheffield AGM

Details Annual General Meeting of CycleSheffield, the group for all kinds of cyclists. It organises events, information and rides, and campaigns for cycling to be taken seriously as a means of transport, with the facilities it needs. www.cyclesheffield.org.uk
