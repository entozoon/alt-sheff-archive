Film: Diamonds in Brown Paper

Date, Time & Location Wed 16 Nov 2011, 4.30pm, Showroom Cinema, Paternoster Row S1 2BX
Details Gill Booth was a founding member of Sheffield Film Co-op, a group of 1970s feminists trying to reach women through film. This documentary is a lively, dramatic insight into the working lives of a group of 'buffers'; women metal-polishers in the Sheffield cutlery industry. Shown as a tribute to Gill Booth (1942-2010). Followed by question and answer discussion with Co-op members. www.showroomworkstation.org.uk/diamondsinbrownpaper
