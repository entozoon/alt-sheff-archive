Say it with flowers - Save our NHS

Date, Time & Location Fri 18 May 2012, 1pm, Barkers Pool, city centre S1 2HB
Details From Friday 18th May, throughout 2012 and across the UK, at every war memorial flowers will be laid. This is in remembrance of the PROMISE of a national health service and welfare state, made to the soldiers and the British people in return for the immense struggle and sacrifices made to defend humanity during the war. www.sheffieldsaveournhs.co.uk
