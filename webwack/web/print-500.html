How to Give Up Plastic

Date, Time & Location Mon 8 Oct 2018, 6pm, The Foundry, University of Sheffield Students Union, Western Bank S10 2TG
Details Author Will McCallum, Head of Oceans at Greenpeace and spokesperson for their anti-plastic campaign, talks about one of the serious environmental/health problems for our planet. There are practical changes you can make to reduce plastics in your everyday life, make a difference and help change the world. Part of the 2018 Off the Shelf festival of words.
