Sheffield Initiative for Fairtrade (SHIFT) FT14 launch event

Date, Time & Location 22 Feb-1 Mar 2010. Launch Mon 22 Feb, 10.30am, Winter Gardens, Sheffield city centre
Details Fun and serious change with Fairtrade in Sheffield. Expect to find vicars serving Fairtrade drinks to high profile council reps, children from Fairtrade schools and Fairtrade drinks at discount prices. Display also on the landing in the Central Library for the fortnight. Sheffield Initiative for Fairtrade (SHIFT) website
