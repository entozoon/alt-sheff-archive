Fair Justice for Bavwidi Mpanzu ('Djoly') campaign meeting

Date, Time & Location Wed 17 Mar 2010, 6.30pm at Scotia Works, Leadmill Road S1 4SE
Details On January 28th Bavwidi Mpanzu (or 'Djoly' as his friends know him) was arrested for working illegally. He was sent to Doncaster prison where he remains, waiting for sentencing at Sheffield Crown Court in March or April. A campaign has been launched to defend Djoly, a well-known community activist from the Democratic Republic of Congo.
