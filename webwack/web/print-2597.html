"Politicians, Racism and the Media: A Case to Answer"

Date, Time & Location Tue 18 Feb 2014, 7-9pm The Workstation, 15 Paternoster Row, S1 2BX
Details A public meeting for South Yorkshire people to demand an end to politicians creating a climate of racism and xenophobia to win votes in the European election 2014 and the Westminster election 2015. Speakers: Emma Briant, Lecturer in Journalism Studies, University of Sheffield. Joint author of the recent 'Bad News for Refugees'. Jon Burnett, Assistant Editor Institute for Race Relations News, carries out research into racial violence and domestic race policy in the UK. John Grayson, vice chair of SYMAAG, author of 'The Shameful 'Go Home' Campaign' and many other anti-racist articles. The meeting is organised by SYMAAG. www.symaag.org.uk Twitter: @SYMAAG Tel: Sheff 2729332 extension 332
