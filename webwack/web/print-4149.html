Sheffield Social Centre Collective meeting

Date, Time & Location Fri 23 Sep 2011, 7.30pm The Rutland Arms, 86 Brown St. S1 2BS
Details Sheffield Social Centre is a collective for libertarian DIY space in the city, a project open to all who share its core principles of non-hierarchy, anti-capitalism and equality/non-discrimination. This meeting will be downstairs and will focus on the forthcoming "Free School" (15th October) http://sheffieldsocialcentre.org.uk
