South Yorkshire Migration and Asylum Action Group (SYMAAG) meeting

Date, Time & Location Tue 18 Oct 2011, 6.30pm Scotia Works, Leadmill Road S1 4SE
Details Next SYMAAG meeting, with a speaker from Sheffield´s Yemeni community to discuss the situation in the country and Yemeni migration to Sheffield. SYMAAG unites refugee, asylum and migrant organisations with campaign groups and supporting individuals. It campaigns both for the rights of those seeking asylum and of those coming to work in this country. If you want to come to any SYMAAG meeting but find it hard to afford transport don´t be put off! Please bring your ticket or a receipt and SYMAAG can pay you back at the meeting. Or check in advance. Contact SYMAAG at dignitynotdetention@yahoo.co.uk or via Sheff 2412780
