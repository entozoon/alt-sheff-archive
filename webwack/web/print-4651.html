Greenpeace Northern Key Activists' Weekend

Date, Time & Location Fri eve Nov 19 to noon Sun 21 Nov 2010 at Kettlewell, North Yorkshire BD23 5QU
Details A weekend in the Yorkshire Dales with Greenpeace. There will be good food, good company, fun activities and some serious discussions, with an international guest and a look behind the scenes at Greenpeace. This event is for Network Coordinators, Trainers, Network Activists, and those involved in actions. Because of the location, transport and limited numbers, priority given to those booking for the whole weekend. Cost free, including accommodation, but you will need to pay for your own transport. Please bring some wine, beer or whatever to share on the Friday night. To book email greenpeacenotts @ googlemail.com or phone 07946 331065. Kettlewell YHA website for accommodation information The nearest rail station to Kettlewell is Skipton, and there is also one at Ilkley. Dalesbus website for full bus timetables. The 72 bus runs from Skipton (rail station or bus station) to Kettlewell via Grassington, 74 bus runs from Ikley to Grassington. For meetings and current activities see the Active Supporters newsletter, at Greenpeace South Yorkshire website
