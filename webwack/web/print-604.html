Soviet Healthcare via Targets: Are governments bringing it into the NHS?

Date, Time & Location Wed 25 Apr 2018, 2-4.30pm and 7-9.30pm, ROCO Creative Co-op, 338-346 Glossop Road, Sheffield, S10 2HW
Details Dr Peter Campbell will present two sessions exploring the threat of target-driven management. Originally a GP, Dr Campbell now helps create and lead health projects in ex-Soviet countries, and has been featured on platforms such as TEDx. He is a principle lecturer at Universities in Heidelberg and Berlin. Each session includes breakout and strategy groups, protest songs on NHS themes, and an exhibition on the ubiquitousness of PFI.
