Cycling Film Night

Date, Time & Location Mon 22 Feb 2010, 8-10.30pm at Showroom Cinema, Paternoster Row, Sheffield S1 2BX
Details Cycling films at the Showroom. The rarely screened, 1979, Oscar-winning 'Breaking Away' a coming-of-age and cycling full feature movie, rated in the top 10 sporting movies of all time. Also short films including Japanese film 'Fixed in the City'. Discounts for CTC and CycleSheffield members. CycleSheffield website Showroom Cinema website
