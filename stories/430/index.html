Sheffield plans for growth to 2034

Deadline Friday 15 January 2016

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

The city council needs comments on "The Sheffield Plan: Our City, Our Future - Citywide Options for Growth to 2034". This consultation is open to everyone to comment on the future of Sheffield. The result will be the key statutory development plan for the city, to be adopted in 2018, covering housing, the economy, community and leisure facilities, transport infrastructure, etc.

This is a statutory public consultation over the busy 6 week period from Wednesday 11 November to Wednesday 23 December. Comments will still be accepted until midnight on Friday 15 January 2016, which suggests they realise that Christmas is not the best time for a meaningful consultation.
