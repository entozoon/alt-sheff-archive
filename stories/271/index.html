Arms trade map reveals Sheffield links

September 2012

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

A new map by Campaign Against Arms Trade (CAAT) shows companies manufacturing for the arms industry. A quick search for the Sheffield area reveals:

  • Trelleborg Sealing Solutions UK, Bradmarsh Business Park, Rotherham S60 1BX
    Source: www.bdec-online.com/bd-cmpy/bd-ct.cfm
  • Magnomatics Ltd. Park House, Bernard Rd. Sheffield S2 5BQ
    Source: www.magnomatics.com/contacts/Directions.aspx
  • MTL Group Ltd. Grange Lane, Brinsworth, Rotherham S60 5AE
    Source: www.mtlgrp.com/contact.aspx
  • Synectics Surveillance Technology, 3-4 Broadfield Close, Sheffield S8 0XN
    Source: www.synx.com
  • The Panic Room Company, Unit 3, Meadowhall Riverside, Meadowhall Rd. S9 1BW
    Source: www.thepanicroomcompany.com/?page_id=78
    Their website does not seem to work at the moment but there is a nice quote from them on the website of DSEI arms dealers fair:
    www.dsei.co.uk/page.cfm/link=79
CAAT has a long established history of opposition to arms dealing in the guise of "defence". Many companies supply parts for weapons without staff realising this, because manufacturing is widely distributed.

There may well be other local companies involved; try searching "Sheffield" on BDEC-Online (British Defence Equipment Catalogue)

www.caat.org.uk/arms-trade-map
