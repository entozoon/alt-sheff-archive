Interesting enviro-project for volunteers

News March 2010

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

The Environmental Law Foundation are looking for volunteers on their Sustainable Communities project to assist local communities to use the law to address local environmental concerns. For example, the effect of increased rainfall from climate change.

Can you offer 3 days a month over at least 6 months? Training and support provided, see for details: www.elflaw.org/site/index.php?id=13
