Call for 10000 Sheffield climate jobs

Deadline 28th November/7 December 2011

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Climate change campaigners are pushing for Sheffield to be a low carbon enterprise zone. This could help to make 10,000 climate jobs. They will present this call at a council meeting on 7 December. If their petition gets 5,000 signatures it will force a 40 minute debate in the council otherwise it will be 3 minutes. Please help to keep up the pressure and to meet the target. The website link below also has paper petition forms, for return by 28th November.

Pass this petition on to your friends and workplace colleagues or print out the attached paper version for actual signing if you prefer. Sheffield Climate Alliance is broad grouping of local organisations and individuals, it is not affiliated to any political party

To read and sign the petition click: www.sheffieldclimatealliance.net
