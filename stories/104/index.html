Support Palestine: buy olive oil direct

April 2010

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

At long last Palestinian Zaytoun Olive Oil returns to Sheffield. It is now more important than ever to show solidarity with Palestinian farmers. They need our support, after a poor harvest which has seen prices rise sharply, the bombing of Gaza, and the military procedures in the West Bank that may see many Palestinians forcibly expelled or imprisoned.

Buying Palestinian produce of any sort is also a way of supporting the Boycott Divestment and Sanctions (BDS) campaign. In Sheffield there is a very active BDS group; some of their exploits can be seen on YouTube at www.youtube.com/user/sheffieldpsc. To find out more about the BDS movement nationally, see bigcampaign.org and for a more global view bdsmovement.net.

There are two sizes of oil now arrived: 500ml now £6.50 and 750ml, £10. You can get in touch, or order oil, by emailing: Sheffield PSC Boycott campaign boycottisraeligoods at live.co.uk
