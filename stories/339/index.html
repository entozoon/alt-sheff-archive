Freecycle & Freegle scams warning

January 2014

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Freecycle and Freegle are free-to-join networks that help people to give away things they would otherwise put into waste disposal. So they do something for recycling and re-use, as well as helping people to build community spirit, save money and de-clutter. Sadly the nasty scammers are always looking for new targets, and administrators of Freecycle and Freegle projects have to spend a lot of time trying to keep them safe from scams.

Recent warnings are that members should be aware of offers of high-value items like laptops or plasma screen TVs, only to be told that the owner has moved, and a courier fee is requested ... and then the item never arrives. If you experience this, or you are ever unsure about an offer that is too good to be true, just get in touch with the group admin (moderators), do not enter into discussions or arrangements with the "offering" person. Help to keep yourself and everyone safe.
