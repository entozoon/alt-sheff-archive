Return of the Scrapstore

February 2014

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Following the closure of the one remaining children's scrapstore in the region, Shrodoba, good news that Sheffield Out of School Network is running a new one. Open 3 days a week, opening times are staggered to suit people with different working hours. Scrapstores take donations of materials and resources from businesses or individuals, suitable for children's play and creative activities, redistributing them in exchange for a small donation. They are always looking for anything unusual and interesting as well as items such as suitcases, old phones, keyboards, dressing up clothes, hats, bags etc, soft toys, ribbon, wool. If you're having a clear out and have any of these items available please give them a call.

Why not go along and say hello, have a look around and see what's on offer?

Unit 9 Edmund Road Business Centre, 135 Edmund Road, S2 4ED
tel. 2494941 or email ykoszalinski@osn.org.uk
