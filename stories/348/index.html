Allotment seeks allotment-plotter

Feb/March 2014

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Six people sharing two plots at Meersbrook Allotments are looking to find two new members, due to someone leaving, the cost of plots, and the work involved. The group is mixed (male and female) average age around 45. Each has their own crops to look after in a "buddy" system but share all produce. They usually aim to put some time in every Sunday at least, and also have social events too.

There will be two Open Days ("Meet The Forkers") one in Feb and one in March where you can meet the team and find out how they organise things. If you are interested please email shelleyis@hotmail.co.uk
