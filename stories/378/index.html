Sheffield Live! TV first screening

Summer 2014

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Sheffield Live! TV (SLTV) will be broadcasting from this autumn on Freeview, Virgin cable and over the internet, and its first ever screening showcase came out at the beginning of August. Now online at Showreel #1 it gives a taste of some of the programmes to come, along with beautiful animated graphics based on scenes around Sheffield. The Zero Budget Film Festival and Loxley Valley Community Farm get a mention, as does underground music presenter Alex Deadman who describes SLTV as "a bit like a pirate radio station, but on TV".

SLTV springs from the long-running Sheffield Live! radio station, which gives a voice to people from all the communities of Sheffield (the broadcasters are volunteers). The earlier mini-video Imagine a TV channel... focusses on the fact that SLTV is locally owned and run for community benefit, with an invitation for local people to invest as 'community shareholders'.
