Cycling job vacancies

Closing date Thursday 23rd January 2014

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Heeley Development Trust is looking to recruit 4 people to join its cycling social enterprise, Recycle Bikes. The new roles will help the existing team deliver Cycleboost services on behalf of Sheffield and Barnsley Councils over the next 12 months. The vacancies are;

Fleet Manager 11 months full time
Bike Mechanic Plus 12 months full time
Dr Bike Mechanic 8 months 3 days a week
Admin Support 12 months 3 days a week

For more information about the jobs, and application forms, see www.sheffieldcycleboost.org

For more information about Recycle Bikes, see www.recyclebikes.co.uk
