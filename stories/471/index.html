Let's talk Current Affairs

From 22nd September 2016

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

A Current Affairs discussion course run by the Workers Educational Association moves to a new venue this autumn. If you know anyone who might be interested, please do pass this on.

Social, environmental, political and ethical topics (UK and worldwide) are discussed, as agreed with the class, over 11-sessions from Sept 22nd until 8th Dec 2016 (no meeting 27th Oct. - half-term). Enrolment is for the full term. Fees for 11 weeks £73 or FREE if on means-tested state benefits, or Working Tax Credit with income under £15,276. Enrol online at www.wea.org.uk/yh, or by phone to WEA Regional Office (Leeds) 0113 2453304 or on the day - if so, please contact first.

Thursdays, 10.30am-12.30pm, from 22 Sep 2016, Central United Reformed Church, 60 Norfolk Street S1 2JB
