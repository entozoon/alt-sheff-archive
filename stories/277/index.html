South Yorkshire cyclists have started an e-petition to the government urging that the 2015 trial of light railway (tram-train) between Rotherham and Sheffield should include a trial of carrying of bicycles. The light rail will be a test for future similar UK schemes so evaluation on transporting bikes could be nationally significant for integration of mass transport with cycling. In other countries light rail carries bicycles with no safety problems or conflicts of interests with wheelchairs, pushchairs or mobility scooters.

The petition asks the Minister for Transport to request Stagecoach, and the project sponsors, South Yorkshire Passenger Transport Executive, to change their decision not to allow the carriage of standard bicycles for these trials.
