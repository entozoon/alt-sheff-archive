Anti-nuclear bomb protests exploding

August 2014

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

The Atomic Weapons Establishment (AWE) at Aldermaston and Burghfield are the nuclear bomb factories which make Britain's nuclear warheads. Ahead of an expected Parliamentary vote in 2016 on whether the government should spend over Â£100 BILLION on a new nuclear weapons system, there will be increasing demonstrations at AWE calling for Trident and its replacement to be scrapped.

Hundreds of people have been knitting beautiful pieces of pink wool and crochet to connect the AWE factories with a seven mile pink scarf.

The scarf will make an appearance at the Peace Picnic on Sunday 3rd August 2014, from 2pm in Meersbrook Park, south of Sheffield, S8 9FL

On Saturday 9th August it will be at Aldermaston for the national demonstration on the 69th anniversary of the bombing of Nagasaki. Transport from Sheffield will be available.
