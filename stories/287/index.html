Transition Sheffield Awards announced

2012

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

Transition Sheffield Awards for 2012 were announced at an exciting and oversubscribed event at the end of November. The winners, selected through a non-competitive and inclusive process, were:

  • Energy and Environment: Sheffield Climate Alliance
  • Transport: Assist Bike Project
  • Reuse and recycling: Sheffield Freegle
  • Food growing: Grow St Marys
  • Food outlets: Burngreave Food Bank
  • Wealth and Livelihoods: Portland Works
  • Education: Meersbrook Walled Garden Outdoor Playgroup
  • Wellbeing: Sheffield Healthy Walks
  • Cultural: Creative Action Network
See also the impressive list of nominations received for the awards at www.transitionsheffield.org.uk
