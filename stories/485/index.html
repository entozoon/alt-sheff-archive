"Right to Remain" Sheffield group launches

October 2016

Share on

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Digg

For over 20 years, Right to Remain has been putting into practice mutual aid and practical solidarity to support refugees and migrants here in the UK. Sheffield people have been co-operating to help by sending aid to those suffering in Calais for quite some time. Now, Sheffield supporters have launched as a group, with a graphic based on the iconic 'Yorkshire Tea' box, 'subvertised' to read "Yorkshire Solidarity; Let's say it loud and clear: refugees and migrants are welcome here".

Right to Remain Sheffield group launch

Right to Remain's 'Toolkit', a guide for migrants, asylum seekers and their allies on the cruel, over-complicated British immigration and asylum systems.
