Sharrow Folk Festival

Fri 14 Nov 2014, 8pm-2am Hagglers Corner, 586 Queens Rd. S2 4DU

The first fundraiser for Sharrow Festival 2015, with a massive line up:

Early Cartographers: Acoustic, bouncy, alternative, occasionally sinister but normally upbeat collective.
www.earlycartographers.co.uk - YouTube - SoundCloud - Bandcamp - Facebook

Wreck The Place: Acoustic Punk with a Folk, Gypsyesque twist.
Bandcamp - www.labelship.com/artists/steve-allen - www.sameold.co.uk - SoundCloud - Facebook

The Miser On The Faerie Gump: Sheffields' premier purveyors of Folk-Metal!
Facebook

All Star Ceilidh Band: Details to follow.

Chrystine Moon: Singer-songwriter, political musician, feminist comedian.
YouTube - www.comedycv.co.uk/christinemoon - Facebook

Charlie Barker: It has been a busy few years for Charlie who made her TV debut on BBC1's 'The Next Big Thing', was a BBC Radio 2 Young Folk Awards semi finalist, released her first album Sleeping at the Station and second Ghosts & Heroes.
www.charliebarkermusic.com - YouTube - ReverbNation

12 Dead In Everett: Playing syndicate-folk, anarcho-Americana and old time rebel songs - music to fan the flames of discontent and tell the Boss to go to hell. Guitar, banjo, accordion, and sweet harmonies of reason in a world deaf to exploitation. All members or fellow travellers of the anarcho-syndicalist IWW Union ('The Wobblies').
SoundCloud Facebook

Andy Whitehouse (The Silver Darlings): Solo set from The Silver Darlings' singer/guitarist. A varied collection playing the songs of downbeat romantics, helpless optimists, wandering forth glass in hand...
ReverbNation Facebook - Facebook-Andy Whitehouse

Paul Pearson: Affectionately known as the Steel Town Troubadour, Paul, from Sheffield, specializes in taking songs from excellent but obscure songwriters and turning them into PP Originals.
ReverbNation

Break a Leg! (Chris Rust & Karen Hisom): Playing a mixture of folk, jazz and blues songs, chosen for their emotional and narrative qualities and the opportunity for voice, clarinet and guitar to work together.
www.breakalegmusic.wordpress.com - Facebook

Vinna Bee: Alt contemporary folk: A solo, a collective, a duo, a band, a project... But mostly the single visions of Sian Vinna Bevan helped out by various members of her musical collective.
www.vinnabee.com - Bandcamp - YouTube

Syd Hux: Performs solo on-stage, using a loop station, mini grand-piano and vocal effects to build up a textured sound.
YouTube - Bandcamp - Facebook

Westfield and Halfway Save Our Community meeting

Fri 14 Nov 2014, 10am, Outside Westfield Health Centre

Westfield and Halfway Tenants and Residents Association are protesting about the loss of the district Post office, Doctors (closing 31st December), Community unity (in danger of going) and there is no library, not even a church. If Westfield and Halfway is your community, get together and make a difference.

For any more details call 07772 887562 or email aprilellis.1968@outlook.com
