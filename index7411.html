Two day workshop: Making Rustic Jointed Stools

Wed 13 & Thu 14 Aug 2014, 9.30-4pm, Woodland Discovery Centre, Ecclesall Woods, Abbey Lane S7 2QZ

A course to introduce greenwood craft and the basic skill required to make and take away a rustic jointed stool. Run by Low Impact Living Initiative (LILI) which offers publications, free factsheets, a huge database and forum, products, services and courses in everything from co-operatives to wildfood camping. In Sheffield, courses are often held at the Woodland Discovery Centre, an educational facility in the largest ancient woodland in South Yorkshire. Full course details are on the LILI website. "Cut off" - an exhibition of photos

All of Aug 2014, Weds-Sat 11am-4pm, Bank St. Arts

"Cut off" is an exhibition of photos taken by people who have been refused asylum, exploring their circumstances, experiences, hopes and frustrations.

It forms part of a general exhibition on the theme "Cut", at Bank Street Arts, 32-40 Bank Street, Sheffield, S1 2DS.
