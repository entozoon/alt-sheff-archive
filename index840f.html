Event 3 for Green Homes Sheffield

Sat 3-Mon 5 May 2014, 11am-5pm

Green Homes Sheffield aims to give householders ideas and confidence on making their homes more energy efficient. A range of homes across the city will show features like insulation, draught-proofing, solar panels, heat pumps, energy-efficient windows and extensions, and different levels of carbon saving. People who have made a good start on the path to a low carbon home are opening their homes to the public to enable others to learn from the energy-saving improvements made and inspire them to make changes themselves.

The initiative is part of national network www.greenopenhomes.net. This is one of 4 events during April and May.

For more details see www.greenhomessheffield.net or email jenny.patient@regather.net or call or text 07760 174335.
